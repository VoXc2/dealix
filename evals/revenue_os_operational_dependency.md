# Revenue OS Operational Dependency Eval Pack

## Objective

Measure whether Dealix is operationally embedded, not just technically impressive.

## Minimum Eval Dimensions

1. Workflow success quality
2. Governance compliance
3. Reliability under retry/failure
4. Business impact signal

## Required Monthly Outputs

- Eval report ID
- Compliance pass/fail summary
- Reliability trend
- ROI-linked interpretation

## Blocking Condition

Do not scale to new tenant if governance compliance or audit completeness is below threshold.
