# Logo Usage — `<PARTNER>` × Dealix

The Dealix wordmark may be used in **co-sell** contexts only. This is
not white-label permission.

## Allowed

- Inclusion of the Dealix wordmark as the SECONDARY mark on co-sell
  pages, proposals, and pitch decks.
- Inclusion of Dealix trust badges (see
  `https://dealix.example.com/assets/badges/`).
- The phrase **"Powered by the Dealix Governed AI Operations Doctrine"**
  with a link to `https://dealix.example.com/api/v1/dealix-promise`.

## Disallowed

- Implying Dealix endorses, certifies, or warrants the partner's work
  beyond what the doctrine adoption checklist establishes.
- Removing the doctrine version label from any badge or banner.
- Using the Dealix wordmark as the primary visual element on a page,
  proposal, or deck.

## Required Citation

Any public artifact using a Dealix badge must cite the doctrine
version. Suggested footer line:

> Doctrine: Governed AI Operations Doctrine v1.0.0 ·
> `https://dealix.example.com/api/v1/doctrine?version=v1.0.0`

## Revocation

Logo permission is granted by signing the adoption checklist. It is
revoked by:
- Violating any of the eleven non-negotiables.
- Failing to update the doctrine version when bumping.
- Misrepresenting the relationship as white-label.

Suspension policy lives in
`docs/40_partners/PARTNER_SUSPENSION_POLICY.md`.
