# Doctrine Adoption Checklist — `<PARTNER>`

A partner that wants to co-sell Dealix's Governed AI Operations under
their own brand commits to all eleven below. Sign once. The signature
is published as part of the partner's Trust Pack.

## The Eleven

- [ ] **1. Source Passport before AI use.** No AI run operates on data
      that does not have a recorded source, owner, and lawful basis.

- [ ] **2. Human approval before external action.** No external action
      (send, publish, transact) is executed by an AI without an
      explicit human approval gate.

- [ ] **3. Governance Runtime before client-facing output.** Every
      output destined for a client passes through a policy decision
      (ALLOW, DRAFT_ONLY, REQUIRE_APPROVAL, REDACT, BLOCK, ESCALATE)
      before release.

- [ ] **4. Proof Pack before any claim.** No public or commercial
      claim is made without a Proof Pack that links the claim to a
      verified value event.

- [ ] **5. No scraping.** No automated collection of data from
      third-party surfaces without that party's lawful authorization.

- [ ] **6. No cold WhatsApp.** No mass or automated outreach via
      WhatsApp to non-consenting recipients.

- [ ] **7. No LinkedIn automation.** No scripted scraping,
      connection-spam, or DM-spam on LinkedIn. Human-initiated
      messages only.

- [ ] **8. No guaranteed sales claims.** No promise of revenue, leads,
      or outcomes that is not backed by a Proof Pack from a comparable
      delivered engagement.

- [ ] **9. No agent without identity.** Every AI agent has a
      documented identity card listing its owner, allowed tools, and
      kill switch.

- [ ] **10. Capital Asset registration before invoice.** No commercial
      delivery is invoiced before its associated capital asset (Proof
      Pack, Trust Pack, dataset, retainer entry) is registered.

- [ ] **11. Verifiable, not merely trusted.** Every commitment above
      is locked by at least one automated test that fails if the
      commitment is violated.
