# Enterprise Ready Definition

Dealix is enterprise-ready when:
- Multi-tenant operational data is persisted in Postgres.
- Approval/rollback/policy-edit flows are auditable and tested.
- Agent mesh, contracts, safety, value, and self-evolving gates are wired end-to-end.
- CI release gates are mandatory and green.
- Admin control surfaces exist for runs, approvals, agents, safety, value, and proposals.
