# Client Pilot Ready Criteria

Minimum pilot bar:

- 1 tenant configured.
- 3 users + 2 roles.
- 1 governed sales workflow.
- 1 registered sales agent in mesh.
- 1 assurance contract for external action.
- 1 approval rule + 1 safety rule.
- 1 measured value metric with `source_ref`.
- 1 trace walkthrough and rollback drill.
