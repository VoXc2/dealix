# 11 Non-Negotiables

A team adopting the Governed AI Operations Doctrine commits to all
eleven of the following. These are not aspirations. They are operating
constraints.

1. **Source Passport before AI use.** No AI run operates on data that
   does not have a recorded source, owner, and lawful basis.

2. **Human approval before external action.** No external action (send,
   publish, transact) is executed by an AI without an explicit human
   approval gate.

3. **Governance Runtime before client-facing output.** Every output
   destined for a client passes through a policy decision (ALLOW,
   DRAFT_ONLY, REQUIRE_APPROVAL, REDACT, BLOCK, ESCALATE) before
   release.

4. **Proof Pack before any claim.** No public or commercial claim is
   made without a Proof Pack that links the claim to a verified value
   event.

5. **No scraping.** No automated collection of data from third-party
   surfaces without that party's lawful authorization.

6. **No cold WhatsApp.** No mass or automated outreach via WhatsApp
   to non-consenting recipients.

7. **No LinkedIn automation.** No scripted scraping, connection-spam,
   or DM-spam on LinkedIn. Human-initiated messages only.

8. **No guaranteed sales claims.** No promise of revenue, leads, or
   outcomes that is not backed by a Proof Pack from a comparable
   delivered engagement.

9. **No agent without identity.** Every AI agent has a documented
   identity card listing its owner, allowed tools, and kill switch.

10. **Capital Asset registration before invoice.** No commercial
    delivery is invoiced before its associated capital asset (Proof
    Pack, Trust Pack, dataset, retainer entry) is registered.

11. **Verifiable, not merely trusted.** Every commitment above is
    locked by at least one automated test that fails if the
    commitment is violated.

## Adoption

A team that publishes its adoption of this doctrine should be able to
point to:

- the test that locks each commitment, **or**
- a documented exception with a sunset date and a compensating control.

## Refusing Adoption

A team that refuses any of the eleven is welcome to use other
frameworks. This doctrine is not for everyone. It is for teams that
believe verification is part of the product.
