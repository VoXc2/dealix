# Dealix Transformation Maturity Model

## Levels

1. **L1 Assisted**: workflow suggestions, human execution.
2. **L2 Governed Assist**: approvals + traceability + controlled execution.
3. **L3 Governed Autonomy**: policy-bound autonomous steps with rollback.
4. **L4 Organizational Intelligence**: cross-function orchestration with measurable impact.
5. **L5 Infrastructure Grade**: enterprise-wide operating layer with continuous eval gates.

## Graduation criteria

- policy compliance rate
- approval integrity
- execution recoverability
- measurable business impact
