## Training Objectives

By the end of training, the client team should be able to:

1. Use the Revenue Command Room for daily pipeline review
2. Process the draft approval queue independently
3. Navigate and update the Company Brain
4. Understand the WhatsApp integration and its safety defaults
5. Generate basic reports and proof packs

## Training Sessions

### Session 1: Revenue Command Room
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes

- [ ] Pipeline overview and deal stages
- [ ] Draft message queue: review, approve, reject
- [ ] Daily actions: what to do each morning
- [ ] Booking flow: how inbound leads arrive
- [ ] WhatsApp overview panel

**Key message:** This is your daily cockpit. Spend 10-15 minutes here every morning.

### Session 2: Company Brain
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

- [ ] Creating and reviewing signals
- [ ] Logging decisions with context (owner, metric, assumption)
- [ ] Risk register: how to flag and track risks
- [ ] Opportunity register: how to capture and evaluate
- [ ] Weekly brain review cadence

**Key message:** This keeps your strategic context visible and disciplined.

### Session 3: WhatsApp and Communication
**Duration:** 30 minutes

- [ ] How draft-only mode works
- [ ] Template management
- [ ] Conversation tracking
- [ ] What happens when you approve a message
- [ ] Safety defaults and why they matter

**Key message:** Nothing goes out without your approval. That is the design.

### Session 4: Operations and Reporting
**Duration:** 30 minutes

- [ ] Running daily operational commands
- [ ] Understanding the production check
- [ ] Where to find reports
- [ ] Escalation: what to do when something does not work

## SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)

### Daily SOP
1. Open Command Room
2. Review pipeline changes
3. Process draft queue (approve / reject / edit)
4. Check WhatsApp inbox for new conversations
5. Log any new signals in Brain OS

### Weekly SOP
1. Review Brain OS: signals, decisions, risks
2. Update opportunity statuses
3. Run governance check
4. Review proof pack progress (if in active engagement)

### Monthly SOP
1. Revenue scorecard review
2. Compliance check review
3. Client delivery progress update
4. Operating system optimization recommendations

## Handoff Checklist

- [ ] Client can log into the system independently
- [ ] Client knows the daily workflow
- [ ] Client can approve / reject drafts
- [ ] Client understands what is automated vs. manual
- [ ] Client knows who to contact for support
- [ ] Documentation has been shared (links to relevant docs)
- [ ] Follow-up session scheduled for 1 week after handoff

## Support Plan

| Support Level | Response Time | Channel |
|--------------|---------------|---------|
| Critical (system down) | 4 hours | WhatsApp / Phone |
| Standard (question/issue) | 24 hours | Email / WhatsApp |
| Enhancement request | Next review | Email |
