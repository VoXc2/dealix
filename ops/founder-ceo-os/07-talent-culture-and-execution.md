# Talent Culture and Execution

## Goal
Scale the team without losing speed, ownership, or quality.

## Operating principles

- One owner per outcome.
- Written decisions beat memory.
- Customer evidence beats opinion.
- Small teams with clear ownership beat large unclear teams.
- Speed is valuable only when quality and trust stay intact.

## Execution cadence

- Daily: top blockers and owner updates.
- Weekly: business review and decision log.
- Monthly: team capacity and role gaps.
- Quarterly: org design and leadership gaps.

## Anti-patterns

- Hiring before demand is validated.
- Meetings without decisions.
- Tasks without owners.
- Strategy without tradeoffs.
- Roadmap without customer evidence.

## Required artifacts

- Role scorecards
- Operating principles
- Meeting cadence
- Decision log
- Performance expectations
- Hiring plan
