## Strategic no-list

For the next 90 days, Dealix should avoid:

- Building features not tied to activation, revenue, retention, or compliance.
- Serving too many ICPs at once.
- Custom work that cannot become reusable product capability.
- Vanity partnerships without pipeline or credibility.
- AI claims that cannot be explained, audited, or measured.

## Decision filter

Before accepting a major opportunity, answer:

1. Does this deepen the wedge?
2. Does this improve the product platform?
3. Does this create reusable data or proof?
4. Does this shorten sales cycle or increase trust?
5. Does this create margin or strategic learning?
6. What must be stopped to make room?
