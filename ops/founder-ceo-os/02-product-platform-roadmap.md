# Product Platform Roadmap

## Objective
Turn Dealix from a launch product into a durable platform.

## Product thesis

Dealix should become the operating layer for compliant B2B revenue intelligence, account research, opportunity scoring, outreach preparation, workflow approval, customer proof, and executive reporting.

## Platform layers

| Layer | Purpose | 12-month outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Data ingestion | Bring target accounts, CRM data, campaign data, proof events | Reliable account and opportunity graph |
| Intelligence | Score, explain, prioritize, summarize | Transparent decision support |
| Workflow | Assign, approve, execute, review | Daily operating system for GTM teams |
| Governance | Controls, audit trail, data boundaries | Enterprise trust and compliance |
| Reporting | Founder and client dashboards | Evidence-backed executive narrative |
| Integrations | CRM, email, calendar, analytics, data sources | Lower switching cost and stronger stickiness |

## Roadmap horizons

### Horizon 1: Launch readiness
- Stable core flows
- Manual review and approval
- Basic dashboards
- Proof pack support
- CRM discipline

### Horizon 2: Workflow depth
- Account and opportunity graph
- Approval workbench
- Better scoring and explanations
- Customer onboarding analytics
- Retention and expansion signals

### Horizon 3: Platform expansion
- Partner workflows
- Multi-team permissions
- Advanced audit trail
- Executive reporting automation
- API and integration layer

## Feature intake decision rules

A feature is approved only if it supports at least one of:

- Activation
- Revenue
- Retention
- Compliance or trust
- Workflow frequency
- Strategic data asset
- Enterprise readiness

## Product scorecard

| Metric | Definition | Owner | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Activation rate | Accounts reaching first meaningful value | Product | Weekly |
| Time to value | Time from onboarding start to first value | CS/Product | Weekly |
| Weekly active teams | Active customer teams using core flow | Product | Weekly |
| Workflow completion rate | Tasks completed inside Dealix | Product | Weekly |
| Explainability coverage | Percent of AI-assisted decisions with explanation | AI/Product | Weekly |
| Critical defects | P0/P1 product defects | Engineering | Daily |

## Product operating cadence

- Weekly product review: metrics, blockers, customer proof, roadmap tradeoffs.
- Monthly roadmap review: what to build, stop, delay, or convert to manual process.
- Quarterly platform review: moat, integration, enterprise readiness, data asset growth.
