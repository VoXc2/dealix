# Executive Command Center

## Purpose
Create one source of truth for the founder and executive team.

## CEO weekly questions

1. What changed in the business this week?
2. What is the single biggest constraint?
3. What decision must be made now?
4. What metric is lying or incomplete?
5. What customer evidence did we learn from?
6. What risk is growing quietly?
7. What should be stopped immediately?
8. What should be doubled down on?

## Operating rules

- One owner per critical path.
- One source of truth per metric.
- One decision record per major tradeoff.
- One weekly narrative that explains numbers, not just reports them.
- No priority without resource allocation.
- No launch claim without proof.
- No AI automation without review and fallback.

## Friday board-style narrative

Use this format every week:

1. Executive summary
2. KPI movement
3. Customer evidence
4. Product progress
5. Revenue progress
6. Cash and runway
7. Key risks
8. Decisions made
9. Decisions needed
10. Next week top 5 outcomes
