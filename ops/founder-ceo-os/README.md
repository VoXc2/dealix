# Dealix Founder CEO OS

This directory is the executive operating system for Dealix after launch readiness.

It converts founder thinking into durable company systems across strategy, product, AI governance, enterprise sales, finance, partnerships, talent, board readiness, and resilience.

## How to use this pack

1. Start with `00-executive-command-center.md`.
2. Review `01-strategy-and-moat.md` and decide the 12-month strategic thesis.
3. Use `02-product-platform-roadmap.md` to translate strategy into platform bets.
4. Use `03-ai-governance-and-model-risk.md` before shipping AI-driven workflows.
5. Use `04-enterprise-sales-and-procurement.md` to prepare for serious B2B buyers.
6. Use `05-finance-board-and-investor-system.md` before fundraising or board reviews.
7. Use `06-partnerships-and-ecosystem.md` for Saudi/GCC ecosystem leverage.
8. Use `07-talent-culture-and-execution.md` to scale the team without losing speed.
9. Use `08-scenario-planning-and-resilience.md` to stay alive under uncertainty.
10. Use `09-artifact-index.md` as the master list of required company artifacts.
11. Use `10-source-and-license-notes.md` for attribution and reuse notes.

## Weekly CEO cadence

- Monday: strategy, top outcomes, cash, pipeline, launch risks.
- Tuesday: product, customer evidence, AI quality, reliability.
- Wednesday: sales pipeline, enterprise deals, partnerships.
- Thursday: team, hiring, execution blockers, operating debt.
- Friday: board-style narrative, metrics, decisions, next week.

## Non-negotiable rule

Every executive system must produce one of these artifacts:

- Decision record
- Scorecard
- Dashboard
- Playbook
- Policy
- Checklist
- Customer proof
- Financial model
- Risk register
- Roadmap

No artifact means the work is not operational yet.
