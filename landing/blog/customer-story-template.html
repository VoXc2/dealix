Customer story template — pilot to renewal in 90 days

12 May 2026 · Sami Assiri

Every Saudi enterprise renewal hinges on four trust gates: procurement, security, operations, value. Below is the canonical case-study skeleton we use at Dealix; clone it for your own commercial team.

Header block

Customer: <Trade name + sector + size>
Started: <Pilot start date>
Renewed: <Renewal date + term>
ACV: <SAR X,XXX,XXX>

Gate 1 — Procurement

Billing entity: <Dealix For AI Co. (SA)>
Invoice format: ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoice (UBL 2.1)
Payment method: SAR via Moyasar (card) + Tabby split-payment optional
Sub-processors disclosed: Anthropic, Moyasar, ZATCA, Resend, Sentry, PostHog

Gate 2 — Security

SSO: SAML via WorkOS
SCIM: Provisioned 47 users at go-live
Audit log: Exported to customer's S3 + Datadog
Data residency: <In-region or specified>
BYOK: <Enabled with customer KMS key or N/A>

Gate 3 — Operations

Onboarding wizard: Completed in 4 steps, 38 minutes
Seats: <X> sales / <Y> managers / <Z> admin
Vertical bundle: <e.g. real-estate>
Support channel: Plain ticketing, Knock notification routing

Gate 4 — Value

Outcome by day 14: <e.g. first AI-drafted proposal sent>
Outcome by day 30: <e.g. first deal closed via Dealix-sourced lead>
Outcome by day 60: <e.g. + N % qualified leads vs prior CRM>
Outcome by day 90: <e.g. payback period reached, renewal signed>
