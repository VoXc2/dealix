## Key Rules

1. **Gross margin must stay above 60%** — If it drops, raise prices or reduce delivery time
2. **Pre-collect payment** — 50% upfront minimum for all engagements
3. **Track CAC religiously** — If CAC > 20% of LTV, fix the funnel
4. **Weekly cash check** — Know exactly how much cash is collected vs. invoiced
