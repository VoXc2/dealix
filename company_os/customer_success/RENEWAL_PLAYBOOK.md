# Renewal & Expansion Playbook

Turn a delivered engagement into recurring revenue — honestly, with proof, without
pressure.

---

## Triggers

- first successful workflow shipped
- weekly value proof delivered (≥ L3)
- positive client feedback
- 21–30 days after delivery
- a new department / lead source / campaign appears

---

## The rule that protects trust

> Every renewal or expansion draft must **cite delivered value with evidence ≥ L3**,
> suggest exactly **one** next step, avoid pressure, and **require approval**.

A draft that cites no delivered value is blocked at review. Health records below L3
cannot be `renewal_ready` (enforced in `tests/test_renewal_and_payment_rules.py`).

---

## Expansion ladder

```
Diagnostic → Follow-up Recovery → AI Revenue Ops Starter
   → Full Revenue OS → Monthly Optimization → Custom Company OS
   → multi-department rollout
```

Map the next rung to the value already proven, not to the biggest possible deal.

---

## Draft contents

delivered value (with evidence level) · the one recommended next step · scope ·
timeline · price range (→ founder approval) · risks · clear opt-out.

---

## What renewal is NOT

- Not a guaranteed-results pitch.
- Not a pressure sequence.
- Not auto-sent — it is a draft for founder approval.
