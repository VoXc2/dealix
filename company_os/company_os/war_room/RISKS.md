## Escalation Criteria
- Critical risk with probability increase → Immediate founder attention
- Any data/privacy risk → Stop all AI operations until resolved
- Cash runway below 30 days → Emergency fundraising mode
- No clients by day 30 → Pivot messaging or pricing

## Review Schedule
- Weekly: Every Sunday during War Room review
- Trigger-based: Any risk score increase
- Monthly: Full risk reassessment
