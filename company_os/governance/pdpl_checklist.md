# SDAIA PDPL Compliance Checklist

## Overview

Dealix processes client business data for revenue intelligence purposes. This checklist ensures compliance with Saudi Data Protection Personal Data Law (PDPL) issued by SDAIA.

| Right | Implementation |
|-------|---------------|
| Right to know | ✅ Intake form discloses all processing |
| Right to access | ✅ Client can request their data |
| Right to correction | ✅ Client can request corrections |
| Right to destruction | ✅ 90-day auto-deletion + on-request |
