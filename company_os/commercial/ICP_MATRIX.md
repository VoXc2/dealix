# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Matrix

Who Dealix sells to first, and who to decline. The pattern across every primary ICP:
**recurring inbound leads that leak in follow-up** — not a lead-generation problem, a
revenue-operations problem.

---

## Primary ICPs

| # | Segment | Core pain | First offer | Proof speed |
|---|---------|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Marketing agencies | Scattered lead follow-up across campaigns | Revenue Intelligence Sprint | Fast |
| 2 | Training companies | WhatsApp/form enrollment inquiries lost | Follow-up Recovery | Fast |
| 3 | Clinics | Appointment inquiries / no-shows / follow-up | Follow-up Recovery | Medium |
| 4 | Real-estate teams | Slow lead response, viewing follow-up gaps | Revenue Leakage Diagnostic | Fast |
| 5 | Recruitment agencies | Candidate/client follow-up gaps | Follow-up Recovery | Medium |
| 6 | Professional services | Inquiry → proposal leakage | Proposal + Proof | Medium |
| 7 | Local SaaS / service firms | Sales ops + reporting gaps | AI Revenue Ops Starter | Medium |
| 8 | Education providers | Admissions follow-up | Follow-up Recovery | Medium |
| 9 | Logistics / service ops | Quote + follow-up workflows | Revenue Leakage Diagnostic | Medium |
| 10 | Restaurant groups | Customer/campaign inquiry follow-up | Readiness Scan → Diagnostic | Slow |

---

## Scoring dimensions (0–100)

Lead-flow maturity · sales-process maturity · urgency · budget fit · decision-maker
access · delivery complexity (inverse) · proof speed · compliance sensitivity
(inverse). Higher = prioritize.

---

## Disqualifiers — do **not** prioritize if the prospect…

- has no recurring lead flow
- has no decision-maker access
- has no ability to pay
- wants spam / mass blasting
- wants **guaranteed** sales claims
- refuses the approval-first process
- refuses basic data/privacy requirements
- asks for illegal / terms-violating scraping
- wants heavy unpaid custom work
- presents delivery risk too high for the price

Disqualified ≠ rude. Decline respectfully, leave the door open, move on. See
`../commercial/WALK_AWAY_RULES` philosophy and `RISKS.md`.
