# Conversion Scorecard (Dealix)

## المؤشرات
- Booked meetings (this week, this month)
- Proposal sent
- Proposal → close conversion %
- Average time in stage
- Lost deal reasons distribution

## العتبات
- Conversion < 20% → off_track
- Conversion 20–35% → watch
- Conversion > 35% → on_track

## الإجراء
- off_track: tighten close criteria + revisit offer matching
- watch: focus on top 3 accounts
- on_track: expand to next segment

## No vanity
- لا عدد نقرات
- لا عدد متابعين
- لا عدد إيميلات مفتوحة
