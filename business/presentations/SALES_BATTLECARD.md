## Objection Handling

### "We already have a CRM."
**Response:** Great. Dealix doesn't replace your CRM — it sits on top of daily operations. CRMs store history. Dealix tells you what to do today and makes sure follow-up actually happens.

### "This sounds expensive."
**Response:** We start with a 5,000 SAR Diagnostic Sprint. That's a low-risk way to see exactly where revenue is leaking and what operating system to build first. Most companies find the value within the first sprint.

### "We're not ready for AI."
**Response:** Perfect. Dealix is human-in-the-loop by design. AI drafts, you approve. Nothing happens automatically. You stay in control while getting the productivity benefits.

### "Can you guarantee ROI?"
**Response:** We don't make fabricated ROI claims. What we guarantee is a reviewable system: you'll see your pipeline clearly, your follow-up will become consistent, and your decisions will be documented. The revenue impact follows from those operational improvements.

### "How long until we're live?"
**Response:** Diagnostic Sprint: 3–5 days. First operating system (Command Room or Brain): 1 sprint (1–2 weeks). Full platform: 4–6 weeks depending on scope.

### "What about compliance?"
**Response:** Compliance is built in, not bolted on. We're aligned with PDPL and SDAIA AI Ethics. All data stays in Saudi Arabia. Every sensitive action is logged and approval-gated.

## Discovery Questions

### Revenue
1. How do leads currently arrive?
2. What does your follow-up process look like today?
3. How many deals have you lost to slow follow-up in the last 6 months?
4. What tools do you use for pipeline tracking?

### Decisions
1. How do you currently document important decisions?
2. Where do strategic discussions happen? (WhatsApp, meetings, email)
3. How do you track risks and opportunities?
4. What happens when a key team member leaves?

### Operations
1. Who manages client messages day-to-day?
2. How do you prioritize what to work on each morning?
3. What's your current weekly review process?
4. How do you ensure nothing falls through the cracks?

### Compliance
1. How do you handle client data today?
2. Are you aware of PDPL requirements?
3. How do you govern AI or automation usage?
4. Do you have audit logs for sensitive actions?

## Sales Process

### Step 1: Discovery Call (30 min)
- Understand pain points
- Identify decision makers
- Determine fit
- Qualify budget and timeline

### Step 2: Diagnostic Sprint (3–5 days, 5,000 SAR)
- Operating diagnosis
- Revenue leakage review
- Follow-up audit
- Priority recommendations
- Blueprint for first OS

### Step 3: Proposal (1–2 days)
- Scope of work
- Timeline
- Pricing
- Success criteria

### Step 4: Build Sprint (1–2 weeks)
- Implement Command Room or Brain
- Configure workflows
- Train team
- Deliver proof pack

### Step 5: Monthly Partner (ongoing)
- Weekly operating reviews
- KPI tracking
- Continuous optimization
- Compliance review

## Red Flags (Don't Sell)

- Looking for fully automated outbound
- Not willing to review drafts
- Unrealistic ROI expectations
- No decision-maker access
- No budget or timeline
- Not interested in compliance
- Expecting magic without operational change

## Green Flags (Prioritize)

- Founder actively involved
- Acknowledges follow-up problem
- Uses WhatsApp for business
- Has leads but inconsistent process
- Values compliance and trust
- Willing to start small (Diagnostic Sprint)
- Can make quick decisions
