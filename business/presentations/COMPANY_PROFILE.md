## What We Do

Dealix provides **AI Operating Systems** for Saudi B2B companies. Unlike chatbots or traditional CRMs, Dealix is a daily operating platform that connects:

| Operating System | Purpose |
|------------------|---------|
| **Revenue Command Room** | Daily cockpit for pipeline, drafts, approvals, and founder actions |
| **Company Brain** | Structured decision discipline with signals, risks, and opportunities |
| **WhatsApp Follow-up** | Governed client communication with human approval |
| **AI Trust & Compliance** | PDPL + SDAIA alignment with auditability |
| **Client Delivery** | Standardized delivery lifecycle from intake to proof |

---

## The Problem We Solve

Saudi B2B companies have leads, conversations, spreadsheets, and tools — but they lack:

- **Daily clarity:** Founders don't know what to do next
- **Follow-up discipline:** Leads go cold due to delayed responses
- **Decision visibility:** Important decisions live in chat threads
- **Pipeline truth:** Stage status is often guesswork
- **Compliance confidence:** AI usage without governance creates risk

### Impact
- Lost revenue from poor follow-up
- Wasted founder time on low-value searching
- Missed opportunities due to weak prioritization
- Reputational risk from ungoverned outbound communication

---

## Our Solution

Dealix is a **daily operating system**, not another tool.

**Key principles:**
1. **AI suggests, humans approve** - Nothing happens automatically without review
2. **Daily operating rhythm** - 15-minute morning Command Room review replaces hours of searching
3. **Strategic + tactical** - Revenue execution and decision discipline in one platform
4. **Compliance-first** - Built with PDPL and SDAIA in mind from day one
5. **Trust over speed** - Approval gates protect reputation and data

## Compliance & Governance

Dealix is designed with compliance at its core:

- **PDPL (Personal Data Protection Law)** - Data minimization, purpose limitation, retention controls
- **SDAIA AI Ethics** - Human oversight, transparency, accountability, fairness
- **Audit Logging** - All actions recorded and reviewable
- **Approval Gates** - Sensitive actions require human review
- **Data Residency** - Saudi Arabia data storage
