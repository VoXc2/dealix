## Slide 6: Product in Action

### Morning Routine: 15 Minutes in Command Room

1. **Pipeline snapshot** → see what's moved
2. **Draft queue** → approve/reject/edit AI-generated messages
3. **WhatsApp inbox** → check conversations needing attention
4. **Daily actions** → know exactly what to do today

### Weekly Routine: 30 Minutes in Brain

1. **Signals review** → what changed in market/customer landscape
2. **Decisions log** → status of strategic calls
3. **Risk register** → what's threatening progress
4. **Opportunities** → what to prioritize
5. **Action ownership** → who's doing what

**Result:** Founders move from reactive to reviewable operations.
