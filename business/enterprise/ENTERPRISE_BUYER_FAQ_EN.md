# Enterprise Buyer FAQ (EN)

## 1. Is Dealix a CRM?
No. Dealix is the operating layer between your CRM and outcomes. We fill the CRM with verified drafts, follow-up queues, and proof reports.

## 2. Do you support SSO?
V1: no. V2+: planned (SAML + OIDC).

## 3. Do you have 2FA?
V1: no. V2+: planned.

## 4. Where is the data?
V1: KSA only. Postgres with at-rest encryption + TLS in transit.

## 5. Can we keep our data on our side?
Yes. CSV export is available. A read API is planned.

## 6. What is the pricing model?
Setup + Monthly. Setup refundable within 14 days. Monthly cancellable with 30-day notice.

## 7. Do you guarantee ROI?
No. We guarantee workflow + cadence + proof. Numbers come from the client.

## 8. How long is onboarding?
14 days for Revenue OS / Delivery OS / Review OS. 30 days for Custom Enterprise.

## 9. Can you serve our industry?
Yes — agency, training, clinic, real estate, logistics, consulting, B2B each have a specific play.

## 10. Do you have customer references?
No fake testimonials. We have case study templates and demo files. After signing, we co-produce a case study with the client's consent.
