# Service Level Boundaries (Dealix)

## What we commit to
- 24-hour response to any support request
- Weekly proof report on every active client
- 14-day onboarding for tier 1-4
- 30-day onboarding for tier 5 (custom enterprise)

## What we do NOT commit to
- Specific revenue numbers
- Specific conversion rates
- Specific lead counts
- "100% success"
- "ROI of X%"

## Outages
- P0: 1-hour response, 4-hour resolution target
- P1: 4-hour response, 24-hour resolution
- P2: 1-day response
- P3: 1-week response

## What we own
- Our code, our scripts, our data layer
- Our AI providers (errors fall back to deterministic)
- Our documentation

## What the client owns
- Their data accuracy
- Their team adoption
- Their customer relationships
- Their compliance with PDPL for any data they bring
