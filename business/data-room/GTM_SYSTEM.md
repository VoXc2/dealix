# GTM System (Dealix)

## Channels
1. Inbound (Diagnostic Sprint form)
2. Outbound (manual research + drafts)
3. Partner referrals
4. LinkedIn / X (founder-led)
5. Industry events

## Cadence
- 1 LinkedIn post / day
- 1 X post / day
- 1 industry case-card / week
- 1 proof report / week
- 1 long-form / month

- Sales machine (`business/sales-machine/`)
- Content engine (`scripts/generate_content_calendar.py`)
- Campaign OS (`scripts/generate_campaign_pack.py`)
- Daily operator (`scripts/dealix_daily_operator.py`)

## KPIs
- Drafts generated / day
- Drafts approved / day
- Proposals sent / week
- Wins / month
- MRR added / month
