# Revenue OS Readiness Scorecard (EN)
> 0-100 — how ready are you for a Revenue OS install?

## Lead routing (0-25)
- 0-5: no rules
- 6-15: undocumented rules
- 16-25: clear rules with owners

## Outreach drafts (0-25)
- 0-5: no drafts
- 6-15: drafts not reviewed
- 16-25: drafts + review queue + approval

## Follow-up cadence (0-25)
- 0-5: no follow-up
- 6-15: sporadic follow-up
- 16-25: clear weekly cadence

## Proof reporting (0-25)
- 0-5: no reports
- 6-15: sporadic reports
- 16-25: weekly proof report sent

## Total
- 0-40: need diagnostic before OS
- 41-70: Revenue OS is a fit
- 71-100: ready to install
