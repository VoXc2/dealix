# Workflow Leakage Checklist (EN)
> 15 points that show where your operations are leaking.

## Sales
- [ ] No one responds to inbound leads in <60 min
- [ ] No first-response draft ready
- [ ] No follow-up after first contact
- [ ] No clear routing rule

## Delivery
- [ ] No weekly report to the client
- [ ] No single owner per deliverable
- [ ] No audit log on external actions
- [ ] No proof report before invoice

## Reputation
- [ ] No replies to Google reviews
- [ ] No appointment reminders
- [ ] No no-show system

## Decision
- [ ] No fixed weekly review
- [ ] No 5 KPIs with owners
- [ ] No daily standup
- [ ] Founder is in most decisions
