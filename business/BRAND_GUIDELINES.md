## Typography

### Font Families
- **Headings:** Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- **Body:** Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- **Arabic:** Noto Sans Arabic, system-ui, sans-serif

### Type Scale
| Style | Size | Weight | Usage |
|-------|------|--------|-------|
| Display | 48–64px | 800 | Hero headlines |
| H1 | 36–40px | 700 | Page titles |
| H2 | 28–32px | 600 | Section headings |
| H3 | 20–24px | 600 | Card titles |
| H4 | 18px | 600 | Subsection headings |
| Body | 16px | 400 | Paragraphs |
| Small | 14px | 400 | Captions, metadata |
| Tiny | 12px | 500 | Labels, tags |

### Typography Rules
- Use bold weight sparingly
- Maintain strong contrast for readability
- Arabic text should be slightly larger (1.1x) than English equivalent
- Line height: 1.5 for body, 1.2 for headings

## Logo Usage

### Logo Mark
The Dealix logo consists of the wordmark "Dealix" in a modern sans-serif typeface.

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "D".

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 120px width
- Print: 40mm width

### Color Variations
- **Primary:** Dealix Teal on white
- **Dark:** Dark on light backgrounds
- **Light:** White on dark/teal backgrounds

### Incorrect Usage
- Don't stretch or distort the logo
- Don't change the logo colors
- Don't add effects (shadows, gradients) unless approved
- Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds

## Voice and Tone

### Voice Characteristics
1. **Direct** — Say what we mean without filler
2. **Founder-led** — Speak the language of operators
3. **Confident** — We know our value, no exaggeration
4. **Honest** — No fabricated claims
5. **Professional** — Enterprise-ready without being cold

### Tone by Context

#### Marketing
- Bold but not arrogant
- Aspirational but grounded
- Local but global in quality

Example: "See what your company should do next."

#### Product
- Clear and actionable
- Helpful but concise
- Safety-first

Example: "Review this draft before it sends."

#### Sales
- Consultative
- Evidence-based
- Respectful of time

Example: "A Diagnostic Sprint will show you exactly where revenue is leaking."

#### Support
- Empathetic
- Solution-oriented
- Patient

Example: "I understand this is urgent. Let's resolve it step by step."

### Words We Use
- Operating system
- Command Room
- Brain
- Reviewable
- Approval-gated
- Daily rhythm
- Decision discipline
- Revenue leakage
- Operating diagnosis
- Proof pack

### Words We Avoid
- Autopilot
- Fully automated
- Magic
- Guaranteed ROI
- Certified (for compliance)
- AI replaces humans
- Set and forget

## Imagery and Photography

### Photography Style
- Real Saudi business environments
- Diverse teams collaborating
- Focused founders and operators
- Modern offices and meeting spaces
- Authentic moments, not stock poses

### Illustration Style
- Clean, geometric illustrations
- Dealix Teal primary
- Abstract representations of systems/flows
- Minimal, professional

### Iconography
- Lucide icons (line style)
- Consistent stroke width
- Teal or slate color
- Simple, recognizable metaphors

### Image Treatment
- Subtle teal tint overlay for brand cohesion
- Avoid overly saturated images
- Prefer natural lighting

## Brand Dos and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Lead with clarity and discipline
- Put humans in control
- Use local Saudi context
- Keep messaging evidence-based
- Maintain consistent colors
- Use approved templates
- Proofread all content

### ❌ Don't
- Promise magic or autopilot
- Make unverified ROI claims
- Use overly complex language
- Ignore compliance context
- Mix brand colors randomly
- Use low-quality images
- Send automated messages without approval (in product or marketing)
