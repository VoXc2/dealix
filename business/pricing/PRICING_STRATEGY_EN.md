# Pricing Strategy (EN)

## Principle
- Full transparency: setup + monthly are explicit
- No hidden onboarding fees
- No silent auto-renewal
- 14-day refund on setup
- 30-day cancellation on monthly

## Why this pricing
- **High setup** reflects the value of the first 14 days (intake + workflow + Command Center)
- **Reasonable monthly** sustains the relationship
- **Free tier 0** protects quality and does not devalue the offer
- **Custom Enterprise** opens the door to large groups

## Negotiation rules
- No more than 10% discount on setup in the first 30 days
- No more than 15% discount on monthly after 6 months
- Every discount requires a Change Request
- No "friend" or "well-known company" discounts

## Payment
- Setup: 50% on signing, 50% on Day 14
- Monthly: upfront on the first day of the month
- Custom Enterprise: bespoke terms, separate invoice
