# Industry Weakness Taxonomy (Dealix)

## Marketing Agency
- Slow inbound response
- Reporting cadence
- Capacity planning

## Training / Consulting
- Cohort follow-up
- Renewal cycle
- Trainer utilization

## Clinic / Local Service
- No-show rate
- Reputation response
- Recall cadence

## Real Estate
- Lead-to-viewing ratio
- Agent consistency
- Listing promotion

## Logistics / B2B
- Dispatch visibility
- Customer updates
- SLA reporting

## Consulting Firm
- Pipeline visibility
- Engagement reporting
- Senior time allocation

## Retail / Multi-branch
- Branch-level reporting
- Stock visibility
- Customer complaint response

## B2B Services (general)
- Lead routing
- Proposal conversion
- Account expansion
