# Sales Daily Operating System

## Daily (15 min)
1. Review queue: approve or reject drafts
2. Send approved drafts manually
3. Update stage + add follow-up
4. Generate Daily CEO brief

## Weekly (60 min)
1. Pipeline review by stage
2. Stuck accounts + why
3. Next 7 days: drafts to generate, calls to book
4. Proof items to log from delivery

## Monthly (90 min)
1. Conversion scorecard
2. Revenue vs target
3. Top 3 industry plays review
4. Expansion offers to existing clients
5. One new industry play (research only)

## What we never do
- Auto-send any outreach
- Promise ROI we cannot measure
- Report demo data as traction
- Scrape private data
