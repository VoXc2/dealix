# Weakness Hypothesis Guide (Dealix)

## How to write a hypothesis
1. State the visible signal
2. State the assumed weakness
3. State what you would do to verify in week 1
4. Connect to an offer

## Templates
- "We observed [signal]. We assume [weakness]. We would verify by [action]. The right offer is [offer]."
- "Based on the public surface, [account] likely has [weakness]. A [offer] install would [outcome]."

## Forbidden
- "We guarantee X% increase"
- "We have seen similar companies do Y"
- "This is definitely the problem"
- "The founder is to blame"
