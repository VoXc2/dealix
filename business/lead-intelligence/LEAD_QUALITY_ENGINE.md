# Lead Quality Engine (Dealix)

## Signals (10)
1. Source reliability (0-10)
2. Signal specificity (0-10)
3. Website quality (0-10)
4. CTA clarity (0-10)
5. Booking flow exists (0-10)
6. Reputation signal (0-10)
7. B2B fit (0-10)
8. Estimated proofability (0-10)
9. Retainer potential (0-10)
10. Compliance risk penalty (-10 to 0)

## Score
- Total: 0-100
- Threshold: 60 = qualified

## Compliance penalties
- Source without note: -10
- No demo flag: -5
- PII in note: -20
- Auto-send attempted: -100 (block)
