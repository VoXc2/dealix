# Revenue OS Delivery Playbook

## Purpose

Deliver one governed revenue workflow from intake to measurable impact with evidence.

## Standard Delivery Sequence

1. Discovery and data access validation.
2. Tenant and RBAC setup.
3. Configure lead qualification workflow.
4. Connect one execution target (CRM or sheet).
5. Configure approvals for high-risk actions.
6. Enable observability and alerting.
7. Run eval pack and cross-layer test.
8. Hand over dashboard and weekly review cadence.

## Minimal Client Pilot Package

- 1 tenant configured
- 3 users and 2 roles active
- 1 sales agent registered
- 1 governed lead workflow running
- 1 approval rule active
- 1 integration active
- 1 traceable end-to-end run
- 1 eval report
- 1 ROI summary
- 1 rollback drill report

## Acceptance Criteria

Playbook gate `G-PLY-001` is `PASS` when all minimal package items are evidenced and signed off.
