# Monthly Review Playbook (Revenue OS)

## Outcome

Convert operational telemetry into executive decisions and measurable ROI accountability.

## Monthly Agenda

1. KPI trend review (volume, speed, conversion).
2. Governance health review (approvals, denials, incidents).
3. Reliability review (failures, retries, MTTR).
4. ROI review (delta vs baseline).
5. Prioritized improvements for next cycle.

## Required Inputs

- Workflow metrics export
- Eval report
- Audit incident summary
- ROI report

## Executive Decisions

- Keep / tune / pause automation segments
- Tighten or relax approval rules
- Expand to new teams or keep current scope

## Exit Criteria

- Signed monthly decision log
- Approved next-month optimization plan
