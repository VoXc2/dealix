# Bad Revenue Filter

Reject when: scope open, margin weak, risk high, client rejects governance, scraping requested, guaranteed sales requested, no proof path, no retainer path, no productization signal.

Forbidden triggers (any one flips the verdict): requests_scraping, requests_guaranteed_sales, client_rejects_governance.

> Revenue that destroys focus is debt.

Typed: `operating_finance_os.bad_revenue_filter.bad_revenue_check()`.
