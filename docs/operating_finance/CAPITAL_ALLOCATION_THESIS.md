# Capital Allocation Thesis

> Invest only where learning compounds.

Invest in: offers that sell repeatedly, workflows that become retainers, features that reduce delivery cost, governance that reduces risk, proof that increases trust, partners that increase distribution, standards that increase authority, ventures that emerged from proof.

Do not invest in: custom work without repetition, features without usage, clients who reject governance, segments without a clear buyer, unsafe automation, content without category purpose, partnerships without QA.
