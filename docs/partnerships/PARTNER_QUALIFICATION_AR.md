# Partner Qualification — تأهيل الشركاء
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** 7 معايير لتأهيل الشركاء، hard و soft، مع walk-away conditions.

---

## 1. The 7 Qualification Criteria

### 1.1 Has Client Access
- **Hard requirement:** yes
- **Why:** بدون access = لا leads
- **Validation:** references, network check, LinkedIn

### 1.2 Not Selling Spam
- **Hard requirement:** yes
- **Why:** brand + PDPL + DMARC
- **Validation:** review their materials, ask directly

### 1.3 Understands B2B Services
- **Hard requirement:** yes
- **Why:** B2B ≠ B2C، different cycles
- **Validation:** discovery, references

### 1.4 Accepts Approval-First Policy
- **Hard requirement:** yes
- **Why:** our governance
- **Validation:** explicit agreement

### 1.5 Legal Entity
- **Hard requirement:** yes (for referral+)
- **Why:** contracts, payments, taxes
- **Validation:** business registration

### 1.6 Quality Reputation
- **Soft requirement:** positive
- **Why:** brand association
- **Validation:** references, online presence

### 1.7 Strategic Value (optional)
- **Soft requirement:** alignment
- **Why:** long-term fit
- **Validation:** strategy alignment

| Score | Tier | Action |

## 3. Hard Disqualifiers (No Override)

### 3.1 ❌ Reject If
- ❌ Sells spam
- ❌ Sells guaranteed revenue
- ❌ No client access
- ❌ Refuses approval workflow
- ❌ Refuses PDPL
- ❌ No legal entity
- ❌ Bad reputation (verified)
- ❌ Abusive behavior
- ❌ Illegal activity
- ❌ Competing AI/RevOps

---

## 4. Soft Disqualifiers (Founder Override)

### 4.1 ⚠️ Caution If
- ⚠️ New (no track record)
- ⚠️ Small client base
- ⚠️ Different industry focus
- ⚠️ No strategic value
- ⚠️ High support need
- ⚠️ Geographic mismatch
- ⚠️ Cultural mismatch

### 4.2 Override Requirements
- Founder approval
- Reason logged
- Risk acknowledged
- Term review (3-6 months)

---

## 5. Industry-Specific Considerations

### 5.1 Marketing Agency
- ✅ Strong fit (co-sell)
- ⚠️ Watch: if agency also does automation (conflict)
- ⚠️ Watch: if agency has internal team (vs Dealix)

### 5.2 CRM Implementer
- ✅ Strong fit (referral, implementation)
- ⚠️ Watch: if they push their own AI (conflict)
- ⚠️ Watch: if they have delivery capacity

### 5.3 Biz Consultant
- ✅ Good fit (referral)
- ⚠️ Watch: if they don't see AI value
- ⚠️ Watch: if they have their own tools

### 5.4 Training Company
- ✅ Good fit (referral)
- ⚠️ Watch: if they only do training (no consulting)
- ⚠️ Watch: if they have a content platform

### 5.5 Accounting Firm
- ✅ Good fit (referral)
- ⚠️ Watch: if they want to be implementation
- ⚠️ Watch: if they have CRM practice

### 5.6 Web Agency
- ✅ Good fit (referral)
- ⚠️ Watch: if they push their own automation
- ⚠️ Watch: if they have technical capacity

### 5.7 Software Reseller
- ⚠️ Caution (channel)
- ⚠️ Watch: if they have competing product
- ⚠️ Watch: if they have low technical capacity

### 5.8 Operations Consultant
- ✅ Good fit (co-delivery)
- ⚠️ Watch: if they have competing OS
- ⚠️ Watch: if they have implementation team

## 6. The Discovery Call (with Potential Partner)

### 6.1 Questions
- Tell me about your business
- Who are your typical clients?
- How do you currently help them?
- What tools/frameworks do you use?
- Have you worked with AI/RevOps before?
- How do you handle delivery quality?
- What are your concerns about partnering?
- How do you envision the partnership?
- What margin model do you prefer?
- What's your capacity (deals/month)?

### 6.2 Red Flags
- ❌ Won't share client info
- ❌ Vague about delivery
- ❌ Asks for spam tools
- ❌ Wants guaranteed leads
- ❌ Asks for upfront payment
- ❌ Bad-mouths competitors aggressively
- ❌ No legal entity
- ❌ No references

### 6.3 Green Flags
- ✅ Has client references
- ✅ Clear delivery process
- ✅ Quality-focused
- ✅ Long-term thinker
- ✅ B2B experience
- ✅ Local presence
- ✅ Saudi understanding

---

## 7. The Decision

### 7.1 Approve
- Move to negotiation
- Founder approval
- Document decision

### 7.2 Reject
- Send polite rejection
- Reason (if appropriate)
- Door open for future

### 7.3 Nurture
- Quarterly check-in
- Re-evaluate in 6-12 months
- Stay in touch

---

## 8. Re-qualification Triggers

- Performance change
- Industry shift
- Reputation change
- Strategy alignment change
- Annual review

---

## 9. The Walk-Away

### 9.1 When
- Red flags appear
- Bad-faith behavior
- Quality issues
- Spam activity
- Legal issues
- Reputation damage

### 9.2 Process
- Document reason
- Notify partner
- 30 days notice (or immediate if material breach)
- Handoff in-flight
- Pay pending
- Stop brand usage

### 9.3 Documentation
- Reason
- Date
- Evidence
- Founder approval
