# Partner Pricing and Margin — تسعير وهامش الشراكات
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كيف نحسب margin الشركاء، ومتى نرفع، ومتى نقلل، وكيف نحمي Dealix.

---

## 1. Margin Philosophy

**الشريك يحصل على قيمة عادلة، Dealix يحمي هامشه.**

- Default: 10-20% referral fee
- Trust = more margin (over time)
- Performance = more margin
- Risk = less margin (or no deal)

---

## 2. Margin by Model (Recap)

| Model | Partner % | Dealix % |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| Referral | 10-20 | 80-90 |
| Co-Sell | 20-30 | 70-80 |
| Implementation | 50 (impl fee) | 50 (diagnostic) |
| Co-Delivery | 30-50 | 50-70 |
| Channel Reseller | 30-40 | 60-70 |
| White Label | 40-50 | 50-60 |

---

## 3. Margin Calculation

### 3.1 For Dealix (Net Margin)
```
Net = Price - Delivery Cost - Partner Commission - Overhead
```

### 3.2 Example
- Diagnostic: 9,999 SAR
- Partner commission: 1,500 SAR (15%)
- Delivery cost: 2,000 SAR (founder time)
- Net to Dealix: 9,999 - 1,500 - 2,000 = 6,499 SAR (65%)

### 3.3 Margin Floor for Partner-Sourced
- Diagnostic: 50% net to Dealix minimum
- Workflow: 40% net
- Starter: 35% net
- Full OS: 30% net
- Retainer: 50% net (recurring)

---

## 4. Margin Triggers

### 4.1 Increase Partner Margin When
- Partner brings 5+ qualified leads/month
- Partner closes 3+ deals/quarter
- Partner provides co-marketing
- Partner commits to long-term
- Strategic value (founder approval)

### 4.2 Decrease Partner Margin When
- Partner provides unqualified leads
- Partner creates spam
- Partner damages brand
- Partner requires high support

---

## 5. Recurring vs One-Time

### 5.1 One-Time
- Diagnostic, Workflow, Starter, Full OS, Custom
- Referral fee: on first payment
- No recurring (unless renewal attributed)

### 5.2 Recurring
- Retainer
- 5-10% to partner on renewals (if partner attributed)
- Or per agreement

### 5.3 Hybrid
- Implementation + retainer
- Partner fee on implementation only
- Recurring: per agreement

---

## 6. Payment Terms

### 6.1 To Partner
- Net 30 from client payment

### 6.2 Minimum Payout

- Quarterly payout
- Or per agreement

### 6.3 Payment Approval
- All payouts: L3+ (per `approval_policy.yaml`)

## 8. Margin Erosion Risks

### 8.1 ❌ Don't Do
- ❌ Increase margin without value
- ❌ Stack discounts on top of partner fee
- ❌ Change model mid-engagement
- ❌ Skip payment
- ❌ Hide margin info

### 8.2 ✅ Do
- ✅ Be transparent
- ✅ Pay on time
- ✅ Document changes
- ✅ Reward performance
- ✅ Adjust based on value

## 9. The Negotiation

### 9.1 When Partner Asks for More
```
"أقدّر طلبك. الحسبة الحالية:
- أنت تحصل على [X]%
- Dealix يحصل على [Y]%
- هامش Dealix: [Z]%

لزيادة [X]:
- نتوقع [N] leads/شهر
- 3+ صفقات/ربع
- اشتراك طويل

إذا تحقق، نرفع [X]%. هذا تقدير، 
نتفق على review ربع سنوي."
```

### 9.2 When We Want to Adjust
```
"بناءً على review ربع سنوي:
- أنت أحضرت [N] leads (target [M])
- حققت [P] صفقات (target [Q])

النسبة الحالية [X]%. نقترح:
- [Maintain/Increase/Decrease]

إذا عندك ملاحظات، نسمع."
```

## 11. The Bottom Line

**الشراكة تربح عندما:**
- ✅ كلا الطرفين يربح
- ✅ العميل يربح
- ✅ Long-term
- ✅ Brand safe
- ✅ Margin protected

**الشراكة تخسر عندما:**
- ❌ One side over-takes
- ❌ العميل يخسر
- ❌ Spam behavior
- ❌ Brand damage
- ❌ Margin erosion
