## القسم 1 — Board Deck Template (Quarterly)

### 1.1 هيكل العرض (15-20 شريحة)

**Slide 1: Title (5 sec)**
- اسم الشركة + الربع + التاريخ
- "Board Update — Q[X] 2026"

**Slide 2: Executive Summary (60 sec)**
- 3-5 أبرز إنجازات الربع
- 3-5 أبرز التحديات
- 1-3 قرارات مطلوبة من المجلس

**Slide 3: Key Metrics (60 sec)**
- MRR + Customers + Churn + NPS
- مقارنة بالربع السابق + target
- Big Number one (مثل: +40% MRR QoQ)

**Slide 4: Financial Snapshot (60 sec)**
- P&L summary
- Cash position + runway
- Burn rate + burn multiple

**Slide 5: GTM Performance (90 sec)**
- Pipeline + Closed deals
- CAC + Payback period
- Top 3 wins, Top 3 losses
- Channel performance

**Slide 6: Product & Engineering (60 sec)**
- Major releases shipped
- Technical debt progress
- Team growth

**Slide 7: Customer Success (60 sec)**
- NPS + CSAT
- Top customer feedback
- Expansion + churn
- Reference customers count

**Slide 8: Team & Culture (60 sec)**
- Headcount + key hires
- Voluntary turnover
- Hiring pipeline

**Slide 9: Strategic Initiatives (90 sec)**
- 3 Big Bets in flight
- Progress vs plan
- Pivots/Changes

**Slide 10: Risks & Mitigations (60 sec)**
- Top 5 risks
- Probability × Impact matrix
- Mitigation status

**Slide 11: Competitive Landscape (60 sec)**
- Market changes
- Competitor moves
- Our response

**Slide 12: Asks from Board (30 sec)**
- Specific decisions needed
- Specific introductions needed
- Specific advice needed

**Slide 13: Next Quarter Priorities (60 sec)**
- Top 5 OKRs
- Top 3 hiring
- Top 3 product launches

**Slide 14: Financial Projections (60 sec)**
- Updated 12-month forecast
- Variance vs last quarter
- Series A readiness (if applicable)

**Slide 15: Q&A (30 min)**
- Open discussion

**Slide 16: Appendix**
- Detailed metrics
- Customer case studies
- Product roadmap

### 1.2 Board Meeting Logistics
- **Duration:** 90 min (60 min deck + 30 min Q&A)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly (Mar/Jun/Sep/Dec)
- **Attendees:** Board members + Founder + Key executives
- **Materials sent:** 48 hours before
- **Minutes:** Distributed within 7 days
- **Voting items:** Listed in advance

- Monthly: 5th of each month
- 1:1 calls: quarterly (with lead)
- Annual review: December

## القسم 3 — Board Composition (المقترح)

### 3.1 Y1 (Seed)
- **Founder/CEO** (Sami) — 1 vote
- **Lead Investor** (1) — 1 vote
- **Independent Director** (1) — 1 vote
- **Advisors** (non-voting)

### 3.2 Y2 (Post-Seed)
- **Founder/CEO** — 1 vote
- **Lead Investor (Seed)** — 1 vote
- **Series A Lead (incoming)** — 1 vote
- **Independent Director #1** — 1 vote
- **Independent Director #2** — 1 vote
- **Observer seats** (advisors, follow-on investors)

### 3.3 Independent Director Profile
- Senior SaaS founder/exec
- Saudi market expertise
- Network in VCs or enterprise
- Time commitment: 4-6 hours/quarter
- Compensation: 0.3-0.5% equity + $20K/year cash

### 3.4 Advisory Board
- 3-5 advisors
- Domain expertise (AI, B2B SaaS, GCC market)
- 0.1-0.3% equity each
- 1-2 hours/month engagement

## القسم 5 — Voting Items (أمثلة)

### 5.1 قرارات روتينية (Founder authority)
- Hiring under $20K monthly cost
- Tooling purchases <$5K
- Routine operational decisions
- New customer contracts <$50K ARR

### 5.2 قرارات تتطلب موافقة المجلس
- New equity issuance (>5%)
- Acquisitions (>SAR 500K)
- Major strategic pivots
- Fundraising rounds
- Executive hires (C-Suite)
- Office leases (>12 months, >SAR 200K)
- Partnership agreements with exclusivity
- Material changes to business model

### 5.3 قرارات تتطلب Lead Investor فقط
- Bridge financing
- Convertible notes
- Major expense variances (>20%)

## القسم 7 — Key Performance Indicators (Board-Level)

### 7.1 Financial KPIs
- **MRR Growth Rate** (MoM, QoQ, YoY)
- **ARR** (current + projection)
- **Gross Margin** (target >75%)
- **CAC Payback** (target <12 months)
- **LTV/CAC** (target >5)
- **Burn Multiple** (target <2)
- **Cash Runway** (target >12 months)
- **Net Revenue Retention** (target >110%)

### 7.2 Customer KPIs
- **Net New Customers** (monthly, quarterly)
- **Logo Churn** (target <3% monthly)
- **Revenue Churn** (target <5% annual)
- **NPS** (target >40)
- **Logo Retention** (target >95% annual)
- **Net Promoter Score trend**

### 7.3 Product KPIs
- **MAU / DAU** (active users)
- **Feature Adoption** (new features)
- **Time to Value** (TTV)
- **Daily Decisions Processed**
- **API Uptime** (target 99.9%)
- **Customer Health Score** (NPS + usage + tickets)

### 7.4 Team KPIs
- **Headcount** (current vs plan)
- **Voluntary Turnover** (target <10% annual)
- **Time to Hire** (target <45 days)
- **Diversity Metrics** (gender, nationality)
- **Engagement Score** (quarterly survey)

## القسم 8 — Board Member Engagement

### 8.1 Communication Cadence
- Monthly: Investor update (email)
- Quarterly: Board meeting (in-person or video)
- Ad-hoc: Major decisions, crises
- Annual: Offsite strategy session

### 8.2 Information Asymmetry Principle
- Board gets same data as CEO
- No surprises in board meetings
- Bad news early, not buried
- Decisions documented

### 8.3 Confidentiality
- All board materials confidential
- No sharing without permission
- NDA for advisors
- Material non-public info (MNPI) compliance
