# Saudi 12-Month Go-to-Market Plan

## 1. Context

Existing `docs/GTM_PLAYBOOK.md` and `docs/V6_GTM_EXECUTION_PLAN.md` give horizontal motion guidance. This document is the time-boxed Saudi-specific 12-month execution plan: who, what, when, with measurable monthly outputs. It assumes the Lead Engine (T31), ICP (T5), and 3 verticals (T1) are operational; if they slip, the plan slips.

## 2. Audience

CEO, CRO, marketing lead, partner lead, CS lead. Used as the source of monthly business reviews (T30).

## 3. Decisions / Content

### 3.1 Quarter-by-quarter plan

**Q1 (Months 1–3) — Foundation & anchor logos**
- Goal: 2 anchor logos signed (1 BFSI + 1 retail OR healthcare).
- Pipeline target: SAR 15M.
- Activities:
  - Lead Engine in production, ≥25 Saudi data sources registered.
  - ABM playbook on 50 Tier-1 accounts (T5).
  - Hire: 1 SDR, 1 enterprise AE, 1 customer success lead.
  - 3 events: 1 industry conference (Money 20/20 Riyadh or LEAP), 2 vertical roundtables.
  - 6 published Saudi case-relevant content pieces (bilingual).
- Channels: outbound 60% / events 25% / inbound 15%.

**Q2 (Months 4–6) — Repeatability**
- Goal: 4 more logos (cumulative 6); 2 paid pilots converted.
- Pipeline target: SAR 30M cumulative.
- Activities:
  - 2 channel partners signed (SI + sectoral reseller — T6).
  - Sales enablement v1 launched (T28); 100% rep certification.
  - Bilingual ROI model adoption per deal (T2).
  - Procurement pack v1 (T15) used in 100% of enterprise opps.
- Channels: outbound 50% / partner 20% / events 15% / inbound 15%.

**Q3 (Months 7–9) — Expansion**
- Goal: 6 more logos (cumulative 12); first land-and-expand (T19).
- Pipeline target: SAR 60M cumulative.
- Activities:
  - Open Jeddah field presence (1 AE remote + monthly visits).
  - Healthcare vertical at full ramp.
  - First customer summit (15–25 Saudi customers in person).
  - Self-serve onboarding for SME tier live.
- Channels: outbound 40% / partner 30% / events 15% / inbound 15%.

**Q4 (Months 10–12) — Scale**
- Goal: 8 more logos (cumulative 20); 2 multi-year enterprise renewals.
- Pipeline target: SAR 100M cumulative.
- Activities:
  - Second customer summit (Riyadh).
  - Public reference customers: 5 quotable logos.
  - Vision 2030 alignment narrative on Dealix.sa.
  - Begin GCC expansion scoping (UAE/Bahrain via Saudi anchors' subsidiaries).

### 3.2 Channel mix (annual)

- Outbound (Lead Engine + SDR): 45%
- Partner: 25%
- Events: 15%
- Inbound (content, SEO bilingual): 15%

### 3.4 Funding & spend gates

- Q1 budget: SAR 2.5M (people 60%, events 20%, marketing 15%, tools 5%).
- Q2–Q4 each: SAR 3M baseline; +SAR 1M if Q-1 pipeline target met.
- Gate: do not hire AE #N+1 unless AE #N has SAR 3M closed in trailing 6 months.

### 3.5 Monthly business review (consumed by T30)

Reviewed monthly:
- Pipeline coverage (target 3× quarter quota by month -2).
- Logos signed vs plan.
- Win rate per vertical.
- Lead Engine throughput (T31 KPIs).
- CAC / CAC payback.
- Partner-sourced %.
- Bilingual asset freshness (T20).
