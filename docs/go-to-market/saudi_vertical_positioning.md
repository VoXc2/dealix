# Saudi Vertical Positioning — BFSI, Retail/eCommerce, Healthcare

## 1. Context

Generic horizontal positioning loses Saudi enterprise deals to bilingual local incumbents and US-translated tools. Vertical positioning anchored to one painful, well-defined use case per sector accelerates trust and shortens the sales cycle. This doc selects three priority verticals — chosen for size, urgency, and Dealix product-market fit — and defines value proposition, pain matrix, ROI thesis, and messaging per vertical.

## 2. Audience

CRO, sales, marketing, partners. Persona-value matrix (T8) extends this doc.

## 3. Decisions / Content

### 3.1 Vertical 1 — BFSI (Banks, Finance Companies, Fintechs)

- **Why now**: SAMA digital-banking licenses, fintech sandbox graduates, mandatory PDPL + Open Banking + Anti-Fraud frameworks.
- **Pain**: cost of acquiring qualified SME business banking customers; manual SAMA reporting on customer outreach; bilingual sales operations.
- **Dealix value**: PDPL-grade Lead Engine + Decision Passport (auditable outreach evidence) + bilingual sales playbooks. Substitute hours of sanctioned-list/CR checks with one API call.
- **ROI thesis**: 30% reduction in cost-per-qualified-business-account, 4× outbound throughput per BDR, 100% audit-grade evidence.
- **Sample use cases**: business banking acquisition, SME credit cross-sell, fintech B2B onboarding.
- **Messaging** (EN): "PDPL-compliant business banking acquisition with audit-grade evidence."
- **Messaging** (AR): "اكتساب عملاء البنوك التجارية بأدلة قابلة للتدقيق ومتوافق مع PDPL."
- **Anchor accounts (logos to land)**: 1 tier-1 SAMA bank, 1 fintech with SAR 100M+ funding.

### 3.2 Vertical 2 — Retail / eCommerce

- **Why now**: Salla/Zid platform maturity, omnichannel mandates, ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing enforcement, GCC cross-border expansion.
- **Pain**: B2B wholesale acquisition (not B2C), supplier and franchisee acquisition, multi-region expansion outside Riyadh.
- **Dealix value**: outlet-footprint enrichment via Maps + Maroof, expansion-signal triggers (new openings, Saudization hires), bilingual outreach to mid-market merchants.
- **ROI thesis**: 2× pipeline density in target sub-segments (e.g., F&B chains, fashion retail), 50% lower CAC for partner/franchise pipelines.
- **Sample use cases**: wholesale buyer acquisition, franchise-partner discovery, supplier diversification programs.
- **Messaging** (EN): "Find every Saudi retail buyer before your competitor opens the next store."
- **Messaging** (AR): "اكتشف كل مشتري تجزئة سعودي قبل أن يفتتح منافسك المتجر التالي."
- **Anchor accounts**: 1 top-10 retail chain, 1 Salla/Zid platform-level deal.

### 3.3 Vertical 3 — Healthcare (Hospitals, Medtech, Pharma B2B)

- **Why now**: Health-sector privatization (NUPCO procurement, Tabadul integrations), SFDA licensing of medtech, MoH digital-health initiatives, Cluster transformation.
- **Pain**: long, opaque procurement cycles; bilingual clinical/admin stakeholders; PDPL Class A health data sensitivity.
- **Dealix value**: SFDA/NUPCO tender triggers, hospital-network entity resolution (cluster-level), Decision Passport with PDPL Art. 27 (health data) gates.
- **ROI thesis**: 40% faster procurement engagement, 2× win-rate on healthcare tenders due to pre-positioned evidence.
- **Sample use cases**: medtech B2B sales, pharma KAM acquisition, hospital-IT vendor positioning.
- **Messaging** (EN): "Win Saudi healthcare procurement with PDPL Art. 27-ready evidence."
- **Messaging** (AR): "اربح مناقصات الرعاية الصحية السعودية بأدلة جاهزة لـ PDPL المادة 27."
- **Anchor accounts**: 1 cluster, 1 medtech distributor.
