## Mini Diagnostic intake (when prospect says yes)

Reply with these 10 questions, wait for ALL answers, then deliver
the Diagnostic within 24h:

```
ممتاز يا [الاسم]، أرسل لي ١٠ معلومات سريعة وأرجع لك خلال ٢٤ ساعة:

1. اسم الشركة
2. الموقع الإلكتروني
3. القطاع
4. المدينة الرئيسية / السوق
5. العرض الرئيسي في جملة (offer)
6. العميل المثالي (ICP)
7. متوسط قيمة الصفقة بالريال (إذا متاح)
8. القنوات الحالية (LinkedIn / إيميل / inbound / referrals / other)
9. أكبر تحدّي حالي
10. لو عندك قائمة، شو مصدرها وحالة الموافقة
```

## What NOT to do today

- ❌ Send more than 10 DMs (volume comes after first paid pilot)
- ❌ Use any LinkedIn automation tool / extension / API
- ❌ WhatsApp anyone you're not already talking to
- ❌ Promise specific revenue numbers
- ❌ Skip the live board logging
- ❌ Push prospects who say no

## Definition of done — "first 10" wave

- [ ] 10 manual Arabic warm DMs sent
- [ ] All 10 logged in `docs/FIRST_CUSTOMER_LIVE_BOARD.md`
- [ ] At least 1-2 replies received (~15-20% reply rate is healthy for warm)
- [ ] At least 1 Mini Diagnostic delivered within 24h of intake
- [ ] At least 1 pilot offered
- [ ] Friday weekly review written

If the wave produces 0 replies → don't send 10 more next day. Re-read
the messages in `FIRST_CUSTOMER_OUTREACH_MESSAGES.md`, sharpen the
prospect selection in `FIRST_10_WARM_PROSPECTS.md`, then try again.
