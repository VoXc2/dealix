# Proof Economy

Dealix treats proof as a unit of currency. No project closes without a Proof Pack. No claim is made publicly without a corresponding proof asset. No retainer is offered without proof to justify it.

## 1. Proof Pack contents

Every Proof Pack contains:

- **Problem** — the stated business problem.
- **Inputs** — data, scope, constraints.
- **Work Completed** — what was done, by whom, when.
- **Metrics** — before/after, deltas, baselines.
- **Before/After** — qualitative + quantitative comparisons.
- **AI Outputs** — examples, with governance status.
- **Governance Events** — approvals, redactions, blocks.
- **Business Value** — translated into revenue, time, quality, or risk units.
- **Risks** — what remains open.
- **Recommended Next Step** — sprint, retainer, or pause.

## 2. Proof types

| Type | Example evidence |
| --- | --- |
| Revenue Proof | Pipeline value generated, opportunities created, deals influenced |
| Time Proof | Hours saved per workflow per week |
| Quality Proof | Error reduction, rework reduction, defect rate |
| Risk Proof | Unsafe actions blocked, approvals logged, audit coverage |
| Knowledge Proof | Insufficient-evidence rate, citation coverage, knowledge gaps closed |

## 3. Proof-to-Retainer protocol

Every Proof Pack ends with one of three explicit motions:

- **Continue** — same scope, recurring.
- **Expand** — add a new OS or BU.
- **Pause** — stop, and record why.

There is no fourth option. Engagements do not drift.

Common patterns:

- Strong **Revenue Proof** → Monthly RevOps OS.
- Strong **Knowledge Proof** → Monthly Company Brain.
- Strong **Risk Proof** → Monthly Governance.
- Strong **Time/Quality Proof** → Monthly AI Ops.

## 4. Proof governance

- Proof Packs reference `EvidenceLevel` (see `auto_client_acquisition/proof_engine/evidence.py`).
- Public marketing requires L4+ and explicit consent.
- Private sales proof requires L3+.
- Internal-only material stays at L1/L2.
- Public claims that exceed the underlying evidence level are a doctrine violation.

## 5. Proof Library

Dealix maintains a Proof Library by:

- Sector
- Capability axis
- Proof type
- Engagement size
- Outcome size

The library powers:

- Sales conversations (with consent)
- Capability Diagnostic baselines
- Benchmarks (`MARKET_POWER.md`)
- Academy and Partner enablement

## 6. Anti-patterns

- Verbal proof claimed without a Proof Pack row.
- Proof reused publicly without consent.
- Metrics chosen after delivery to look favorable.
- Single-period numbers without baselines.
- Hero charts without governance context.

## 7. Operating discipline

- Reporting OS owns Proof Pack generation.
- Capital OS picks up reusable assets from the pack.
- Intelligence OS feeds proof data into the decision engine.
- Governance OS contributes the audit and approval evidence.
