# Capability Diagnostic

The Capability Diagnostic is the strongest entry point Dealix offers. It is a paid, structured 60–90 minute engagement that produces a written Capability Score Report. It is the single most important conversion artifact in the stack.

## 1. Why it exists

- It shifts the first conversation from “buy AI service” to “diagnose AI operating capability.”
- It gives the buyer a strategic artifact they keep regardless of whether they engage further.
- It produces the data needed to recommend the right sprint.
- It generates the first row in the client’s Source Passport.

## 2. Scoring axes

Each axis is scored 0–5:

- **Revenue Capability** — pipeline hygiene, ICP clarity, scoring discipline.
- **Data Capability** — sources, ownership, quality, residency.
- **Governance Capability** — policy, approval, audit, runtime.
- **Knowledge Capability** — sources, citations, retrieval.
- **Operations Capability** — workflows, owners, SLAs.
- **Proof Capability** — measurement, evidence, executive reporting.

## 3. Output sections

The report ships with:

- **Current Capability Score** — per-axis and composite.
- **Transformation Gap** — what is missing to reach the next stage.
- **Data Readiness** — pass/fail for the wedge sprint.
- **Governance Risk** — exposure, channels, current rules.
- **Highest-Value Opportunity** — the single best next move.
- **Recommended Sprint** — the offer from the stack that fits.
- **Expected Proof** — what the next 30–90 days will demonstrate.
- **Retainer Path** — which recurring OS becomes natural after the sprint.

## 5. Delivery format

- 60–90 minute working session with the buyer and one operations leader.
- A structured workbook drives the conversation.
- A draft report is shared within 5 business days.
- The buyer receives both Arabic and English versions.

## 6. Commercial structure

- Fixed price, productized.
- Refundable against the first paid sprint within 30 days.
- Sold via inbound or partner referral — never via cold outreach.

## 7. Why this changes the sales motion

- Buyers anchor to **score**, not to **service**.
- Dealix never has to argue about the value of AI in the abstract.
- The Diagnostic naturally seeds Monthly Governance and Monthly RevOps conversations.
- The recommendation comes from a written instrument, not a sales pitch.

## 8. Failure modes

- Letting the Diagnostic become a free pitch deck. It must be paid and written.
- Producing the report without scoring axes — the score is the product.
- Skipping the data readiness check, which then derails the sprint.
- Recommending more than one sprint. The Diagnostic recommends *one*.
