# Academy Strategy

The Dealix Academy is **not** a course catalog. It is a distribution channel, an authority engine, and a partner enablement platform — built on top of the Dealix Method.

## 1. Pre-conditions

The Academy opens only when:

- 10+ paid engagements completed.
- 3+ proof-backed case assets exist.
- The Dealix Method is documented and stable.
- A repeatable templates library exists.
- A QA rubric is in production use.

Until then, the Academy does not exist publicly. Teaching what is not yet proven is doctrine violation.

## 2. Tracks

| Track | Audience |
| --- | --- |
| AI Ops Executive | Senior leaders responsible for AI adoption |
| Revenue AI Operator | Sales/RevOps practitioners |
| Company Brain Builder | Knowledge ops and enablement teams |
| AI Governance Lead | Risk, compliance, security, internal AI office |
| Dealix Certified Partner | External agencies and consultancies |
| Dealix Delivery Analyst | Operators inside Dealix and partners |

## 3. Outputs

Each track produces:

- A workbook tied to the Dealix Method.
- A capstone Proof Pack the learner authors against a real workflow.
- A certification with an expiry — recertification is required.

## 4. Operating model

- **Authoring** — owned by Office of the Standard.
- **Delivery** — cohort-based, instructor-led, plus async.
- **Assessment** — independent QA reviewers (not the instructors).
- **Recertification** — annual; tied to current Method version.

## 5. Why the Academy matters strategically

- It turns Dealix Method into market language.
- It creates a partner labor pool that delivers Dealix-compatible engagements.
- It feeds the hiring pipeline with vetted operators.
- It generates a stream of Proof Packs from learners’ real workflows (with consent).

## 6. Partner enablement

The Partner track is the **only** path for external agencies to deliver Dealix-branded engagements. Partners must:

- Pass certification.
- Use the productized offer stack.
- Adhere to the governance runtime.
- Submit Proof Packs for QA.
- Accept periodic audits.

## 7. Failure modes

- Launching the Academy as content marketing instead of certification.
- Skipping recertification — the Method moves; certificates must move with it.
- Letting partners sell custom scope under the Dealix name.
- Treating the Academy as a revenue line independent of proof quality.

## 8. Outcome

The Academy is the mechanism that turns Dealix from a firm into a **standard**.
