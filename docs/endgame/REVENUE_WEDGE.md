# Revenue Wedge — Revenue Intelligence Sprint

The wedge is the smallest, sharpest offer that opens the door to the full Dealix operating model. The chosen wedge is the **Revenue Intelligence Sprint**.

## 1. Why this wedge

- Closest to money — easiest to justify.
- Easy to explain to a non-technical buyer.
- Pain is obvious in almost every B2B company.
- Produces a Proof Pack quickly.
- Opens a natural recurring contract (Monthly RevOps).
- Forces the engagement to exercise `data_os`, `revenue_os`, and `reporting_os`.

## 2. The promise

> We turn scattered sales data and opportunities into a Data Quality Score, an organized account list, a Top Opportunities list, a Draft Pack for review, a mini pipeline, and a Proof Pack — in a fixed-scope sprint.

## 3. What is delivered

- **Data Quality Score** — duplicate rate, completeness, freshness.
- **Organized account list** — deduplicated, segmented, ICP-scored.
- **Top Opportunities** — ranked with rationale.
- **Draft Pack** — outreach drafts, *draft-only*, never auto-sent.
- **Mini pipeline** — stage definitions and instrumented next steps.
- **Proof Pack** — the reporting closure (`PROOF_ECONOMY.md`).

## 4. What it is *not*

- Not lead generation.
- Not scraping.
- Not cold outreach.
- Not list buying.

Traditional lead generation: lists + scraping + cold outreach + promises. Dealix: permissioned data + scoring + draft-only outreach + governance + proof + pipeline discipline.

## 5. Fixed-scope structure

- **Duration:** 2–3 weeks.
- **Price:** fixed.
- **Inputs:** the client’s own data + approved public data sources.
- **Outputs:** the six artifacts above.
- **Exit:** Proof Pack and a retainer offer.

## 6. Operating discipline

- Every output passes through `governance_os`.
- Every account scoring run is logged in the AI Run Ledger.
- No external action is taken — only drafts.
- Any unsafe action attempted by the model is recorded as a governance event and surfaced in the Proof Pack as Risk Proof.

## 7. Expansion path

- Sprint → Monthly RevOps OS (recurring).
- Sprint → Company Brain Sprint (when knowledge gaps surface).
- Sprint → Monthly Governance (when risk surface is high).
- Sprint → Governed AI Operations Program (enterprise).

## 8. Failure modes

- Treating the sprint as a “list project.” It is an operating capability install.
- Allowing scope creep into outbound execution. Outbound action belongs to the client until governance proves the runtime.
- Missing the Proof Pack. A sprint without a Proof Pack is not finished.
