# Productized Offer Stack

Dealix sells a small number of fixed-scope, fixed-price, fixed-proof offers. Custom work is the exception, not the rule. Every offer points to the same Core OS and the same proof economy.

## 1. Stack overview

| Tier | Offer | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Entry | Capability Diagnostic | Score current AI operating capability and recommend the next step |
| Wedge | Revenue Intelligence Sprint | Convert sales data into a governed revenue capability |
| Trust | AI Governance Review | Audit, rule pack, runtime install |
| Expansion | Company Brain Sprint | Index knowledge and add cited Q&A |
| Expansion | AI Quick Win Sprint | Govern one high-leverage workflow |
| Recurring | Monthly RevOps OS | Ongoing operation of `revenue_os` |
| Recurring | Monthly Company Brain | Ongoing operation of `brain_os` |
| Recurring | Monthly Governance | Ongoing operation of `governance_os` |
| Recurring | Monthly AI Ops | Ongoing operation of `operations_os` |
| Enterprise | Governed AI Operations Program | Multi-OS engagement with SLAs |

## 2. Entry — Capability Diagnostic

See `CAPABILITY_DIAGNOSTIC.md`. Sold standalone; refundable against any sprint within 30 days.

## 3. Wedge — Revenue Intelligence Sprint

See `REVENUE_WEDGE.md`.

## 4. Trust — AI Governance Review

- Audit of current AI usage, exposure, and data handling.
- Custom rule pack added to the governance runtime.
- Approval matrix + escalation rules installed.
- Audit log and AI Run Ledger turned on.

Output: Risk Proof Pack + a recommended Monthly Governance retainer.

## 5. Expansion — Company Brain Sprint

- Index approved knowledge sources into `brain_os`.
- Wire `answer_with_citations` to internal channels.
- Install `knowledge_eval` to track insufficient-evidence rate.

Output: Knowledge Proof Pack + Monthly Company Brain retainer.

## 6. Expansion — AI Quick Win Sprint

- Identify one bottleneck workflow.
- Govern it end-to-end via `operations_os`.
- Run approval flow + audit.

Output: Time/Quality Proof Pack + Monthly AI Ops retainer.

## 7. Recurring — Monthly OS retainers

Every retainer follows the same shape:

- Operating cadence (weekly review, monthly executive report).
- A dedicated operator and a delivery analyst.
- Quarterly proof updates.
- Quarterly governance review.
- Annual price review tied to proof.

## 8. Enterprise — Governed AI Operations Program

- Multiple OS modules under one program.
- SLAs on uptime, audit, approval latency.
- Quarterly executive readout.
- Joint roadmap with the client’s internal AI office.

## 9. What is *not* in the stack

- Custom AI development projects with open scope.
- “Pilot” engagements without a proof commitment.
- Generic “chatbot” work.
- Lead generation campaigns.
- Hourly consulting.

## 10. Pricing principles

- Price the **outcome and the operating capability**, not hours.
- Every recurring retainer is priced above the value of *one* proof item per month.
- The Diagnostic is intentionally cheap; everything else is intentionally not.
