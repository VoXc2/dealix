# Founder Daily Runbook

## Purpose
Keep the founder focused on the highest-leverage actions every day.

## Morning block

1. Review executive dashboard.
2. Pick top 3 outcomes for today.
3. Identify one blocker to remove.
4. Review sales pipeline and customer risk.
5. Confirm the most important decision needed today.

## Midday block

1. Customer or sales action.
2. Product or proof action.
3. Team unblock.
4. Partner or investor follow-up when relevant.

## Evening block

1. What actually moved?
2. What did not move?
3. What did we learn from customers?
4. What risk increased?
5. What gets stopped tomorrow?
6. What is the first action tomorrow?

## Rules

- No day without customer or market contact.
- No meeting without decision or owner.
- No new idea enters execution without stopping something else.
- No AI/customer claim without evidence.
- No dashboard metric without a decision attached.
