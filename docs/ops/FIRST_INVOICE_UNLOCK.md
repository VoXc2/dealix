# First Invoice Unlock

## Purpose
Invoice #1 is not a celebration event.
Invoice #1 is the first proof that the Dealix operating system sells.

## Non-Negotiable
Before invoice:
- Register a Capital Asset
- Confirm scope
- Confirm exclusions
- Confirm data boundary
- Confirm governance boundary
- Define proof target

## 8-Action Cascade
1. Register Capital Asset first.
2. Generate or queue Trust Pack.
3. Confirm buyer, scope, and owner.
4. Confirm exclusions:
   - no scraping
   - no cold WhatsApp automation
   - no LinkedIn automation
   - no guaranteed sales outcomes
   - no external action without approval
5. Confirm data boundary and Source Passport requirement.
6. Send invoice.
7. Activate sprint or retainer scheduler.
8. Record proof target and value ledger expectation.

## No Celebration Rule
No celebration tweets.
No public claims.
No case study.
No autonomous upsell.
Public communication requires verified proof and client approval.

## Invoice #1 Proof Target
Invoice #1 must produce at least:
- one Proof Pack
- one Value Ledger entry
- one Capital Asset
- one Retainer or next-step recommendation
