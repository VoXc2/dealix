# Founder 90-Day Cadence — إيقاع المؤسس لـ٩٠ يوماً
## Macro view · Wave 17 · 2026-Q2

---

## How to use this doc — كيف تستخدم هذه الوثيقة

The daily and weekly cadence lives in [`docs/sales-kit/DAILY_EXECUTION_SCHEDULE_AR.md`](../sales-kit/DAILY_EXECUTION_SCHEDULE_AR.md) and the per-day operating loop lives in [`docs/ops/DAILY_OPERATING_LOOP.md`](DAILY_OPERATING_LOOP.md). This doc is the macro view that connects ninety days of execution into three thirty-day arcs. Each arc has a single primary outcome, four weekly milestones, and one binary decision gate at the end. If a gate fails, the arc does not advance — outreach pauses, the founder runs a one-day retro, and the qualification or pricing assumption that broke is fixed before any new prospect is engaged.

الإيقاع اليومي والأسبوعي في `DAILY_EXECUTION_SCHEDULE_AR.md` وحلقة التشغيل اليومية في `DAILY_OPERATING_LOOP.md`. هذه الوثيقة هي النظرة الكلية التي تربط تسعين يوماً من التنفيذ في ثلاثة أقواس مدة كل قوس ثلاثون يوماً. لكل قوس مخرَج رئيسي واحد، وأربعة معالم أسبوعيّة، وبوّابة قرار ثنائية في النهاية. لو فشلت البوّابة، لا يتقدم القوس — يتوقف التواصل، ويُجري المؤسس استرجاع يوم واحد، ويُصلَح افتراض التأهيل أو التسعير الذي انكسر قبل التواصل مع أي عميل محتمل جديد.

## Days 1–30: Diagnostic engine — أيّام ١-٣٠: محرّك التشخيص

**Primary outcome — المخرَج الرئيسي.** Eight Strategic Diagnostics delivered to qualified Saudi B2B services prospects, one Governed Ops Retainer signed (4,999 SAR/month), and the diagnostic-to-retainer playbook documented as a Capital Asset.

**Why diagnostics first.** The free Strategic Diagnostic is the only intake on the 2026 ladder, and it is the single instrument that calibrates which prospects are real. A founder running outreach without first having ten diagnostics under their belt does not yet know which qualification questions matter. The first thirty days exist to learn that calibration in the open, not to chase the retainer prematurely.

**Weekly milestones.**

| Week | Milestone — المعلَم |
|---|---|
| Week 1 | Outreach list of 24 named B2B services prospects compiled from existing relationships and Source Passports. Two diagnostics booked. Daily routine running cleanly for 5 mornings. |
| Week 2 | First two diagnostics delivered (each = a 60-minute session + a 6-page bilingual report). One follow-up retainer conversation opened. |
| Week 3 | Five diagnostics delivered cumulative. First retainer LOI conversation. The diagnostic template stabilises (no major edits needed). |
| Week 4 | Eight diagnostics delivered cumulative. One signed Governed Ops Retainer (4,999 SAR/month, 3-month minimum). Day-30 retro written. |

**Day-30 decision gate.** Were ≥ 8 diagnostics delivered? If yes, advance to Days 31–60. If no, halt all outbound outreach and review the qualification screen — almost always the failure is not effort but unfit prospects entering the diagnostic. Do not re-open outreach until the qualification screen is rewritten and the founder can recite the new screen from memory.

## Days 31–60: Retainer scaling — أيّام ٣١-٦٠: توسيع الريتينر

**Primary outcome — المخرَج الرئيسي.** Three Governed Ops Retainers committed (cumulative), the first Revenue Intelligence Sprint conversation opened with a qualified prospect, and one anchor partner LOI signed from one of the three archetypes in [`docs/sales-kit/ANCHOR_PARTNER_OUTREACH.md`](../sales-kit/ANCHOR_PARTNER_OUTREACH.md).

**Why retainers before the flagship.** A 4,999 SAR/month retainer with a 3-month minimum is 15,000 SAR of committed revenue per signed account — three signed accounts is 45,000 SAR of recurring base, enough to fund the delivery infrastructure that makes the 25,000 SAR Sprint deliverable. Selling the Sprint without the retainer base underneath produces a deliverability cliff after the second Sprint, because the founder is already overcommitted to delivery and pipeline collapses simultaneously.

**Weekly milestones.**

| Week | Milestone — المعلَم |
|---|---|
| Week 5 | Two retainers committed cumulative. Diagnostic flow running at 4 per week. First anchor partner conversation booked (Big-4 governance lead or VC investment partner). |
| Week 6 | Three retainers committed cumulative. First Sprint conversation opened with a retainer client who has revenue forecast pain. |
| Week 7 | Anchor partner LOI signed (one of the three archetypes). Partner Covenant counter-signed. |
| Week 8 | Sprint scoping doc shared with the qualified prospect. Day-60 retro written. |

**Day-60 decision gate.** Were ≥ 3 retainers committed? If yes, advance to Days 61–90. If no, do not lower the 4,999 SAR floor — instead revisit which prospects are entering the diagnostic and whether the diagnostic is closing on the right pain. The pricing reframe ([`docs/sales-kit/PRICING_REFRAME_2026Q2.md`](../sales-kit/PRICING_REFRAME_2026Q2.md)) is explicit: the floor does not move below 4,999 this year.

## Days 61–90: Flagship + partner activation — أيّام ٦١-٩٠: تفعيل الرئيسي والشركاء

**Primary outcome — المخرَج الرئيسي.** One Revenue Intelligence Sprint closed (25,000 SAR, 30-day fixed), one anchor partner contract signed (LOI converted), and ≥ 100,000 SAR ARR committed cumulative across retainers + Sprint annualization.

**Why this arc closes Wave 17.** The Sprint is the proof-of-conviction sale. After one closed Sprint with a Proof Pack and a Capital Asset, the next Sprint becomes a quoted reference, not a leap. The anchor partner contract converts the LOI from days 31–60 into a signed referral arrangement. The 100,000 SAR ARR threshold is the trigger for Wave 18 planning — live charge cutover, the first full-time hire, and beachhead expansion into one regulated sector (banking is the leading candidate).

**Weekly milestones.**

| Week | Milestone — المعلَم |
|---|---|
| Week 9 | Sprint kicked off with the prospect from week 8. Anchor partner contract drafted and counter-signed within the same week. |
| Week 10 | Sprint at midpoint — 3-source merge complete, governance pack drafted. Two new retainer LOIs in flight from anchor partner referrals. |
| Week 11 | Sprint at week-3 — Proof Pack assembly underway, Capital Asset registered. |
| Week 12 | Sprint closed with Proof Pack PDF delivered. ARR run-rate computed against the 100,000 SAR threshold. Day-90 retro written. |

**Day-90 decision gate.** Did we hit (1) one Sprint closed, (2) one anchor partner contract, and (3) ≥ 100,000 SAR ARR committed? If yes, plan Wave 18 — live charge cutover (flip the `intent_only` flag for retainer renewals), hire #1 (delivery operator, not a salesperson), and the banking sector unlock conversation with one named SAMA-licensed prospect. If no, run a two-day retrospective and reframe the offer ladder before any new outreach. The reframe doctrine permits the floor to be revisited only if recurring SAR slips below 100,000 — and even then, never below 2,999.

## Anti-pattern catalog — قائمة الأنماط المحظورة

The 90-day cadence collapses if any of the following are tolerated. None of these is a small leak; each one converts directly into a missed gate.

- **Do NOT code.** Founder calendar is 4h SELL / 2h DELIVER / 0h CODE. Claude does the code. Every founder hour spent coding is a non-event for revenue and a missed prospect call.
- **Do NOT read AI Twitter / X.** The signal-to-noise ratio is negative for a Saudi B2B services founder. Replace it with one Saudi business publication during morning routine.
- **Do NOT pivot the offer ladder before Day 90.** Three rungs only — Diagnostic, Retainer, Sprint. Adding a fourth rung mid-cycle restarts the funnel learning curve from zero.
- **Do NOT lower the 4,999 SAR floor.** The pricing reframe is explicit: not below 4,999 this year. Below the floor, the buyer reclassifies the engagement as a side project and renewals collapse.
- **Do NOT discount the 25,000 SAR Sprint to chase a no-fit prospect.** A no-fit prospect at half price produces double the delivery cost and zero usable Capital Asset. Walk away with a polite no, not a discount.
- **Do NOT skip the Proof Pack on a closed engagement.** Commitment #10 is a CI test, not a guideline. A closed engagement without a Proof Pack cannot be invoiced, cannot be referenced, cannot be called closed.

لا تَكتب كود. لا تتابع AI Twitter. لا تُغيّر سلّم العروض قبل اليوم ٩٠. لا تُنزل الأرضية تحت ٤٬٩٩٩ ر.س. لا تخصم سبرنت الـ٢٥ك لعميل غير مناسب. لا تتجاوز Proof Pack في ارتباط مغلق.
