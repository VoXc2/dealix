# Executive Dashboard Specification

## Purpose
Define the minimum dashboard a founder needs to run Dealix weekly.

## Dashboard sections

### 1. Revenue

| Metric | Definition | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| New pipeline | Value of new qualified opportunities | Weekly |
| Closed won | Signed revenue | Weekly |
| Cash collected | Received cash | Weekly |
| Sales cycle | Days from first meeting to close | Monthly |

### 2. GTM

| Metric | Definition | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Target accounts added | New ICP accounts added | Weekly |
| Meetings booked | Qualified meetings booked | Weekly |
| Conversion by stage | Stage-to-stage movement | Weekly |
| Channel source | Source of opportunity | Weekly |

### 3. Product

| Metric | Definition | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Activation rate | Accounts reaching first value | Weekly |
| Time to value | Time to first measurable outcome | Weekly |
| Weekly active teams | Customer teams using core flows | Weekly |
| Critical defects | P0/P1 issues | Daily |

### 4. Customer

| Metric | Definition | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Health score | Red/yellow/green customer health | Weekly |
| Churn risk | At-risk accounts | Weekly |
| Expansion signal | Accounts showing growth potential | Weekly |
| Support SLA | First response and resolution | Weekly |

### 5. AI quality

| Metric | Definition | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Reviewed AI outputs | Percent approved before use | Weekly |
| AI incident count | AI-related defects or trust issues | Weekly |
| Evaluation pass rate | Test cases passing | Release |
| Unsupported claims | Claims without evidence | Weekly |

### 6. Finance and operations

| Metric | Definition | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Runway | Operating months remaining | Weekly |
| Burn | Cash outflow | Monthly |
| Gross margin | Revenue minus delivery cost | Monthly |
| Open P1 risks | Critical unresolved risks | Weekly |

## Weekly narrative template

1. What changed?
2. Why did it change?
3. What is the constraint?
4. What decision is needed?
5. What will be done next week?

## Founder red flags

- Pipeline grows but meetings do not convert.
- Product usage grows but value proof is weak.
- AI output volume grows without evaluation.
- Revenue grows but margin collapses.
- Team grows but ownership becomes unclear.
- Dashboard exists but decisions do not change.
