# Dealix Offer Architecture

## Offer ladder

| Level | Offer | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Revenue Diagnosis | Find leaks |
| 2 | Paid Sprint | Ship one visible result |
| 3 | Monthly OS | Operate revenue/growth |
| 4 | Enterprise OS | Govern AI, clients, proof, integrations |

## Productized services

### Revenue OS Sprint
ICP map, pipeline map, lead scoring, follow-up, founder cockpit.

### Marketing OS Sprint
Offer architecture, landing pages, campaigns, proof assets, AI visibility.

### Operations OS Sprint
Workflow map, SLA, approvals, weekly report.

### Trust OS Sprint
AI governance, risk register, evidence pack, audit trail.

### Client Portal OS Sprint
Client portal, proof room, renewal dashboard, expansion signals.
