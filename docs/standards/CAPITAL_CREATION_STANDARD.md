# Capital Creation Standard

Every project produces at least:

- 1 Trust Asset
- 1 Product or Knowledge Asset
- 1 Expansion Path

## Asset types

Service Capital, Product Capital, Knowledge Capital, Trust Capital, Market Capital, Standard Capital, Partner Capital, Venture Capital.

## Worked example — Revenue Intelligence Sprint

- Trust Asset = Revenue Proof Pack
- Product Asset = scoring improvement
- Knowledge Asset = B2B objections library
- Expansion Path = Monthly RevOps OS

## Rule

> Projects that fail this minimum are strategically incomplete regardless of revenue.

Typed: `command_control_os.capital_command.engagement_strategically_complete()`.
