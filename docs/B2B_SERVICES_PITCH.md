## What the Pilot delivers (Day 1 → Day 7)

| Day | Deliverable |
|---|---|
| 1 | Diagnostic + Pilot offer + Moyasar invoice URL (test mode) |
| 2 | First 2 inbound captures + qualification + Arabic draft replies (founder reviews + sends manually) |
| 3 | 2 more captures + drafts |
| 4 | 3 captures + 7-day follow-up plan per opportunity |
| 5 | 3 captures (total 10) + customer reviews drafts |
| 6 | Customer approves drafts; founder sends; sign-off doc draft |
| 7 | Bilingual Proof Pack assembled + sent + upsell call scheduled |

---

## Why 499 SAR (not free)

- Free trials don't produce real customer behavior. 499 SAR forces
  the customer to commit, which produces accurate Pilot data.
- 499 is the introductory price; locked until customer #5.
- After customer #5, the Pilot retires and is replaced by a higher-priced
  Standard tier.

---

## What we will NOT do

- ❌ Cold WhatsApp / cold email
- ❌ LinkedIn DM automation
- ❌ Scraping / purchased lists
- ❌ Auto-charge (Moyasar test-mode for the first 5; no live charge until Decision §S5)
- ❌ Promises of guaranteed leads/sales/rankings
- ❌ Sending anything to a customer's customers without explicit per-message approval

---

## Disqualification (when to politely decline)

| Pattern | Action |
|---|---|
| Wants guaranteed conversion numbers | Decline. |
| Pre-revenue, no existing lead flow | Recommend free Diagnostic only; defer Pilot. |
| Wants you to send cold messages on their behalf | Decline. |
| Asks for scraping or list-building from public sources | Decline. |
| Refuses to do bilingual outputs | Decline politely; not our ICP. |
| > 200 employees | Direct to enterprise sales (separate motion). |

---

## After-Pilot path

Successful Pilot → 14-day cooldown → Executive Growth OS (2,999/mo) call.

The upsell call is a 30-min review of:
- What the Pilot proved
- What the next 30 days could look like
- Founder commitment expectations

If "no upsell": move to nurture for 90 days (one valuable touch).
