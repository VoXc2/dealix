## Executive summary (English)

### Headline

> Dealix is an AI Revenue Workforce for Saudi B2B companies: strategy,
> growth, sales support, partnership discovery, safe outreach drafts,
> delivery workflows, and proof reporting — under approval-first controls.

### What Dealix does

1. **Understands your company** — Company Brain from a 30-min Diagnostic + your files
2. **Surfaces opportunities** — Market Radar (licensed sources + internal data)
3. **Drafts messages** — Arabic + English drafts, founder-reviewed before send
4. **Prevents risks** — ComplianceGuard blocks cold WA, LinkedIn automation, scraping
5. **Manages delivery** — Delivery Plan per service from the 32-service matrix
6. **Produces Proof Packs** — bilingual, evidence-backed, signed by you

### What Dealix does NOT do

- ❌ No cold WhatsApp / cold email
- ❌ No LinkedIn automation
- ❌ No scraping or purchased lists
- ❌ No auto-charge from your account
- ❌ No revenue/ranking guarantees (we commit to work + Proof Pack)
- ❌ Doesn't replace your team — adds an execution layer above

### Why safety is a selling point

In KSA, PDPL requires:
- Consent before marketing contact
- Disclosure of data types
- Clear opt-out path
- Consent timestamp + audit log

Dealix protects you from non-compliant growth.
