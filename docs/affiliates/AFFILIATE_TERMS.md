# Affiliate Terms — شروط برنامج الشركاء

Plain-language terms for the Dealix affiliate program. These terms encode the
program doctrine; the `affiliate_os/` module enforces them in code.

## Eligibility

- Affiliates apply and are approved individually. Approval is at Dealix's
  discretion, informed by a fit score.
- An affiliate must have a genuine audience. No purchased lists, no scraping,
  no cold outreach on Dealix's behalf.

## Commissions

- A commission accrues **only** when a referred deal's first invoice is paid
  and that payment is confirmed with evidence.
- Commission rate is set by tier at approval time: standard 10%, preferred 15%,
  strategic 20% of the first paid invoice amount.
- One commission per referred invoice. No stacking, no retroactive re-tiering.

## Payouts

- A payout is requested against an accrued commission and **requires human
  approval** before it settles.
- A payout below the minimum (`min_payout_sar` in `affiliate_rules.yaml`) is
  not released until the balance clears the minimum.
- Payout evidence (the underlying paid invoice) is re-verified at payout time.

## Conduct

- Affiliates may only use copy that has passed the asset review gate.
- No guaranteed-outcome claims. No claims about security, compliance, legal,
  privacy, or ROI without an approved source. See `FORBIDDEN_CLAIMS.md`.
- Every affiliate post must carry the required disclosure. See `DISCLOSURE_GUIDE.md`.

## Termination

- Dealix may suspend or remove an affiliate who breaches these terms.
- Accrued, evidence-backed commissions earned before a good-faith termination
  are honored through the normal approval-gated payout process.
