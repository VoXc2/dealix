## Topic rotation — التناوب الأسبوعي

Every week is the same three-day skeleton, three topic lanes:

| Day | Lane | Goal | Hashtags |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monday — الإثنين** | Technical proof / إثبات تقني | Show the governance machinery. Source passports, decision matrices, ledger snapshots (no PII). | `#SaudiAI` `#PDPL` |
| **Wednesday — الأربعاء** | Case-safe pattern / نمط آمن دون أسماء | Sector outcome, anonymized. Numbers from value_ledger only when Client-Confirmed. | `#RevenueOps` `#B2B` |
| **Saturday — السبت** | Counter-narrative / الموقف المضاد | Why we refuse a market shortcut, and what we built instead. | `#SaudiAI` `#B2B` |

Rotation rule: the four hashtags `#SaudiAI #PDPL #RevenueOps #B2B` are mandatory across the month. Each post carries two of the four; Monday adds `#PDPL`, Wednesday adds `#RevenueOps`, Saturday alternates `#SaudiAI`+`#B2B`. The Arabic equivalents `#السعودية` `#حماية_البيانات_السعودية` `#عمليات_الإيراد` are appended on every post.

## Operating notes — ملاحظات التشغيل

- **Drafting time / وقت الصياغة:** 25–35 minutes per post, founder voice only. Drafts are reviewed against the non-negotiables before publishing.
- **Approval gate / بوابة الموافقة:** every Saturday post is reviewed against the constitution document. Counter-narrative content must name what we refuse precisely; it must not insult competitors by name.
- **Engagement reply policy / سياسة الرد:** all replies are answered within 24 hours, in the language used by the commenter. PII in DMs is redacted and the conversation moved to the standard intake.
- **Measurement / القياس:** we measure replies that convert to a Free Diagnostic, not vanity follower counts. The metric goes into `value_ledger` as tier 1 (data support) until a paid sprint follows.
