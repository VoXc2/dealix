Revenue workflow — where follow-up and deals leak. CRM data quality — whether your data is ready for a decision to be made on it. And AI usage — where it is used without an evidence trail or without review.

The output is not a report that gets filed. It is three practical decisions you act on this month, and a 14-section Proof Pack documenting everything.

This offer is not a new price. It is an entry into the existing offer ladder: it starts at the Free Mini Diagnostic, or at the 499 SAR Revenue Proof Sprint.
