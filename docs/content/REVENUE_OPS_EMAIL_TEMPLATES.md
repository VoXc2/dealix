# Revenue Ops — Email & Outreach Templates — قوالب البريد والتواصل

> Bilingual founder-voice templates, one per funnel touchpoint, for the Governed Revenue Ops Diagnostic. AR first, EN second. No emojis. No model names. No guaranteed-outcome language. Every template is a DRAFT — it enters the founder approval queue before any external send. Nothing here is sent automatically.
>
> Cross-link: [REVENUE_OPS_MACHINE.md](./REVENUE_OPS_MACHINE.md), [LINKEDIN_CADENCE_PLAN.md](./LINKEDIN_CADENCE_PLAN.md), landing page `/dealix-diagnostic`, Sample Proof Pack `/assets/proof/sample-proof-pack.html`.

---

## How to use these — كيفيّة الاستخدام

- Each template maps to one funnel touchpoint. Fill the `[bracketed]` fields with real, verified detail.
- Keep them short. Founder voice, plain language, no marketing adjectives.
- Never insert a guaranteed sales number, conversion rate, or ROI figure. Use "estimated" or "evidenced opportunities".
- Every template ends with a draft-status line. That line is internal context for the founder approving the send; remove it only at the moment of approval, never before.
- Templates 3 (meeting brief) is internal-facing — it never goes to a customer.
