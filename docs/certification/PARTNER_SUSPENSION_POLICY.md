# Partner Suspension Policy

A partner is paused immediately when:

- Unsafe claims.
- Skipping Proof Pack.
- Bypassing QA.
- Misusing client data.
- Selling prohibited automation under Dealix brand.

## Restoration

- Root-cause review.
- Method recertification.
- QA-supervised delivery for the next two engagements.
- Capital Ledger entry recording the incident and the new rule.

## Principle

> The covenant is a contract. Violations are paused, not warned.
