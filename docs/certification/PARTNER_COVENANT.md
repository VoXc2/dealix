# Partner Covenant

Every partner accepts the following in writing:

- No scraping systems.
- No cold WhatsApp automation.
- No LinkedIn automation.
- No fake proof.
- No guaranteed outcome claims.
- No client-facing output without QA.
- No external action without approval.

Typed: `operating_manual_os.partner_covenant.PartnerCovenant` + `evaluate_partner_covenant()`.

A violation pauses the partner; not a warning.
