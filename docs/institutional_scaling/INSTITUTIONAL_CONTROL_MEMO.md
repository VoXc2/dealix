# Institutional Control Memo

A monthly 10-section memo that turns the firm into a holding-company operator.

## 1. Sections

1. Revenue
2. Proof
3. Trust
4. Productization
5. Capital
6. Clients
7. Units
8. Market
9. Stop List
10. Strategic Bet

## 2. Discipline

- Drafted by the founder or COO; reviewed by BU leads.
- Numbers cite the Decision Log and the Proof Library.
- Every "stop" cites a kill criterion.
- Every "scale" cites the six strong-scale conditions.
- "none" is an acceptable answer; vague prose is not.

## 3. Typed surface

`institutional_scaling_os.control_memo.InstitutionalControlMemo` validates section completeness; `render_control_memo` produces stable Markdown.

## 4. The principle

> Leadership is documented monthly, in writing, even when nobody outside the firm asks for it.
