# Institutional Rules

The top rule of scaling.

## 1. The rule

> Scale only what is: proven, governed, repeatable, profitable, and owned.

**AR:** لا توسع إلا ما كان: مثبتًا، محكومًا، متكررًا، مربحًا، وله مالك واضح.

## 2. Do not scale

- A service without proof.
- A feature without usage.
- A partner without QA.
- An agent without an owner.
- A workflow without approval.
- A market without a clear buyer.

## 3. Scale

- A service with proof and a retainer cadence.
- A feature that reduces delivery time.
- A partner who follows the method.
- A unit with KPIs and a named owner.

## 4. The principle

> Scaling weakness produces stronger weakness. Strengthen first; scale second.
