# Dealix Institutional Scaling Doctrine

> Dealix doesn't grow by adding more services. Dealix grows when **everything** becomes repeatable, governable, measurable, provable, and distributable.

Eighth strategic layer. Built on the prior seven.

## 1. Scaling model

```
Founder-led Proof
→ Productized Delivery
→ Governed Core OS
→ Retainers
→ Client Workspace
→ Business Units
→ Certified Partners
→ Academy
→ Venture Factory
→ Holding Company
```

## 2. Scaling stages

1. Prove the pain.
2. Sell a Sprint.
3. Deliver with quality.
4. Produce Proof.
5. Convert into Retainer.
6. Convert repetition into Product.
7. Convert product into Business Unit.
8. Convert mature unit into Venture.

## 3. The institutional rule

> Scale only what is: **proven, governed, repeatable, profitable, and owned**.

Do not scale:
- A service without proof.
- A feature without usage.
- A partner without QA.
- An agent without an owner.
- A workflow without approval.
- A market without a clear buyer.
