# Institutional Scaling — Consolidated Doctrine

This document consolidates the remaining sections of the Institutional Scaling doctrine. See also `INSTITUTIONAL_SCALING_DOCTRINE.md`, `INSTITUTIONAL_RULES.md`, and `ENTERPRISE_TRUST_ENGINE.md` in this folder.

## 1. Institutional Flywheel

```
Paid Sprint → Proof Pack → Retainer → Capital Asset → Product Module →
Higher Margin → Stronger Delivery → More Proof → Higher Price →
Partner Distribution → More Clients → Stronger Core OS
```

Every engagement must produce: cash, proof, capital, product signal, retainer path, market insight.

## 2. Agent Identity Doctrine

Every agent has the canonical card from `endgame_os/agent_control.py`, extended by `institutional_control_os/agent_control_plane.py` with `allowed_tools`. Autonomy: MVP allows 0–3 only. Level 4 needs contract clause; Level 5 enterprise-only; Level 6 forbidden.

## 3. Privacy-by-Runtime

Every AI task passes Source Passport, PII Detector, Allowed Use Checker, Redaction, Policy Decision, Audit Event, Approval Requirement. Privacy is enforced **at runtime**, not in a PDF.

## 6. Institutional Product Stack

- **Core:** Capability Diagnostic, Data Readiness Engine, Governance Runtime, Proof Pack Generator, Capital Ledger, Founder Command Center.
- **Business:** Revenue Intelligence Sprint, Company Brain Sprint, AI Quick Win Sprint, AI Governance Review.
- **Recurring:** Monthly RevOps OS, Monthly Company Brain, Monthly Governance, Monthly AI Ops.
- **Enterprise:** AI Control Plane, Audit Exports, Policy Registry, Agent Registry, Risk Index, Executive AI Value Dashboard.
- **Ecosystem:** Academy, Certification, Partner Portal, Benchmark Reports, Vertical Playbooks.

## 7. Institutional Metrics

Company: MRR, Gross Margin, Proof Packs delivered, Proof-to-retainer conversion, Capital assets created, Manual steps productized, Governance incidents, AI run audit coverage, Client health, BU maturity.

Trust: % sources with Source Passport, % outputs with governance decision, % AI runs logged, % external actions approved, PII flags, unsafe actions blocked.

Market: Inbound diagnostics, Partner referrals, Proof Pack requests, Capability Score mentions, Benchmark downloads, Academy waitlist, Enterprise trust inquiries.

**Rule:** If trust metrics are weak, do not scale. If proof is weak, do not raise claims. If productization is weak, you are becoming an agency.

## 8. Operating Cadence

- **Daily:** top revenue opportunity / delivery risk / governance risk / proof to create.
- **Weekly:** sold / delivered / proved / learned / productize / retainer-ready.
- **Monthly:** strongest offer / weakest offer / maturing unit / recurring risk / accumulated asset / service to kill.
- **Quarterly:** closer to holding / MRR up / market language adoption / partner channel / venture candidate.

## 9. Institutional Control Memo

Monthly 10-section memo: Revenue, Proof, Trust, Productization, Capital, Clients, Units, Market, Stop List, Strategic Bet.
