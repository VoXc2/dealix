# Institutional Product Stack

Five product categories, with strict order of build.

## 1. Core Products

Capability Diagnostic, Data Readiness Engine, Governance Runtime, Proof Pack Generator, Capital Ledger, Founder Command Center.

## 2. Business Products

Revenue Intelligence Sprint, Company Brain Sprint, AI Quick Win Sprint, AI Governance Review.

## 3. Recurring Products

Monthly RevOps OS, Monthly Company Brain, Monthly Governance, Monthly AI Ops.

## 4. Enterprise Products

AI Control Plane, Audit Exports, Policy Registry, Agent Registry, Risk Index, Executive AI Value Dashboard.

## 5. Ecosystem Products

Academy, Certification, Partner Portal, Benchmark Reports, Vertical Playbooks.

## 6. Build order

Core → Business → Recurring → Enterprise → Ecosystem. Do not jump.

## 7. The principle

> Five categories, one operating model, one Core OS.
