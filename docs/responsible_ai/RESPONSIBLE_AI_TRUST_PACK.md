## صفحة «What Dealix refuses to build»

- No scraping systems.  
- No cold WhatsApp automation.  
- No LinkedIn automation.  
- No fake proof.  
- No guaranteed sales claims.  
- No PII in logs.  
- No source-less knowledge answers.  
- No external action without approval.  

هذه الصفحة وحدها ترفع Dealix من «AI vendor» إلى **trusted operating partner**.
