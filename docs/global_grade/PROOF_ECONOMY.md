# Proof Economy — Enterprise View

This document extends `docs/endgame/PROOF_ECONOMY.md` with the enterprise-grade rules for producing, governing, and using Proof Packs.

## 1. Proof Pack contents

- Problem
- Inputs
- Work Completed
- Metrics
- Before/After
- AI Outputs
- Governance Events
- Business Value
- Risks
- Recommended Next Step

## 2. Proof types

| Type | Example evidence |
| --- | --- |
| Revenue Proof | Pipeline value, opportunities created, deals influenced |
| Time Proof | Hours saved per workflow per week |
| Quality Proof | Error reduction, rework reduction |
| Risk Proof | Unsafe actions blocked, approvals logged, audit coverage |
| Knowledge Proof | Insufficient-evidence rate, citation coverage |

## 3. Proof-to-Retainer

Every Proof Pack ends with one explicit motion:

- **Continue** — same scope, recurring.
- **Expand** — add a new OS or BU.
- **Pause** — stop, with the reason recorded.

| Proof type strong | Retainer |
| --- | --- |
| Revenue | Monthly RevOps OS |
| Knowledge | Monthly Company Brain |
| Risk | Monthly Governance |
| Time / Quality | Monthly AI Ops |

## 4. Enterprise rules

- Proof Packs reference the EvidenceLevel scale (`auto_client_acquisition/proof_engine/evidence.py`).
- L0/L1 — internal only.
- L2 — customer reviewed, private.
- L3 — customer approved, private sales proof.
- L4 — public publish approved.
- L5 — revenue / expansion evidence (after commitment and tracking).
- Public claims at L4+ require explicit consent.
- Cross-customer aggregation requires written consent and aggregation rules.

## 5. Reproducibility

- Every metric is reproducible from raw records inside the engagement workspace.
- Before/after comparisons include a baseline definition.
- Governance events cite Audit Event IDs.
- Hero numbers without baselines are doctrine violation.

## 6. Operating discipline

- Reporting OS owns Proof Pack generation.
- Capital OS sweeps reusable assets from each pack.
- Intelligence OS feeds proof data into the decision engine.
- Governance OS contributes the audit and approval evidence.

## 7. Failure modes

- Selecting metrics after delivery to look favorable.
- Reporting single-period numbers without a baseline.
- Reusing a Proof Pack publicly without rechecking consent.
- Closing engagements without a pack and promising it “next week.”

## 8. The principle

Proof is the unit of Dealix economics. Every retainer, every standard, every academy claim, and every venture launch is denominated in it.
