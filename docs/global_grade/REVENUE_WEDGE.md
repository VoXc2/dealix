# Revenue Wedge — Enterprise View

The endgame layer defines the wedge offer (`docs/endgame/REVENUE_WEDGE.md`). This document specifies the enterprise-grade execution: governance posture, deliverables in detail, and pricing principles.

## 1. Outputs at engagement close

- **Data Quality Score** — quality, duplication, completeness, freshness per source.
- **Cleaned Account List** — deduplicated, segmented, ICP-scored.
- **Top 50 Ranked Accounts** — ranked with feature attribution.
- **Top 10 Next Actions** — specific, owned, dated.
- **Draft Pack** — outreach drafts, *draft-only*.
- **Mini Pipeline** — instrumented stages with definitions.
- **Proof Pack** — Revenue Proof Pack with before/after.

## 2. Difference vs. lead generation

| Traditional lead gen | Dealix Revenue Intelligence |
| --- | --- |
| Lists + scraping | Permissioned data only |
| Cold outreach | Draft-only outputs |
| Vague promises | Proof Pack with metrics |
| No governance | Runtime governance + audit |
| No pipeline | Mini Pipeline + discipline |

## 3. Enterprise execution

- Every data source enters via Source Passport.
- Every output passes through the governance runtime.
- Every model run is logged in the AI Run Ledger.
- No external action is taken — drafts only.
- Risk surfaces (PII, channel, claim) are recorded as Risk Proof entries inside the Revenue Proof Pack.

## 4. Pricing principles

- Fixed price, productized.
- Priced **above** the value of one month of the resulting Monthly RevOps OS retainer.
- Enterprise add-ons (multi-region, multi-product, multi-language) are priced as line items, not bundled.

## 5. Expansion paths

- → Monthly RevOps OS (most common).
- → Monthly Governance (if Risk Proof was the strongest signal).
- → Company Brain Sprint (if knowledge gaps surfaced during ICP scoring).
- → Governed AI Operations Program (multi-OS enterprise tier).

## 6. Anti-patterns

- Allowing scope to drift into auto-sent outbound campaigns.
- Treating the sprint as a “list build.”
- Skipping the mini pipeline because the buyer “already has a CRM.”
- Closing without a Proof Pack.

## 7. Why this remains the wedge

- It is the offer the market understands fastest.
- It produces the strongest Proof Pack in the shortest time.
- It establishes the firm’s governance discipline early.
- It opens the natural retainer that funds Dealix’s operating cadence.
