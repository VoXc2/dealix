- Academy without a proven method.
- Partner program without QA.
- Venture without retainers.
- SaaS without client pull.
- Enterprise without a Trust Pack.
- Business Unit without a Product Module.
- Client Workspace without a Product Module that supports it.

## 3. Chain health metric

`Stage-to-stage conversion` is the operating metric of the chain. The Intelligence OS reports the conversion rate per quarter and recommends `SCALE`, `KILL`, `RAISE_PRICE`, or `PRODUCTIZE` based on the pattern.

## 4. Why the chain is sacred

It is the difference between a firm that wins on hype and a company that compounds. Every off-chain action is one of:

- A signal that the chain needs an extension (rare).
- An exception that must be paid for in capital (acceptable, but recorded).
- A doctrine violation (refused).

## 5. The single line summary

> Convert a market signal into compounding capital, with proof and governance at every step. Refuse any path that skips one.
