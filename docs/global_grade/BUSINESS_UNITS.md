# Business Units — Enterprise View

Restates the BU map from `docs/endgame/BUSINESS_UNITS.md` and adds enterprise-grade operating notes.

## 1. Map

| BU | Primary Offer | Recurring Offer | Product Module | Proof Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dealix Revenue | Revenue Intelligence Sprint | Monthly RevOps OS | Revenue OS | Revenue Proof Pack |
| Dealix Brain | Company Brain Sprint | Monthly Company Brain | Knowledge OS | Knowledge Proof Pack |
| Dealix Governance | AI Governance Review | Monthly Governance | Governance Runtime | Risk Proof Pack |
| Dealix Operations | AI Quick Win Sprint | Monthly AI Ops | Workflow OS | Time/Quality Proof Pack |

## 2. Dealix Revenue — enterprise notes

- Multi-region pipeline support.
- Localized ICP scoring (Arabic + English).
- Outbound is always draft-only at enterprise tier.
- Pipeline integrates with the buyer’s CRM via the Platform addenda.

## 3. Dealix Brain — enterprise notes

- Tenant-isolated knowledge bases.
- Citation coverage tracked and reported.
- Insufficient-evidence rate gating answers.
- Periodic knowledge eval cycles.

## 4. Dealix Governance — enterprise notes

- Rule packs versioned and signed.
- Audit exports per quarter, with SLA.
- Risk Index dashboards per agent and per workflow.
- Drift detection across rule packs.

## 5. Dealix Operations — enterprise notes

- SOP versioning with change history.
- Approval flows integrated with the buyer’s identity provider.
- Error reduction tracked against baselines.
- Time savings reproducible from instrumented logs.

## 6. Cross-BU rules

- All BUs share `core_os`, `governance_os`, `reporting_os`, `intelligence_os`, `capital_os`.
- No BU forks the governance runtime.
- No BU runs an offer that bypasses the productized stack.
- Cross-BU work uses the same Proof Pack format.

## 7. Promotion gate

A capability becomes a BU when:

- 5+ paid engagements completed.
- 2+ active retainers.
- Repeatable delivery (QA ≥ 80%).
- Clear product module dependency.
- Healthy gross margin.
- Named owner ready.
- Proof library ≥ 3 strong assets.

## 8. Failure modes

- A BU forms before retainers exist.
- A BU forms around a single hero operator.
- A BU runs custom scope to hit numbers, breaking the productized model.
- BUs compete for the same buyer with different stories.
