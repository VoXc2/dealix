> AI value does not come from models alone. AI value comes from a **governed operating system** around the models.
>
> بالعربي: قيمة AI لا تأتي من النموذج وحده. قيمة AI تأتي من نظام تشغيل محكوم حول النموذج.

This document is the second strategic layer of Dealix — built on top of `docs/endgame/ENDGAME_OPERATING_DOCTRINE.md`. The endgame layer defines the constitution. This layer defines **how Dealix becomes enterprise-grade, regional, investable, and partnership-ready**.

The two layers are complementary, not duplicative:

- **Endgame layer** — doctrine, refusal, chain, capital, kills.
- **Global-grade layer** — enterprise trust, runtime governance as a product, Arabic-first quality, data intelligence, partner stack.

