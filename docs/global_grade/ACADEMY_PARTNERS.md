# Academy + Partners

The Academy and the Partner Program are the **distribution and authority arms** of Dealix. They run on top of the Dealix Method.

## 1. Academy tracks

- AI Ops Executive
- Revenue AI Operator
- Company Brain Builder
- AI Governance Lead
- Dealix Certified Partner
- Dealix Delivery Analyst

## 2. Academy launch conditions

- 10+ paid engagements completed.
- 3+ proof-backed case assets exist.
- The Dealix Method is documented and stable.
- A repeatable templates library exists.
- A QA rubric is in production use.

Until all five hold, the Academy does not exist publicly.

## 3. Partner tiers

- **Referral Partner** — refers inbound; no delivery rights.
- **Advisory Partner** — co-delivers diagnostic and roadmap, not sprints.
- **Implementation Partner** — delivers sprints under Dealix QA.
- **Certified Partner** — full delivery rights for a subset of offers.
- **Strategic Partner** — co-go-to-market with shared accounts.
- **White-label Partner** — delivers under their brand with Dealix runtime and Proof Pack.

## 4. Partner gate

A partner must:

- Understand and use the Dealix Method.
- Use the Proof Pack format.
- Accept Dealix QA.
- Accept the governance runtime.
- Not make guaranteed-outcome claims.
- Not sell unsafe automation under the Dealix brand.

## 5. Operating discipline

- Partner audits are quarterly.
- Partner-delivered Proof Packs go through Dealix QA before client sign-off.
- Partner training is recertified annually.
- A partner that violates governance is paused, not warned.

## 6. Why this matters

- Partners convert market demand into delivery without Dealix headcount.
- The Academy turns the method into market language.
- Together they form the distribution backbone of the holding company.

## 7. Failure modes

- Launching tiers before the method is stable.
- Letting partners sell custom scope under the Dealix brand.
- Treating partner revenue as net new without QA cost.
- Allowing certification to expire without recertification.
