## Context

Saudi B2B buyers trust partners they already work with — CRM
consultants, accounting firms, marketing agencies, cybersecurity
providers, business consultants, training providers. Dealix scales
by becoming the AI operations layer their existing partners
recommend. The Partner Operating System defines who is an ideal
partner, the promise we make them, the partnership models, and the
qualification gate. It extends `docs/AGENCY_PARTNER_PROGRAM.md`,
`docs/business/AGENCY_PARTNER_REVENUE_MODEL.md`, and
`docs/growth/AGENCY_RESELLER_PLAYBOOK.md`.

## Ideal Partner Types

| Type | Why they fit |
|---|---|
| **CRM consultants** | Sit inside RevOps decisions; sell our Lead Intelligence layer. |
| **ERP / accounting firms** | Sit inside Ops/Finance; sell our AI Ops layer. |
| **Marketing agencies** | Need defensible AI angles; resell Content/Lead Intelligence. |
| **Cybersecurity firms** | Already govern data; co-pitch Trust/Governance Retainer. |
| **Business / management consultants** | Set strategy; recommend Dealix as the AI execution arm. |
| **Training / academy providers** | Educate the market; refer clients post-training. |

## Partner Promise

> **Dealix becomes the AI operations layer for your clients —
> Arabic-first, governance-first, proof-led — without competing
> for your core business.**

What this means concretely:

- We never resell the partner's primary service.
- We bring AI delivery; partner brings client relationship.
- Proof Packs are co-branded by default; Dealix anonymizes if
  partner prefers.
- Partner gets visible credit and revenue share.

## Partnership Models

| Model | When | Compensation |
|---|---|---|
| **Referral** | Partner introduces a qualified buyer | 10% of first contract value (one-time) |
| **Revenue share** | Partner stays active in deal + light delivery | 15-20% recurring on retainer revenue |
| **Joint delivery** | Partner co-delivers, we lead AI | Split per scope, documented in SOW |
| **White-label (later)** | Partner sells under their brand using Dealix infra | Tiered; requires Stage 4+ productized infra per `docs/product/PRODUCTIZATION_SYSTEM.md` |

White-label is **not** available until a Stage 4 internal tool has
proved in two clients with QA pass.

## Partner Qualification Criteria

A partner is signed only if:

- They have **named active clients** that match Dealix target ICPs.
- They have **decision-maker access** at those clients.
- They are **Arabic-fluent** for Saudi-market delivery contexts.
- They agree to the **Dealix governance model** (PDPL, approvals,
  no spam/scrape).
- They commit to **at least one co-marketing motion / quarter**.
- They sign the **partner NDA + revenue share template**.

If any criterion is missing, the relationship stays informal (lead
exchange) and is not granted Partner status in the registry.

## Partner Lifecycle

1. **Discovery** — partner intro call, scored against criteria.
2. **Pilot deal** — one co-pitched opportunity to validate motion.
3. **Sign** — Partner agreement, NDA, revenue share template.
4. **Enable** — partner enablement pack (one-pager, decks, demo
   script, objection bank, Proof Pack samples).
5. **Operate** — monthly partner sync, quarterly co-marketing.
6. **Review** — annual review; promote / hold / exit.

## Cadence

- **Weekly** — Partnerships Lead reviews pipeline by partner.
- **Monthly** — partner syncs; revenue reconciliation.
- **Quarterly** — co-marketing motion; refreshed enablement pack.
- **Annual** — partner tier review.

## Metrics

- **# active partners** — track quarterly.
- **% pipeline sourced via partners** — target ≥30%.
- **Partner-sourced revenue share / quarter** — track.
- **Partner-sourced deal margin** — target ≥60% (after share).
- **Partner satisfaction** — quarterly survey ≥4/5.
