# Dealix Certification

## Purpose

Ensure every person delivering Dealix services follows the same **quality**, **governance**, and **proof** standards — not tribal knowledge.

**Status:** Internal roadmap only; not a public certification until 10–20 supervised projects exist.

## Certification levels

1. **Delivery Associate** — executes checklists under supervision  
2. **AI Ops Specialist** — runs simulations independently; drafts proof packs  
3. **Dealix Method Lead** — owns end-to-end sprint delivery + client review  
4. **Governance Reviewer** — can block external-facing outputs  
5. **Enterprise AI OS Architect** — multi-workflow design, integrations, SLA patterns  

## Requirements (each level)

- Complete **training** modules in [`docs/training/`](../training/)
- Pass **service simulation** (see `simulations/`)
- Pass **QA review** on sample outputs
- Deliver **supervised project** with clean [`docs/audit/CLIENT_PROJECT_AUDIT.md`](../audit/CLIENT_PROJECT_AUDIT.md)

## Naming (future public)

> **Dealix Certified AI Operations Practitioner** — levels 1–5 above.

Record internal progress in HR / Notion; do not issue external certificates until legal review.
