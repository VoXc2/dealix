## Context
Dealix Academy is how the Dealix Method becomes a product the firm can
sell without consuming delivery capacity. The Academy Path declares
the purpose, the course portfolio, the certification, and the gating
conditions that prevent premature launch. It complements the customer
success motion in `docs/CUSTOMER_SUCCESS_PLAYBOOK.md`, the strategic
narrative in `docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`, and the
master operating model captured in
`docs/strategic/DEALIX_MASTER_OPERATING_MODEL_AR.md`.

## Purpose

Turn the Dealix Method into:

- **Training** — paid courses that deliver real operational uplift.
- **Authority** — credibility that compounds Market Capital.
- **Partner enablement** — a curriculum partners can use to qualify
  their own teams on AI operations.

The Academy is not an HR-style training catalog. Every course must
produce a measurable change in the trainee's organization.

## Course portfolio

| Course | Audience |
|---|---|
| AI Ops for SMEs | Founders and operators of small and mid-size firms. |
| AI Readiness | Decision makers exploring AI adoption. |
| AI for Sales Ops | Revenue leaders and CRM owners. |
| Company Brain Bootcamp | Knowledge owners and operations teams. |
| AI Governance for Managers | Compliance, risk, and senior management. |

Each course follows the same structural backbone: principles → method
→ workshop → proof exercise. Trainees leave with an applied artifact,
not slides.

## Certification

**Dealix Certified AI Ops Practitioner** is the single certification
the Academy issues. Holders have completed the AI Ops for SMEs course
and submitted an applied artifact reviewed against the Proof Pack
standard.

The certification is intentionally narrow. Multiple certifications
dilute brand and create maintenance burden the firm cannot yet carry.

## Launch conditions

The Academy launches commercially only when **all** of the following
hold:

- 10+ delivered projects across the canonical service set.
- 3+ published case studies referenced from
  `docs/strategic/DEALIX_MASTER_OPERATING_MODEL_AR.md`.
- The Dealix Method is stable, with no major revision in the
  preceding two quarters.
- Training materials are production-grade, not draft, with bilingual
  parity.

Until all four are true, Academy work happens in private with design
partners only.

## Pricing and packaging stance

- Per-seat pricing for individuals.
- Cohort pricing for organizations.
- Partner pricing tied to the Partner Strategy.

Detailed pricing is set when the launch gate is cleared.

## Metrics
- Pre-launch gate status — count of launch conditions met; target 4/4 before commercial launch.
- Certified practitioners — number issued post-launch; target 25 in first six months.
- Course NPS — trainee net promoter score; target ≥ 50.
- Applied-artifact rate — share of trainees submitting a real artifact; target ≥ 80%.
