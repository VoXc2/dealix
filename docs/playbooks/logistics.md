# Vertical Playbook: Logistics

## Sector overview

Freight, last-mile, warehousing, fleet-adjacent operators selling B2B contracts with operational complexity.

## Common problems

- Quote / tender response latency
- Partner and shipper comms scattered across WhatsApp/email
- SLAs not tracked in one place
- Account prioritization by route / volume is manual

## Best Dealix services

1. Lead Intelligence Sprint (shipper / partner lists)
2. Tender / proposal acceleration (structured scope + checklist)
3. Monthly ops + revenue review pack

## Data usually available

- CRM or ERP exports
- Rate sheets and lane tables (often messy)
- Tender documents (RFPs) — client-provided
- Incident / SLA logs (aggregated)

## Best KPIs

- Tender win rate
- Quote turnaround time
- Revenue per lane / key account
- On-time performance correlation to expansion

## Common objections

- "Our ERP is the source of truth" → integrate **exports** first; API later
- "Margins are sensitive" → strict redaction in proof packs

## Recommended scripts

- Map **decision chain** for large deals
- Anchor proof on **cycle time** and **deal cleanliness**, not "AI magic"

## Governance notes

- No scraping of partner data; client-supplied only
- Confidential pricing in secure channels only

## Proof examples

- Tender response checklist + prior win patterns (anonymized)
- Top accounts by strategic fit
