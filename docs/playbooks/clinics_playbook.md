# Vertical Playbook: Clinics

## Common problems

- inconsistent follow-up on leads / consult requests  
- front-desk load on WhatsApp without governance  
- weak source attribution for campaigns  
- ops reporting split across EMR + sheets  

## Best offers

1. Lead Intelligence Sprint (with **redacted / allowed exports only**)  
2. AI Quick Win for a **single** recurring ops report  
3. Governed messaging **drafts** for reminders (approval-first)  

## Data needed

What is **legally and operationally allowed** to export? Who approves patient- or client-facing text?

## Scoring rules

Prioritize operational and commercial signals that do **not** require sensitive clinical fields.

## Objections

- we cannot share patient data → scope aggregates / redacted pipelines only  
- doctors will not adopt new tools → drafts + human approval, minimal UI  

## Responses

Dealix does not replace clinicians or medical judgment. We tighten **ops, attribution, and governed communication drafts**.

## KPIs

- qualified consult requests  
- time-to-first-response  
- source-to-booking (where measurable)  
- proof of **safe** workflow change, not “AI diagnosis”  

## Risk note

Higher **data sensitivity** than generic B2B — run Governance + PDPL-aware review early.
