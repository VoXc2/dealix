# Vertical Playbook: Retail (omni / franchise / wholesale)

## Sector overview

Retailers and wholesalers juggling stock, promos, and B2B accounts — often with heavy WhatsApp commerce.

## Common problems

- Promo performance unclear across stores/channels
- Wholesale account follow-up is personality-driven
- Stock-outs and delays drive churn — not surfaced in CRM
- Discount leakage and inconsistent pricing narratives

## Best Dealix services

1. Lead Intelligence Sprint (wholesale accounts)
2. Weekly operating report — top accounts + risks
3. Governed campaign messaging drafts

## Data usually available

- POS / ERP exports (aggregated)
- Wholesale customer lists
- Promo calendars
- CRM or sheet pipeline for B2B

## Best KPIs

- Repeat order rate
- Basket size / tier movement
- Wholesale revenue growth by cohort
- Margin after promos (where shared)

## Common objections

- "POS data is dirty" → start with **weekly truth** slices, not full MDM
- "We already have a loyalty app" → focus on **decision hygiene** and B2B rhythm

## Governance notes

- No scraping competitor prices without explicit policy
- Pricing claims require human approval

## Proof examples

- Wholesale account tiering — reasons + next actions
- Promo post-mortem structure (anonymized)
