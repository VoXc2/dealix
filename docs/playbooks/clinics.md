# Vertical Playbook: Clinics (operations & patient funnel)

## Sector overview

Clinics and outpatient providers balancing appointments, billing, and patient communication — focus on **ops and allowed data**, not clinical decision-making.

## Common problems

- No-show follow-up inconsistency (manual)
- Lead sources (ads, referrals) not attributed cleanly
- Front-desk workflows depend on WhatsApp without governance
- Reporting across EMR + sheets silos

## Best Dealix services

1. Lead Intelligence Sprint (referral / campaign attribution)
2. Operating reports for front-office rhythm
3. Governed messaging drafts (appointment reminders — approval-first)

## Data usually available

- Appointment exports (where permitted)
- CRM or sheet lead logs
- Campaign spreadsheets
- Call log summaries (policy-dependent)

## Best KPIs

- Qualified consult requests
- Show-up rate
- Time-to-first-response
- Source-to-booking conversion
- Revenue per campaign (where measurable)

## Common objections

- "We cannot share patient data" → agree; scope **aggregates / redacted** pipelines only
- "Doctors won't use new tools" → anchor on **drafts for staff approval**, not new UI burden

## Recommended scripts

- **Discovery:** what is allowed to export? who approves patient-facing text?
- **Angles:** fewer dropped leads; clearer source ROI; safer communication workflow

## Governance notes

- PDPL / health sensitivity: minimize PII; redact by default
- No unsolicited patient outreach without consent framework
- Clinical claims are out of scope for AI copy
