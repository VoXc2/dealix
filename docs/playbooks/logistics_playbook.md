# Vertical Playbook: Logistics

## Common problems

- quote / tender latency  
- partner and shipper comms fragmented  
- SLAs not visible in one place  
- account prioritization by lane/volume is manual  

## Best offers

1. Lead Intelligence Sprint (shippers / partners lists)  
2. Workflow sprint for tender or quoting checklist  
3. Monthly ops + revenue review pack  

## Data needed

CRM/ERP exports, lane tables, RFP samples (client-provided), SLA logs (aggregated).

## Scoring rules

Emphasize deal size potential, lane fit, relationship depth, and response-cycle history (if available).

## Objections

- ERP is source of truth → start with **exports**, APIs later  
- margins are sensitive → strict redaction in proof packs  

## Responses

Proof focuses on **cycle time** and **deal cleanliness**, not hype.

## KPIs

- quote turnaround  
- tender win rate (where tracked)  
- revenue per lane / key account
