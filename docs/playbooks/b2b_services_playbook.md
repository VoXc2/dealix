## Context
B2B services is Dealix's **first ICP**. This playbook codifies how we
target, pitch, deliver, and convert in this sector. It removes the
constraint of "general AI consultancy" by giving Sales and Delivery a
sector-specific operating manual aligned with
`docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`,
`docs/DEALIX_REVENUE_PLAYBOOK_FINAL.md`, and the loop in
`docs/growth/GROWTH_MACHINE.md`.

## Common Problems

- Scattered leads across sheets, WhatsApp, email, paper.
- Weak or no follow-up discipline.
- Unclear offer positioning ("we do everything").
- Manual proposal drafting from scratch.
- No CRM discipline (CRM empty or abandoned).
- Sales pipeline visibility is anecdotal, not data-driven.

## Best Offers (in order)

1. **Lead Intelligence Sprint** —
   `docs/sales/LEAD_INTELLIGENCE_SALES_PAGE.md`.
2. **AI Quick Win Sprint** —
   `docs/sales/AI_QUICK_WIN_SALES_PAGE.md` (for ops/report pain).
3. **Monthly RevOps OS** — the retainer that follows.

## Data Needed (Intake Checklist)

- Lead / account list (any format).
- Service descriptions and pricing.
- Target industries / ICPs in the client's language.
- Previous outreach examples (last 90 days if available).
- CRM export, if a CRM exists.
- Note on lawful basis for any personal contact data —
  `docs/ops/PDPL_RETENTION_POLICY.md`.

## Scoring Rules

Used during the Lead Intelligence Sprint to rank opportunities:

| Signal | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Sector fit (match to client's strongest case studies) | High |
| Company size (decision-maker accessibility) | High |
| City priority (Riyadh / Jeddah / EP first) | Medium |
| Website maturity (active, content, contact info) | Medium |
| Service complexity match | Medium |
| Relationship / source quality (referral > inbound > cold) | High |

A lead with **no source** scores 0 and is excluded.

## Common Objections + Responses

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "We already have leads." | We don't sell lists — we **rank YOUR** opportunities and tell you which 20 to call this week. |
| "We tried CRM, it didn't work." | The CRM stage is downstream of the Sprint output. We hand back a small, used board you can build on. |
| "AI is generic; this is just ChatGPT." | Dealix uses governed, source-cited outputs with human review. No public chatbot, no scraping. |
| "Why a Sprint, not a project?" | A Sprint is a fixed-scope, fixed-price 10-day test that produces a Proof Pack. Project follows only if proof is real. |
| "Can you guarantee X meetings?" | No. We guarantee deliverables and proof. Anyone promising guaranteed meetings is selling risk. |

## KPIs Per Engagement

- Accounts cleaned.
- Accounts scored.
- Top opportunities ranked.
- Pipeline actions executed in first 10 days post-delivery.
- Pilot conversion rate.

## Delivery Notes

- Always create a `before` snapshot of the data on Day 1.
- Always end with a Proof Pack and a recommended retainer.
- Never auto-send any outreach drafted in the Sprint.
