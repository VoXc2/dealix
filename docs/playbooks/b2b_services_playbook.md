# Vertical Playbook: B2B Services

## Common problems

- scattered leads  
- weak follow-up  
- unclear offer positioning  
- manual proposals  
- no CRM discipline  

## Best offers

1. Lead Intelligence Sprint  
2. AI Quick Win Sprint  
3. Monthly RevOps OS  

## Data needed

- lead/account list  
- service descriptions  
- target industries  
- previous outreach samples  
- CRM export if exists  

## Scoring rules

- sector fit  
- company size  
- city priority  
- website maturity  
- service complexity  
- relationship/source quality  

## Objections

- we already have leads  
- we tried CRM  
- AI will be generic  
- we need meetings, not reports  

## Responses

- we do not sell lists; we rank and operationalize **your** opportunities.  
- the sprint creates proof before a larger commitment.  

## KPIs

- accounts cleaned  
- accounts scored  
- top opportunities  
- pipeline actions  
- pilot conversion
