# Vertical Playbook: Education (B2B training & institutions)

## Sector overview

Training providers, private schools / programs (non-clinical), and B2B edu vendors selling cohorts or corporate L&D.

## Common problems

- Enrollment pipeline tracked in sheets
- Partner / corporate leads slow to qualify
- Content + sales narratives misaligned
- Renewal and upsell tracking weak

## Best Dealix services

1. Lead Intelligence Sprint (corporate / parent funnels — policy-bound)
2. Proposal / cohort planning assistant (scoped)
3. Knowledge OS for FAQs and program positioning

## Data usually available

- CRM or sheet pipelines
- Program brochures (canonical offers)
- Email campaign exports
- Survey / NPS aggregates (non-sensitive)

## Best KPIs

- Cohort fill rate
- Sales cycle length for B2B deals
- Cost per qualified lead by channel
- Renewal rate

## Common objections

- "We are seasonal" → sprint aligned to **intake windows**
- "Parents want human contact" → drafts for staff, not bots blasting cold WhatsApp

## Governance notes

- Child/parent PII minimization; consent on messaging
- No automated outreach to minors

## Proof examples

- Pipeline cleanliness before intake season
- Shortlist of corporate prospects with next actions
