# LinkedIn Content Calendar — 30 Posts (Arabic, ready to publish)

> One post per business day for 6 weeks. Each post is **hook + body + CTA**,
> ≤ 1,300 chars (LinkedIn's "see more" cutoff is at ~210 chars).
> Founder polishes 5 minutes per post, schedules in Buffer/Hootsuite or
> publishes manually at 9 AM KSA.

**Why this calendar matters:** MENA LinkedIn reply rate is the lowest globally
(7.24%), but reach via content is healthy because most Saudi B2B accounts post
once a month. Daily Arabic content from a real founder = top 1% of feed.

**Posting cadence rules:**
- 9 AM KSA (highest engagement window per LinkedIn 2026 data)
- Mon/Wed/Fri: case study or PDPL story (highest virality)
- Tue/Thu: tactical tip or "lesson learned"
- One CTA every post: link to `/onboarding.html` OR DM "Diagnostic"
- Ask for engagement on every post (LinkedIn algorithm rewards replies)

### Day 12 (Tue) — tactical
**Hook:** الفرق بين CAC و CAC Payback - وأيهما يقتل startup سعودي.

**Body:**
CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): كم تنفق لاكتساب عميل.
CAC Payback: كم شهرًا قبل ما يدفعه العميل ≥ ما أنفقته.

في السعودية، CAC Payback < 6 أشهر = صحي.
CAC Payback > 12 شهر = موت بطيء (cash بيخلص).

Dealix Pilot 499:
- CAC ≈ ساعتين عمل ($50 LLM costs) ≈ 280 SAR
- Payback يوم الدفع.
- LTV/CAC = 2,999 × 6 / 280 = 64x (لو رقّى 30%)

**CTA:** احسب unit economics مشروعك. أرسل لي الأرقام، أقدم تحليل مجاني.
