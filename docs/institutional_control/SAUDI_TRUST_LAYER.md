# Saudi Trust Layer (Institutional Edition)

Dealix is **Saudi-aware by design** — not a translated global AI tool.

## 1. The layer

- Arabic executive QA
- PDPL-aware data handling
- Source Passport with residency
- Consent / relationship status
- WhatsApp sensitivity rules
- No cold outreach automation
- Bilingual executive reporting
- Local business etiquette
- Responsible AI education

## 2. Defaults

- Data residency in-region unless explicitly waived.
- Personal data redacted before any external transit.
- WhatsApp never used for cold outreach.
- Executive reports produced in Arabic and English at the same time.
- Public claims require a Proof Pack ID.
- Engagements default to draft-only outputs.

## 3. Position

- **EN:** Saudi Arabia is building AI infrastructure. Dealix helps companies operationalize AI safely on top of it.
- **AR:** السعودية تبني بنية الذكاء الاصطناعي. Dealix تساعد الشركات على تشغيل AI داخل أعمالها بأمان وقياس.

## 4. Why institutional Saudi posture matters

The Saudi market is moving fast on AI adoption with broad use of generative AI, but with explicit concern about privacy, misinformation, and the need for responsible guidance. Dealix sells **responsible Saudi AI operations**, not generic automation.

## 5. Productization

- **Saudi Trust Brief** — a one-page artifact shipped with every engagement.
- **PDPL-aware checklist** — used during diagnostic.
- **Arabic disclosures pack** — boilerplate for executive reports.
- **Channel risk rule pack** — WhatsApp / SMS / call sensitivity rules.

## 6. Operating discipline

- Every engagement's data handling is documented in the Source Passport(s).
- Every outbound channel is checked against the rule pack at runtime.
- Consent records are first-class objects, not free-text notes.
- Bilingual outputs are reviewed by an Arabic-native voice.

## 7. Failure modes

- Trust layer reduced to a marketing line.
- Defaulting to English-only reporting in Arabic-led organizations.
- Cold outreach automation under any branding.
- Sharing engagement data across tenants without consent and aggregation.

## 8. The principle

> Dealix earns Saudi trust by encoding it in the runtime, not by claiming it in marketing.
