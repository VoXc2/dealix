# Enterprise Readiness Ladder (Institutional Edition)

Five operating levels. Sell only what you operate.

## Level 1 — Trust Pack
- Data handling documented
- AI policy in writing
- No unsafe automation
- Proof standard
- Human oversight model

## Level 2 — Auditability
- Audit events
- AI run logs
- Approval records
- Source passports
- Governance decisions

## Level 3 — Control
- Policy engine
- Role-based approvals
- Risk index
- Incident response
- Data retention

## Level 4 — Enterprise Platform
- RBAC
- SSO
- Audit exports
- SLA
- Support process
- Admin console

## Level 5 — Enterprise AI OS
- AI Control Tower
- Multi-workflow governance
- Business unit reporting
- Executive value dashboard
- Enterprise benchmarks

## Rule

> Do not sell Level 5 if you operate at Level 1. Design the path from Level 1 to Level 5 from day one.

## Operating discipline

- Each level has explicit operational evidence.
- The ladder applies to the firm and to each BU independently.
- Enterprise contracts cite the level they bind.
- The Command Center shows current level and gaps to the next.
