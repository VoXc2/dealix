# First 10 Warm Prospects — Manual Fill Template

> Founder fills this manually. No scraping. No automation. No purchased lists.
> Every row must be a real warm or semi-warm 1st-degree connection.

## Selection criteria (all must be true)

1. Saudi or GCC B2B company OR agency.
2. Person is the founder, owner, head of growth, head of sales, or
   agency owner.
3. You are connected on LinkedIn at the 1st degree, OR you have a
   warm referral path (mutual friend, former colleague, prior client).
4. They have a clear B2B SaaS / services / agency / training / recruitment
   offer that benefits from more qualified opportunities.
5. They are NOT on a public spam list and you are NOT messaging them via
   purchased phone numbers.
6. You have a plausible Arabic-language angle for them.

## Preferred segments (in order)

1. **B2B marketing / growth agencies** in Riyadh / Jeddah / Dammam —
   highest fit for Partnership Growth + Data to Revenue.
2. **SaaS / software companies** with an existing inbound funnel —
   Growth Starter fits perfectly.
3. **Consulting / training firms** selling to corporates — Mini Diagnostic
   resonates.
4. **B2B services companies** (HR services, IT integrators, MarTech) —
   safe outreach is exactly their pain.
5. **Recruitment / HR services** — they have the data, need safe channels.

## Hard rules

- Maximum 10 messages today. No more.
- No follow-up sooner than 24 hours.
- Maximum 3 follow-ups total per prospect, then `nurture`.
- If a prospect says "no", say thank you and move on. Do NOT escalate.
- Every send is manual. Founder uses their own LinkedIn UI.
- No bot, no extension, no API.
- No cold WhatsApp ever. If a prospect messages first via `wa.me`, you
  may reply manually within the 24-hour customer-service window.
