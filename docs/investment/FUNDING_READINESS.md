# Dealix Funding Readiness

## Board-Level Strategy (ستة محاور)

| الركن | سؤال المجلس |
|-------|----------------|
| **Revenue** | هل نبيع أكثر وبهوامش أفضل؟ |
| **Retention** | هل المشاريع تتحول لـ retainers؟ |
| **Proof** | هل كل تسليم ينتج دليل أثر؟ |
| **Governance** | هل الثقة محفوظة؟ صفر حوادث حرجة؟ |
| **Productization** | هل العمل اليدوي يقل؟ |
| **Capital Creation** | هل كل عميل يجعل Dealix أقوى؟ |

## Investor narrative

We started with a **revenue wedge** because it is closest to measurable business value.

Every service delivery creates **proof** and **reusable capital**.

Repeated delivery becomes **software**. Software becomes **modules**. Modules become **business units**. Business units become **venture candidates**.

All units share the same **governed Core OS**.

## Funding readiness checklist

1. Clear wedge  
2. Repeated paid demand  
3. Gross margin healthy  
4. Retainer conversion  
5. Strong proof library  
6. Core OS used internally  
7. Productization from services  
8. Governance moat  
9. Saudi/MENA expansion thesis  
10. Venture pipeline

### أمثلة مؤشرات

**Revenue:** project revenue · MRR · gross margin · ACV · proposal win rate  

**Retention:** sprint-to-retainer · expansion · client health · renewal likelihood  

**Delivery:** QA average · on-time · rework · scope creep incidents  

**Governance:** PII incidents · blocked unsafe actions · approvals · audit coverage · source attribution  

**Proof:** packs delivered · proof strength · proof-to-retainer · case candidates  

**Product:** steps productized · module reuse · internal hours saved · AI cost per workflow  

**Capital:** assets per project · reuse rate · playbook maturity · feature candidates  

**Market:** inbound diagnostics · partner referrals · content conversations · benchmark requests  

**Units:** unit revenue/margin · retainer count · playbook maturity  

**Ventures:** readiness score · paid clients per unit · module usage · owner readiness
