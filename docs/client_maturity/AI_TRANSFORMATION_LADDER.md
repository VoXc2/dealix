# Dealix AI Transformation Ladder & Client Maturity Engine

> Dealix doesn't sell the same thing to everyone. It measures each client's maturity, then moves them one level at a time from AI chaos to governed AI operation.

Layer 26 consolidated doctrine. Typed surfaces in `auto_client_acquisition/client_maturity_os/`.

## 1. The ladder — 8 levels

- **Level 0 — AI Chaos** — shadow AI, no policy, no source clarity.
- **Level 1 — AI Awareness** — leadership convinced, but no use case.
- **Level 2 — Structured Use Case** — owner + source + workflow.
- **Level 3 — AI-Assisted Workflow** — AI in drafts/scoring/summary with human review.
- **Level 4 — Governed AI Workflow** — every output has governance status; AI Run Ledger active.
- **Level 5 — Operating AI Capability** — workflow in weekly/monthly cadence; proof improves monthly.
- **Level 6 — Multi-Workflow AI OS** — multiple departments using Dealix together.
- **Level 7 — Enterprise AI Control Plane** — audit exports, agent registry, policy registry, RBAC, incident response.

## 2. Maturity Engine — inputs

leadership alignment, data readiness, workflow clarity, governance maturity, proof score, adoption score, monthly cadence, audit need.
