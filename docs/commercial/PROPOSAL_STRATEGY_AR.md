# Proposal Strategy — استراتيجية العرض
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كيف نبني عرض (proposal) يفوز، يحمي التسعير، ويضع توقعات صحيحة. مكمل لـ `os/15_PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md`.

---

## 1. The Proposal Mission

**اقتراح = وعد منضبط. يفوز إذا:**
- Pain واضح ومرتبط
- Scope محدد
- Timeline معقول
- Price معقول
- Evidence كافية
- Next step واضح

---

## 2. The 12 Sections of a Strong Proposal

1. **Executive Summary** (1 paragraph)
2. **Client Context** (who they are, why now)
3. **Problem Statement** (pain, evidence, severity)
4. **Why Now** (urgency, trigger, consequence)
5. **Solution** (offer, why this offer, approach)
6. **Scope** (in/out, clear list)
7. **Deliverables** (concrete, dated)
8. **Timeline** (phases, milestones)
9. **Investment** (price, terms, value framing)
10. **Assumptions** (what we assume)
11. **Risks** (honest, mitigated)
12. **Next Step** (1 clear action, dated)

**Plus:**
- Evidence level label
- Founder approval signature
- Validity (14 days default)

---

## 3. The 5-Question Filter

قبل إرسال أي proposal، أجِب:
1. **هل تم discovery؟** (yes required)
2. **هل pain category معروف؟** (yes required)
3. **هل يوجد product match؟** (yes required)
4. **هل success metric محدد؟** (yes required)
5. **هل founder وافق؟** (yes required)

إذا أي إجابة "لا" → لا ترسل.

---

## 4. Discovery-to-Proposal Linkage

كل proposal يجب أن يشير لـ:
- Discovery note ID
- Pain category
- Qualified score
- Recommended offer + why
- Founder approval log

---

## 5. Pricing in Proposal

### 5.1 What to Show
- Final price (within range)
- Discount (if any, with reason)
- Payment terms
- VAT (15%)
- Total

### 5.2 What NOT to Show
- ❌ Margin
- ❌ Internal cost
- ❌ Competitor pricing
- ❌ Future pricing (without reason)

### 5.3 Anchoring
- Show investment, not cost
- Show value, not hours
- Show ROI as `is_estimate` not "guaranteed"

---

## 6. ROI/Value Statements (with care)

### 6.1 Allowed ✅
- "بناءً على تقدير، الـ workflow يمكن أن يسترد قيمته في 60-90 يوم"
- "معظم العملاء يشوفون [X] نتيجة في [Y] وقت (تقدير)"
- "التكلفة المتوقعة لـ status quo: [X]-[Y] سنوياً (تقدير)"

### 6.2 Forbidden ❌
- "ضمان 5x ROI"
- "نتائج مضمونة"
- "ستحقق 50% زيادة"
- بدون `is_estimate` label

## 8. Proposal Length

| Offer | Length | Reason |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Diagnostic | 2-3 pages | small, fast |
| Workflow | 3-5 pages | mid |
| AI Starter | 5-8 pages | more complex |
| Full OS | 8-12 pages | comprehensive |
| Custom | 10-20 pages | SOW-level |

**القاعدة:** لا تطوّل بدون قيمة. لا تقصّر بدون وضوح.

---

## 9. Multi-Stakeholder Proposals

### 9.1 For Each Stakeholder
- Address their cares
- Use their language
- Show value to them
- Address their fears

### 9.2 Common Stakeholders
- **DM:** ROI, strategy, governance
- **Champion:** ease, success metrics
- **User:** daily workflow, ease of use
- **Finance:** cost, terms, ROI
- **Procurement:** compliance, terms, references

---

## 10. Proposal Negotiation

### 10.1 Common Asks
- Lower price
- More scope
- Longer payment terms
- Free pilot
- Faster delivery
- Case study promise

### 10.2 Responses
- Lower price → tier or scope
- More scope → change order
- Longer terms → founder approval
- Free pilot → small paid diagnostic
- Faster delivery → scope reduction
- Case study → discount after delivery

---

## 11. Proposal Rejection

### 11.1 Common Reasons
- Bad fit (not ICP)
- Wrong pain
- No budget
- Lost to competitor
- Internal politics
- Timing

### 11.2 Post-Rejection
- Log reason
- Update ICP if pattern
- Consider re-engage (3-6 months)
- Send nurture (if appropriate)

---

## 12. Proposal Best Practices

### 12.1 Do ✅
- Reference discovery specifically
- Use client's language
- Show clear value
- Provide evidence level
- Founder approval signature
- Clear next step
- Realistic timeline
- Honest risks

### 12.2 Don't ❌
- Generic proposals
- Overpromise
- Hide scope limits
- Skip risks
- Skip assumptions
- Skip founder approval
- Use forbidden claims

---

## 13. Proposal Workflow

```
Discovery Completed
   ↓
Proposal Agent drafts
   ↓
founder reviews
   ↓
[if changes] revise
[if approved] finalize
   ↓
Send to client (founder manual)
   ↓
Client reviews
   ↓
[accept] → payment handoff
[negotiate] → revise or accept
[reject] → log + nurture
```
