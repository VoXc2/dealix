# Channel Strategy — استراتيجية القنوات
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كل قناة (channel) لغرض، volume، rules، metrics. حماية السمعة + PDPL + DMARC + WhatsApp policy.

---

## 1. Channel Principles

### 1.1 Inbound First
- Inbound = highest intent
- Build content → leads come
- SEO, public pages, demo requests

### 1.2 Warm Before Cold
- Engaged before reaching out
- Manual before automated
- Permission-based outreach

### 1.3 Compliance Always
- PDPL, DMARC, SPF, DKIM
- WhatsApp Business Platform rules
- LinkedIn ToS
- No spam behavior
- No scraping

### 1.4 Founder Touch
- All external = founder approved
- 1:1 > 1:many
- Personalized > template

## 2. The 10 Channels

| # | Channel | Volume | Allowed | Forbidden |
|---|---------|--------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | **Inbound** | open | demo, pricing, learn, lead capture | spam |
| 2 | **Warm email** | 50/day max | reply-after-interest | cold blast |
| 3 | **WhatsApp** | consent | service messages | cold, bulk |
| 4 | **LinkedIn** | 1:1 manual | engage, comment, message | automation |
| 5 | **Partners** | relationship | co-sell, referral | pay-per-lead |
| 6 | **Content** | weekly | blog, AEO, social | autogenerated low-quality |
| 7 | **Press** | low | customized PR | mass PR |
| 8 | **Referrals** | warm | track, reward | pay-per-lead |
| 9 | **Events** | monthly | speaking, networking | mass booth |
| 10 | **Job signal** | low | founder search | automation |

## 5. Channel Stop Conditions

### 5.1 Stop Immediately If
- 🚨 Spam complaints > 0.1%
- 🚨 Bounce rate > 10%
- 🚨 Domain blacklisted
- 🚨 WhatsApp template rejected
- 🚨 LinkedIn account restricted
- 🚨 Partner breach

### 5.2 Pause and Review If
- ⚠️ Reply rate < 1%
- ⚠️ Conversion < 0.5%
- ⚠️ Cost per lead > 500 SAR
- ⚠️ ROI < 1x

---

## 6. Channel Compliance

### 6.1 Email Compliance
- SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured
- One-click unsubscribe (CAN-SPAM, GDPR)
- Sender reputation maintained
- List hygiene
- Bounce handling

### 6.2 WhatsApp Compliance
- WhatsApp Business Platform
- Pre-approved templates only
- 24h window respected
- Opt-out honored
- No bulk messaging
- Consent documented

### 6.3 LinkedIn Compliance
- Manual 1:1 only
- No automation tools
- No scraping
- Real profile
- Personal messages (not pitch)

### 6.4 PDPL Compliance
- Consent captured
- Lawful basis
- Retention policy
- Deletion on request
- Cross-border restrictions

---

## 7. Channel Budget

### 7.1 Year 1 Default
- Content: 30% of GTM budget
- Events: 20%
- Tools (CRM, etc.): 20%
- LinkedIn outreach: 10%
- Email warmup: 10%
- Press: 5%
- Misc: 5%

### 7.2 Adjustment
- Monthly review
- Cut low-ROI
- Increase high-ROI

## 8. Channel Risk

### 8.1 High-Risk Channels

- WhatsApp (template rejection)
- Press (backlash)

### 8.2 Low-Risk Channels
- Inbound (organic)
- Content (organic)
- Referrals (warm)
- Events (real)
