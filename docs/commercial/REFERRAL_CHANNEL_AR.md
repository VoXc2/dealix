# Referral Channel — قناة الإحالات
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** تحويل العملاء الراضين إلى advocates يجلبون إحالات (referrals) دافئة.

---

## 1. The Core Rule

**Referrals = warm, trust-based, mutual. لا pay-per-lead، لا paid reviews.**

---

## 2. Why Referrals

- **Highest trust** — friend/colleague
- **Highest conversion** — 30-50% close rate
- **Lowest CAC** — minimal cost
- **Highest LTV** — referred customers stay longer
- **Brand building** — natural advocacy

---

## 3. Referral Sources

### 3.1 Customer Referrals
- Happy customers refer others
- 5-15% referral rate (industry benchmark)
- Reward: discount, case study, thanks

### 3.2 Partner Referrals
- See PHASE 10 for full model
- Margin: 10-20% of first payment

### 3.3 Network Referrals
- Founder's network
- Personal connections
- Industry peers

### 3.4 Community Referrals
- Events attendees
- Content readers
- Social followers

---

## 4. When to Ask for Referrals

### 4.1 Best Moments
- ✅ After successful delivery
- ✅ After positive feedback
- ✅ After renewal
- ✅ After case study provided
- ✅ After 30-60-90 day review

### 4.2 Avoid
- ❌ Before value delivered
- ❌ During complaint
- ❌ During churn
- ❌ When client is busy

---

## 5. The Ask

### 5.1 How to Ask
- "شكراً على ثقتكم. إذا تعرف أحد يقدر يستفيد، أقدّر توصية."
- "هل عندك colleague يواجه نفس التحدي؟"
- After delivery: "إذا نقدر نساعد أحد معارفك..."

### 5.2 Make it Easy
- Provide referral template
- One-click share
- "Dealix — Revenue OS" link

### 5.3 Reward (if any)
- Discount on next service
- Case study credit
- Gift (small, optional)
- Public thanks (with permission)

---

## 6. Referral Program Structure

### 6.1 Light Program (default)
- No formal program
- Founder asks personally
- Reward = thanks + case study

### 6.2 Structured Program (if scale)
- Formal referral page
- Tracking system
- Reward structure
- Approval: founder

### 6.3 Partner Program
- See PHASE 10
- Margin-based
- Contract-based

## 8. Referral Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Customer referral rate | > 10% |
| Partner referral rate | > 20% of wins |
| Close rate (referred) | > 30% |
| Avg deal size (referred) | track |
| Time to close (referred) | < 14 days |
| Reward cost | track |

## 9. The Conversation

### 9.1 Post-Delivery
```
"شكراً على الثقة. سعيد بالنتائج.

عندي سؤال بسيط: هل تعرف أحد 
يواجه نفس التحدي؟ أقدر أساعده 
بنفس الطريقة.

إذا عندك توصية، أرسل لي اسمه 
وراح أتواصل معه."
```

### 9.2 At Renewal
```
"مبروك على التجديد. 

إذا زملائك يواجهون نفس التحدي،
يسعدني أساعدهم. أرسل لي أسماء."
```

### 9.3 When Asked "How Can I Help?"
```
"أفضل طريقة تساعدنا:
1. توصية لزملاء بنفس التحدي
2. case study (anonymized or named)
3. testimonial on LinkedIn

أيهم يناسبك؟"
```

---

## 10. Don't Do

### 10.1 ❌ Forbidden
- ❌ Pay-per-lead (low quality)
- ❌ Fake reviews
- ❌ Spam-style "refer 10 friends"
- ❌ Bribes (cash)
- ❌ Coercion
- ❌ Without permission (using names)

### 10.2 ✅ Do
- ✅ Personal ask
- ✅ Mutual benefit
- ✅ Easy share
- ✅ Track
- ✅ Reward (if structured)
- ✅ Thank publicly (with permission)
