```

    ↓

    ↓ value proven

    ↓ system proven
```

**Plus:** Path to partnership → Strategic Partner (90,000+ annually)

## 2. Conversion Logic

### 2.1 From Awareness to Scan
- **Channel:** inbound, content
- **Conversion target:** 5-10%
- **Trigger:** content engagement, demo request, pricing page

### 2.2 From Scan to Diagnostic
- **Channel:** scan report, follow-up email
- **Conversion target:** 25%
- **Trigger:** score shows pain, ready to invest

### 2.3 From Diagnostic to Workflow
- **Channel:** diagnostic report, founder call
- **Conversion target:** 50%
- **Trigger:** value demonstrated, pain priority clear

### 2.4 From Workflow to Starter
- **Channel:** workflow delivery, value proof
- **Conversion target:** 60%
- **Trigger:** more pains surfaced, system proved valuable

### 2.5 From Starter to Full OS
- **Channel:** account management
- **Conversion target:** 30%
- **Trigger:** scale needs, more teams

### 2.6 From Full OS to Retainer
- **Channel:** delivery handoff
- **Conversion target:** 70%
- **Trigger:** ongoing optimization needed

### 2.7 From Retainer to Custom
- **Channel:** strategic account
- **Conversion target:** 20%
- **Trigger:** complex needs, M&A, growth phase

## 3. Ladder Economics

### 3.1 LTV by Path
| Path | LTV Estimate (SAR) | Margin |
|------|---------------------|--------|
| Scan → Diagnostic → Lost | 5,000 | low |
| Scan → Diagnostic → Workflow → Lost | 20,000 | medium |
| Scan → Diagnostic → Workflow → Starter → Lost | 50,000 | high |
| Scan → Diagnostic → Workflow → Starter → Retainer | 200,000+ | highest |
| Above + Full OS + Custom | 500,000+ | highest |

### 3.2 Margin by Product
| Product | Avg Price (SAR) | Est. Margin |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Readiness Scan | 0-499 | 100% (just time) |
| Revenue Leakage Diagnostic | 3,000 | 60% |
| Follow-up Recovery | 12,000 | 50% |
| AI Revenue Ops Starter | 25,000 | 40% |
| Full Revenue OS | 60,000 | 35% |
| Monthly Optimization Retainer | 7,000/mo | 60% |
| Custom Company OS | 120,000+ | 30% |

**Insight:** Retainer = highest margin, longest LTV.

## 4. Time at Each Stage

| Stage | Avg Time | Trigger |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Awareness → Scan | 1-7 days | content, demo request |
| Scan → Diagnostic | 7-30 days | scan report follow-up |
| Diagnostic → Workflow | 14-30 days | report + call |
| Workflow → Starter | 30-60 days | value proven, more pain |
| Starter → Full OS | 60-120 days | scale needs |
| Full OS → Retainer | 30 days | end of delivery |
| Retainer → Custom | 6-12 months | strategic needs |

**Total time Scan → Custom:** 6-18 months (typical)

---

## 5. Drop-off Points

### 5.1 Highest Drop-off
- **Awareness → Scan:** 90% don't take scan
- **Diagnostic → Workflow:** 50% drop (no budget, no urgency)
- **Starter → Full OS:** 70% drop (no scale needs)
- **Retainer → Custom:** 80% drop (no complexity)

### 5.2 Strategies to Reduce Drop-off
- Re-engagement campaigns (warm only)
- Expansion pitches at right time
- Case studies at right stage
- Health score triggers

---

## 6. Bundling & Skipping

### 6.1 Skipping Allowed
- Diagnostic → Starter (if pain is clear + budget fits) — founder approval
- Workflow → Full OS (if scale needs are clear) — founder approval
- Scan → Workflow (rare, founder approval)

### 6.2 Bundling Allowed
- Workflow + Starter (if adjacent) — founder approval
- Starter + Retainer (early) — founder approval
- Full OS + Retainer (default)

### 6.3 Bundling Not Allowed
- Diagnostic + Custom (too far apart)
- Scan + Custom (impossible)
- Multiple diagnostics (anti-pattern)

---

## 7. Anchor & Decoy

### 7.1 Anchoring Strategy
- Show 4,999 starter → makes 9,999 standard "value"
- Show 9,999 standard → makes 25,000 starter "investment"
- Show 25,000 starter → makes 60,000 full OS "complete"
- Show 60,000 full OS → makes 7,000/mo retainer "ongoing"

### 7.2 Decoy Effect
- Standard 9,999 as "popular choice" (decoy)
- Make starter look small, executive look big
- Founder decides on actual anchor

---

## 8. Re-Entry Paths

### 8.1 Lost Customer Returns
- 6-12 months later: re-engage with new offer
- Different pain: new diagnostic
- New owner: re-discovery

### 8.2 Cross-Sell
- From Diagnostic → Retainer (without full implementation) — rare, founder approval
- From Starter → Custom (for multi-team)

### 8.3 Up-Sell Triggers
- Success metric met + new pain
- New team onboarded
- New region/expansion
- M&A activity

## 9. Decision Matrix (Next Offer)

| Signal | Recommended Next Offer |
|--------|------------------------|
