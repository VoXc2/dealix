# Packaging Strategy — استراتيجية التغليف
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كيف نحوّل كل عرض إلى package واضح (name، promise، deliverables، timeline، price، proof). التغليف الجيد = إغلاق أسرع، توقعات أنظف، تسليم أنظف.

---

## 1. Package Principles

### 1.1 The 5 Elements
1. **Name** — memorable, outcome-focused
2. **Promise** — single sentence, measurable
3. **Deliverables** — concrete list
4. **Timeline** — clear days
5. **Price** — within range, founder-approved

### 1.2 Naming Convention
- **Format:** "[Outcome] + [Method] + [Timeframe]"
- **Example:** "Revenue Leakage Diagnostic — 7 days"
- **Avoid:** vague terms ("Premium", "Enterprise")
- **Arabic first:** الاسم بالعربية، EN optional

## 2. Package Templates

### 2.1 Readiness Scan
- **Name:** Readiness Scan — فحص جاهزية
- **Promise:** "اعرف وين أنت واقترح أول خطوة"
- **Deliverables:** readiness score, 1-page report, top 3 priorities, recommended path
- **Timeline:** 24-48h
- **Price:** Free or 499 SAR
- **Format:** online form + auto report

### 2.2 Revenue Leakage Diagnostic
- **Name:** Revenue Leakage Diagnostic — تشخيص تسرّب الإيراد
- **Promise:** "اكتشف أين يضيع إيرادك في 5 أيام"
- **Deliverables:** leakage map, priority issues, first workflow recommendation, executive summary
- **Timeline:** 3-5 days
- **Price:** 1,500-5,000 SAR (4 tiers)
- **Format:** 2-3 calls + 1 report

### 2.3 Follow-up Recovery Workflow
- **Name:** Follow-up Recovery Workflow — استرداد المتابعة
- **Promise:** "استعد العملاء المفقودين + نظّم المتابعة في 14 يوم"
- **Deliverables:** follow-up queue, draft templates, approval workflow, weekly report
- **Timeline:** 7-14 days
- **Price:** 8,000-18,000 SAR
- **Format:** 1 workflow + 2 weeks support

### 2.4 AI Revenue Ops Starter
- **Name:** AI Revenue Ops Starter — بداية عمليات الإيراد
- **Promise:** "نظام إيراد + AI يعمل بحوكمة في 30 يوم"
- **Deliverables:** 3-5 workflows, 1 dashboard, weekly review, draft/follow-up process
- **Timeline:** 14-30 days
- **Price:** 18,000-35,000 SAR
- **Format:** multi-workflow + 4 weeks delivery + 2 weeks support

### 2.5 Full Revenue OS
- **Name:** Full Revenue OS — نظام الإيراد الكامل
- **Promise:** "نظام شامل + governance في 60 يوم"
- **Deliverables:** 5-10 workflows, 2-3 dashboards, weekly review, training, delivery reporting
- **Timeline:** 30-60 days
- **Price:** 35,000-90,000 SAR
- **Format:** full implementation + 30-day support

### 2.6 Monthly Optimization Retainer
- **Name:** Optimization Retainer — تحسين شهري
- **Promise:** "تحسين مستمر + توسعة كل شهر"
- **Deliverables:** weekly review, 2-3 experiments/mo, monthly report
- **Timeline:** ongoing (min 3 months)
- **Price:** 3,000-15,000 SAR/month
- **Format:** weekly 1h + ad-hoc

### 2.7 Custom Company OS
- **Name:** Custom Company OS — نظام مخصص
- **Promise:** "حل مخصص لمؤسستك"
- **Deliverables:** per SOW
- **Timeline:** 60-120 days
- **Price:** 90,000+ SAR (founder approval)
- **Format:** custom SOW

## 3. Tier Differentiation

### 3.1 Inside Each Tier
- **Diagnostic:** Starter (1,500) vs Standard (3,500) vs Executive (5,000) vs Enterprise (custom)
- **Workflow:** Basic (8,000) vs Standard (12,000) vs Advanced (18,000)
- **Starter:** Lite (18,000) vs Standard (25,000) vs Pro (35,000)
- **Full OS:** Standard (35,000) vs Pro (60,000) vs Enterprise (90,000)
- **Retainer:** Bronze (3,000) vs Silver (7,000) vs Gold (15,000)

### 3.2 Tier Adds
- More workflows
- More dashboards
- More integrations
- Longer support
- More reports

### 3.3 Tier Removes
- Premium features (in lower tiers)
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated support

## 5. Cross-Sell and Up-Sell Packaging

### 5.1 Common Add-ons
- Extra workflow: +3,000-5,000 SAR
- Extra dashboard: +2,000-4,000 SAR
- Extra integration: +2,000-8,000 SAR
- Extra training: +1,500-3,000 SAR
- Extra support month: +2,000-5,000 SAR

### 5.2 Bundle Discount
- 2 products: -5%
- 3 products: -10%
- 4+ products: -15% (founder approval)

### 5.3 Pilot to Retainer
- Pilot = first 30 days at -20%
- Then full retainer
- Founder approval required

---

## 6. Anti-Package Patterns

### 6.1 Don't Do ❌
- ❌ "Everything for one price"
- ❌ "Custom quote without scope"
- ❌ "Unlimited revisions"
- ❌ "Premium service for free"
- ❌ "Unlimited users"
- ❌ "24/7 support without cost"

### 6.2 Why
- Lead to scope creep
- Lead to margin loss
- Lead to delivery failure
- Lead to client disappointment

## 7. Package Approval

### 7.1 Who Approves
- Within published range: founder
- Discount: founder per `DISCOUNT_POLICY_AR.md` (PHASE 5)
- Custom: founder + legal

### 7.2 What to Log
- Package name
- Final price
- Customizations
- Approver
- Date
- Client

---

## 8. Package Refresh

### 8.1 Cadence
- **Monthly:** Review conversion by package
- **Quarterly:** Adjust pricing if needed (founder approval)
- **Yearly:** Add/remove packages

### 8.2 Sunset Criteria
- 0 wins in 6 months → review
- Churn > 30% → review
- Margin < 25% → raise or sunset
