# Buyer Personas — شخصيات المشتري
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** 10 buyer personas مفصّلين، كل واحد يحدد: cares about, fears, buying trigger, objection, best CTA, best proof, best first offer, language tone, decision speed.

---

## 1. Founder / Owner

| العنصر | الوصف |
|--------|-------|
| **cares about** | revenue, growth, team productivity, decision speed, vendor reliability |
| **fears** | wasting money, vendor lock-in, bad reputation, missing opportunities |
| **buying trigger** | revenue stagnation, growth chaos, team overwhelm, competitor move |
| **objection** | "I don't have time for this", "We're too small", "We can do this ourselves" |
| **best CTA** | "5 minutes → see where you leak revenue" |
| **best proof** | before/after workflow + revenue math (with `is_estimate` label) |
| **best first offer** | Diagnostic 4,999-9,999 SAR |
| **language tone** | direct, results-focused, no fluff |
| **decision speed** | 7-14 days (fast if pain is clear) |
| **channels** | inbound, founder LinkedIn, warm email |
| **forbidden phrases** | "best practice", "synergy", "innovative" |

## 11. How to Use Personas

### 11.1 في Discovery
- ابدأ بالسؤال الخاص بالشخصا
- استخدم language tone المناسب
- استهدف fears/triggers

### 11.2 في Outreach
- Match CTA للشخصا
- Use proof they value
- Avoid forbidden phrases

### 11.3 في Proposal
- Highlight benefits they care about
- Address their specific fears
- Match decision speed to their pattern

### 11.4 في Customer Success
- Adapt communication style
- Align value reports to their cares
