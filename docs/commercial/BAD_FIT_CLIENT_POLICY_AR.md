# Bad-Fit Client Policy — سياسة العميل السيء
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كيف نكتشف، نتعامل، ونمشي من العميل السيء (Bad-Fit). حماية الفريق والهامش والسمعة.

---

## 1. The Bad-Fit Client Profile

### 1.1 Common Patterns
- ❌ No budget ولا planning
- ❌ "I want everything in 2 days"
- ❌ Refuses governance
- ❌ Pressure + aggression
- ❌ History of vendor conflicts
- ❌ Unrealistic timeline
- ❌ Discount-only focus
- ❌ Custom but no clarity
- ❌ No internal owner
- ❌ Decisions change often

### 1.2 Red Flags During Sales
- Aggressive tone
- Vague scope
- Refusal to commit
- Pressure for free
- Demands final price without discovery

### 1.3 Red Flags During Delivery
- Constant changes
- No engagement
- Unreasonable demands
- Unpaid extra work
- Verbal abuse

### 1.4 Red Flags During Renewal
- Demands massive discount
- Threatens to leave
- No clear value
- New owner (no relationship)

---

## 2. Detection Strategy

### 2.1 During Sales
- Apply `QUALIFICATION_RULES_AR.md` strictly
- Watch for red flags
- Don't override unless approved
- Document everything

### 2.2 During Delivery
- Track engagement
- Watch for warning signs
- Founder check-in on red flags
- Document deviations

### 2.3 During CS
- Health score drop
- Negative feedback
- Hesitation
- Founder escalation

### 2.4 Quarterly Review
- Identify patterns
- Update bad-fit profile
- Adjust qualification

---

## 3. The 5-Step Response

### 3.1 Step 1: Identify
- Note the red flag(s)
- Document with date
- Don't ignore

### 3.2 Step 2: Communicate
- Address the issue directly
- Founder-led conversation
- Document outcome

### 3.3 Step 3: Set Boundaries
- Reaffirm terms
- Refuse unreasonable requests
- Document

### 3.4 Step 4: Decide
- Continue with conditions
- Reduce scope
- Pause
- Exit

### 3.5 Step 5: Document
- Reason for decision
- Lessons learned
- Update patterns

---

## 4. Conversation Scripts

### 4.1 When Red Flag Appears (Early)
```
"أحب نتأكد إننا على نفس الصفحة.
الـ scope هو [X]، الـ timeline [Y]، الـ price [Z].
أي تغيير يحتاج change order.
هل هذا OK؟"
```

### 4.2 When Client Pushes Back
```
"أقدّر شفافيتك. ما أقدر أتنازل عن [X]، 
لكن أقدر أنظر في [Y] أو [Z].
أيهما يناسبك؟"
```

### 4.3 When Client Demands Unreasonable
```
"هذا خارج نطاق اتفاقنا. أقدر أحضّر change order
بـ [cost]. هل تريد؟"
```

### 4.4 When Client Abuses
```
"أقدّر اهتمامك، لكن ما أقدر أكمل النقاش 
بهذا الأسلوب. أرسل لك عرضنا الرسمي، 
تقرر."
```

### 4.5 When Walking Away
```
"بناءً على ما حصل، أرى أن شراكتنا 
غير مثالية حالياً. أرسل لك:
- [final deliverable]
- [refund if applicable]
- [thank you]

بالتوفيق."
```

## 5. Refund Policy for Bad-Fit

### 5.1 When to Refund
- Delivery failure (Dealix fault)
- Material scope mismatch
- Founder decision
- Material breach by client

### 5.2 Process
- Founder approval (L3)
- Document reason
- Process refund
- Communicate

### 5.3 Default Refund
- 0-30 days post-delivery: 50% (founder)
- 30-60 days: 25% (founder)
- > 60 days: 0%
- Material failure: 100% (founder)

---

## 6. Termination Process

### 6.1 Pre-Termination
- 1+ warning (verbal)
- 1+ formal (written)
- Founder conversation
- Last chance (if appropriate)

### 6.2 Termination
- 30 days notice (or immediate for material breach)
- Final report
- Knowledge transfer
- Brand usage stop
- Payment settlement

### 6.3 Post-Termination
- Lessons learned
- Update patterns
- No re-engagement (unless cleared)

---

## 7. Save Attempts

### 7.1 When to Try Saving
- First red flag
- New client (relationship building)
- Strategic value
- Worthwhile

### 7.2 How
- Founder outreach
- Clear expectations
- Restructure if needed
- Time-bound improvement

### 7.3 When to Stop Trying
- 3+ red flags
- No engagement
- Refusal to change
- Time wasted > value

## 9. The Pattern Detection

### 9.1 When 3+ Clients Have Same Red Flag
- Update qualification
- Add to bad-fit profile
- Train team

### 9.2 When 1 Client Has 5+ Red Flags
- Likely bad fit
- Consider termination
- Founder decision

### 9.3 When ICP Shows Pattern
- Update ICP
- Add to disqualification
- Adjust channel

---

## 10. Anti-Patterns (Founder Mistakes)

### 10.1 ❌ Don't Do
- ❌ Ignore red flags hoping they'll go away
- ❌ Accept abuse to "close the deal"
- ❌ Discount for bad-fit (makes it worse)
- ❌ Blame client publicly
- ❌ Continue after multiple save attempts
- ❌ Skip documentation

### 10.2 ✅ Do
- ✅ Catch red flags early
- ✅ Address directly
- ✅ Save or exit fast
- ✅ Document everything
- ✅ Learn from patterns

---

## 11. Team Protection

### 11.1 If Team Member Affected
- Founder intervenes
- Documentation
- Decision: continue or exit
- Team support

### 11.2 If Repeated
- Blacklist
- No re-engagement
- Pattern documented
