**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** الشراكات كقناة GTM. مكمل لـ PHASE 10 (Partnership Commercial Model).

---

## 1. Why Partnerships

- **Trust transfer** — partner's trust → Dealix
- **Access** — partner's clients
- **Cost-effective** — lower CAC than direct
- **Scale** — multiple partners = scale

---

## 2. Partner Types (channels)

| Type | Why | How |
|------|-----|-----|
| Marketing agencies | co-sell with their clients | referral + co-deliver |
| CRM implementers | clients need readiness first | referral + handoff |
| Biz consultants | trust + access | referral + co-deliver |
| Training companies | seasonal + relevant | referral |
| Accounting firms | trust + recurring | referral |
| Web agencies | project overlap | referral + co-deliver |
| Software resellers | channel | channel model |
| Operations consultants | high fit | co-deliver |

---

## 3. Channel Mechanics

### 3.1 Referral
- Partner refers client
- Dealix leads
- Partner gets 10-20% of first payment
- Per `partner_rules.yaml`

### 3.2 Co-Sell
- Partner and Dealix sell together
- Shared commission
- Per agreement

### 3.3 Co-Deliver
- Partner provides some service
- Dealix provides the rest
- Margin split per agreement

### 3.4 Channel Reseller
- Partner resells Dealix
- Channel margin
- Contract-based

### 3.5 White Label
- Partner brands Dealix as their
- 3+ paid pilots first
- Legal review mandatory

## 6. Channel ROI

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Partner-sourced leads | > 2/mo (per partner) |

---

## 7. Channel Risks

### 7.1 High Risk
- Partner damages brand
- Partner shares confidential
- Partner creates spam
- Partner over-promises

### 7.2 Mitigation
- Approval-first policy
- Co-branded materials only
- Quality monitoring
- Termination clause

---

## 8. Channel Activities

### 8.1 Weekly
- 1 partner check-in (call/email)
- 1 co-sell discussion
- Update partner pipeline

### 8.2 Monthly
- Partner pipeline review
- Performance by partner
- New partner sourcing

### 8.3 Quarterly
- Partner program review
- Adjust margins
- Add/remove partners
