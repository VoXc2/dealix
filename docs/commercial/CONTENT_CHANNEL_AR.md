# Content Channel — قناة المحتوى
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كيف نستخدم content (blog, AEO, social) لجذب leads بدون spam.

---

## 1. The Core Rule

**Content = inbound magnet. Long-term compounding. No spam.**

❌ لا autogenerated low-quality
❌ لا clickbait
❌ لا misleading
❌ لا plagiarized
❌ لا keyword-stuffed

---

## 2. Content Types

### 2.1 Blog (SEO)
- 1-2 posts/week
- Long-form (1500+ words)
- Saudi-specific topics
- Real value, not fluff
- Arabic primary

### 2.2 AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)
- FAQ-style content
- Question + structured answer
- Optimized for AI search
- Schema markup

### 2.3 Case Studies
- Anonymized (L2-L4)
- Real results, real data
- Methodology disclosed
- Permission-based

### 2.4 Founder Content
- LinkedIn posts
- Personal voice
- Industry observations
- Behind-the-scenes

### 2.5 Video
- Optional, low priority
- Walkthroughs
- Webinars
- Recordings

### 2.6 Whitepapers
- Quarterly
- Lead magnet (gated)
- Founder-approved

### 2.7 Email Newsletter
- Weekly/monthly
- Value, not pitch
- Optional

- 1 social (as fits)

### 4.2 Monthly

- 1 case study (if available)

### 4.3 Quarterly
- 1 comprehensive guide
- 1 benchmark report
- 1 trend report

---

## 5. Content Quality Standards

### 5.1 Every Post Must
- ✅ Have a clear topic
- ✅ Have a takeaway
- ✅ Be original
- ✅ Be Saudi-relevant
- ✅ Have evidence (if claims)
- ✅ Have CTA (subtle, not pushy)
- ✅ Be founder-approved

### 5.2 Avoid
- ❌ "10 reasons why..."
- ❌ Listicle without substance
- ❌ Empty thought leadership
- ❌ Clickbait
- ❌ Ghostwriting without disclosure

---

## 6. SEO + AEO

### 6.1 SEO Best Practices
- Title with keyword
- Meta description
- H1, H2 structure
- Internal links
- External links (to source)
- Image alt
- Schema markup

### 6.2 AEO Best Practices
- FAQ schema
- Question-answer format
- Concise, factual
- Source citation
- Update freshness

### 6.3 Tools
- Search Console
- Analytics
- Schema validators
- AI search tracking

## 7. Social Media (LinkedIn Focus)

### 7.1 LinkedIn
- Founder's personal profile

- Manual posting
- 2-3x/week
- Personal voice

### 7.2 Other (optional)

- ❌ Auto-DM
- ❌ Mass connection
- ❌ Engagement pods
