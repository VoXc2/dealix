## 2. The Sales Process (recap)

1. **Signal** → qualify
2. **Qualified** → draft outreach
3. **Contacted** → reply → discovery
4. **Discovery** → proposal
5. **Proposal** → negotiation
6. **Won** → delivery handoff

**التفصيل:** `SALES_PROCESS_AR.md` (PHASE 6)

## 3. Daily Sales Rhythm

### 3.1 Morning (5 min)
- Open `/[locale]/ops/founder`
- Check pipeline
- Check approvals
- Check today's calendar

### 3.2 Mid-day (2-3 hours)
- 1-2 discovery calls
- 1-2 follow-ups
- 1 proposal review
- 1 outreach

### 3.3 Afternoon (1-2 hours)
- 1 negotiation call
- 1 closing conversation
- Admin (CRM updates, notes)

### 3.4 Evening (5 min)
- 1 evidence event
- Plan tomorrow

## 5. The Discovery Flow (60 min)

### 5.1 Phase 1: Warm-up (5 min)
- شكر + agenda
- rapport

### 5.2 Phase 2: Current State (10 min)
- Current lead flow
- Tools
- Bottlenecks

### 5.3 Phase 3: Pain Deep-Dive (15 min)
- Specific pain
- Impact
- Previous attempts
- Why failed

### 5.4 Phase 4: Future State (10 min)
- Ideal outcome
- Metric
- Value

### 5.5 Phase 5: Fit + Next Step (15-20 min)
- Budget
- Authority
- Timing
- Next step

## 6. Common Sales Mistakes

### 6.1 ❌ Don't Do
- ❌ Pitch before pain
- ❌ Skip discovery
- ❌ Discount for negotiation
- ❌ Promise ROI
- ❌ Skip follow-up
- ❌ Lose to silence
- ❌ Skip CRM updates

### 6.2 ✅ Do
- ✅ Pain first
- ✅ Always discovery
- ✅ Hold price + scope
- ✅ Label estimates
- ✅ Consistent follow-up
- ✅ Revive lost deals
- ✅ Update CRM daily

## 8. The Follow-Up Sequence

### 8.1 Standard
- Day 0: contact
- Day 3: no reply → follow-up #1
- Day 7: no reply → follow-up #2 (last)
- Day 14: no reply → move to nurture

### 8.2 Post-Discovery
- Day 0: discovery done, send note
- Day 1: thank-you + next step
- Day 3: if no next step → follow-up
- Day 7: if no proposal → follow-up
- Day 14: if no decision → follow-up

### 8.3 Post-Proposal
- Day 0: proposal sent
- Day 3: if no response → follow-up
- Day 7: if no response → follow-up #2
- Day 14: if no response → re-evaluate
- Day 30: re-engage or close

## 9. The Sales Mindset

### 9.1 Founder's Mindset
- ✅ Service-oriented (help, don't push)
- ✅ Selective (say no to bad fit)
- ✅ Long-term (LTV > one deal)
- ✅ Honest (no overclaim)
- ✅ Disciplined (rhythm, process)

### 9.2 Avoid
- ❌ Desperation (sell anything)
- ❌ Aggression (pressure)
- ❌ Dishonesty (fake case study)
- ❌ Short-term (one deal, no future)
- ❌ Chaos (no process)

## 10. Sales Training

### 10.1 For New Team Member
- Read all `docs/commercial/` files (1-2 hours)
- Shadow 2-3 discovery calls
- Run 1-2 discovery with support
- Full ownership after 1 month

### 10.2 For Founder
- Refresh weekly (review 1 doc)
- Monthly: review metrics
- Quarterly: review strategy

### 10.3 Continuous
- Objection bank update
- New case studies
- New sample workflows
- Refined scripts
