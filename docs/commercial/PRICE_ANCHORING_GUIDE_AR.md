# Price Anchoring Guide — دليل تثبيت الأسعار
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كيف نستخدم علم النفس السعري (price anchoring) لتوجيه العميل للعرض المناسب، بدون أن نبالغ أو نخدع.

---

## 1. The Core Principle

**Anchoring = presenting high first to make mid seem reasonable.**

**No deception** — كل الأرقام حقيقية، لكن الترتيب والتقديم يوجّه القرار.

---

## 2. Anchoring Strategies

### 2.1 High-Anchor Strategy
- Show premium first (Custom OS 90K+)
- Then mid (Full OS 60K)
- Then standard (Starter 25K)
- Client perceives mid as "good value"

### 2.2 Low-Anchor Strategy
- Show entry first (Diagnostic 5K)
- Then mid (Workflow 12K)
- Then premium (Starter 25K)
- Client perceives mid as "step up"

### 2.3 Triangulation
- Show 3 options: low, mid, high
- Mid = "popular" (decoy)
- 60%+ choose mid

## 5. The Three-Option Pattern

```
Option A: Basic
  - [Minimum features]
  - [Lower price]
  - [Best for: just starting]

Option B: Standard (POPULAR) ← Decoy/Magnet
  - [Most features]
  - [Mid price]
  - [Best for: most clients]
  - [Most chosen: 60%+]

Option C: Premium
  - [All features]
  - [Higher price]
  - [Best for: complex needs]
```

**Why mid wins:** perceived as "best value" vs low/high.

## 6. Frame the Price

### 6.1 Don't Just Quote a Number
- ❌ "السعر 25,000 ريال"
- ✅ "الاستثمار 25,000 ريال يشمل [3 workflows + 1 dashboard + 4 أسابيع تسليم + 2 أسبوع دعم]"

### 6.2 Show ROI (with `is_estimate`)
- ❌ "ضمان 5x ROI" (forbidden)
- ✅ "بناءً على تقدير، الـ workflow يسترد قيمته في 60-90 يوم (هذا تقدير وليس ضمان)"

### 6.3 Compare to Cost of Doing Nothing
- ❌ Just quote price
- ✅ "السعر 25,000. التكلفة المتوقعة لـ status quo: 50,000-100,000 سنوياً في leaked revenue (تقدير)"

## 7. The Anchoring Triggers

### 7.1 When Client Says "Too Expensive"
- Move down the ladder (smaller tier)
- Reframe value
- Walk away (sometimes best)

### 7.2 When Client Says "Not Enough"
- Move up the ladder (more comprehensive)
- Add scope (with price increase)
- Suggest retainer path

### 7.3 When Client Says "Why Not Lower?"
- Show the floor (margin)
- Offer discount for value (fast pay, case study)
- Refuse without approval

### 7.4 When Client Says "We Can Do It Cheaper"
- Show differentiation
- Note "cheap" is not always "value"
- Reframe on quality

---

## 8. Decoy Effect Application

### 8.1 Standard Decoy
- Show low (basic) → makes mid (standard) look good
- Example: 8,000 vs 12,000 vs 18,000 → 12,000 is "value"

### 8.2 Premium Decoy
- Show high (premium) → makes mid (standard) look reasonable
- Example: 12,000 vs 25,000 vs 60,000 → 25,000 is "reasonable"

### 8.3 Which to Use
- **Budget client:** low anchor (start cheap, expand later)
- **Premium client:** premium anchor (start high, settle to mid)
- **Mid client:** triangulation (show all)

---

## 9. Anti-Patterns (Manipulation)

### 9.1 ❌ Don't Do
- ❌ Fake anchors (price that doesn't exist)
- ❌ Hidden fees (added later)
- ❌ "Was 50K, now 25K" (if wasn't really 50K)
- ❌ Time pressure fake ("offer ends today")
- ❌ Comparison to fake competitor
- ❌ Hidden costs in "discount"

### 9.2 ✅ Do (Ethical Anchoring)
- ✅ Real prices
- ✅ Real options
- ✅ Real scope differences
- ✅ Honest framing
- ✅ Clear value communication
- ✅ Real ROI estimates (labeled)

---

## 10. The Negotiation Reframe

When client negotiates, reframe to **value, not price**:

```
"السعر يعكس [deliverables] و [timeline] و [support]. 
الذي يهم هو: ما القيمة اللي تحصل عليها؟

إذا كان [X outcome] يساوي [Y SAR] لك، فالاستثمار 
في [Z SAR] يحقق ROI في [N] شهر.

أو إذا كان الميزانية محدود:
1. Tier أقل
2. Pilot صغير
3. Payment plan

ما الذي يناسبك؟"
```
