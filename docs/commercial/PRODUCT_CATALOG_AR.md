# Product Catalog — كتالوج المنتجات
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** 7 منتجات في سلم واضح (ladder)، كل واحد له promise، buyer، pain، deliverables، timeline، price range، scope، out of scope، requirements، proof، success metric، risks، handoff، renewal.

---

## 1. The 7-Product Ladder

```
[Readiness Scan] — Free/low — convert confused
       ↓
[Revenue Leakage Diagnostic] — 1,500-5,000 SAR — paid entry
       ↓
[Follow-up Recovery Workflow] — 8,000-18,000 SAR — proof of value
       ↓
[AI Revenue Ops Starter] — 18,000-35,000 SAR — core implementation
       ↓
[Full Revenue OS] — 35,000-90,000 SAR — larger implementation
       ↓
[Monthly Optimization Retainer] — 3,000-15,000 SAR/mo — recurring
       ↓
[Custom Company OS] — 90,000+ SAR — enterprise
```

## 9. Cross-Cutting Concerns

### 9.1 Compliance
- All products require PDPL review (PHASE 13)
- Health/regulated data = compliance review first

### 9.2 Approval
- All final prices require founder approval (L1-L5)
- All custom enterprise quotes require legal review

### 9.3 Delivery Handoff
- After payment, handoff to delivery OS (`os/13_PROJECT_CONTROLS_AI_OS.md`)
- Use `os/18_HANDOVER_TEMPLATE.md`

### 9.4 Renewal Path
- Each product has a defined renewal/expansion path
- Customer success owns this (PHASE 11)

### 9.5 Evidence
- All product claims must have evidence level
- Customer-facing numbers must be `is_estimate` labeled
