# Press Channel — قناة الصحافة
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** استخدام PR (Press) لبناء credibility بدون spam. Low volume, high quality.

---

## 1. The Core Rule

**Press = low volume, high quality, customized. لا mass PR، لا fake news.**

---

## 2. When to Use Press

### 2.1 Good Times
- ✅ Major company milestone
- ✅ Significant product launch
- ✅ Industry trend alignment
- ✅ Founder thought leadership
- ✅ Customer success (with permission)
- ✅ Partnership announcement

### 2.2 Bad Times
- ❌ For vanity
- ❌ To hide bad news
- ❌ Without story
- ❌ Mass PR blast

---

---

## 4. Press Types

### 4.1 News Release
- Major announcement only
- 1-2x/year max
- Founder-approved
- Distribution list

### 4.2 Bylined Article
- Founder thought leadership
- Industry trend
- Original research
- 2-4x/year

### 4.3 Interview
- Founder on relevant topic
- Quote + context
- 2-4x/year

### 4.4 Award Submission
- Industry awards
- Recognition
- Marketing use

### 4.5 Speaking
- Conference talks
- Panel participation
- 1-2x/year

---

## 5. Press Approval

### 5.1 Required
- All press: founder approval
- All quotes: founder + legal review
- All data: source verification
- All claims: `claim_policy.yaml` compliance

### 5.2 Forbidden
- ❌ Embargoed info without permission
- ❌ Customer data without consent
- ❌ Embarrassing customers
- ❌ "Fake news" style
- ❌ Comparative ads

---

## 6. Press Materials

### 6.1 Boilerplate
- About Dealix
- Founder bio
- Contact info
- Approved quotes

### 6.2 Press Kit
- Existing: `docs/BRAND_PRESS_KIT.md` (to be created if not exists)
- Logo (high-res)
- Founder photo
- Company facts
- Recent milestones

### 6.3 Spokesperson
- Founder primary
- Designation: CEO
- Backup if founder unavailable

---

## 7. Press Process

```
Story idea
   ↓
Founder approves
   ↓
Draft (founder + comms)
   ↓
Legal review (if claim)
   ↓
Distribute (target list)
   ↓
Track (mentions, shares)
   ↓
Follow-up (if needed)
```

---

## 8. Press Risk

### 8.1 High Risk
- Misquote
- Out of context
- Backlash
- Legal issue

### 8.2 Mitigation
- Approve all quotes in writing
- Pre-approve article (if possible)
- Monitor after release
- Crisis plan ready

---

## 9. Press Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Press releases | 1-2/year |
| Mentions | track |
| Reach | track |
| Inbound from press | track |
| Quality (tier 1, 2, 3) | tier 1+ preferred |

---

## 10. When Press is Not the Right Channel

- ❌ For volume (use content)
- ❌ For quick wins (use inbound)
- ❌ For "fake" credibility (use real proof)
- ❌ Without story (use content)
