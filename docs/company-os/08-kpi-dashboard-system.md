# KPI Dashboard System

## Purpose
Define the company metrics that guide decisions instead of creating passive reporting.

## KPI principles

- Every KPI has an owner.
- Every KPI has a formula.
- Every KPI has a source.
- Every KPI has a decision rule.
- Every KPI has a review cadence.

## Dashboard sections

1. Executive summary
2. Revenue and pipeline
3. Product and activation
4. Customer health
5. AI quality and governance
6. Engineering reliability
7. Security and risk
8. Finance and runway
9. Decisions needed

## Weekly narrative

For every dashboard review, write:

1. What moved?
2. Why did it move?
3. What is blocked?
4. What decision is needed?
5. What changes next week?

## Dashboard anti-patterns

- Metrics with no owner.
- Metrics with no decision rule.
- Vanity numbers without customer proof.
- Multiple definitions for the same KPI.
- Reporting that does not change action.
