# Company Command Center

## Purpose
Create a single operating view for the whole company.

## Weekly operating questions

1. What changed in revenue?
2. What changed in product usage?
3. What changed in customer trust?
4. What changed in reliability or security?
5. What changed in cash or runway?
6. What decision is blocked?
7. What must be stopped?
8. What must be doubled down on?

## Company operating rule

- One owner per metric.
- One owner per customer.
- One owner per launch risk.
- One owner per enterprise opportunity.
- One owner per AI workflow.
- One owner per incident.

## Founder focus

The founder should spend most time on:

- Customers
- Sales
- Product direction
- Hiring leaders
- Strategic partnerships
- Capital and runway
- Removing blockers

## Weekly output

Every week must produce:

- KPI scorecard
- Decision log
- Risk update
- Customer evidence
- Revenue narrative
- Product narrative
- Next week top 5 outcomes
