# Weekly Business Review

## Purpose
Run Dealix every week with clarity, evidence, and decisions.

## Meeting rules

- Duration: 60 minutes.
- Owner: Founder or operating lead.
- Inputs must be written before the meeting.
- Every discussion ends with owner and decision.
- No metric is reviewed without action.

## Agenda

| Time | Section | Goal |
|---:|---|---|
| 5 min | Executive summary | What changed? |
| 10 min | KPI scorecard | Green/yellow/red review |
| 10 min | Revenue and GTM | Pipeline, conversion, blockers |
| 10 min | Product and customer | Activation, usage, proof, issues |
| 10 min | Risk and incidents | P1 risks, security, AI quality |
| 10 min | Decisions | Decide, assign, document |
| 5 min | Next week | Top 5 outcomes |
