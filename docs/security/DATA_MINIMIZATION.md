# Data Minimization (Dealix)

## What we collect (minimum)
- Company name (public)
- Sector
- City
- Visible signal (URL or quote)
- Weakness hypothesis
- Score
- Stage in pipeline

## What we never collect (without consent)
- Personal phone numbers
- Personal email addresses
- ID numbers
- Financial figures
- Private message content

## Retention
- Non-qualified lead: 90 days, then archive
- Signed client: lifetime of relationship + 24 months
- Rejected draft: 12 months for learning

## How we delete
- Client request: delete within 14 days
- Auto-cleanup: after retention period
- No backup after deletion
