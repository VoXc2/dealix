## Tiers

- 85+: Ideal
- 70–84: Good
- 55–69: Diagnostic only
- <55: Avoid

## Avoid clients who

Want scraping, want guaranteed sales, do not own the data, reject approvals, optimize for lowest price, do not care about Proof.
