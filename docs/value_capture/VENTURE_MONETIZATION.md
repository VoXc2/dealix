# Venture Monetization

Ventures emerge from mature units, not from ideas.

## Venture-ready when

- 5+ paid clients
- 2+ retainers
- 10+ Proof Packs
- average proof score ≥ 80
- repeatable delivery
- product module used
- owner exists
- healthy margin
- Core OS dependency

## Models

Standalone SaaS, managed vertical OS, joint venture with partner, spinout with Dealix ownership, internal business unit.

## Examples

Dealix Revenue OS, Dealix Governance Cloud, Dealix Company Brain, Dealix Clinics OS, Dealix Logistics OS.

`ecosystem_os.venture_gate.evaluate_venture_gate_v2()`.
