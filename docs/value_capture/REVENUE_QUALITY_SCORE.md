# Revenue Quality Score

Measure revenue *quality*, not just volume.

## Weights (total 100)

| Dimension | Weight |
| --- | ---: |
| Margin | 20 |
| Repeatability | 20 |
| Retainer potential | 20 |
| Proof strength | 15 |
| Governance safety | 15 |
| Productization signal | 10 |

## Tiers

- 85–100: Excellent
- 70–84: Good
- 55–69: Caution
- <55: Bad revenue

## Principle

> Some revenue destroys focus. Track quality, not just amount.
