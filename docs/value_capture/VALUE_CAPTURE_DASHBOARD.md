# Value Capture Dashboard

Measures capture, not just generation.

## Indicators

Revenue by offer, gross margin by offer, Revenue Quality Score, Client Quality Score, proof-to-retainer conversion, expansion revenue, platform pull signals, partner revenue, academy revenue, venture readiness, **bad revenue rejected**.

## The under-tracked metric

Bad revenue rejected. The amount of revenue Dealix declined because it would have damaged margin, governance, or focus.
