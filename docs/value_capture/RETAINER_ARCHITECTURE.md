# Retainer Architecture

A retainer is operation of a capability, not generic support.

## Monthly RevOps OS

Pipeline update, opportunity scoring, data quality review, scoring improvement, draft packs, monthly proof report, decision session.

## Monthly Company Brain

Source refresh, answer-quality tests, knowledge-gap detection, Q&A set additions, citation coverage report.

## Monthly Governance

AI use case review, rule updates, approval audits, blocked-risk report, incident report, policy improvement.

## Monthly AI Ops

Workflow operation, SOP improvement, time-saved measurement, QA review, automation boundary updates.

## Rule

> No retainer without cadence. No cadence without owner. No owner without metrics. No metrics without proof.
