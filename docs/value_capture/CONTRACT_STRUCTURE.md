# Contract Structure

Every contract protects Dealix commercially and operationally.

## 10 sections

1. Scope
2. Exclusions
3. Client Inputs
4. Data Responsibility
5. AI Limitations
6. Human Approval Requirement
7. No Guaranteed Outcomes
8. Proof Pack Definition
9. Change Request Process
10. Retainer Option

## Fixed exclusions

No scraping systems. No cold WhatsApp automation. No LinkedIn automation. No fake proof. No guaranteed sales claims. No unapproved external sending. No unsupported claims.

## Data responsibility

> Client must confirm they have the right to use uploaded data. Dealix classifies and governs use but does not validate every upstream legal basis unless contracted.
