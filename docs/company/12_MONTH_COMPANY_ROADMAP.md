# Dealix — 12-Month Company Roadmap

Execution-focused roadmap. Aligns with [`../strategy/GROWTH_12_MONTH_ROADMAP.md`](../strategy/GROWTH_12_MONTH_ROADMAP.md) and [`NEXT_90_DAYS_EXECUTION_PLAN.md`](NEXT_90_DAYS_EXECUTION_PLAN.md).

## Phase 1: Days 1–30 — Sellable core

### Goal

Prove Dealix can sell and ship a real outcome.

### Build

```text
docs:
- service catalog + v1 commercial doors
- lead intelligence offer
- AI quick win offer
- company brain offer
- proof pack template
- governance rules
- quality standard

code:
- data_os.import_preview
- data_os.data_quality_score
- governance_os.policy_check
- revenue_os.scoring
- reporting_os.proof_pack
```

### Sell

```text
Lead Intelligence Sprint
AI Quick Win Sprint
```

### Targets

```text
1–3 paid customers
SAR 10k–40k revenue (indicative)
1 proof pack
0 PII incidents
```
