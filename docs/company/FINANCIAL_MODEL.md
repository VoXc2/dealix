## Context
This is the Growth-Machine-aligned financial model: not a spreadsheet,
but the policy and targets that the spreadsheet must satisfy. It
removes the constraint of "revenue tracked without margin discipline"
and aligns to `docs/UNIT_ECONOMICS_AND_MARGIN.md`,
`docs/FINANCE_DASHBOARD.md`, and the strategic spine in
`docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`. It defines what we count,
how we count it, and what targets each window must hit.

## Revenue Streams

| Stream | Definition |
|---|---|
| Diagnostics | Short paid discovery / readiness engagements. |
| Sprints | 10-day fixed-scope productized engagements. |
| Pilots | 30-day managed pilots (`docs/business/MANAGED_PILOT_OFFER.md`). |
| Retainers | Monthly recurring contracts (RevOps OS / AI Ops / Company Brain / Governance). |
| Enterprise | Multi-month, multi-workstream enterprise programs. |
| Academy | Training and certification revenue (post-case-studies). |
| Platform | Productized software revenue (post-5-clients). |

## Targets — First 90 Days

- 3 paid Sprints.
- SAR 30k–60k project revenue.
- 1 retainer signed.
- Gross margin ≥ 60%.
- 1 reusable proof template.

## Targets — First 6 Months

- 10 paid clients (cumulative).
- 3 retainers.
- SAR 50k+ MRR.
- 5 Proof Packs produced.
- 3 case studies published (anonymized).

## Targets — First 12 Months

- 25+ clients served (cumulative).
- 8–12 retainers live.
- SAR 150k+ MRR.
- 1 enterprise pilot launched.
- 3+ reusable product modules in production.

## What We Measure (Beyond Revenue)

Revenue alone is misleading. We track seven categories:

1. **Revenue** — invoiced & collected.
2. **Margin** — gross margin by stream.
3. **Proof Packs** — produced per delivery.
4. **Retainers** — count and MRR.
5. **Reusable assets** — added to repo per quarter.
6. **Delivery capacity** — concurrent active engagements.
7. **Productized workflows** — features promoted from
   `docs/product/PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.md`.

## Cost Discipline

- Reference: `docs/UNIT_ECONOMICS_AND_MARGIN.md`.
- Each Sprint must clear the unit-economics threshold defined there.
- Tooling spend reviewed monthly against the AI cost stance in
  `docs/AI_STACK_DECISIONS.md`.
- No hiring before a repeated workflow exists
  (`docs/growth/GROWTH_MACHINE.md`).

## Cash Discipline

- All Sprints: 50% kickoff, 50% delivery.
- All Pilots: milestone-paid.
- Retainers: monthly invoice on day 1; net-15 max.
- No discount > 15% without founder sign-off.
- ZATCA compliance per `docs/INVOICING_ZATCA_READINESS.md`.

- **MRR**.
- **Gross margin %** by stream.
- **Retainer count** live.
- **Proof Packs / quarter**.
- **CAC / payback** as the model matures.
