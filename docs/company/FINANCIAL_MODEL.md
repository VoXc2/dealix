# Dealix Financial Model

## Revenue streams

1. Diagnostics  
2. Sprints  
3. Pilots  
4. Retainers  
5. Enterprise  
6. Academy (later)  
7. Platform (later)  

## Targets — first 90 days

- 3 paid sprints  
- SAR 30k–60k project revenue (indicative band)  
- 1 retainer  
- gross margin 60%+  

## Targets — 6 months

- 10 paid clients  
- 3 retainers  
- SAR 50k+ MRR (aspirational band)  
- 5 proof packs that qualify as case-style assets  
- 3 case studies (anonymized acceptable)  

## Targets — 12 months

- 25+ clients served  
- 8–12 retainers  
- SAR 150k+ MRR (aspirational band)  
- at least one enterprise-style pilot  

## Do not measure revenue alone

Also track:

```text
margin
proof packs
retainers
reusable assets
delivery capacity
productized workflows
```
