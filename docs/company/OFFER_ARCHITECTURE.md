# Offer Architecture

Keep the **surface** simple; organize SKUs in **layers**.

## Front-end offers (entry, low-risk)

- AI Ops Diagnostic ([`../services/ai_ops_diagnostic/README.md`](../services/ai_ops_diagnostic/README.md))
- **Data Readiness Assessment** ([`../services/data_readiness_assessment/README.md`](../services/data_readiness_assessment/README.md))
- Revenue / readiness diagnostic narratives (positioning — align with catalog)
- AI Quick Win Sprint (when Beta/Official per matrix)

## Core offers (implementation)

- Lead Intelligence Sprint
- Company Brain Sprint
- Workflow / automation sprint (as registered—e.g. AI Quick Win framing)
- AI Support Desk Sprint

## Continuity offers (recurring)

- Monthly RevOps OS
- Monthly AI Ops
- Monthly Support AI
- Monthly Company Brain Management

## Enterprise offers (custom, gated)

- Enterprise AI OS (only per [`../enterprise/ENTERPRISE_DECISION.md`](../enterprise/ENTERPRISE_DECISION.md))
- AI Governance Program
- Multi-branch / multi-workflow RevOps (scoped SOW)

## Offer ladder

```text
Entry Offer → Core Sprint → Pilot → Retainer → Enterprise
```

**Rule:** no jump to enterprise **before** proof + governance capacity.
