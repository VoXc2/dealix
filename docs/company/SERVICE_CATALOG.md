## Rungs at a glance

- **Rung 1 — Free Diagnostic:** 1-page bilingual diagnostic in 48h; 3 ranked
  opportunities + 1 message draft + 1 risk + next-step decision passport.
- **Rung 2 — Sprint:** 10 working days; Company Brain v1, cleaned lead board,
  Top 20 opportunities, Arabic Draft Pack, Proof Pack. Full refund in 14 days.
- **Rung 3 — Pilot:** 30 days; 4 weekly pipeline operating cycles, weekly
  executive reports, closing Proof Pack.
- **Rung 4 — Retainer:** monthly operating cadence (revenue + support + ops +
  delivery + proof); weekly audits; board pack at the upper band.
- **Rung 5 — Enterprise / Custom AI:** custom AI build + integrations +
  governance program; scope fixed in a signed SOW.

## Outcome doors (an orthogonal view)

The five rungs describe **engagement depth**. The
[`SERVICE_FIVE_DOORS_AR.md`](SERVICE_FIVE_DOORS_AR.md) describes the
**outcomes** a customer buys (grow revenue, serve customers, automate
operations, build company brain, govern AI). Any rung can be scoped
toward any outcome door.

## Hard rules

Every offering declares the 8 immutable hard gates (`no_live_send`,
`no_live_charge`, `no_cold_whatsapp`, `no_linkedin_auto`, `no_scraping`,
`no_fake_proof`, `no_fake_revenue`, `no_blast`). KPI language is
commitment, never guarantee. Every price is an estimate.
