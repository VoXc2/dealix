# Service Catalog (Summary)

> Concise customer-facing summary of the Dealix offering. The canonical
> catalog with prices, scope, and OS-module mapping is
> `docs/strategy/service_portfolio_catalog.md`.

## 5 customer pillars

| # | Pillar | Buyer outcome | Anchor offers |
|---|--------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | **Grow Revenue** | Pipeline, qualified leads, conversion lift | Revenue Diagnostic, Lead Intelligence Sprint, Saudi Lead Engine (T31), AI Sales Assistant |
| 2 | **Serve Customers** | Faster, higher-quality customer responses | AI Support Assistant, WhatsApp/Inbox Operations, Customer Feedback Intelligence |
| 3 | **Automate Operations** | Recurring processes automated end-to-end | Workflow Automation Sprint, Executive Reporting Automation, SOP Automation, Data Cleanup |
| 4 | **Build Company Brain** | Files turned into a cited internal assistant | Company Brain, Sales Knowledge Assistant, Policy Assistant |
| 5 | **Govern AI** | PDPL-safe AI program, auditable | AI Readiness, AI Usage Policy, PDPL-Aware Data Review |

## 8 internal OS modules (engine room)

Intake · Data Quality · Scoring · Workflow · Knowledge · Outreach ·
Reporting · Governance. Every offering composes from these — no
per-customer bespoke modules.

## Three starting offers (productized entry points)

| Offer | Price (SAR) | Duration | Pillar |
|-------|-------------|----------|--------|
| Revenue Intelligence Sprint | 9,500 | 10 days | Grow Revenue |
| AI Quick Win Sprint | 12,000 | 7 days | Automate Operations |
| Company Brain Sprint | 20,000 | 21 days | Build Company Brain |

Every prospect lands on one of these three in the first meeting.
Bespoke quotes are not permitted in conversation 1.

## High-velocity packages (beyond starting offers)

| Package | Price (SAR) | Duration |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| AI Support Desk Sprint | 12,000–30,000 | 14 days |
| AI Operations Automation Sprint | 15,000–50,000 | 2–4 weeks |
| Monthly AI Ops Retainer | 15,000–60,000 / mo | ongoing |
| Enterprise AI Operating System | 85,000–300,000 setup + 35,000–150,000 / mo | 8–16 weeks |

## Three-phase rollout

- **Months 1–6 — Cash services.** Revenue Diagnostic, Lead Intelligence,
  AI Quick Win, AI Support Desk. Goal: 20–30 customers, prove
  repeatability per pillar.
- **Months 7–12 — Retainers.** Monthly RevOps, Monthly AI Ops, Customer
  Support AI, Reporting Automation. Goal: 15+ retainers, predictable MRR.
- **Year 2+ — Enterprise.** Company Brain, Enterprise AI OS, Governance
  Program. Goal: 5+ enterprise logos, SAR 1M+ ARR per logo.

## Catalog rules

1. ≥80% of revenue must come from listed catalog offerings by Month 12.
2. Repeat bespoke work (≥2 customers) is productized within 30 days.
3. Every offering names its primary OS modules (see canonical catalog).
4. Every offering ends with a Proof Pack (Stage 7 Prove).
5. Every offering is PDPL-compliant and ZATCA-invoiced.
