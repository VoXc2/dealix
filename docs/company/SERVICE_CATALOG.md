# Service Catalog

## Commercial headline

Dealix is a **Governed Revenue & AI Operations** company.

## Primary offer to start with

### 1) Governed Revenue Ops Diagnostic

Best first offer for outbound and discovery calls.

- **For:** B2B services, consulting, SaaS, fintech, agencies, enterprise sales teams, VC portfolios
- **Solves:** CRM disorder, weak follow-up, unclear pipeline risk, unreliable forecast, AI usage without governance
- **Core outputs:**
  - Revenue workflow map
  - CRM/source quality review
  - pipeline risk map
  - follow-up gap analysis
  - decision passport baseline
  - proof-of-value opportunities
  - sprint/retainer recommendation
- **Price:** 4,999–15,000 SAR (15,000–25,000 SAR for larger enterprises)

## Offer ladder

### 2) Revenue Intelligence Sprint

- **Outputs:** account prioritization, deal-risk scoring, next-best-action drafts, follow-up templates, revenue opportunity ledger, decision passport, proof pack
- **Price:** starts from 25,000 SAR

### 3) Governed Ops Retainer

- **Monthly outputs:** revenue review, pipeline quality review, AI-assisted decision review, approved follow-up queue, risk register, value report, board memo
- **Price:** 4,999–15,000 SAR/month

### 4) AI Governance for Revenue Teams

- Allowed vs forbidden AI actions
- Approval boundaries
- Source rules
- No autonomous external send policy
- Evidence logging requirements

### 5) CRM / Data Readiness for AI

- CRM hygiene report
- source mapping
- missing fields and duplicates
- lifecycle stage quality review
- data readiness score
- AI readiness recommendation

### 6) Board Decision Memo

- Top revenue decisions
- Pipeline risk summary
- AI governance risk summary
- Capital allocation notes
- Build / Hold / Kill recommendations

### 7) Trust Pack Lite (on demand)

- AI action policy
- approval matrix
- evidence handling
- forbidden actions
- agent safety rules

## Delivery model (now)

Service-led + software-assisted:

1. Diagnostic
2. Sprint
3. Retainer
4. Repeatable playbook
5. Internal platform modules
6. Client workspace / SaaS modules later
