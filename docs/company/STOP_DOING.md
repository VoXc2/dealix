# Stop Doing

Dealix protects margin and trust by **refusing** recurring failure modes—complement [`DO_NOT_SELL_YET.md`](DO_NOT_SELL_YET.md) (what SKUs stay off the menu).

## Execution-engine “hard no”

Dealix will not:

- Build features **before** repeated, evidenced demand.
- Sell **enterprise** before delivery is repeatable.
- Promise **guaranteed** leads, meetings, or sales.
- Accept **unknown-source** personal contact lists.
- Build **cold WhatsApp** automation or **LinkedIn** automation.
- Deliver **client-facing AI output** without QA.
- Start heavy AI implementation **before** a data/readiness gate.
- Take projects that do not create **proof** or **reusable assets**.

## Existing operating rules

Dealix **will not**:

1. Sell services **below** documented readiness / score gates ([`SELLABILITY_DECISION.md`](SELLABILITY_DECISION.md)).
2. Accept clients who demand **unsafe automation** (cold spam, unapproved scraping, etc.) — redirect per [`../sales/YES_BUT_PLAYBOOK.md`](../sales/YES_BUT_PLAYBOOK.md).
3. Build product features **without** the product loop discipline ([`../product/PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.md`](../product/PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.md), [`../product/BUILD_DECISION.md`](../product/BUILD_DECISION.md)).
4. Deliver client reports **without** QA threshold ([`../delivery/DELIVERY_DECISION.md`](../delivery/DELIVERY_DECISION.md)).
5. Close projects **without** proof pack + learning capture ([`CLOSED_LOOP_EXECUTION.md`](CLOSED_LOOP_EXECUTION.md)).
6. Offer **enterprise AI OS** before readiness ([`../enterprise/ENTERPRISE_DECISION.md`](../enterprise/ENTERPRISE_DECISION.md)).
7. Compete as a **cheap generic AI agency**—position on operations, governance, and proof.
8. **Promise guaranteed sales outcomes** unsupported by client inputs and lawful evidence.

Log exceptions (if any) in [`../ledgers/DECISION_LEDGER.md`](../ledgers/DECISION_LEDGER.md) with **owner** and **review date**.
