## Context
This is the umbrella blueprint that ties Dealix's five operating layers
(Capital, Capability Operating Model, Value Realization, AI Capability
Factory, Enterprise Governance) into a single executive document. It is
the first file a new hire, board member, or enterprise prospect should
read. It is the bridge between long-form strategy in
`docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`, the non-negotiable rules in
`docs/DEALIX_OPERATING_CONSTITUTION.md`, and the day-to-day rhythm in
`docs/V14_FOUNDER_DAILY_OPS.md`. Everything below is normative: if a
sibling document disagrees with this blueprint, this blueprint wins
until amended through a dated entry in the change log.

## North Star
> "Dealix is the AI Operating Partner for Saudi companies: turning data,
> operations, and knowledge into measurable operating capabilities — with
> governance, approvals, Proof Packs, and continuous monthly improvement."

This sentence is the single test of every offer, hire, build, and
campaign. If a proposed action does not advance the North Star, it is
either deferred or killed.

## What Dealix is NOT
- Not an AI agency selling hours.
- Not a CRM clone or sales SaaS.
- Not a lead scraper.
- Not a chatbot provider.
- Not an automation freelancer.

If a prospect, partner, or hire describes Dealix in those terms, the
first job is to reframe the conversation before any pricing or scope
discussion.

## What Dealix IS
A Governed AI Operations Company. We run eight business functions —
Revenue, Customer Support, Operations, Knowledge, Data, Governance,
Reporting, Delivery — as a unified, repeatable, sellable system. Every
output is governed, source-cited, approved, and shipped with a Proof
Pack. Every engagement produces a capability asset that compounds.

## Business Lines (8)
1. **Grow Revenue** — Leads, scoring, pipeline, outreach drafts, RevOps.
2. **Serve Customers** — Inbox, suggested replies, support insights, SLA.
3. **Automate Operations** — Workflows, reports, approvals, SOPs.
4. **Build the Company Brain** — KB, RAG, source-cited answers.
5. **Govern AI** — Policies, approvals, audit, PDPL-aware controls.
6. **Data & Intelligence** — Cleaning, dashboards, metrics, readiness.
7. **Managed AI Ops** — Monthly cadence and continuous improvement.
8. **Enterprise AI OS** — Multi-team, multi-workflow, governed platform.

## Product Systems (15)
Strategy OS · Revenue OS · Customer OS · Marketing OS · Operations OS ·
Knowledge OS · Data OS · Governance OS · Delivery OS · Reporting OS ·
LLM Gateway · AI Workforce · Proof Ledger · Founder Command Center ·
Client Workspace.

Each Product System is operated by an internal module owner, has a
dedicated quality bar in `docs/quality/QUALITY_STANDARD_V1.md`, and
emits Proof events using `docs/templates/PROOF_PACK_TEMPLATE.md`.

## Revenue Motion
Diagnostic → Sprint → Pilot → Retainer → Enterprise → Academy / Platform.

Every prospect enters at the lowest paid step that proves value within
14 days. Upsell is structural, never improvised: the upsell path for
each service is documented in
`docs/company/SERVICE_READINESS_BOARD.md` and priced in
`docs/OFFER_LADDER_AND_PRICING.md`.

## Mandatory 10-per-service rule
Every formally-sold service must have:

1. Offer (one-pager)
2. Scope (in / out)
3. Intake form
4. Data request list
5. Delivery checklist
6. QA checklist
7. Product module (or "n/a" with reason)
8. Governance rules
9. Proof pack template
10. Upsell path

A service missing any element is **Beta** (sell as pilot) or **Concept**
(do not list publicly). See `docs/company/SERVICE_READINESS_BOARD.md`.

## Closing rule — every project must produce 5 outputs
1. **Cash now** — invoiceable revenue or retained payment.
2. **Proof for the next sale** — an anonymized Proof Pack.
3. **Knowledge for the future** — playbook update, prompt update,
   capital ledger entry.
4. **Product improvement** — at least one feature candidate or bug
   logged.
5. **Trust for the brand** — a public-safe artifact (case study,
   testimonial, metric) or a documented reason it must stay private.

If a project closed without producing all five, it is reopened in the
Sunday CEO Review until the gap is filled.

## Metrics
- **Layer coverage** — % of L1–L7 layers with a current canonical doc
  reviewed in the last 90 days. Target: 100%.
- **Ready services** — count of services at "Ready" status on the
  Service Readiness Board. Target: ≥ 3 by day 90, ≥ 6 by day 180.
- **Proof-pack rate** — % of paid engagements that end with a signed
  Proof Pack filed. Target: 100%.
- **QA pass rate** — % of deliveries with overall score ≥ 85 and
  Compliance = 100. Target: ≥ 95%.
- **Capital asset rate** — number of new capital ledger entries per
  paid engagement. Target: ≥ 1.
