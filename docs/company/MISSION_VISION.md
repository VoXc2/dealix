# Mission, Vision, 10-Year Ambition

## Mission

> **Run real operational outcomes for Saudi companies using AI — and prove
> them with evidence, in SAR, every time.**

We close the gap between "we have AI" and "AI moved a KPI." Every
engagement ends with a Proof Pack: before/after numbers, source-linked,
PDPL-compliant.

**AR:** "نُشغّل نتائج حقيقية للشركات السعودية بالذكاء الاصطناعي، ونُثبتها
بالدليل والريال."

## Vision (5-year horizon)

By 2030, Dealix is the default AI Operating Partner for the top 1,000
mid-market and enterprise companies operating in Saudi Arabia. Three of
five customer pillars are profitable revenue streams; ≥80% of revenue is
productized; a Saudi data moat (≥10,000 anonymized ProofEvents) makes
Dealix the most accurate AI delivery system in Kingdom verticals.

## 10-Year Ambition (2036)

1. **The Kingdom layer.** Every Vision-2030-aligned SME and enterprise
   has access to one of five Dealix outcome programs as the default way
   to operate AI.
2. **The trust layer.** Dealix is referenced in Saudi regulatory and
   procurement guidance as the audit-grade AI delivery standard.
3. **The compounding layer.** The Proof Ledger contains 100,000+
   structured outcomes; benchmarks per sector are published quarterly
   and consumed by SAMA, MoF, MoH, and SDAIA-aligned bodies.
4. **The expansion layer.** Saudi anchors carry Dealix into GCC
   subsidiaries (UAE, Bahrain, Oman, Kuwait) under the same operating
   standard — but the Kingdom remains the gravitational center.

## Operating principle (non-negotiable)

If we cannot prove the outcome in numbers, we did not deliver it.
Operating Principles in `docs/company/OPERATING_PRINCIPLES.md` are
binding for every PR, every SOW, every AI output.
