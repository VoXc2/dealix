## Company Overview

Dealix is a Saudi-registered AI operations platform that delivers governed, Arabic-first business workflows for Saudi B2B companies. Every action taken by the platform requires explicit human approval, every output carries a bilingual audit trail, and every engagement closes with a 14-section Proof Pack and a computed evidence score.

Dealix operates at the intersection of two structural gaps in the Saudi market: the demand for operational AI adoption and the compliance requirements of PDPL and ZATCA Phase 2 that most offshore platforms do not address.

---

## Problem Statement

Saudi B2B operators have data but lack the governed methodology to convert it into defensible business decisions. Three specific failures repeat across sectors:

1. **Priority gap:** Sales teams cannot answer "which accounts do we contact this week?" with an auditable, evidence-based answer.
2. **Compliance gap:** AI-generated outputs are being used externally without a lawful basis, approval records, or governance documentation — creating PDPL exposure.
3. **Proof gap:** No structured audit trail connects AI-driven work to measurable business outcomes, making internal justification of AI investment difficult.

These gaps are not unique to a single sector. They appear in real estate, healthcare, professional services, logistics, and marketing agencies throughout Saudi Arabia.

## Differentiation

Three structural advantages that are not easily replicated:

1. **Approval-before-action architecture:** Every external output requires a logged human approval. This is not a feature — it is the platform's constitutional constraint. It satisfies PDPL Article 8 requirements by design.

2. **Arabic-first methodology:** All deliverables are bilingual Arabic-English. The scoring rubric, governance log, and Proof Pack are written in Arabic. This is not a translation layer — the methodology is Arabic-native.

3. **Evidence standard:** Deliverables include a 14-section Proof Pack with a computed score. Clients receive an audit-ready document, not a PDF summary. This positions Dealix as a compliance partner, not just a service vendor.
