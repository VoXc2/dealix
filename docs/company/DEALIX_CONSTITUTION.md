## Context
This is the company-scope constitution for Dealix. It declares identity,
mission, what Dealix builds, the operating beliefs that govern every
decision, and the non-negotiables that no commercial pressure can
override. It sits above operating runbooks and below the canonical
`docs/DEALIX_OPERATING_CONSTITUTION.md`, which remains the source of
truth for runtime rules. This document exists to give every employee,
partner, agent, and AI workflow a single, short reference for the
question: "what is Dealix and what will it never do?" It plugs into
`docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md` (strategic direction),
`docs/BUSINESS_MODEL.md` (commercial design), and
`docs/company/DEALIX_MASTER_OPERATING_BLUEPRINT.md` (operating system).

## Identity
Dealix is a governed AI operations company and platform for Saudi
companies. We sell capabilities, not features. We deliver proven
business lift, not technology demos. We operate in Arabic and English
with equal native quality.

## Mission
Turn messy data, manual workflows, and scattered knowledge into governed
AI operating capabilities with measurable proof of value.

## What Dealix Builds
Dealix builds seven AI-supported business capabilities inside our
clients:

1. **Revenue capabilities** — opportunity discovery, scoring, and
   pipeline.
2. **Customer support capabilities** — fast, sourced, governed customer
   replies.
3. **Operations automation capabilities** — repetitive process
   automation with audit.
4. **Company knowledge capabilities** — sourced answers from company
   documents.
5. **Data readiness capabilities** — datasets fit for AI and decisions.
6. **AI governance capabilities** — safe, approved, logged AI use.
7. **Executive reporting capabilities** — leadership clarity from
   evidence.

These capabilities are mapped in detail in
`docs/company/CAPABILITY_OPERATING_MODEL.md` and graded by the levels
defined in `docs/company/CAPABILITY_MATURITY_MODEL.md`.

## Operating Beliefs
These ten beliefs are the constitutional priors of Dealix. They are
quoted in offers, contracts, runbooks, and AI agent prompts. They are
not aspirational; they are operating constraints.

1. AI is not valuable until embedded in workflow.
2. Workflow is not safe until governed.
3. Delivery is not complete until proven.
4. A project is not strategic unless it creates reusable capital.
5. Arabic quality is not translation; it is native business
   communication.
6. No source means no knowledge answer.
7. No approval means no external action.
8. No proof means no upsell.
9. No repeated demand means no product feature.
10. No governance means no enterprise trust.

## Non-Negotiables
Dealix will not, under any commercial, time, or political pressure,
perform or accept any of the following. These items are wired into the
runtime governance layer
(`docs/governance/RUNTIME_GOVERNANCE.md`) and the forbidden actions
register (`docs/governance/FORBIDDEN_ACTIONS.md`).

- No scraping personal data.
- No cold WhatsApp automation.
- No cold LinkedIn automation.
- No fake proof or fabricated metrics.
- No guaranteed-outcome sales claims.
- No PII in logs.
- No source-less knowledge answers.
- No external sending without explicit approval.
- No AI agent without owner, scope, and audit.
- No autonomous external action.

## Constitutional Hierarchy
1. **This Constitution** — identity, mission, beliefs, non-negotiables.
2. **`docs/DEALIX_OPERATING_CONSTITUTION.md`** — canonical operating
   rules and runtime verbs.
3. **Capability Operating Model** — the seven capabilities and their
   maturity levels.
4. **Service Catalog and Quality Standard** — what is sold and at what
   bar.
5. **Runtime Governance and Approval Matrix** — how every action is
   checked, approved, and logged.
6. **Delivery Standard and Templates** — how work is run end-to-end.

A rule in a lower layer that contradicts a rule in a higher layer is
void and must be amended.

## How To Amend
- Amendments must reference the existing rule by ID and explain the
  change.
- Amendments to non-negotiables require explicit Founder approval and a
  written rationale.
- All amendments are logged in the change log at the bottom of this
  file and propagated to the Arabic mirror.
