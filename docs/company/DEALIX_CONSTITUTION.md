## Identity

Dealix is a governed AI operations company and platform for Saudi companies.

## Mission

Turn messy data, manual workflows, and scattered knowledge into governed AI operating capabilities with measurable proof of value.

## What Dealix Builds

- Revenue capabilities
- Customer support capabilities
- Operations automation capabilities
- Company knowledge capabilities
- Data readiness capabilities
- AI governance capabilities
- Executive reporting capabilities

## Operating Beliefs

1. AI is not valuable until embedded in workflow.
2. Workflow is not safe until governed.
3. Delivery is not complete until proven.
4. A project is not strategic unless it creates reusable capital.
5. Arabic quality is not translation; it is native business communication.
6. No source means no knowledge answer.
7. No approval means no external action.
8. No proof means no upsell.
9. No repeated demand means no product feature.
10. No governance means no enterprise trust.

## Non-Negotiables

- No scraping systems.
- No cold WhatsApp automation.
- No LinkedIn automation.
- No fake proof.
- No guaranteed outcome claims.
- No PII in logs.
- No source-less answers.
- No external sending without explicit approval.
- No AI agent without owner, scope, and audit.

## The Final Rule

From now on, everything in Dealix must answer:

```text
هل يبيع؟
هل يسلّم؟
هل يثبت؟
هل يحكم؟
هل يتكرر؟
هل يبني أصلًا؟
هل يقلل تكلفة التسليم القادم؟
هل يفتح Retainer؟
```

If the answer is consistently no, **do not build it now**.
