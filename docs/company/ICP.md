# Ideal Customer Profile (Summary)

> Short company-level ICP summary. Canonical 3-tier spec, deal-breakers,
> scoring, and Lead Engine taxonomy in `docs/go-to-market/icp_saudi.md`.

## Who we sell to

Saudi-registered companies (active CR), VAT-registered, with at least
one urgent operational pain we have a productized service for.

**AR:** "نبيع للشركات السعودية ذات السجل التجاري الفعّال والمسجّلة في ضريبة
القيمة المضافة، التي لديها ألم تشغيلي عاجل نملك له خدمة جاهزة."

## Three tiers

| Tier | Revenue (SAR) | Headcount | Deal size (ARR) | Cycle |
|------|---------------|-----------|-----------------|-------|
| **Tier 1 — Enterprise core** | 200M+ | 200+ | 500K–3M | 90–180 days |
| **Tier 2 — Mid-market** | 30M–200M | 50–200 | 100K–500K | 30–90 days |
| **Tier 3 — SME** | 5M–30M | 10–50 | 24K–100K | 7–30 days |

## Priority verticals (Phase 1)

BFSI (SAMA-regulated), Retail/eCommerce (chains, Salla/Zid merchants),
Healthcare (SFDA-licensed networks), Logistics (CR-active ≥3y, 30–200
employees), Clinics, Real Estate, Education/Training, Multi-branch
Retail, B2B Services. Vertical depth in `docs/strategy/VERTICAL_PLAYBOOKS.md`.

## Buying committee (Tier 1 default)

- **Sponsor**: CEO / Managing Director.
- **Economic buyer**: CRO / CCO.
- **Technical buyer**: CIO / CTO.
- **Compliance buyer**: DPO / Legal.
- **Gatekeeper**: Procurement.

Mid-market: founder / Head of Sales / Head of Tech. SME: founder only.

## Highest-intent triggers

1. Tender on Etimad matching ICP keywords.
2. Senior hire (Head of Sales / CRO / Head of AI / CDO).
3. Funding round ≥ SAR 20M (Wamda / MAGNiTT).
4. M&A or geographic expansion (Argaam / SPA).
5. CR amendment indicating activity expansion.
6. Vision 2030 program enrollment (PIF portfolio, NTP linkage).
7. PDPL DPO appointment.

## Deal-breakers (immediate disqualify)

- No CR or expired CR.
- Active regulatory enforcement action.
- < SAR 5M revenue and no growth signal.
- Not VAT-registered (Enterprise tier).
- Data residency outside KSA required (cannot fulfil on non-Sovereign).
- Demands replacement of human judgment in hiring, lending, or clinical
  decisions (anti-positioning, see `docs/company/POSITIONING.md`).

## Pipeline target

200 ICP-qualified accounts in CRM within 60 days. ≥70% of new
opportunities tagged Tier 1 within 90 days. < 20% Opportunity-stage
disqualification rate.
