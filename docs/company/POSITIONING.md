# Dealix Positioning

> **Dealix is the AI Operating Partner for Saudi companies.**
> **ديليكس هو الشريك التشغيلي بالذكاء الاصطناعي للشركات السعودية.**

We do not sell "an AI subscription" or "a lead tool." We sell five
outcome programs and own the operational gap between AI capability
and measurable business results.

## What we sell

Five customer-facing pillars, all SAR-priced and time-boxed:

1. **Grow Revenue** — pipeline, lead intelligence, outreach, conversion.
2. **Serve Customers** — AI support, inbox/WhatsApp, feedback intelligence.
3. **Automate Operations** — workflow automation, executive reporting, SOPs.
4. **Build Company Brain** — RAG knowledge base, sales/policy assistants.
5. **Govern AI** — readiness, AI usage policy, PDPL-aware data review.

All powered by 8 reusable internal OS modules (Intake, Data Quality,
Scoring, Workflow, Knowledge, Outreach, Reporting, Governance).

## Why we win

- **Outcome-bearing**: every offer ships a fixed deliverable in 7–28
  days, with quantified before/after numbers in a Proof Pack.
- **Productized**: ≥80% of revenue from 30+ catalog offerings — no
  bespoke per-customer code, no scope creep.
- **PDPL-native**: every action passes Governance OS gates; Decision
  Passport audit trail for every record.
- **Bilingual by default**: Khaliji Arabic + English, not translated MSA.
- **Saudi-anchored**: ZATCA Phase 2 invoicing, in-Kingdom data
  residency on Enterprise/Sovereign tiers.

## What we do NOT do (anti-positioning)

- No "AI subscription with vague benefits."
- No replacement of human judgment in hiring, lending, clinical decisions.
- No spam outreach without consent (PDPL Art. 13/14 enforced).
- No analytics-only engagements without an attached action.
- No custom work that we do not productize after the second repeat.

## Elevator (30 seconds)

> "Dealix runs five outcome programs for Saudi companies — grow revenue,
> serve customers, automate operations, build a company brain, govern AI
> — delivered as fixed-scope SAR-priced sprints (7–28 days) with a
> PDPL-compliant audit trail. We do not sell AI; we sell results."
