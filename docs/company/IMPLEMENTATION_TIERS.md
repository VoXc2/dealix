# Implementation Tiers

> Every Dealix engagement fits one of five tiers. The tier defines the
> goal, the deliverable shape, and the price band. Reps map prospects
> to a tier in conversation 1 — no bespoke quotes without CRO approval.

## The 5 tiers

### Tier 1 — Diagnostic

- **Goal**: assess the 7 capabilities, surface 3 prioritized
  opportunities, produce a Capability Roadmap.
- **Deliverables**: AI Ops Diagnostic Report · 7-capability scorecard ·
  prioritized opportunity list · Proof Pack (diagnostic flavor) ·
  Capability Roadmap for the customer.
- **Duration**: 5–7 business days.
- **Price**: SAR 4,500–9,500.
- **Standard**: gateway product. ~70% of customers should enter here.

### Tier 2 — Sprint (productized)

- **Goal**: take one capability from L1 to L3 with proof.
- **Deliverables**: 1 of Revenue Intelligence Sprint, AI Quick Win
  Sprint, Company Brain Sprint, or Data Cleanup Sprint. Each ships
  with: live output, Proof Pack, training, governance log, next-step
  proposal.
- **Duration**: 7–21 business days.
- **Price**: SAR 9,500–20,000.
- **Standard**: 8 Dealix Standards enforced. Quality Score ≥ 85.

### Tier 3 — Pilot (paid, conversion-priced)

- **Goal**: prove the capability operates in production with a real
  team for 30–60 days.
- **Deliverables**: Sprint outputs deployed + 30–60 days of supervised
  operation + weekly executive reports + Monthly Operating Review +
  retainer proposal.
- **Duration**: 30–60 days.
- **Price**: SAR 18,000–45,000 (credits toward retainer Y1).
- **Standard**: target L4 (Governed AI Workflow) by end of pilot.

### Tier 4 — Managed OS (retainer)

- **Goal**: operate the capability month-over-month with measurable
  KPI deltas and continuous improvement.
- **Deliverables**: Monthly RevOps OR Monthly AI Ops OR Customer
  Support AI OR Reporting Automation OR Company Brain (operating).
  Includes monthly proof pack, KPI dashboard, improvement backlog,
  governance audit.
- **Duration**: Ongoing (12-month default).
- **Price**: SAR 5,000–60,000 / mo (per retainer line). Setup adders
  apply.
- **Standard**: L4 stable; movement toward L5.

### Tier 5 — Enterprise OS

- **Goal**: operate multi-capability AI Operations across an
  enterprise with VPC isolation, governance program, and procurement-
  grade contract.
- **Deliverables**: Enterprise AI OS deployment (data, knowledge,
  governance, reporting modules) + AI Governance Program + dedicated
  CSM + SLAs + audit exports + private model routing.
- **Duration**: 8–16 weeks setup + ongoing.
- **Price**: SAR 85,000–300,000 setup + SAR 35,000–150,000 / mo.
- **Standard**: target L5 across at least 3 capabilities. Enterprise
  gate (see `docs/enterprise/CONTROLS_MATRIX.md`) must be passed.

## Tier movement rules

1. No skipping Tier 1 unless the customer arrives with a clear written
   capability target and pre-paid budget ≥ SAR 25,000.
2. No Tier 3 Pilot without a completed Tier 2 Sprint, OR a paid Tier 1
   Diagnostic that explicitly maps the target capability.
3. No Tier 4 Retainer without a successful Tier 2 or Tier 3 outcome.
4. No Tier 5 Enterprise OS without 10+ paid Sprints inside Dealix
   AND a successful Tier 4 retainer with the same customer (or sister
   company in the same group).
