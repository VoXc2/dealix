# Pricing Decision

> When to raise prices, when to discount, when to lock list price.

## Pricing principles
1. Price reflects business value + delivery complexity + risk + scope size + required QA/governance + timeline urgency.
2. List price is sticky; promotions are not. Discounts are negotiated only against value, not against pressure.
3. Raising prices is a feature, not a betrayal. Customers respect a confident price tied to confident outcomes.

## Price-raise triggers (raise list 20–30% when ≥ 3 of these hold)

- ✅ ≥ 3 closes of the same service at list price with no significant push-back.
- ✅ Sprint→Retainer conversion ≥ 40% for that service.
- ✅ Average Quality Score ≥ 90 across last 5 deliveries.
- ✅ Demand exceeds delivery capacity (waiting list forms).
- ✅ Anchor logo signed at list price.
- ✅ Proof packs strong (named outcomes in SAR).

## Price-drop triggers (rare, time-bounded only)

- 2 anchor-logo discounts per vertical, max 30% off, in exchange for: case study rights, referral rights, written quote.
- Beta-pilot pricing: typically 50% of eventual list.
- Multi-year prepay discounts per `docs/pricing/pricing_packages_sa.md` (15% / 20% / 25%).

## Discount approval matrix

| Discount % | Approver |
|-----------:|----------|
| ≤ 10 | AE |
| 11–20 | CRO |
| 21–30 | CEO + CRO co-sign (anchor logos only) |
| > 30 | Not permitted; CEO + CFO exception only |

## Pricing review cadence

- **Quarterly**: review every Sellable service's pricing.
- **Trigger-based**: any of the price-raise triggers above → review within 7 days.
- **Annually**: full list refresh, communicated 60 days in advance to existing customers.

## Discount risk

A discount is a cost. The CEO calculates the gross-margin floor before approving:
- Sprint margin floor: ≥ 55%.
- Retainer margin floor: ≥ 70%.
- Enterprise margin floor: ≥ 60% (higher SLA + governance burden).

Any deal below the margin floor requires CFO sign-off + a written learning capture (why we accepted, what we got in exchange).

## Discount tracking

Captured in the CRM custom field "discount_pct_off_list" + reviewed in monthly business review (T30 ops cadence).

## Owner & cadence

- **Owner**: CRO drafts; CEO + CFO ratify.
- **Cadence**: quarterly + trigger-based.
