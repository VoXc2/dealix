# Capability Value Map

| Capability | Business Value | Services | Primary Proof |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue Capability | more pipeline clarity and faster follow-up | Lead Intelligence, RevOps OS | Revenue Proof |
| Customer Capability | faster and more consistent support | Support Desk | Time / Quality Proof |
| Operations Capability | fewer manual steps and errors | Quick Win, Workflow Automation | Time / Quality Proof |
| Knowledge Capability | faster source-backed answers | Company Brain | Knowledge Proof |
| Data Capability | cleaner inputs for AI and decisions | Data Readiness | Quality / Risk Proof |
| Governance Capability | safer AI usage | AI Governance | Risk Proof |
| Reporting Capability | better executive decisions | Executive Reporting | Quality / Time Proof |

## How to use in sales

Instead of:

> “We’ll build you a Company Brain.”

Say:

> “We’ll raise your Knowledge Capability from scattered files to source-backed answers, which cuts search time and improves answer consistency.”
