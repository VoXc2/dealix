# Internal Controls

## Sales controls

- no unsafe services promised  
- no guaranteed claims  
- no enterprise promises before readiness  

## Delivery controls

- no delivery without QA  
- no project close without proof pack  
- no out-of-scope work without change request  

## Product controls

- no feature without build decision  
- no agent without inventory  
- no AI output without provenance  

## Governance controls

- no PII in logs (where policy forbids)  
- no external action without approval  
- no source-less knowledge answer when evidence is required  

This functions as lightweight internal audit discipline for the company.
