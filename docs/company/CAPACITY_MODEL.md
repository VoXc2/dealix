# Dealix Capacity Model (Execution)

Companion to [`DELIVERY_CAPACITY_MODEL.md`](DELIVERY_CAPACITY_MODEL.md) and [`../ledgers/CAPACITY_LEDGER.md`](../ledgers/CAPACITY_LEDGER.md). Weights here include **diagnostics** and **retainers**.

## Capacity units

```text
Revenue Diagnostic = 1 unit
Lead Intelligence Sprint = 2 units
AI Quick Win Sprint = 2 units
Company Brain Sprint = 4 units
Support Desk Sprint = 3 units
Governance Program = 4 units
Retainer = 1–2 units/month (depends on scope)
```

## Founder solo capacity

```text
Max active capacity: ~4–5 units
```

## Decision

```text
If you are at ~5 units:
- do not accept a new project at the same price
- raise price, extend timeline, reduce scope, or add vetted contractor capacity
```

Track actual load weekly in the capacity ledger.
