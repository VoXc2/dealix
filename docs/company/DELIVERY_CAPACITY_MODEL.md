# Delivery Capacity Model

Protect **QA and governance** by capping concurrent intensity.

## Capacity units (example weights)

| SKU | Units |
|-----|------:|
| Lead Intelligence Sprint | 2 |
| AI Quick Win Sprint | 2 |
| Company Brain Sprint | 4 |
| Support Desk Sprint | 3 |
| AI Governance Program | 4 |

 Tune weights to your reality; keep **documented**.

## Founder solo (typical)

**Max ~4–5 active units** at once (depends on leverage and admin load).

## Rule

Do not exceed capacity unless:

- delivery support exists **and** is onboarded on workbench, **or**
- scope is reduced, **or**
- timeline extended, **or**
- price reflects urgency ([`RISK_ADJUSTED_PRICING.md`](RISK_ADJUSTED_PRICING.md))

Track reality in [`../ledgers/CAPACITY_LEDGER.md`](../ledgers/CAPACITY_LEDGER.md).
