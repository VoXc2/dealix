# Pricing Ladder (Summary)

> SAR-denominated, ZATCA-compliant, PDPL-aware. The canonical
> packages, add-ons, and discount terms are in
> `docs/pricing/pricing_packages_sa.md`. All prices exclude 15% VAT.

## Entry — Productized Sprints (one-shot, fixed scope)

| Offer | Price (SAR) | Duration | Best for |
|-------|-------------|----------|----------|
| Revenue Intelligence Sprint | 9,500 | 10 days | B2B sales teams with messy data |
| AI Quick Win Sprint | 12,000 | 7 days | Operations leaders with recurring manual work |
| Company Brain Sprint | 20,000 | 21 days | Teams drowning in PDFs / policies |
| AI Support Desk Sprint | 12,000–30,000 | 14 days | Support teams with slow response times |

## Core — SaaS Subscription Tiers (recurring)

| Tier | SAR / month | Annual (SAR) | Seats | Leads / mo |

## Retainer — Monthly Operating Programs

| Retainer | SAR / month | Outcome |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Monthly RevOps | 15,000–60,000 | Ongoing pipeline + lead intelligence + outreach drafts |
| Monthly AI Ops | 15,000–60,000 | Continuous automation + governance + reporting |
| Customer Support AI | 5,000–20,000 (+ setup 12,000–40,000) | Suggested-reply ops, weekly insights |
| Executive Reporting Automation | 5,000–15,000 (+ setup 12,000–40,000) | Weekly CEO / sales / ops / risk reports |
| Company Brain (operating) | 8,000–35,000 (+ setup 20,000–90,000) | Internal cited assistant, freshness tracking |

## Enterprise — AI Operating System (custom)

- **Enterprise AI OS**: 85,000–300,000 setup + 35,000–150,000 / mo
- **AI Governance Program**: 20,000–100,000 / engagement
- **90-day paid Enterprise pilot**: 60,000 fixed (100% credit toward annual contract within 30 days of pilot end)

## Discounts (binding)

- Annual prepay: 15% off list.
- 2-year prepay: 20% off list.
- 3-year prepay: 25% off list (Enterprise / Sovereign only).
- Anchor logo discount: up to 30% off Y1 for case study + reference rights (CRO approval, 1 per vertical).
- VAT 15% always added at invoice. Net-30 standard; Net-60 for govt / Sovereign with CFO approval.

## Pricing rules

1. No bespoke quote in conversation 1 — map prospect to one of three starting offers.
2. Discount > 12% requires CRO sign-off; > 25% requires CEO sign-off.
3. Every quote is generated from the canonical catalog (≥80% catalog-hit rate).
4. ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing on every invoice.
