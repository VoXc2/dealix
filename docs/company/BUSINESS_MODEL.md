# Business Model

> Three revenue layers, one operating system. SAR-denominated, PDPL-
> compliant, ZATCA-invoiced. Long-form evidence in `docs/BUSINESS_MODEL.md`.

## Layer 1 — Productized services (cash, fast)

Fixed-scope SAR-priced sprints. 7–28 day delivery. No bespoke quoting
in conversation 1.

| Anchor offer | SAR | Days | Pillar |
|--------------|-----|------|--------|
| Revenue Intelligence Sprint | 9,500 | 10 | Grow Revenue |
| AI Quick Win Sprint | 12,000 | 7 | Automate Operations |
| Company Brain Sprint | 20,000 | 21 | Build Company Brain |

Margins ≥ 55%. Every sprint produces a Proof Pack and an upsell into
Layer 2.

## Layer 2 — Retainers (predictable, compounding)

Monthly operating programs through the 8 OS modules. Customers buy a
monthly outcome, not a tool.

| Retainer | SAR / mo |
|----------|----------|
| Monthly RevOps | 15K–60K |
| Monthly AI Ops | 15K–60K |
| Customer Support AI | 5K–20K (+ setup) |
| Executive Reporting | 5K–15K (+ setup) |
| Company Brain (operating) | 8K–35K (+ setup) |

Margins ≥ 60%. Target: ≥ 40% sprint-to-retainer conversion within 60
days post-close.

## Layer 3 — Enterprise AI OS (anchor, defensible)

Multi-pillar, multi-quarter deployments inside one enterprise.

- Enterprise AI OS: 85K–300K setup + 35K–150K / mo.
- AI Governance Program: 20K–100K per engagement.
- 90-day paid pilot: SAR 60K fixed; 100% credited to annual contract
  if signed within 30 days of pilot end.

Margins ≥ 65% steady state. Target: 5+ enterprise logos at SAR 1M+
ARR per logo by end of Year 2.

## Revenue-mix targets

| Horizon | Layer 1 | Layer 2 | Layer 3 |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| Month 6 | 70% | 25% | 5% |
| Month 12 | 40% | 45% | 15% |
| Year 2+ | 20% | 45% | 35% |

## Unit-economics floors (binding)

- Gross margin ≥ 65% by Month 18; ≥ 75% steady state.
- LLM cost per workflow ≤ SAR 1.20 median; ≤ 3.00 p95.
- CAC payback: ≤ 9 mo Starter / ≤ 12 mo Growth / ≤ 18 mo Sovereign.
- NRR ≥ 110% by Month 12.
- Productized revenue share ≥ 80% by Month 12.

## Compounding logic

1. Every Sprint produces a Proof Pack (sellable, reusable evidence).
2. Every customer dataset feeds shared OS modules — next answer is
   better.
3. Every repeated bespoke pattern (≥ 2 customers) becomes a catalog
   item within 30 days.
4. Every retainer raises switching cost (data, workflow, bilingual
   content library).

## Discount discipline

Annual prepay 15% off; 2-year 20%; 3-year 25% (Enterprise/Sovereign
only). Anchor logo up to 30% Y1 (CRO approval, 1 per vertical).
Discount > 12% requires CRO; > 25% requires CEO.

## What we will NOT do

- No bespoke-only engagements that never productize.
- No pure-success-fee billing in Year 1 (cash-flow risk).
- No discounted retainers without a 12-month commitment.
- No pricing changes without CRO + CFO + CEO sign-off.
