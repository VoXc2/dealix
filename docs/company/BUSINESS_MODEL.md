# Business Model

> Three revenue layers, one operating system. SAR-denominated,
> PDPL-compliant, ZATCA-invoiced. Cross-links to `docs/BUSINESS_MODEL.md`
> for the long-form business-model evidence.

## Layer 1 — Productized services (cash, fast)

**What:** fixed-scope, time-boxed SAR-priced sprints. 7–28 day delivery,
no bespoke quoting in conversation 1.

**Anchor offers** (three starting offers, productized entry points):

| Offer | SAR | Days | Pillar |
|-------|-----|------|--------|
| Revenue Intelligence Sprint | 9,500 | 10 | Grow Revenue |
| AI Quick Win Sprint | 12,000 | 7 | Automate Operations |
| Company Brain Sprint | 20,000 | 21 | Build Company Brain |

**Margins:** ≥ 55% (engineer + CS + LLM cost). **Outcome:** each sprint
produces a Proof Pack and an upsell into Layer 2.

## Layer 2 — Retainers (predictable, compounding)

**What:** monthly operating programs delivered through the 8 OS modules.
Customers buy a monthly outcome, not a tool.

| Retainer | SAR / mo | Outcome |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Monthly RevOps | 15,000–60,000 | Ongoing pipeline + lead intelligence |
| Monthly AI Ops | 15,000–60,000 | Automation + governance + reporting |
| Customer Support AI | 5,000–20,000 (+ setup) | Suggested-reply ops, weekly insights |
| Executive Reporting | 5,000–15,000 (+ setup) | Weekly CEO / sales / ops / risk reports |
| Company Brain (operating) | 8,000–35,000 (+ setup) | Cited internal assistant, freshness tracking |

**Margins:** ≥ 60%. **Target:** ≥40% of sprint customers convert to a
retainer within 60 days post-close.

## Layer 3 — Enterprise AI OS (anchor, defensible)

**What:** multi-pillar, multi-quarter deployments inside a single
enterprise. Setup + monthly + governance program.

- Enterprise AI OS: 85,000–300,000 setup + 35,000–150,000 / mo.
- AI Governance Program: 20,000–100,000 per engagement.
- 90-day paid pilot: SAR 60,000 fixed; 100% credited to annual contract
  if signed within 30 days of pilot end.

**Margins:** ≥ 65% steady state. **Target:** 5+ enterprise logos, SAR
1M+ ARR per logo by end of Year 2.

## Revenue mix targets

| Horizon | Layer 1 (services) | Layer 2 (retainers) | Layer 3 (enterprise) |
|---------|-------------------|---------------------|----------------------|
| Month 6 | 70% | 25% | 5% |
| Month 12 | 40% | 45% | 15% |
| Year 2+ | 20% | 45% | 35% |

## Unit-economics floors (binding)

- Gross margin ≥ 65% by Month 18; ≥ 75% steady state.
- LLM cost per workflow ≤ SAR 1.20 median; ≤ SAR 3.00 p95.
- CAC payback: ≤ 9 mo Starter, ≤ 12 mo Growth, ≤ 18 mo Sovereign.
- NRR ≥ 110% by Month 12.
- Productized revenue share ≥ 80% by Month 12 (no bespoke drift).

## Compounding logic

1. Every Sprint produces a Proof Pack — sellable, reusable evidence.
2. Every customer dataset feeds shared OS modules (Knowledge, Scoring,
   Data Quality) — next customer's first answer is better.
3. Every repeated bespoke pattern (≥2 customers) becomes a catalog
   item within 30 days (Operating Principle #7).
4. Every retainer raises switching cost (data lock + workflow integration
   + bilingual content library).

## Discount discipline

- Annual prepay: 15% off list. 2-year: 20%. 3-year: 25% (Enterprise/
  Sovereign only).
- Anchor logo: up to 30% off Y1 for case study + reference rights (CRO
  approval, 1 per vertical).
- Discount > 12% requires CRO sign-off; > 25% requires CEO sign-off.

## What we will NOT do

- No bespoke-only engagements that never productize.
- No outcome-only / pure-success-fee billing in Year 1 (cash-flow risk).
- No discounted retainers without a 12-month commitment.
- No pricing changes without CRO + CFO + CEO sign-off (see Pricing rules).
