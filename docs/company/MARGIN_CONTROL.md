# Margin Control

Every SKU needs a **floor**—do not discount below margin without a **written strategic reason** (pilot label, case study, partner leverage).

**Illustrative** — replace with your actual blended costs.

| Service | Price (SAR) | Est. hours | Delivery cost (SAR) | Gross margin | Minimum acceptable price |
|---------|------------:|-----------:|--------------------:|-------------:|-------------------------:|
| Lead Intelligence | 9,500 | 20 | 2,000 | ~79% | 7,500 |
| AI Quick Win | 12,000 | 25 | 3,000 | ~75% | 9,500 |
| Company Brain | 25,000 | 50 | 6,000 | ~76% | 20,000 |

## Exceptions (only with founder call + ledger note)

```text
- case study قوي
- عميل استراتيجي
- شراكة مبررة
- proof مهم لتوسع السوق
```

Even then: label as **pilot**, narrow scope, and log in [`../ledgers/DECISION_LEDGER.md`](../ledgers/DECISION_LEDGER.md).
