# Scale Decision

> When to hire, when to partner, when to outsource. Scaling chaos is just
> bigger chaos.

## Pre-conditions before any hire

- [ ] The work to be hired is documented (`docs/services/*` + checklists).
- [ ] QA is stable (Quality Score ≥ 85 average across last 5 deliveries).
- [ ] At least 3 paid projects completed.
- [ ] Demand is recurring (≥ 1 deal/month for the next 3 months in pipeline).
- [ ] Founder is spending > 60% of week on delivery (vs. sales/product).
- [ ] Gross margin supports the hire (cost ≤ 30% of projected MRR from the work the hire will absorb).

If any box is unchecked: **do not hire**. Document the gap, fix the gap, then re-run this decision.

## Hiring sequence (per `docs/go-to-market/saudi_gtm_12m.md` §3.4)

1. **Enterprise AE** (BFSI focus) — Week 1.
2. **SDR Riyadh** — Week 1.
3. **CS Lead** — Week 2.
4. **Marketing manager** — Week 3.
5. **AE #2 (Retail)** — Week 5.
6. **SDR #2 (Jeddah)** — Week 7.
7. **DPO / Trust officer** — Week 9.
8. **SE / Solutions consultant** — Week 10.

Gate: do not hire AE #N+1 unless AE #N has closed SAR 3M in trailing 6 months.

## Partner vs hire

- **Hire** when: the work is core to the moat (delivery quality, governance, AI quality).
- **Partner** when: the work is non-core (paid media, accounting, legal templating, design).
- **Outsource (freelance)** when: project-specific spike, no recurrence.

## Partner channels (start AFTER first 5 paying customers)

- SI / agency partners with existing CRM / consulting books.
- Sectoral chambers for warm intros.
- Cybersecurity firms (cross-sell on governance).
- Accounting / ERP firms (cross-sell on data + reporting).

## Anti-patterns

- Hiring a "head of growth" when sales process is not productized.
- Hiring engineers to "fix scope creep" — fix the scope process first.
- Hiring AE before SDR (top of funnel must work).
- Outsourcing QA — QA stays in-house, always.

## Cost discipline

A hire is approved only when:
- Loaded cost (salary + benefits + equipment) ≤ 30% of incremental MRR over the next 6 months.
- Replacement plan exists if the hire underperforms after 90 days.

## Owner & cadence

- **Owner**: CEO. Senior hires require board notification.
- **Cadence**: monthly hiring committee (CEO + CFO).
