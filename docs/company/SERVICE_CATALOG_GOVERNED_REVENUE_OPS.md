# Dealix Service Catalog — Governed Revenue & AI Operations

**Status:** Canonical (approved by founder Sami, 2026-05-16).
**Supersedes:** the 5-rung offer ladder (`docs/company/OFFER_LADDER.md`) and the
3-service registry baseline. See
[`docs/strategic/DEALIX_GOVERNED_REVENUE_OPS_POSITIONING.md`](../strategic/DEALIX_GOVERNED_REVENUE_OPS_POSITIONING.md).

This catalog has **7 services**. The catalog is sequenced: the Diagnostic is the
entry offer; the Sprint is the flagship that follows it; the Retainer follows
the first Sprint. Services 4–7 are governance and decision add-ons.

All prices are in SAR. No service carries a guaranteed-outcome claim. Every
output is evidence-backed (`source_ref` + decision passport + Proof Pack).

## Catalog sequencing rule

1. Sell the **Diagnostic** first — it produces a decision-ready plan.
2. The Diagnostic recommends a **Sprint** or a **Retainer**.
3. The **Retainer** follows the first Sprint.
4. Services 4–6 are governance / decision add-ons sold alongside.
5. **Trust Pack Lite** is offered only when the client asks for it — never pushed.

No engagement is sold or delivered without a Proof Pack (score ≥ 70) and at
least one Capital Asset. No guaranteed-outcome language is permitted in any
proposal, page, or message.
