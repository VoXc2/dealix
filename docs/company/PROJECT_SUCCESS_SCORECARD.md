# Project Success Score (Dealix)

Success is not only "the client liked it." A project must compound into company assets.

## Score (max 100)

| معيار | وزن |
|--------|-----|
| سلّمنا في الوقت | 15 |
| QA فوق 85 | 20 |
| proof pack مكتمل | 15 |
| العميل فهم next step | 15 |
| لا مشاكل حوكمة | 15 |
| فرصة retainer واضحة | 10 |
| أصل داخلي جديد تم إنشاؤه (template / playbook / feature candidate / sales asset) | 10 |

**Pass = 85.**

If the client is happy but no internal asset was created, success is **partial** — capture gaps in `POST_PROJECT_REVIEW.md`.
