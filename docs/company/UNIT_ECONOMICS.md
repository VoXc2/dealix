Illustrative bands — tune to your real timesheets and contractor usage.

```text
Gross margin target: 70%+
```

## Pricing rule

```text
If a service consumes heavy time without building reusable assets, raise price or stop selling it.
If a service builds proof + product assets, a limited pilot discount can be acceptable with explicit learning goals.
```
