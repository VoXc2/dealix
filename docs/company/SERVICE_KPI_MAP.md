# Service KPI Map

A service without a clear **primary KPI** is not convincingly sellable.

| Service | Primary KPI | Secondary KPI | Proof type |
|---------|-------------|---------------|------------|
| Lead Intelligence Sprint | qualified accounts ranked / actionable list | data quality + attribution improvement | Revenue / Quality |
| AI Quick Win Sprint | hours saved (honest) | error / rework reduction | Time / Quality |
| Company Brain Sprint | answers with sources (coverage) | time-to-answer vs baseline | Knowledge / Quality |
| AI Support Desk Sprint | response time / triage quality | consistency vs human baseline | Time / Quality |
| AI Governance Program | controls implemented + approvals logged | risk items closed | Risk |

**Rule:** if you cannot name the KPI, **do not sell** the SKU officially ([`SELLABILITY_DECISION.md`](SELLABILITY_DECISION.md)).  
Align proof language with [`../growth/PROOF_TO_UPSELL_MAP.md`](../growth/PROOF_TO_UPSELL_MAP.md).
