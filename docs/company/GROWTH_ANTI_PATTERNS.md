# Growth Anti-Patterns (Do Not)

```text
Do not build full SaaS before 5 paying clients.
Do not build marketplace before 20+ structured projects.
Do not launch Academy before case studies.
Do not build autonomous external agents.
Do not build WhatsApp sending automation now.
Do not sell guaranteed results.
Do not ingest unknown-source personal lists.
Do not expand sectors before one playbook is strong.
Do not hire before a repeated workflow + margin support it.
```

The operating alternative is always: **Sprint → Proof → Asset → Retainer → Product (if repeated 3×)**.
