# Dealix Capital Model

Dealix grows by accumulating **five types of capital**—not only cash.

## 1. Service Capital

Reusable **offers**, **scopes**, **checklists**, delivery systems, and **QA** standards.

## 2. Product Capital

Internal **tools**, **modules**, **APIs**, dashboards, and **automation** that reduce delivery effort.

## 3. Knowledge Capital

**Playbooks**, sector insights, **objections**, data patterns, governance risks, and **templates**.

## 4. Trust Capital

**Proof packs**, case studies, testimonials, audit/governance records, and **safety reputation**.

## 5. Market Capital

**Audience**, partners, referrals, **benchmarks**, and authority **content**.

## Rule

**Every project must increase at least two forms of capital.**  
If it only exchanges time for money, it is **structurally weak**—even when the client pays.
