## Context
The monthly Board Pack is the artifact that proves Dealix is run as a company, not as a project. It binds revenue, delivery, product, governance, market, and decisions into one document the board reads in 15 minutes. The Pack draws from the executive lens in `docs/EXECUTIVE_DECISION_PACK.md`, the evidence in `docs/DEALIX_MASTER_EXECUTION_EVIDENCE_TABLE.md`, and the phase closure planning in `docs/V14_PHASE_K_CLOSURE_PLAN.md`.

## Pack sections

### Revenue
- Booked revenue (month, quarter, year-to-date).
- Pipeline by stage and quality.
- Retainers active and at-risk.
- Gross margin trend.
- One-line narrative on revenue health.

### Delivery
- Active projects (count, type, stage).
- On-time delivery rate.
- QA score trend.
- Proof events delivered.
- One-line narrative on delivery health.

### Product
- Features shipped (with build decision references).
- Manual steps removed.
- AI cost trend (per `docs/company/AI_FINOPS.md`).
- Usage and adoption.
- One-line narrative on product health.

### Governance
- Incidents (count, severity, resolution time).
- Blocked actions (by class).
- Risk trends from the risk register.
- Internal Controls scorecard.
- One-line narrative on governance health.

### Market
- Top sectors by booked revenue and pipeline.
- Best-performing services and offers.
- Content output (publications, talks, mentions).
- Partnerships activated.

### Decisions Needed
- **Pricing decisions** the board must take.
- **Hiring decisions** the board must take.
- **Product decisions** the board must take.
- **Service focus decisions** the board must take.

Each decision item carries a recommendation, a one-paragraph rationale, and the deadline by which the decision is needed.

## Production
- Cadence: monthly, with a quarterly extended pack including strategy review.
- Owner: CEO, with sections drafted by function leads and signed off by COO.
- Format: PDF and in-workspace doc; archived per the retention policy.
- Length: maximum 12 pages including charts.

## Interfaces
| Inputs | Outputs | Owners | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Function dashboards, incident logs, AI cost data | Section drafts | Function leads | Monthly |
| Strategic plan + phase plan | Decisions Needed section | CEO + COO | Monthly |
| Board feedback | Action items + tracker updates | COO | Monthly |

## Metrics
- Pack On-Time Rate — % of monthly Board Packs delivered before the board meeting.
- Decision-Made Rate — % of "Decisions Needed" items resolved before next pack.
- Action-Closed Rate — % of last-pack action items closed by next pack.
- Pack Length Compliance — % of packs at or below the 12-page limit.
