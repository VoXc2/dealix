# Dealix Execution Engine

Dealix operates through six execution loops:

## 1. Market Loop

Find painful business problems in a focused vertical.

## 2. Sales Loop

Convert pain into paid diagnostic or sprint.

## 3. Delivery Loop

Deliver measurable output with QA and governance.

## 4. Proof Loop

Turn delivery into proof, case assets, and value metrics.

## 5. Product Loop

Turn repeated manual work into product modules.

## 6. Retainer Loop

Turn proven value into monthly operating cadence.

## Rule

Every week must move at least one item in each loop.

---

## The ideal week

```text
Market:
Identify ~20 target accounts.

Sales:
Send ~10 thoughtful manual messages.

Delivery:
Ship one deliverable or one demo.

Proof:
Produce a proof artifact or sample report.

Product:
Build or improve one module.

Retainer:
Offer continuation to a client or qualified prospect.
```

This is the **company machine**.
