The **Control Plane** turns ledgers into **decisions**—what to sell, block, build, improve, upsell, and publish.

## Outputs (weekly minimum)

1. **What to sell** — Official vs Beta vs diagnostic ([`SELLABILITY_DECISION.md`](SELLABILITY_DECISION.md))
2. **What to block** — unsafe asks; log in Governance ledger
3. **What to build** — from Product ledger + [`BUILD_DECISION.md`](../product/BUILD_DECISION.md)
4. **What to improve** — playbooks, templates, checklists from Learning ledger
5. **What to upsell** — expansion-ready clients ([`../growth/CLIENT_HEALTH_SCORE.md`](../growth/CLIENT_HEALTH_SCORE.md))
6. **What to publish** — authority from Proof + Learning ([`../growth/AUTHORITY_SYSTEM.md`](../growth/AUTHORITY_SYSTEM.md))
7. **What capital compounded** — update [`../ledgers/CAPITAL_LEDGER.md`](../ledgers/CAPITAL_LEDGER.md) vs [`DEALIX_CAPITAL_MODEL.md`](DEALIX_CAPITAL_MODEL.md)

## Control Plane questions (answer from ledgers)

1. What services are **sellable now**?
2. Which clients are ready for **expansion**?
3. What risks are **repeating**?
4. What proof can become **case studies**?
5. What feature should be built **next**?
6. What service should be **repriced**?
7. What sector deserves a **playbook** update?
