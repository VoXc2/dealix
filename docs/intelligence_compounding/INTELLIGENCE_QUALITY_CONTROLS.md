# Intelligence Quality Controls

- Anonymize before benchmark.
- Separate client data from pattern data.
- Human review for insights.
- No PII in intelligence layer.
- No confidential metrics in public content.
- Confidence score per pattern (Low 1–2 / Medium 3–5 / High 6+).

Rules: do not turn weak pattern into benchmark; do not turn anecdote into strategy; do not publish without anonymization.
