# Dealix Category Blueprint — Governed Revenue & AI Ops (GCC)

## Category line

**Dealix = Governed Revenue & AI Ops Infrastructure for GCC companies.**

---

## Strategic positioning

Dealix ليست "AI tool" عامة، وليست CRM بديل، وليست وكالة أتمتة.  
Dealix هي طبقة تشغيل محكومة للإيراد والذكاء الاصطناعي داخل workflows حساسة للثقة.

**النتيجة المقصودة:** قرارات تنفيذية يمكن اعتمادها وتدقيقها وتوسيعها.

---

## Strategic narrative

الشركات لا تحتاج AI أكثر.  
الشركات تحتاج AI يمكن الوثوق به داخل workflow حقيقي.

الثقة تأتي من:

- source clarity
- approval boundaries
- evidence trails
- proof of value

---

## Category bundle (5-in-1)

1. AI موجود بلا governance.
2. CRM موجود بلا موثوقية كافية.
3. Pipeline موجودة بلا Proof.
4. Automation موجودة بلا approval discipline.
5. Dashboards موجودة بلا decision ownership.

---

## Risk register (operating)

1. Too much building -> weekly no-build gate.
2. Weak sales focus -> paid diagnostic as short-term objective.
3. Generic AI positioning -> always use governed category language.
4. Trust breach -> approval center + evidence ledger.
5. Delivery inconsistency -> diagnostic checklist + proof pack template.
6. Partner confusion -> Dealix diagnoses, partner implements.
7. Agent sprawl -> registry + permissions + logs + approval gates.

---

## 12-month arc (proof-first)

- **0–30:** first paid diagnostic + proof pack + objection log.
- **31–90:** 3–5 paid diagnostics + first sprint + retainer candidate.
- **3–6 months:** retainers + monthly revenue reviews + proof library.
- **6–9 months:** repeated workflows stabilize as module candidates.
- **9–12 months:** client workspace beta + partner workspace beta + first standard draft.

---

## Execution rules now

1. Keep diagnostic as the primary entry offer.
2. Route every lead through a governed state machine.
3. Turn every meeting into scope or nurture.
4. Turn every scope into invoice draft.
5. Turn every payment into proof pack.
6. Turn every proof pack into upsell or referral.

## Governing sentence

**Dealix لا تبيع الذكاء الاصطناعي. Dealix تبيع القدرة على تشغيل الذكاء الاصطناعي والإيراد داخل الشركات بثقة: مصدر واضح، موافقة واضحة، دليل واضح، وقرار يخلق قيمة.**
