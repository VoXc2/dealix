## 1. Positioning statement

**EN —** Dealix helps companies turn AI experiments and revenue operations into
governed, measurable, evidence-backed workflows.

**AR —** Dealix تساعد الشركات على تحويل تجارب الذكاء الاصطناعي وعمليات الإيراد إلى
تشغيل محكوم، قابل للقياس، ومربوط بالأدلة.

Dealix is a **Governed Revenue & AI Operations Company**. We do not resell AI
tools and we do not run spam motions. We operate revenue and AI workflows for
our clients with a governance and evidence layer underneath every action.

## 2. The differentiator

Ordinary RevOps firms tidy a CRM and leave. Dealix is different on one axis that
matters: **every recommendation, every follow-up, and every sales decision is
tied to `source_ref` + approval + evidence + a decision passport.**

- No claim without a source reference.
- No external action without founder/owner approval.
- No engagement without a Proof Pack and a recorded decision trail.

The lead commercial entry point is **Governed Revenue Operations** (Revenue
Intelligence & Governed Ops) — *not* an ordinary CRM clean-up.

## 3. Who we serve (ICP)

B2B services firms · consulting firms · SaaS companies · fintech · agencies ·
enterprise sales teams · VC portfolio companies.

Common pain we address: messy CRM, unclear pipeline, weak follow-up, an
untrusted forecast, unprioritized leads, ungoverned AI usage, and no decision
trail.

## 4. The 7 moats

| Moat | What it means |
|------|---------------|
| **Proof moat** | Every engagement ships a Proof Pack; value is shown, not asserted. |
| **Trust moat** | Approval-first, source-referenced, auditable decisions build durable trust. |
| **Revenue moat** | We attach to the client's revenue workflow, not a peripheral tool. |
| **Workflow moat** | We own the governed revenue workflow itself — hard to rip out. |
| **Saudi/GCC localization moat** | Arabic-first, PDPL-aware, Saudi B2B sales reality built in. |
| **Approval-first safety moat** | No autonomous external send; draft → approval → send always. |
| **Service-to-platform learning moat** | Every delivered service feeds the platform we build later. |

## 5. Company evolution sequence

Service → Repeatable Sprint → Retainer → Internal Platform → Client Workspace →
SaaS Module.

**Current form:** a service-led company with software-assisted delivery and a
governance/evidence layer. We do **not** skip ahead to a full SaaS product. The
platform is earned by delivering services first.
