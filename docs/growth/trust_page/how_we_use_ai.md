# How Dealix Uses AI

- AI assists **inside workflows** with **human-in-the-loop** where risk warrants it.  
- Outputs are **draft-first** for sensitive or external actions; **approval** is required before send or publish.  
- We use **prompt versioning**, **evals**, and **governance checks** aligned with the Trust Layer (see product operating map).  
- We **do not** rely on source-less knowledge answers when citations are required.  
- **Costs** are tracked per engagement so margin and budgets stay controlled.
