# Governance Standard (Public Summary)

Dealix aligns delivery with:

- **Runtime checks** — data source, PII, permissions, claims, external actions, approvals, logs.  
- **Permission mirroring** — AI inherits the user’s access; no bypass.  
- **Action taxonomy** — higher-risk actions require approval or are blocked in MVP.  
- **Incident response** — defined steps for exposure, wrong outputs, or misuse.  

Details: `docs/governance/RUNTIME_GOVERNANCE.md`, `PERMISSION_MIRRORING.md`, `AI_ACTION_TAXONOMY.md`, `INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md`.
