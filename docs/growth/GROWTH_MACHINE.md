# Dealix Growth Machine

Dealix grows through a repeatable loop:

1. Target a painful business function.  
2. Sell a focused Sprint.  
3. Deliver measurable proof.  
4. Convert proof into a Pilot.  
5. Convert Pilot into Retainer.  
6. Turn repeated delivery into Product.  
7. Turn proof into market trust.  
8. Turn market trust into inbound demand.  

## Rule

No growth activity is complete unless it creates:

- revenue  
- proof  
- reusable asset  
- next offer  

## Growth loop

```text
Target Sector
→ Diagnostic
→ Sprint
→ Proof Pack
→ Case-style Asset
→ Retainer
→ Product Feature
→ Playbook
→ Next Sector
```

This is the growth engine.

## First ICP (90 days)

Focus **B2B service companies** (not every company at once).

### Why B2B services first

```text
B2B services often have:
- scattered leads
- manual sales
- proposals that need tightening
- weak or missing CRM
- high customer value
- faster decisions than enterprise
- usually lower sensitivity than healthcare (still use PDPL discipline)
```

Clinics are a strong second vertical once Trust OS and delivery are proven.

### ICP v1

```text
Company type: B2B services
Team size: 5–50
Location: Riyadh / Jeddah / Eastern Province
Pain: leads, follow-up, proposals, weak CRM
Budget: SAR 7.5k–25k for sprint
Buyer: founder, GM, sales manager
Best offer: Lead Intelligence Sprint or AI Quick Win Sprint
```

## Customer success as a distribution channel

After a Proof Pack is delivered, do **not** say "we're done". Customer success
is itself a growth channel. Ask these five questions — each routes to a next move:

| السؤال — Question | يفتح — Routes to |
|-------------------|------------------|
| هل القيمة واضحة؟ — Is the value clear? | تأكيد + مرجع — confirmation |
| هل عندكم workflow ثانٍ؟ — Another workflow? | Sprint ثانٍ — second Sprint |
| هل تحبّون نكمل؟ — Continue? | Retainer (Managed Revenue Ops) |
| هل تعرفون وكالة/عميلاً عنده نفس المشكلة؟ — Know an agency/client with the same problem? | Referral / Partner intro |
| هل تسمحون باستخدام insight مجهول؟ — Consent for an anonymized insight? | Anonymized case — [`../CASE_STUDY_FACTORY.md`](CASE_STUDY_FACTORY.md) |

> **قاعدة:** كل Proof Pack يجب أن يفتح واحداً على الأقل من: Sprint · Retainer ·
> Referral · Partner intro · Anonymized case.
>
> **Time-to-Proof target:** in the pilot, deliver Proof Pack v1 in **under 48
> hours** — the first 48 hours after payment set the trust.

## Final line

**Dealix scales when each client produces three assets: cash now, proof that sells the next client, and knowledge or product that lowers the cost of the next delivery.**
