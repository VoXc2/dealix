## Context
The Growth Machine is the operating loop that turns Dealix from a project
shop into a compounding revenue + proof + product engine. It defines how
we acquire, deliver, prove, and re-sell — without ever decoupling growth
from the constraints set by `docs/DEALIX_OPERATING_CONSTITUTION.md` and
the strategic spine in `docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`. The
constraint it removes: scattered, one-off sales activity that does not
produce reusable assets. Every motion below must produce four outputs —
revenue, proof, a reusable asset, and a next offer.

## The 8-Step Growth Loop

1. **Target a painful business function.** Pick one operational pain in
   one ICP (currently B2B services, 5–50 staff, Riyadh / Jeddah /
   Eastern Province).
2. **Sell a focused Sprint.** Lead Intelligence Sprint or AI Quick Win
   Sprint — fixed scope, fixed price (SAR 7,500–18,000), 10 days.
3. **Deliver measurable proof.** A Proof Pack with before/after metrics,
   time saved, accounts cleaned, opportunities ranked.
4. **Convert proof into a Pilot.** Use the proof to justify a 30-day
   managed pilot (`docs/business/MANAGED_PILOT_OFFER.md`).
5. **Convert Pilot into Retainer.** Move pilot KPIs into a Monthly
   RevOps OS / AI Ops / Company Brain / Governance retainer.
6. **Turn repeated delivery into Product.** Anything done 3+ times moves
   to `docs/product/PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.md`.
7. **Turn proof into market trust.** Anonymized case-style asset,
   pushed through trust page and BRAND_PRESS_KIT channels.
8. **Turn market trust into inbound demand.** Repeatable category
   message reduces cost of next sale.

### Loop rule
> No growth activity is complete unless it creates **revenue + proof +
> reusable asset + next offer**. If a motion produces only revenue, it
> is a leak, not a loop.

## First ICP — B2B Services

Why B2B services first:
- Scattered leads, manual sales motion.
- Proposals that need improvement.
- Weak or absent CRM.
- High customer value per logo.
- Decision cycle faster than enterprise.
- Less sensitive data than healthcare.

Clinics and medical centers are a strong **secondary** opportunity but
require higher data-sensitivity caution — clinics open **after** the
Trust OS (`docs/strategic/ENTERPRISE_TRUST_COMPLIANCE_PACK_AR.md`,
`docs/ops/PDPL_RETENTION_POLICY.md`) is proven on B2B services.

### ICP definition

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Company type | B2B services |
| Team size | 5–50 |
| Location | Riyadh / Jeddah / Eastern Province |
| Pain | leads, follow-up, proposals, weak CRM |
| Sprint budget | SAR 7,500–25,000 |
| Buyer | Founder, GM, Sales Manager |
| Best offer | Lead Intelligence Sprint or AI Quick Win Sprint |

## What NOT to do (closing rules)

- Do NOT build full SaaS before first 5 paying clients.
- Do NOT build a marketplace before 20+ delivered projects.
- Do NOT launch the Academy before case studies exist.
- Do NOT build autonomous agents.
- Do NOT build WhatsApp sending now.
- Do NOT sell guaranteed results.
- Do NOT accept unknown-source data.
- Do NOT expand sectors before one playbook is strong.
- Do NOT hire before a repeated workflow exists.

## Closing rule — Three Assets per Client

Dealix grows when every client becomes three assets:
- **Cash now** — collected revenue.
- **Proof** that sells the next client.
- **Knowledge or product** that lowers next-delivery cost.

If a delivery produces only one of the three, it is a warning signal
that the offer or process is leaking value.

## Interfaces

| Inputs | Outputs | Owners | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| ICP definition, target list, sector pains | Sprint sales, Proof Packs, Retainers | Founder / Head of Growth | Weekly |
| Delivered Proof Packs | Case-style assets, trust-page entries | Growth + CSM | Per delivery |
| Repeated manual steps | Product features in PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER | Product + Delivery | Monthly |
| Retainer signals | New offer additions, playbook updates | Growth + Founder | Quarterly |

## Metrics

- **Sprint→Pilot conversion** — % of Sprints that produce a Pilot offer.
- **Pilot→Retainer conversion** — % of Pilots that become Retainers.
- **Proof-pack production rate** — Proof Packs produced per delivery.
- **Reusable-asset yield** — # of reusable assets created per delivery.
- **Productization rate** — manual steps moved to product per quarter.
- **Inbound share** — share of new logos sourced from trust assets.
