## Context

Market Capital is the public memory of Dealix. Without it, every
sales call starts cold and every category move starts unanchored.
The Market Capital System converts every Proof Pack into at least
one public, safe-to-share asset. It complements
`docs/BRAND_PRESS_KIT.md`, the competitive positioning in
`docs/COMPETITIVE_POSITIONING.md`, and the partner/agency reseller
playbook in `docs/growth/AGENCY_RESELLER_PLAYBOOK.md`. It is the
peer of `docs/company/KNOWLEDGE_CAPITAL_SYSTEM.md` and
`docs/company/TRUST_CAPITAL_SYSTEM.md`.

## Market Asset Types

| Asset | Definition |
|---|---|
| Content post | LinkedIn / X / Substack note tied to a Proof or insight |
| Playbook | Public 5-10 page sector playbook |
| Benchmark report | Anonymized cross-client metric report |
| Partner relationship | Named active partner with co-pitch motion |
| Referral source | Customer or partner who introduces deals |
| Webinar | Recorded session with executive audience |
| Public template | Free downloadable artifact (SOP, prompt, intake form) |
| Authority page | Long-form page that owns a term/topic |

## The 1-to-1 Rule

> **Every Proof Pack must produce one market-safe asset.**

The asset is one of:

- An anonymized insight post.
- A sector lesson note.
- A benchmark data point.
- A case-style story (consented + anonymized + reviewed).

A Proof Pack that does not yield one market asset is incomplete. The
CSM cannot mark engagement Closed until the market asset is created
or explicitly waived by CEO with reason.

## Why Anonymization First

Anonymized assets are publishable immediately. Named assets require
explicit, dated, signed consent (see
`docs/company/TRUST_CAPITAL_SYSTEM.md`). Default is anonymized; named
upgrades happen later.

## Distribution Surfaces

- LinkedIn personal account (CEO + leadership).
- Dealix company page.
- Email newsletter to qualified Saudi/MENA list.
- Partner channels per `docs/growth/AGENCY_RESELLER_PLAYBOOK.md`.
- Authority pages on dealix domain.

## Cadence

- **Per engagement close** — 1 market asset produced.
- **Weekly** — Growth Lead curates and ships content.
- **Monthly** — Benchmark report refresh if cohort ≥5.
- **Quarterly** — Authority page review.

## Anti-Spam Rules

- No vendor name-drops without consent.
- No false numbers; benchmarks always footnote sample size.
- No engagement-bait language ("comment AGREE if you want…").
- No translated English content that breaks Arabic register (per
  `docs/quality/ARABIC_BUSINESS_QUALITY.md`).

## Interfaces

| Inputs | Outputs | Owners | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Proof Packs | Market-safe asset(s) | Growth Lead + CSM | Per close |
| Knowledge Ledger | Topic candidates | CSO | Monthly |
| Benchmark cohort | Benchmark report | Growth Lead | Quarterly |

## Metrics

- **Market assets per Proof Pack** — target ≥1.
- **Authority page count by topic** — track quarterly.
- **Referral-sourced opportunities** — % of pipeline (target ≥20%).
- **Webinar count/quarter** — target ≥1.
- **Public content compliance** — % reviewed before publish (target
  100%).
