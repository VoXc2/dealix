# Founder Content Routine (Dealix)

## Daily (15 min)
- 1 LinkedIn post
- 1 X post
- 1 reply to a relevant thread

## Weekly (60 min)
- 1 industry case-card
- 1 proof report (real, demo-flagged)
- Review the week's content performance

## Monthly (90 min)
- 1 long-form (Medium or blog)
- Quarterly review
- Adjust topics based on engagement

## Tools
- `python3 scripts/generate_content_calendar.py --days 30`
- `python3 scripts/generate_campaign_pack.py --campaign <name> --lang both`
