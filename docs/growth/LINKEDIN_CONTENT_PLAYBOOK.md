# LinkedIn Content Playbook (Dealix)

## Structure (5 lines)
1. Hook (one line, 5-8 words)
2. Pain (one line, 1 sentence)
3. Insight (one line, 1 sentence)
4. Offer (one line, 1 sentence)
5. CTA (one line)

## Sample
Most B2B teams don't have a sales problem.
They have a follow-up problem.
Inbound leads sit for 48 hours before anyone responds.
Dealix installs the response flow with human-reviewed drafts.
20-min diagnostic: [link]

## Rules
- One idea per post
- No hashtag overload (max 3)
- No emoji overload
- Bilingual optional (AR + EN)
- Always end with a question or CTA

## Cadence
- 1 per day, weekdays
- Best time: 9-10am KSA

## What to avoid
- "Excited to announce"
- "We are thrilled"
- "Game-changer"
- "Unprecedented"
- "Synergy"
