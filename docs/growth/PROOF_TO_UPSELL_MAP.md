# Proof → Upsell Map

Every strong proof point should suggest a **natural next offer**—without inventing need.

| Proof found | Meaning | Upsell |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| Top 50 accounts identified | execution capacity needed | Pilot Conversion / follow-through sprint |
| Data quality improved but CRM still messy | ongoing hygiene | Monthly RevOps OS |
| Support categories + macros drafted | sustained ops | AI Support Desk sprint / retainer |
| Documents indexed + Q&A working | knowledge churn | Monthly Company Brain Management |
| Workflow saves hours (measured) | more workflows | Monthly AI Ops |
| Governance risks catalogued | policy + controls | AI Governance Program |
