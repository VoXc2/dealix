# 12-Month Roadmap — Dealix Growth Layer

**Layer:** L6 · Growth Machine
**Owner:** Founder
**Status:** Draft
**Last reviewed:** 2026-05-13
**Arabic mirror:** [12_MONTH_ROADMAP_AR.md](./12_MONTH_ROADMAP_AR.md)

## Context
The 12-Month Roadmap is the Growth-Machine view of how Dealix moves
from "first paid Sprint" to "recognized Dealix Method + enterprise
pilot live." It removes the constraint of "no shared sequencing across
sales, delivery, and product" and binds the operating spine of
`docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md` to concrete monthly
milestones. It extends the near-term plans in
`docs/90_DAY_BUSINESS_EXECUTION_PLAN.md` and
`docs/V5_COMPLETION_ROADMAP.md` into a full year.

## Phase 1 — Months 1–3 (Prove the Loop)

**Month 1**
- 3 offers fully written (Lead Intelligence, AI Quick Win, Company
  Brain).
- Core spine running (CRM v1, proposal template, follow-up sequence).
- 3 discovery demos.
- 1 first paid Sprint.

**Month 2**
- 3 paid Sprints delivered.
- 3 Proof Packs produced.
- 1 first retainer signed.
- `docs/playbooks/b2b_services_playbook.md` battle-tested.

**Month 3**
- Client workspace v1 (Notion / Sheets-based).
- Capital ledger live.
- Proof ledger live.
- Monthly client reports cadence stabilized.

## Phase 2 — Months 4–6 (Productize What Repeats)

- Company Brain module promoted from PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.
- Support Desk module promoted.
- AI Quick Win module promoted.
- 3 retainers live.
- 5 anonymized case studies published.
- Partner channel opened (`docs/growth/AGENCY_RESELLER_PLAYBOOK.md`).

## Phase 3 — Months 7–9 (Scale Infrastructure)

- Multi-client dashboard.
- LLM gateway live (per `docs/AI_STACK_DECISIONS.md`).
- Eval runner integrated.
- Langfuse / PostHog observability online.
- Enterprise governance documents formalized.

## Phase 4 — Months 10–12 (Enterprise + Standard)

- 1 enterprise pilot signed.
- RBAC v1.
- Integration set (CRM connectors prioritized by usage).
- Dealix Method published as a public standard.
- Academy pilot launched.

## Health Metrics Across Timelines

| Window | Targets |
|---|---|
| 30-day | 1 paid client, 3 demos, core spine working, proof template ready. |
| 60-day | 3 paid clients, 1 retainer, 2 Proof Packs, 1 vertical playbook. |
| 90-day | 5 paid clients, 2 retainers, QA average ≥ 85, 0 PII incidents, 3 reusable product modules. |
| 6-month | 10+ clients, 3–5 retainers, SAR 50k+ MRR, 3 case studies, client workspace v1. |
| 12-month | 25+ clients served, 8–12 retainers, SAR 150k+ MRR, enterprise pilot, recognized Dealix Method. |

## Sequencing Rules

- Do not start phase N+1 before the health metrics for phase N pass.
- The PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER governs what is allowed to graduate into
  product modules.
- All enterprise work waits for trust-pack maturity from
  `docs/strategic/ENTERPRISE_TRUST_COMPLIANCE_PACK_AR.md`.

- **MRR trajectory** vs. plan.
- **Productized modules** shipped per quarter.
- **Case studies** published per quarter.
- **Enterprise pipeline** in months 10–12.
