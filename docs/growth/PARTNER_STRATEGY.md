# Partner Strategy

Partners **extend reach** when your **delivery machine** is stable.

## Strong partner profiles

- CRM / RevOps consultants  
- ERP / accounting firms  
- Marketing agencies (outbound-sensitive—align on governance)  
- Cybersecurity / GRC consultants  
- Training companies  
- General business / ops consultants  

## Partner value prop

Dealix is the **AI operations layer**: readiness, workflows, governed drafts, proof—not generic “chatbots.”

## Models

- Referral fee (transparent tiers)
- Revenue share on joint sprint (written SOW)
- **White-label sprint** (only when QA repeatable—[`STOP_DOING.md`](../company/STOP_DOING.md))
- Joint delivery (Dealix owns governance + proof pack)

## Rule

No white-label or joint enterprise **before** delivery quality is boringly consistent. Log pilots in [`../ledgers/DECISION_LEDGER.md`](../ledgers/DECISION_LEDGER.md).
