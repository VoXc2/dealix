# Retainer Decision

> Retainer is the second sale, not the first. Offer it only when the first
> sale has produced trust and evidence.

## Prerequisites (ALL must be true before retainer is offered)

- [ ] Sprint Stage 7 (Prove) completed.
- [ ] Proof Pack delivered to the customer.
- [ ] Customer feedback ≥ 7/10.
- [ ] Quality Score on the sprint ≥ 80.
- [ ] Next-30-day plan is clear (in writing).
- [ ] A named owner exists on the customer side.
- [ ] A measurable monthly KPI is defined.
- [ ] At least one repeating workflow has been observed.

If any box is unchecked, offer a second Sprint instead — never start with retainer.

## Retainer offerings

| Retainer | Price (SAR/mo) | Match when |
|----------|---------------:|------------|
| Monthly RevOps OS | 15,000–60,000 | Leads/data refresh weekly; pipeline review needed |
| Monthly AI Ops | 15,000–60,000 | Recurring automations need maintenance + new ones added |
| Monthly Support AI | 8,000–25,000 | Support volume justifies continuous reply-suggestion tuning |
| Monthly Company Brain Management | 8,000–35,000 | Documents grow / change → freshness + re-indexing needed |
| Enterprise Retainer | 35,000–150,000 | Cross-pillar, multi-team, SLA-bound |

## Retainer offer pattern (3 options to the customer)

After Stage 7 handoff:

- **Option A — Do it yourself**: free plan written by Dealix; no fees. Customer takes it from here.
- **Option B — Pilot with Dealix (30 days)**: a paid pilot at retainer price for one month, decision at day 30.
- **Option C — Monthly OS Retainer**: full retainer agreement, monthly cadence.

Presenting three options removes the "yes/no" framing and lets the customer self-select.

## Retainer success metrics (per retainer)

- Customer health score ≥ 80 in month 1.
- Monthly proof report delivered on time (target 100%).
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR) ≥ 100% within 6 months.
- Renewal probability ≥ 80% by month 3 (CSM-asserted).

## When to NOT offer retainer

- Sprint quality was below 80 — fix that first; offer a free retro instead.
- Customer doesn't have an owner — retainer will fail without one.
- No recurring workflow observed — propose a second one-off Sprint.
- Customer wants "guaranteed leads per month" — refuse (Decision Rule 3).

## Owner & cadence

- **Owner**: HoCS drafts; CRO ratifies.
- **Cadence**: every Stage 7 handoff has a retainer/expansion conversation; mandatory per Stage 8 of `dealix_delivery_standard_and_quality_system.md`.
