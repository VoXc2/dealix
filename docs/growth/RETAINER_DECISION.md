# Retainer Decision

**Do not offer retainer before proof** — sprint + proof pack first.

## Offer retainer only when

- [ ] Sprint (or pilot) **delivered**
- [ ] **Proof pack** completed and reviewed with client
- [ ] Client sees a **recurring** need (not one-off curiosity)
- [ ] **Next 30 days** are concrete (workboard, metrics, cadence)
- [ ] There is an **owner** on client side
- [ ] **Monthly KPI** exists (one primary + secondary optional)

**Operating pattern:** [`RETAINER_OPERATING_MODEL.md`](RETAINER_OPERATING_MODEL.md).

## Retainer types (examples)

- Monthly **RevOps OS** (pipeline, hygiene, reporting cadence)
- Monthly **AI Ops** (automation + governance + reviews)
- Monthly **Support AI** (ticket triage, drafts, QA’d responses)
- Monthly **Company Brain** management (ingest + citations + refresh)

Match to [`CLIENT_JOURNEY.md`](CLIENT_JOURNEY.md) and [`SERVICE_REGISTRY.md`](../company/SERVICE_REGISTRY.md).

## When retainer fits

| Situation | Fit |
|-----------|-----|
| Leads need **monthly** refresh | RevOps OS |
| **Reports** repeat on a cadence | AI Ops |
| **Support** needs continuous improvement | Support AI |
| **Knowledge base** churns | Company Brain management |
