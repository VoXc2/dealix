## Context
Content is how Market Capital compounds. The Content Engine declares
the eight thematic pillars Dealix publishes against and the cadence
that keeps the engine alive without overwhelming the founder. It
implements the brand declared in `docs/growth/BRAND_SYSTEM.md`, fuels
the press posture in `docs/BRAND_PRESS_KIT.md`, and supports the
reseller motion in `docs/growth/AGENCY_RESELLER_PLAYBOOK.md`.

## The eight content pillars

1. **AI projects fail because of data** — Honest analysis of why
   pilots stall.
2. **AI operations vs AI tools** — Why operationalization, not
   tooling, is the binding constraint.
3. **Saudi business AI use cases** — Grounded examples from KSA
   sectors.
4. **Governance-first AI** — Why controls precede deployment.
5. **Proof pack examples** — Anonymized real outcomes from delivered
   sprints.
6. **Company Brain** — How institutional knowledge becomes operational
   AI.
7. **Lead intelligence** — Why data quality drives revenue.
8. **Arabic quality** — Why Arabic-first beats translated AI.

Every public piece must sit cleanly inside one of these pillars. Pieces
that don't fit are either reshaped or held.

## Cadence

| Asset | Frequency |
|---|---|
| Short post (LinkedIn / X / Threads) | 2 per week |
| Case-style post | 1 per month |
| Playbook (long-form) | 1 per month |
| Benchmark report | 1 per quarter (after sufficient data) |

The benchmark cadence is intentionally gated: a benchmark with weak
data harms Trust Capital more than no benchmark at all.

## Sourcing rule

Content sources are restricted to:

- Direct Dealix delivery data (proof packs, anonymized).
- Founder-authored analysis grounded in the Knowledge Graph.
- Public data Dealix has the rights to cite.

No machine-generated filler. No recycled industry talking points
without a Dealix-specific angle.

## Production loop

1. **Monday** founder selects pillar focus for the week.
2. **Tuesday–Thursday** draft and review against the brand voice tests
   in `docs/growth/BRAND_SYSTEM.md`.
3. **Friday** publish in English; Arabic mirror within 72 hours.
4. **End of month** content lead reviews engagement, attribution to
   pipeline, and pillar coverage.

## Distribution

- LinkedIn primary; X and Threads secondary.
- Email digest monthly to opted-in audience.
- Selected pieces repackaged into sales collateral via
  `docs/BRAND_PRESS_KIT.md`.

## Interfaces
| Inputs | Outputs | Owners | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Proof packs | Anonymized content seeds | Delivery lead | Per sprint |
| Knowledge Graph | Pillar topic ideas | Founder | Weekly |
| Engagement data | Distribution insights | Founder | Monthly |
| Sales feedback | Topic prioritization | Founder | Monthly |

## Metrics
- Posts per week — target ≥ 2.
- Pillar coverage — share of pillars touched per quarter; target 100%.
- Content-to-pipeline attribution — share of new pipeline attributable to content within 12 months; target ≥ 15%.
- Bilingual parity — share of pieces shipped EN + AR within 72 hours; target ≥ 90%.
