# Proof to Retainer System

## Rule

Every proof report must end with one of three decisions:

1. Continue operating this workflow monthly.  
2. Expand to adjacent capability.  
3. Pause because value/risk is not proven.  

## Retainer triggers

### Revenue

- top accounts identified  
- follow-up required  
- CRM hygiene needed  
- weekly pipeline review needed  

**Offer:** Monthly RevOps OS  

### Operations

- workflow saved time  
- more workflows identified  
- report cadence needed  

**Offer:** Monthly AI Ops  

### Knowledge

- docs indexed  
- knowledge needs updates  
- usage requires QA  

**Offer:** Monthly Company Brain  

### Governance

- risks found  
- employees use AI without rules  
- approvals needed  

**Offer:** AI Governance Retainer  

## Retainer packages (indicative)

### Monthly RevOps OS

- Price: SAR 15k–60k/month  
- Includes: monthly scoring, CRM hygiene cadence, weekly pipeline review, draft packs, proof ledger touchpoint, executive summary  

### Monthly AI Ops

- Price: SAR 15k–60k/month  
- Includes: 1–3 workflows monitored, weekly report, QA improvements, automation tweaks, risk checks, proof updates  

### Monthly Company Brain

- Price: SAR 8k–30k/month  
- Includes: ingestion for new docs, answer QA samples, freshness checks, usage report, knowledge gap list  

### Monthly Governance

- Price: SAR 10k–40k/month  
- Includes: policy tweaks, approval reviews, risk register updates, audit-friendly summaries, staff guidance
