## Context
The Proof-to-Retainer System converts the output of every Sprint or
Pilot into one of three explicit decisions, then routes accepted
clients into the right monthly retainer. It removes the constraint of
"great delivery, no recurring revenue" and operationalizes the
recurring-revenue thesis from `docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`
and the customer-success motion in `docs/CUSTOMER_SUCCESS_PLAYBOOK.md`.
It is the bridge between delivery and MRR.

## The Three Decisions

Every Proof Pack ends with one of:

1. **Continue** — operate this workflow monthly under a retainer.
2. **Expand** — move to an adjacent capability (e.g., from Lead
   Intelligence to Company Brain).
3. **Pause** — value not proven OR risk too high to continue.

A delivery with no decision is incomplete. The CSM owns the decision
within 7 days of Proof Pack delivery.

## Retainer Triggers

### Revenue triggers → **Monthly RevOps OS**
- Top accounts identified but follow-up still manual.
- CRM hygiene needed continuously.
- Weekly pipeline review needed by leadership.
- Outreach draft packs needed every week/month.

### Operations triggers → **Monthly AI Ops**
- Workflow saved meaningful time in the Sprint.
- More workflows identified but not yet covered.
- Report cadence needed (weekly / monthly).
- Risk checks needed on AI-assisted outputs.

### Knowledge triggers → **Monthly Company Brain**
- Documents ingested and indexed.
- Knowledge changes regularly (new policies, products).
- Usage requires QA and freshness checks.
- Gaps report drives continued ingestion.

### Governance triggers → **AI Governance Retainer**
- Risks found during Sprint or Pilot.
- Employees use AI without rules.
- Approvals required for high-impact AI outputs.
- Audit summaries needed regularly.

## Retainer Packages

| Retainer | Monthly Range (SAR) | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly RevOps OS | 15k – 60k | Lead scoring, CRM hygiene, pipeline review, outreach draft packs, proof ledger, executive report |
| Monthly AI Ops | 15k – 60k | 1–3 workflows monitored, weekly report, QA improvements, automation updates, risk checks, proof updates |
| Monthly Company Brain | 8k – 30k | New doc ingestion, answer QA, freshness checks, usage report, knowledge gaps |
| Monthly Governance | 10k – 40k | Policy updates, approval reviews, risk register, audit summaries, staff guidance |

## Decision Workflow

1. **Delivery day** — Proof Pack handed over.
2. **Day +3** — CSM call to walk through results.
3. **Day +5** — CSM drafts the recommended decision + retainer.
4. **Day +7** — Decision confirmed by client (Continue / Expand / Pause).
5. **Day +10** — Retainer signed OR Pause logged with reason.

## Pause-Path Discipline

A "Pause" is **not failure**. It triggers:
- A root-cause note in the proof ledger.
- A revisit reminder at +90 days.
- An honest entry in `docs/product/PRODUCTIZATION_LEDGER.md` if the
  pause was caused by repeated friction.

## Interfaces

| Inputs | Outputs | Owners | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Proof Pack | Continue / Expand / Pause decision | CSM | Per delivery |
| Decision | Retainer proposal | CSM + Founder | Per decision |
| Retainer signed | MRR added to finance | Finance | Per signing |
| Pause | Root-cause note + 90-day revisit | CSM | Per pause |
