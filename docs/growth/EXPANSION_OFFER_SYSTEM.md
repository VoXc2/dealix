# Expansion Offer System

Every delivery ends with **three expansion paths** (adapt to service).

## Lead Intelligence — example options

```text
Option A: Client executes top 10 actions internally (self-serve with your outputs)
Option B: Pilot “Conversion Sprint” — focused follow-through for 30 days
Option C: Monthly RevOps OS — cadence + hygiene + proof ([`RETAINER_OPERATING_MODEL.md`](RETAINER_OPERATING_MODEL.md))
```

## Company Brain — example options

```text
Option A: Client uses knowledge base internally with your playbook
Option B: Sales Knowledge Assistant sprint — governed Q&A + drafts
Option C: Monthly Company Brain Management — ingest, QA, citations, refresh
```

## Rules

- Tie each option to a **KPI** and **owner**
- Never present options that violate [`GOVERNANCE_DECISION.md`](../governance/GOVERNANCE_DECISION.md)
- Use [`RETAINER_DECISION.md`](RETAINER_DECISION.md) before pricing recurring

See [`CLIENT_REVIEW_MEETING.md`](../delivery/CLIENT_REVIEW_MEETING.md) for meeting close.
