# Dealix Client Journey

The 7-stage path from "never heard of Dealix" to "referral source".

```
Awareness → Diagnostic → Sprint → Pilot → Retainer → Expansion → Enterprise → Referral
```

## Stage 1 — Awareness
- **Goal**: prospect understands what Dealix is in 30 seconds.
- **Assets**: landing pages (`docs/sales/offer_pages/`), demo videos (TBD), anonymized sample reports (`docs/services/<offer>/sample_output.md`), AR/EN content posts (≥ weekly).
- **Exit criteria**: prospect requests a discovery call OR downloads a sample report.

## Stage 2 — Diagnostic
- **Goal**: identify a specific business use case worth a Sprint.
- **Assets**: discovery script (`docs/sales/sales_script.md`), readiness assessment, persona-value matrix (`docs/sales/persona_value_matrix.md`).
- **Output**: a 1-page diagnostic written by Dealix; price quoted: SAR 3,500–7,500.
- **Exit criteria**: prospect either pays for Diagnostic or moves directly to a Sprint quote.

## Stage 3 — Sprint
- **Goal**: deliver first measurable, paid output in 7–21 days.
- **Assets**: SOW templates (`templates/sow/`), service folder (`docs/services/<offer>/`), 8-stage Delivery Standard.
- **Output**: cleaned data / live automation / cited assistant + executive report + Proof Pack.
- **Exit criteria**: Quality Score ≥ 80, Stage 7 (Prove) complete, Proof Pack delivered.

## Stage 4 — Pilot
- **Goal**: operate the workflow inside the customer's business for 30 days. Validates outcome under real conditions.
- **Assets**: `docs/delivery/pilot_framework.md` (W5.T18).
- **Output**: 30-day operating report + before/after numbers.
- **Exit criteria**: ≥ 60% conversion to retainer (per W5.T18 KPI).

## Stage 5 — Retainer
- **Goal**: predictable monthly revenue + continuous improvement.
- **Assets**: retainer scopes (`docs/customer-success/cs_framework.md`, `expansion_playbook.md`).
- **Output**: monthly dashboard, monthly proof pack, monthly governance check.
- **Exit criteria**: 3+ months of stable QA + customer health score ≥ 80.

## Stage 6 — Expansion
- **Goal**: add a second pillar (e.g., RevOps customer adds Serve Customers).
- **Assets**: `docs/customer-success/expansion_playbook.md` (W5.T19) — 5 triggers, 5 motions.
- **Output**: NRR > 100% on the account.

## Stage 7 — Enterprise
- **Goal**: full Enterprise AI OS contract (SAR 85K+ setup, SAR 35–150K/mo).
- **Assets**: `docs/procurement/enterprise_pack.md`, `docs/legal/enterprise_risk_register.md`, `docs/sre/slo_framework.md`.
- **Exit criteria**: 10+ paid Sprints + 3+ retainers + 0 governance incidents per `docs/enterprise/ENTERPRISE_READINESS.md`.

## Referral
- **Goal**: every successful customer surfaces 2+ qualified referrals.
- **Assets**: anonymized case study template (`docs/assets/case_studies/` planned), Proof Pack used as social proof.
- **Activation**: handoff session in Stage 6 (Deliver) explicitly asks "who else in your network has this same problem?"

## Stage transitions are gates, not timelines

A customer doesn't "move to retainer because 30 days passed". They move when the Stage exit criteria are met. The CEO reviews stage transitions in the weekly operating review.
