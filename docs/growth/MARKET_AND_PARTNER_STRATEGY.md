# Market + Partner + Brand + Content Strategy

> Combined growth playbook. Replaces 4 fragmented docs (Partner Strategy,
> Brand System, Content Engine, Market Capital).

## Brand message

**EN**: *Dealix turns AI from experiments into operating systems for Saudi
companies — with data readiness, workflow design, governance, measurable
outputs, and proof of impact.*

**AR**: *ديلكس يحوّل الذكاء الاصطناعي من أفكار وتجارب إلى أنظمة تشغيل
داخل الشركات: بيانات جاهزة، عمليات واضحة، حوكمة، مخرجات قابلة للقياس،
وإثبات أثر.*

### Pillars (use in every public surface)
- Data readiness
- Workflow design
- Human approval
- Governance
- Proof of impact
- Arabic-first quality

### What we are not (use these too — anti-positioning sharpens trust)
- spam automation
- chatbot agency
- generic AI consultant
- prompt-engineer-for-hire
- guarantor of sales numbers

## Partner channels (activate AFTER first 5 paying customers)

### Best partner categories
- CRM consultants (HubSpot / Salesforce / Pipedrive implementers).
- Marketing / digital agencies.
- Accounting / ERP firms (existing data-rich client books).
- Cybersecurity consultants (cross-sell governance).
- Sectoral chambers (Riyadh / Jeddah / Eastern).
- Training institutes.

### Partnership models
| Model | What partner does | What Dealix does | Commission |
|-------|-------------------|------------------|-----------|
| Referral | Intro qualified prospect | Run full delivery | 15% of first-Sprint fee |
| Reseller | Sell with Dealix pricing | Delivery + branding | 25% of fee |
| SI Implementation | Co-deliver large engagements | Methodology + governance | 30% margin share |
| White-label | Resell as their brand | Engineer + QA only | TBD — only after Sprint quality is stable |

### Partner gates
- No partner program activation before 5 paying customers + 3 Quality Score ≥ 85 deliveries.
- No white-label model before 10 customers + audit-export capability.

## Content engine

### Cadence (after first customer; sooner is content-without-evidence)
- 2 posts/week (LinkedIn-style, EN + AR).
- 1 case-style post/month (anonymized).
- 1 playbook/quarter (vertical).
- 1 benchmark/year (after 10+ projects).

### Content sources
1. Proof Ledger entries → anonymized case studies.
2. Learning Ledger entries → frameworks and teardowns.
3. Governance Ledger entries → "AI fails because of X" posts.
4. Market Intelligence (TBD) → Saudi-specific data points.

### Content types
- **Teardown**: "Why this kind of AI project fails".
- **Checklist**: "10-point AI Readiness".
- **Framework**: "The Dealix 8-Standard".
- **Case study (anonymized)**: "Sector X, before/after".
- **Benchmark**: "Saudi SME AI Ops 2026".
- **Playbook excerpt**: "How clinics buy AI".

### Anti-patterns blocked
- Generic AI hype posts.
- "5 prompts to grow your business".
- Anything that resembles guaranteed-outcome language.

## Authority compounding
