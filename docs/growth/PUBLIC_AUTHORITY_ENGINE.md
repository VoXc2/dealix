# Public Authority Engine

Publish from **system**, not ego—so trust scales with evidence.

## Content pillars

1. AI readiness (gates, data, process)
2. Why AI projects fail (environment > model only)
3. Data quality (practical, not academic)
4. AI operations (delivery, QA, proof)
5. Arabic AI quality (reviewer discipline)
6. AI governance (PDPL, approvals, no spam)
7. Saudi sector playbooks (patterns, not names)
8. Proof packs (anonymized patterns)
