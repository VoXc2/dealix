# CEO Strategy

> One-page strategic thesis. Long-form 2026 plan in
> `docs/STRATEGIC_MASTER_PLAN_2026.md`. 90-day execution in
> `docs/strategy/90_DAY_PLAN.md`.

## Thesis

Saudi enterprises do not pay for AI. They pay for outcomes — in SAR,
in 7–28 days, with audit-grade evidence. Vision 2030, PDPL enforcement,
ZATCA Phase 2, and in-Kingdom residency mandates create a window where
the first AI Operating Partner with productized SAR-priced outcomes
wins disproportionately.

**AR:** "الشركات السعودية لا تدفع للذكاء الاصطناعي — تدفع لنتائج موثّقة
بالريال، خلال 7–28 يوم، بدليل قابل للتدقيق."

Dealix is not a CRM, not a chatbot, not an "AI subscription." Dealix is
the operating partner the consulting firm should have become —
productized outcomes, bilingual delivery, PDPL-native, ZATCA-invoiced.

## The wedge — three starting offers

We do not sell 30 services in conversation 1. We sell one of three.

| Offer | SAR | Days | Pillar |
|-------|-----|------|--------|
| Revenue Intelligence Sprint | 9,500 | 10 | Grow Revenue |
| AI Quick Win Sprint | 12,000 | 7 | Automate Operations |
| Company Brain Sprint | 20,000 | 21 | Build Company Brain |

Three offers spread across three pillars test demand simultaneously
and fund the system. Every customer ends with a Proof Pack and an
upsell. Target: 25 starting-offer customers in 90 days.

## Expansion path

**Sprint → Retainer → Multi-pillar → Enterprise AI OS.**

1. **Sprint (Month 0)** — fixed price, fixed scope, proof captured.
2. **Retainer (Month 1–3)** — Monthly RevOps / AI Ops / Support /
   Reporting / Company Brain (SAR 15K–60K / mo). Target: ≥ 40% sprint-
   to-retainer in 60 days.
3. **Multi-pillar (Month 3–9)** — second pillar inside the same account
   (Grow → Automate). Cross-sell raises switching cost.
4. **Enterprise AI OS (Month 9+)** — multi-quarter, multi-BU rollout
   (SAR 85K–300K setup + 35K–150K / mo). Anchor logo + reference rights.

Three-phase rollout: Months 1–6 cash → 7–12 retainers → Year 2+
enterprise.

## Moat (strongest first)

1. **Regulatory** — PDPL Art. 13/14 enforcement + Decision Passport +
   KSA residency. Foreign tools take 12–18 months to match.
2. **Linguistic** — Khaliji Arabic register, sector terms, bilingual
   QA gate at delivery. Translated tools sound foreign.
3. **Architectural trust** — approval-first design;
   `forbidden_claims.py`, `approval_matrix.py`, 8-gate validator, "no
   source = no answer". Buyers can read the code.
4. **Productization** — 30+ catalog offerings, 8 shared OS modules.
   Every repeated bespoke pattern becomes a catalog item in 30 days.
5. **Outcome evidence** — every project ends with a Proof Pack. Over
   time, the Proof Ledger becomes the best Saudi sector benchmark.
6. **Distribution (compounding)** — Saudi SIs / agencies become
   resellers; each onboarded partner adds pipeline with no marketing
   spend.

## Constraints (non-negotiable)

- No bespoke quoting in conversation 1.
- No cold WhatsApp; no LinkedIn automation; no scraping.
- No guaranteed-outcome claims ("نضمن", "100%", "best in").
- ≥ 80% productized revenue share by Month 12.
- Discount > 12% requires CRO; > 25% requires CEO.

## Day-90 success

5+ paying customers (avg deal ≥ SAR 12K, total cash ≥ SAR 60K) · 2
retainers (MRR ≥ SAR 30K) · 3 case studies · Quality Score ≥ 80 on
100% of projects · 0 PDPL violations.
