# CEO Strategy

> One-page strategic thesis. Long-form 2026 plan in
> `docs/STRATEGIC_MASTER_PLAN_2026.md`. 90-day execution in
> `docs/strategy/90_DAY_PLAN.md`.

## Thesis

**Saudi enterprises do not pay for AI. They pay for outcomes — in SAR,
in 7–28 days, with audit-grade evidence.** The Saudi B2B market is at
the inflection point where AI-capability buying is being replaced by
AI-outcome buying. Vision 2030, PDPL enforcement, ZATCA Phase 2,
in-Kingdom residency mandates, and a SAR 4–8B annual digitization spend
together create a window where the first **AI Operating Partner** with
productized SAR-priced outcomes wins disproportionately.

**AR:** "الشركات السعودية لا تدفع للذكاء الاصطناعي — تدفع لنتائج موثّقة
بالريال، خلال 7–28 يوم، بدليل قابل للتدقيق."

Dealix is not a CRM, not a chatbot, not an "AI subscription." Dealix is
**the operating partner the consulting firm should have become** —
productized outcomes, bilingual delivery, PDPL-native, ZATCA-invoiced.

## The wedge — three starting offers

We do not sell 30 services in conversation 1. We sell **one of three**:

| Offer | SAR | Days | Pillar | Purpose |
|-------|-----|------|--------|---------|
| Revenue Intelligence Sprint | 9,500 | 10 | Grow Revenue | Cash + ICP validation per vertical |
| AI Quick Win Sprint | 12,000 | 7 | Automate Operations | Fast operational proof |
| Company Brain Sprint | 20,000 | 21 | Build Company Brain | Knowledge anchor + switching cost |

Three offers spread across three pillars test demand simultaneously and
fund the system. Every customer ends with a Proof Pack and an upsell
conversation. Target: **25 starting-offer customers in 90 days**.

## Expansion path

**Sprint → Retainer → Enterprise AI OS.**

1. **Sprint (Month 0)** — fixed price, fixed scope, proof captured.
2. **Retainer (Month 1–3)** — Monthly RevOps / AI Ops / Support / Reporting
   / Company Brain operating program (SAR 15K–60K / mo). Target: ≥ 40%
   sprint-to-retainer conversion within 60 days.
3. **Multi-pillar (Month 3–9)** — second pillar activated inside the same
   account (e.g., Grow → Automate). Cross-sell raises switching cost.
4. **Enterprise AI OS (Month 9+)** — multi-quarter, multi-BU deployment
   (SAR 85K–300K setup + 35K–150K / mo). Anchor logo, reference rights,
   public case study.

Three-phase rollout: Months 1–6 cash services → Months 7–12 retainers →
Year 2+ enterprise (per `docs/strategy/dealix_operating_partner_positioning.md`).

## Moat (ranked, strongest first)

1. **Regulatory moat** — PDPL Art. 13/14 enforcement + Decision Passport
   audit trail + KSA data residency. Foreign tools take 12–18 months to
   match; by then Dealix has the references.
2. **Linguistic moat** — Khaliji Arabic register, sector terms, bilingual
   QA gate at delivery. Translated tools sound foreign.
3. **Architectural trust moat** — approval-first agent design;
   `forbidden_claims.py`, `approval_matrix.py`, 8-gate validator, "no
   source = no answer". Buyers can read the code.
4. **Productization moat** — 30+ catalog offerings, 8 shared OS modules.
   Every repeated bespoke pattern becomes a catalog item in 30 days. New
   service offerings reuse existing modules — no per-customer code.
5. **Outcome-evidence moat** — every project ends with a Proof Pack. Over
   time, the Proof Ledger becomes the most accurate Saudi sector
   benchmark dataset.
6. **Distribution moat (compounding)** — Saudi SIs and agencies become
   resellers / co-deliverers; each onboarded partner adds pipeline with
   no marketing spend.

## Constraints (non-negotiable)

- No bespoke quoting in conversation 1.
- No cold WhatsApp; no LinkedIn automation; no web scraping.
- No guaranteed-outcome claims ("نضمن", "100%", "best in").
- ≥ 80% productized revenue share by Month 12.
- Discount > 12% requires CRO sign-off; > 25% requires CEO sign-off.

## What success looks like at Day 90

5+ paying customers (avg deal ≥ SAR 12K, total cash ≥ SAR 60K) ·
2 retainers signed (MRR ≥ SAR 30K) · 3 case studies published ·
Quality Score ≥ 80 on 100% of shipped projects · zero PDPL violations.
