## Channel mix (annual)

| Channel | Share | Owner |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Outbound (Lead Engine + SDR) | 45% | CRO + SDR |
| Partner-sourced | 25% | CRO + Partner Manager |
| Events | 15% | Marketing |
| Inbound (content, SEO, AI-engine citations) | 15% | Marketing |

Mix shifts Q1 outbound-heavy → Q3 partner-balanced.

## Outbound rules (hard)

1. **No cold WhatsApp.** Ever. WhatsApp requires PDPL Art. 14 consent
   or a prior business relationship.
2. **No LinkedIn automation.** Manual, approved messages only.
3. **No scraping.** Source-registered providers only (≥ 25 KSA sources).
4. **Every message** carries the PDPL Art. 13 footer (AR + EN).
5. **Every send** logs `drafted → approved → sent` and passes the
   Outreach OS Approval Matrix.

## Inbound

- **Landing site**: 5-pillar pages + 3 offer pages.
- **Service Activation Console** (`/status.html`) — bilingual,
  structured, honest readiness JSON, designed for AI-engine citation.
- **Bilingual content**: 6 pieces by end of Q1; 24 by Q4. Khaliji
  Arabic register.
- **AR-first GEO content**: vertical landing, FAQ, case-study pages.

## Events

- Q1: 1 industry conference (LEAP or Money 20/20 Riyadh) + 2 roundtables.
- Q2: 2 roundtables + Sales Knowledge Day.
- Q3: First customer summit (15–25 customers, Riyadh).
- Q4: Second customer summit + 1 vertical sponsorship.

## Sales motion — 5 binding rules

1. Map to one of three starting offers in 30 minutes.
2. SOW from 1 of 3 fixed templates within 7 days.
3. No bespoke pricing in conversation 1.
4. Quality Score ≥ 80 before handoff / upsell.
5. Day-30 post-close retainer conversation, every customer.

## 90-day pipeline target

200 ICP-qualified accounts in CRM by Day 60 · 37 demos · 18 qualified
opps · **7 closes** · SAR 1.5M pipeline · SAR 1.5M closed · 2 retainers
signed.
