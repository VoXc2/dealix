# Dealix Service Portfolio Catalog

## 1. Context

Dealix as the AI Operating Partner offers a structured catalog of 30+ outcome-bearing services. Externally we surface only 5 customer-facing pillars; internally we maintain 8 service domains and 30+ productized offerings. This document is the canonical price list and scope-of-services reference used by sales, marketing, and CS. Every offering maps to one or more internal OS modules (see W6.T34).

- **Customer**: read-only price/scope reference.

## 6. Rollout phases

- **Phase 1 (Months 1–6) — Cash services**: Revenue Diagnostic, Lead Intelligence Sprint, AI Quick Win, AI Support Desk Sprint. Goal: 20–30 customers, prove pillar repeatability.
- **Phase 2 (Months 7–12) — Retainers**: Monthly RevOps, Monthly AI Ops, Customer Support AI retainer, Reporting Automation retainer. Goal: 15+ retainers, predictable MRR.
- **Phase 3 (Year 2+) — Enterprise**: Company Brain, Enterprise AI OS, AI Governance Program, multi-BU rollouts. Goal: 5+ enterprise customers, SAR 1M+ ARR per logo.
