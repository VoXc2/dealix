# Category Design

## Old category

AI agency · automation shop · generic “chatbot provider.”

## New category

**AI Operations Partner** — builds **capabilities**, not one-off demos.

## Problem with the old category

Tools and models without an **operating model** (data, workflow, governance, proof) → projects stall or create risk.

## New belief

AI only creates durable value when embedded in **data readiness**, **workflow clarity**, **human approval**, **runtime governance**, and **proof**.

## Dealix point of view

**AI is not a feature. AI is an operating capability**—measured, matured, and improved over time ([`CAPABILITY_OPERATING_MODEL.md`](../company/CAPABILITY_OPERATING_MODEL.md)).

## Category names (working)

```text
Arabic-first AI Operations
```

or

```text
AI Operating Systems for MENA Companies
```
