# Partner Program (Summary)

> Three-tier program: Referral / Reseller / SI Implementation. Canonical
> tier rules, commission economics, onboarding curriculum, and KPIs in
> `docs/partnerships/partner_program_sa.md` (W3.T06).

## Why partners

1. Saudi procurement often requires a Saudi-registered vendor co-sign.
2. MoF / MoH / MoI tenders require local language + presence.
3. A small Dealix team cannot personally cover 22+ named accounts.
4. Recognized SI brand co-presence accelerates trust.
5. Implementation arms (data migration, integration, change management)
   are partner-delivered by convention.

## Three tiers

| Tier | Role | Min ARR / yr | Commission |
|------|------|---------------|------------|
| **Referral** | Source qualified opportunity | SAR 60K | 15% Y1 (one-time) |
| **Reseller** | Front contract, invoice customer; Dealix delivers | SAR 250K | 25% Y1 / 15% Y2+ |
| **SI Implementation** | Reseller + paid implementation services | SAR 500K | 30% Y1 / 20% Y2+ + 100% services + 5% co-marketing |

## Four Saudi archetypes

- **A — Tier-1 SI** (banking / govt / telco) → SI Implementation.
- **B — MSP** → Reseller + managed rider.
- **C — Marketing / sales-ops agency** → Reseller.
- **D — Govt / public-sector reseller** (Etimad-listed) → Reseller or
  SI Implementation with govt addendum.

## Worked example

Growth tier SAR 9,500 / mo = SAR 114,000 Y1 ARR:
- Referral: **SAR 17,100** one-time.
- Reseller: **SAR 28,500 Y1 + SAR 17,100/yr Y2+**.
- SI Impl: **SAR 34,200 Y1 + SAR 22,800/yr Y2+** + 100% implementation
  services (typical SAR 60K–150K).

## Saudi-specific terms (non-negotiable)

1. **Saudi entity**: Reseller / SI hold CR + VAT + Etimad supplier ID
   (for govt). Referral may be individuals.
2. **Local content**: Saudi nationals on customer-facing roles where
   feasible; report per tender.
3. **Arabic obligation**: operate in Arabic when the customer requests.
4. **PDPL flow-down**: Dealix Sub-Processor Addendum mandatory.
5. **ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing** on every invoice.
6. **Deal registration**: first-come-first-registered; 5 business-day
   validation; 60/40 default split.

## 4-week onboarding (Reseller / SI)

- **W1 Foundations**: product, ICP, vertical positioning, trust posture.
  6 hrs + 30-Q check.
- **W2 Commercials**: pricing, ROI, persona-value, value metrics.
  Output: SAR-denominated, VAT-aware proposal.
- **W3 Demo & discovery**: sales playbook, Lead Engine, certified SE.
- **W4 Procurement & delivery**: procurement pack, PDPL/DPA, ZATCA,
  deal-registration tool.

Re-certification every 12 months or on major release.

## 90-day KPIs

- 8 active Saudi partners (onboarding + ≥ 1 registered deal).
- Partner pipeline coverage ≥ 2× quota.
- Partner-sourced closed-won ARR ≥ SAR 600K.
- Time-to-first deal ≤ 30 days from kickoff.
- Partner NPS ≥ 40.

## Co-selling plays

1. Joint account planning (quarterly, single shared plan per account).
2. Lead exchange (5-day claim SLA).
3. Co-presented executive briefings (90 min bilingual deck).
4. Co-funded pilots (Enterprise / Sovereign only, max 4 / year /
   partner; 50/50 fee & services waiver).

## Exclusivity

Non-exclusive by default. Segmented preference (vertical × territory ×
tier). Exclusivity requires CRO + HoLegal sign-off + minimum-ARR commit.
