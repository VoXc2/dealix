# Partner Program (Summary)

> Three-tier program: Referral, Reseller, SI Implementation. Canonical
> tier rules, commission economics, onboarding curriculum, and KPIs in
> `docs/partnerships/partner_program_sa.md` (W3.T06).

## Why partners

1. Saudi procurement often requires a Saudi-registered vendor to co-sign.
2. MoF / MoH / MoI tenders require local language + presence.
3. A 7-person Dealix team cannot personally cover 22+ named accounts.
4. Recognized SI brand co-presence accelerates trust.
5. Implementation arms (data migration, integration, change management)
   are partner-delivered by convention.

## Three tiers

| Tier | Role | Min ARR contribution (annual) | Commission |
|------|------|-------------------------------|------------|
| **Referral** | Source qualified opportunity; Dealix sells and delivers | SAR 60K | **15% Y1 ARR** (one-time) |
| **Reseller** | Front the contract, invoice the customer, Dealix delivers | SAR 250K | **25% Y1 / 15% Y2+ ARR** |
| **SI Implementation** | Reseller + paid implementation services | SAR 500K | **30% Y1 / 20% Y2+** ARR + 100% services + 5% co-marketing |

## Four Saudi-specific archetypes

- **A — Saudi Tier-1 SI** (banking / govt / telco) → SI Implementation.
- **B — Saudi MSP / managed-service provider** → Reseller + managed rider.
- **C — Local marketing / sales-ops agency** → Reseller.
- **D — Government / public-sector reseller** (Etimad-listed) →
  Reseller or SI Implementation with govt addendum.

## Commission worked example

Growth tier subscription at SAR 9,500 / month = SAR 114,000 Y1 ARR:
- Referral path: **SAR 17,100** one-time → partner.
- Reseller path: **SAR 28,500 Y1 + SAR 17,100/yr Y2+** → partner.
- SI Implementation: **SAR 34,200 Y1 + SAR 22,800/yr Y2+** + 100% of
  implementation services (typical SAR 60,000–150,000).

## Saudi-specific terms (non-negotiable)

1. **Saudi entity**: Reseller / SI partners hold active CR + VAT + Etimad
   supplier ID (for govt deals). Referral partners may be individuals.
2. **Local content**: Saudi nationals on customer-facing roles where
   feasible; Saudi-content ratio reported per tender requirements.
3. **Arabic obligation**: partners operate in Arabic when the customer
   requests it. Dealix supplies bilingual collateral.
4. **PDPL flow-down**: partners touching personal data execute the
   Dealix Sub-Processor Addendum.
5. **ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing** on every partner-to-Dealix and
   Dealix-to-partner invoice.
6. **Deal registration**: first-come-first-registered, validated within
   5 business days; default 60/40 split if both contributed.

## 4-week onboarding (Reseller / SI)

- **Week 1 — Foundations**: product, ICP, vertical positioning, trust
  posture. 6 hrs recorded + 30-Q check.
- **Week 2 — Commercials**: pricing, ROI model, persona-value matrix,
  value metrics. Output: produce a SAR-denominated, VAT-aware proposal.
- **Week 3 — Demo & discovery**: sales playbook, Lead Engine, live demo
  certification. Output: ≥ 1 certified partner SE.
- **Week 4 — Procurement & delivery**: enterprise procurement pack,
  PDPL/DPA training, ZATCA invoicing, deal-registration walkthrough.

Re-certification every 12 months or on a major product release.

## 90-day KPIs

- **8 active Saudi partners** (completed onboarding + ≥ 1 registered deal).
- **Partner-sourced pipeline coverage** ≥ 2× quota.
- **Partner-sourced closed-won ARR** ≥ SAR 600K.
- **Time-to-first-registered-deal** ≤ 30 days from kickoff.
- **Partner NPS** ≥ 40.

## Co-selling plays

1. Joint account planning quarterly (single shared plan per account).
2. Lead exchange under deal-registration rules (5-day claim SLA).
3. Co-presented executive briefings (90 min, bilingual deck supplied by
   Dealix).
4. Co-funded pilots (Enterprise/Sovereign only, max 4 per partner /
   year; Dealix waives 50% pilot fee, partner waives 50% impl. services).

## Exclusivity policy

**Non-exclusive by default.** Segmented preference granted (vertical ×
territory × tier). Exclusivity (if ever) requires CRO + HoLegal sign-off
and a binding minimum-ARR commitment.
