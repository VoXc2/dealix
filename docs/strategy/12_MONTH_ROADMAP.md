## Q1 — Foundation & first anchors (M1–M3)

**Logos:** 2 anchor (1 BFSI + 1 Retail OR Healthcare).
**MRR:** SAR 30K (2 retainers).
**Pipeline:** SAR 15M cumulative; SAR 1.5M closed.

**Engineering (5 modules live):**
Data / Revenue / Governance / Reporting / Delivery. Lead Engine in
production ≥ 25 sources. 5-gate QA operational; 8-stage Delivery
Standard wired.

**Commercial:**
3 starting offers operating. ABM playbook on 50 Tier-1 accounts.
6 bilingual content pieces. 1 conference (LEAP / Money 20/20 Riyadh) +
2 roundtables.

**Hiring (cum.):** AE #1 (BFSI), SDR #1, CS Lead, Marketing manager.

**Channel mix:** outbound 60% / events 25% / inbound 15%.

## Q2 — Repeatability (M4–M6)

**Logos:** +4 (cum. **6**); 2 paid pilots converted.
**MRR:** SAR 80K. **Pipeline:** SAR 30M cum.; SAR 4M closed.

**Engineering (7 of 8 modules):**
Customer OS + Knowledge OS depth + Operations OS. Multi-tenant
hardening (`tenant_id` row-level isolation). Audit-export endpoint.
A/B framework live; first 3 winning variants in sequences.

**Commercial:**
2 channel partners signed (1 SI + 1 sectoral reseller). Sales
enablement v1; 100% rep certification. Bilingual ROI per deal.
Procurement pack in 100% of enterprise opps. AI Support Desk + Company
Brain proven (≥ 1 each). First Vertical Playbook published (BFSI).

**Hiring:** AE #2 (Retail), Partner manager.

**Channel mix:** outbound 50% / partner 20% / events 15% / inbound 15%.

## Q3 — Expansion (M7–M9)

**Logos:** +6 (cum. **12**); first land-and-expand.
**MRR:** SAR 180K. **Pipeline:** SAR 60M cum.; SAR 10M closed.

**Engineering (8 of 8 modules):**
Strategy OS + Marketing OS launched. Self-serve onboarding (SME tier).
SLA dashboard live. Sovereign single-tenant deployment proven (1
customer).

**Commercial:**
Open Jeddah field presence (1 AE remote + monthly visits). Healthcare
vertical at full ramp. First customer summit (15–25 customers,
Riyadh). 3 case studies published; bilingual landing site refresh.
Begin Vision 2030 alignment narrative on Dealix.sa.

**Hiring:** SDR #2 (Jeddah), AE #3 (Healthcare), DPO / Trust officer.

**Channel mix:** outbound 40% / partner 30% / events 15% / inbound 15%.

## Q4 — Scale (M10–M12)

**Logos:** +8 (cum. **20**); 2 multi-year enterprise renewals.
**MRR:** SAR 300K. **Pipeline:** SAR 100M cum.; SAR 20M closed.

**Engineering:**
Enterprise AI OS reference implementation deployed (1 logo). Full
multi-tenant partner portal. Public Trust Center. Decision Passport
public attestation API (read-only).

**Commercial:**
Second customer summit. 5 public reference logos. 24 published
bilingual content pieces (cum.). Begin GCC expansion scoping (UAE /
Bahrain via Saudi anchors' subsidiaries). 2 enterprise multi-year
renewals.

**Hiring:** SE / Solutions consultant.

**Channel mix:** outbound 35% / partner 30% / events 15% / inbound 20%.

## Year-end gates (binding)

- NRR ≥ 110% by M12.
- Productized revenue share ≥ 80% by M12.
- Gross Margin ≥ 65% trending by M18 (Q4 trajectory).
- ≥ 3 of 5 customer pillars paying by M9; all 5 by M18.
- Zero PDPL enforcement incidents; zero forbidden-claim violations.

## Funding & spend

- Q1 budget: SAR 2.5M (people 60% / events 20% / marketing 15% / tools 5%).
- Q2–Q4 each: SAR 3M baseline; + SAR 1M if Q-1 pipeline met.
- Hiring gate: no AE #N+1 unless AE #N has SAR 3M closed trailing 6 mo.

## Risks & mitigations

1. **Hiring slip** → partner program (Q2) absorbs sales capacity.
2. **PDPL incident** → Approval Matrix + forbidden claims + DPO Q3.
3. **Enterprise slips past Q4** → Sprint + retainer model self-funds;
   enterprise is upside.
4. **LLM cost overruns** → FinOps gates per cost governance doc.
