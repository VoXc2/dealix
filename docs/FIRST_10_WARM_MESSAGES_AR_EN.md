# أوّل ١٠ رسائل ودودة / First 10 Warm Messages

> Templates for the 10 warm-intro messages the founder sends MANUALLY.
> NO automation. NO mass send. NO template variables auto-filled.

**Hard rules baked into every template:**
- Bilingual (Arabic primary, English secondary)
- ≤ 3 lines per message
- No `نضمن` / `guaranteed` / `blast` / `scrape`
- Founder personally types/edits each before sending
- Sent through the channel where the relationship already exists
  (WhatsApp / LinkedIn DM / email / Twitter DM / Slack DM)

---

## Forbidden phrases (never paste)

These would auto-fail the perimeter test in
`tests/test_landing_forbidden_claims.py`:

- ❌ "نضمن لكم زيادة [أيّ رقم]"
- ❌ "guaranteed [N] leads"
- ❌ "blast"
- ❌ "scrape your competitors"
- ❌ "100% conversion"
- ❌ "send to anyone in seconds"

---

## Quality bar

Each message must pass these tests **before sending**:

- [ ] ≤ 3 sentences in Arabic
- [ ] ≤ 2 sentences in English
- [ ] Specific to the recipient (replace EVERY `[bracket]`)
- [ ] No forbidden phrase
- [ ] Has a clear NEXT step (a time, a question, a yes/no ask)
- [ ] Sent through a channel where the relationship already exists

If any item is unchecked, don't send. Edit until all checked.
