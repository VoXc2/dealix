# Lead Intelligence Operating Contract

## This service promises

**Ranked opportunities and next actions** from **client-provided** datasets, with data-quality visibility and governance-safe drafts.

## This service requires

- Client-provided dataset(s) + column semantics
- **Source attribution** fields where applicable (or explicit “unknown source” handling)
- Target **ICP** / fit definition (even if v1 is heuristic)
- Business **offer** context (what they sell, to whom)

## This service must not

- **Scrape** third-party sites without explicit permission and legal review
- **Send messages** on behalf of the client without approval workflow
- **Guarantee** meetings, deals, or revenue
- Use **unsourced** personal data beyond the client’s lawful basis

## This service always delivers

- Data quality / hygiene summary (where in scope)
- Ranking + rationale pattern (human-readable)
- Next actions list
- **Draft** outreach where in scope (**approval** before send)
- **Proof pack** narrative (what/why/how/risks/next)

## This service escalates if

- **PII** appears without documented lawful basis
- Critical **source** fields are missing and client insists on “certainty”
- Client insists on **cold WhatsApp** or other forbidden actions
- **Claims** in outputs cannot be supported from inputs
