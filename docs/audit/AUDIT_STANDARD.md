# Dealix Audit Standard

Every **service**, **project**, and **AI output** can be audited against a single question set.

## Audit questions

1. What was **promised**? (registry / contract / proposal)
2. What was **delivered**? (artifacts, dates)
3. What **data** was used? (sources, lawful basis, redaction)
4. What **sources** supported the output? (citations, or N/A with justification)
5. What **risks** were found? (PII, claims, channel policy)
6. What **approvals** were required and obtained?
7. What **QA score** was achieved?
8. What **proof** was delivered to the client?
9. What **next step** was recommended?
