# Service Readiness Score

A service is official only if score >= 80 (see [`service_readiness`](../../auto_client_acquisition/delivery_os/service_readiness.py) and YAML defaults).

## Criteria (documentation alignment)

- offer clear: 10
- price clear: 10
- intake ready: 10
- scope ready: 10
- delivery checklist: 10
- QA checklist: 15
- report template: 10
- product module: 15
- governance checks: 10

Repo evidence flags map to these themes in [`service_readiness_defaults.yaml`](../../auto_client_acquisition/governance_os/policies/service_readiness_defaults.yaml).
