# Client Pitch Examples (patterns, not scripts verbatim)

## One-liner (Arabic-first positioning)

> نحن نُشغّل ذاكرة الإيرادات والعمليات: بيانات جاهزة للقرار، مسودات بموافقة، وأثر مُوثَّق — بدون وعود مُ inflated.

## Diagnostic angle

- What is the **one metric** that would prove this sprint worked?
- Where does the **truth** about pipeline live today (CRM, sheets, WhatsApp exports)?

## Proof angle

- You receive a **proof pack**: what we did, on what data, with what risks flagged, and what to do next.

## Governance angle

- External actions are **approval-first**; no cold automation; claims stay grounded in your inputs.
