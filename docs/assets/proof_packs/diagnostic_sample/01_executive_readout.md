| الركيزة | الحالة التقديرية | الملاحظة |

### أهم 3 قرارات إيرادية (مدعومة بأدلة من العيّنة)

3. **إضافة سجل أثر بسيط لمسار المتابعة** — لربط كل نشاط بنتيجة قابلة للتحقق.

### المسار المقترح

## English Section

### Client goal

Review revenue workflows before scaling AI tool usage, and confirm that data has a clear source and that approvals have clear boundaries.

### What we delivered

- Revenue Workflow Map across 3 workflows (acquire, follow-up, close).
- CRM / Source Quality Review.
- AI Usage Risk Review (internal use).
- Approval Boundaries Map.
- Evidence Trail Gap Analysis.
- Top 3 evidence-backed revenue decisions.
- Recommended Sprint / Retainer path.

### Estimated four-pillar findings

| Pillar | Estimated state | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Source clarity | Medium | ~22% of rows have no recorded source (illustrative example). |
| Approval boundaries | Weak | No documented approval point before external offers. |
| Evidence trail | Weak | No log linking an activity to an outcome. |
| Proof of value | Medium | Outcomes judged by intuition, not recorded impact. |

> States are estimated from the reviewed work sample — not a final or guaranteed assessment.

### Top 3 revenue decisions (evidence-backed from the sample)

1. **Standardize source capture at the point of acquisition** — because ~22% of rows have no source, it is hard to know which channel produces evidenced opportunities.
2. **Document a single approval point before any external action** — to remove ambiguity about who approves what.
3. **Add a simple evidence trail to the follow-up workflow** — to link each activity to a verifiable outcome.

### Recommended path

7-Day Diagnostic → an execution Sprint addressing decisions 1 and 2 → a Retainer for ongoing monitoring. The decision is the client's, with no prior commitment.
