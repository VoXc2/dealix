## 1. التشخيص الصادق / Honest Diagnosis

### الحقيقة الواحدة / The one truth

بنينا نظامًا تشغيليًا للذكاء الاصطناعي مكتمل البنية تقريبًا، ولم نتحدث
إلى السوق ولا مرة واحدة. هذا ليس تأخّرًا في المنتج — هو خلل في توزيع
الجهد. الشركة تبني لأن البناء مريح، والبيع مخيف. كل سطر كود إضافي قبل
أول عميل هو تأجيلٌ مكلف للحظة الحقيقة.

We built a near-complete AI Operating System and have never spoken to the
market. This is not a product gap — it is a misallocation of courage.
Building is comfortable; selling is frightening. Every new module before
the first customer is expensive procrastination.

### Real vs. not-real

| Dimension | State | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Product / infrastructure | **Real, 9/10** | 117 routers, 426 tests passing, 8 hard gates in code |
| Governance framework | **Real, 9/10** | 11 non-negotiables enforced; payment_confirmed is the only revenue trigger |
| Sales playbook (documented) | **Real, 9/10** | 7-day Sprint SOP, intake forms, agent endpoints live |
| Paying customers | **0** | warm list = 1 real contact; lead machine = 15 scored, 0 outreach sent |
| Revenue (SAR) | **0** | all ledgers are template rows; proof score 63 is an internal demo |
| Recorded market signals | **0** | signal infra live, never fired by a real customer |

### اختلال التوازن / The imbalance

- المنتج: ~95% — Product: ~95% complete.
- إثبات السوق: ~0% — Market proof: ~0%.
- 89 وحدة OS مقابل صفر عميل ليست أصلاً — هي **سطح صيانة** و**انجراف
  استراتيجي** و**شعور زائف بالتقدّم**. Eighty-nine OS modules against zero
  customers is not an asset; it is maintenance surface, strategic drift,
  and a false sense of progress.

### المرض المُسمّى / The named disease

> فخّ المؤسس-المهندس: استخدام البناء كبديل عاطفي عن رعب البيع.
> The founder-engineer trap: using building as an emotional substitute for
> the fear of selling.

الدواء الوحيد ليس خطة منتج أذكى — هو **تجميد البناء** وإجبار الشركة على
السوق. The cure is not a smarter product roadmap. It is a **build freeze**
and a forced return to the market.

## 2. الرؤية والإطار / Vision & Strategic Frame

**Mission:** Help Saudi companies implement AI safely and profitably inside
real business operations.
**Vision:** Become the AI operating layer for Saudi SMEs and mid-market.

**The 5-level evolution path** (from `docs/company/DEALIX_AI_OS_LONG_TERM_AR.md`):

1. Productized Services — 2. Service-Assisted Platform — 3. Client Operating
Workspace — 4. AI Operating System — 5. Regional AI Operations Standard.

نحن الآن بين المستوى 1 والمستوى 2 — لكن **ادّعاءً فقط**. لا يحقّ لنا
المستوى 2 قبل أن يثبت عميلٌ حقيقي أن المنتج سرّع تسليمنا. المنصة
المحوكمة هدفٌ يُكتسَب طبقةً طبقة، لا يُعلَن.

We are *claiming* L1→L2 but have *earned* only L1's first half. The Governed
AI Operations Platform is earned level by level — never declared. Horizon 3
of this plan is where L3–L4 become legitimate.

**Strategic thesis:** Dealix wins not by having the most AI features, but by
being the only Arabic-first AI partner whose every external action is
*governed, drafted, and proven*. The moat is the **capital flywheel**
(Section 6), and it does not spin without real customers.

## 3. المبدأ الحاكم / The Governing Principle — "Proof before Build"

### 3.1 تجميد البناء / The build freeze (effective immediately, 90 days)

**FROZEN — no new work:** new OS modules, new routers, new landing pages,
Enterprise Foundation Core operationalization, multi-tenant SaaS runtime,
agent orchestration, Board OS expansion, new docs sets.

**ALLOWED — only these:**
- Bug fixes that block a *real, named* customer or break CI.
- Changes a paying customer explicitly requested and paid for.
- Keeping `main` green.

كل طلب بناء يُردّ بسؤال واحد: "أي عميل يدفع ينتظر هذا؟" إن لم يوجد اسم،
فالجواب لا. Every build request is met with one question: "Which *paying,
named* customer is waiting for this?" No name → no build. This is Article 11
of the doctrine, now enforced — not aspirational.

### 3.2 المقياس الوحيد المهم / The only metric that matters (next 90 days)

ليست درجة الجاهزية، ولا عدد الاختبارات، ولا عدد الـrouters. المقياس:

1. **Real market signals recorded** (replied, meeting, used-in-meeting…).
2. **SAR collected** (`payment_confirmed`).

كل ما عداهما مقياس غرور حتى تتحقّق أول إشارة. Everything else is a vanity
metric until the first real signal exists.

### 3.3 اللامساومات الـ11 تبقى / The 11 non-negotiables stay

NO_LIVE_SEND · NO_LIVE_CHARGE · NO_GMAIL_LIVE_SEND ·
NO_PRICE_CHANGES_BEFORE_PROOF · NO_LINKEDIN_AUTOMATION / NO_SCRAPING /
NO_COLD_WHATSAPP · NO_LIVE_WITHOUT_GATES · NO_SILENT_CLAIMS_REPHRASE ·
NO_PREMATURE_FEATURE_PROLIFERATION · NO_CUSTOMER_STORIES_WITHOUT_CONSENT ·
NO_AI_RANKING_GAMING · NO_FAKE_PROOF / NO_GUARANTEED_CLAIMS / NO_FAKE_REVENUE.

هذه ليست قيودًا — هي التموضع نفسه. السوق السعودي يشتري الثقة. These are not
constraints; they *are* the positioning. The Saudi market buys trust.

### 3.4 قاعدة رأس المال / The capital rule

كل مشروع يجب أن يزيد **نوعين على الأقل** من رؤوس المال الخمسة. المشروع
الذي يبادل الوقت بالمال فقط ضعيفٌ بنيويًا ولو دفع العميل. Every engagement
must grow ≥2 of the 5 capitals; a pure time-for-money job is structurally
weak even when paid.

## 4. الأفق صفر — أول 14 يوم / Horizon 0 — First 14 days

**الهدف الوحيد:** أول إشارة سوق حقيقية موثّقة. لا إيراد بعد — إشارة.
**Single objective:** the first *recorded, real* market signal. Not revenue
yet — a signal. Owner: **Founder.** Exit gate at Day 14.

### 4.1 Pre-flight (Day 0–1)
- Merge the green Playwright fix; confirm all CI green; redeploy from `main`.
- Resolve the **pricing contradiction** (Section 8.1) — pick ONE ladder.
- Pick the **single ICP** for the first 10 outreaches (Section 5.1).
- Lock the offer to exactly **two rungs**: Free Diagnostic → 499 SAR Sprint.
  Hide tiers 2–5 from all outbound conversation.

### 4.2 Outreach (Day 2–10)
- From the 15 scored lead-machine targets + warm contacts, select **10
  named humans** (not companies — humans the founder can reach personally).
- Send **5 personal, founder-written messages per working day** (no
  automation, no cold WhatsApp — warm routes only). Target: 10 sent by Day 10.
- Log every send and every reply to the **Request Ledger** and the signal
  store *the same day*. A signal not recorded did not happen.

### 4.3 First diagnostics (Day 8–14)
- Convert any reply into a **Free AI Ops Diagnostic** (30 min, 1-page report).
- Deliver the diagnostic personally; end every diagnostic with one sentence:
  "the safe next step is a 499 SAR 7-day proof sprint."

### 4.4 Day-14 exit gate
| Outcome | Verdict | Action |
|---|---|---|
| ≥1 diagnostic delivered + ≥1 Sprint verbally committed | **On track** | Proceed to Horizon 1 |
| Replies but no diagnostic | **Slow** | Tighten the diagnostic offer; keep sending |
| 0 replies after 10 quality sends | **Signal** | Re-examine ICP + message, not effort (Section 13) |

## 5. الأفق الأول — 90 يوم / Horizon 1 — 90 days

**الهدف:** أول 3 عملاء يدفعون، أول Proof Pack موقّع، أول إشارة L4.
**Goal:** first 3 paying customers, first signed Proof Pack, first L4 signal.

### 5.1 The single ICP (choose one, commit for 90 days)

الفائز الموصى به: **شركات SaaS B2B السعودية، 10–50 موظفًا، لديها بيانات
مبيعات/CRM ولا تملك نظام قرار**. سبب الاختيار: أعلى ألمٍ تشغيلي، أسرع
قدرة على رؤية القيمة، وأقرب لشبكة المؤسس. Recommended: **Saudi B2B SaaS,
10–50 staff, has CRM/sales data, no decision system.** Highest pain,
fastest time-to-value, closest to founder's network. Everyone else is
politely declined for 90 days.

### 5.2 The offer — collapse 6 tiers to 1 motion

السلّم ذو الست درجات صحيحٌ كهيكل، لكنه معقّدٌ جدًا للمرحلة صفر. نبيع
**شيئًا واحدًا**: Diagnostic مجاني → Sprint بـ499 ريال. الدرجات الأعلى
(Data Pack, Managed Ops, Command Center, Agency) تبقى مجمّدة حتى توجد
أدلة L5. The 6-tier ladder is a correct *structure* but too complex for
stage 0. Sell exactly one motion. Tiers 2–5 unlock only on L5 evidence.

### 5.3 The 90-day weekly milestone plan

| Weeks | Milestone | Exit criterion |
|---|---|---|
| 1–2 | Deploy green; 10 warm outreaches sent | 10 sent, logged |
| 3–4 | First Diagnostic → first 499 SAR Sprint **paid** | `payment_confirmed` = true |
| 5–6 | Deliver Sprint; first **Proof Pack signed** | Customer sign-off recorded (L2→L3) |
| 7–8 | First expansion conversation (retainer pitch) | Yes/no in writing |
| 9–12 | 2nd + 3rd paying customer; first L4 signal | 3 customers; ≥1 meeting/deal-progressed event |

### 5.4 The Revenue Intelligence Sprint (the one delivery motion)

7-day per-customer loop, run personally by the founder (~9–18 founder-hours):
Source Passport → Data-Quality score → Account scoring → Draft pack
(approval-gated) → Governance review → Proof Pack assembly → Capital-asset
registration → Retainer-eligibility check. Every step records to its ledger.

### 5.5 Evidence ladder & signal taxonomy

القياس الرسمي هو L1–L5 (وليس L1–L7). ما سمّيناه سابقًا L6/L7 هو في
الحقيقة **أنواع إشارات** و**أحداث L5**. The canonical ladder is **L1–L5**
(see `docs/PROOF_AND_CASE_STUDY_SYSTEM.md`); "L6/L7" from earlier framing
are really *signal types* and L5 events.

| Level | Meaning | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| L1 Delivered | Deliverable sent | Internal proposals only |
| L2 Approved | Written customer approval | Pilot offers to similar customers |
| L3 Acted | Customer used the output | "We helped [type] execute [action]" |
| L4 Impacted | Output linked to a business result | "Our work led to [outcome]" |
| L5 Validated | Financial result OR signed testimonial + consent | Full case study, public marketing |

Signal types to classify every reply: `replied_interested`, `meeting_booked`,
`used_in_meeting`, `pilot_intro_requested`, `asks_for_pdf`,
`asks_for_english`, `asks_for_scope`, `invoice_sent`, `invoice_paid`,
`no_response_after_follow_up`.

### 5.6 90-day decision gates

- **Day 30:** ≥1 paid Sprint? If no → ICP/message problem, not effort.
- **Day 60:** first Proof Pack signed + L3 reached?
- **Day 90:** 3 customers, 1 L4, 1 retainer conversation → **scale**;
  otherwise **hold or reposition** (Section 13).

**الهدف:** من أول إثبات إلى إيرادٍ متكرّر قابل للتكرار، وبدء دوران
دولاب رأس المال. **Goal:** from first proof to *repeatable* recurring

### 6.1 Targets (months 4–12)

|---|---|

| Active retainers (Managed Ops) | 2–4 |
| MRR | 30,000–60,000 SAR |
| Signed Proof Packs (L3+) | ≥8 |
| L5 case studies (with consent) | ≥2 |
| Agency partners (signed MoU) | 1–2 |

### 6.2 The capital flywheel — the real moat

| Capital | Grows by | 12-month target |
|---|---|---|
| Service | Each delivery refines the Sprint checklist | Sprint at ≤6 founder-hours |
| Product | Only customer-funded module improvements | 2–3 real enhancements |
| Knowledge | Sector playbooks, objection patterns | 1 deep SaaS-B2B playbook |
| Trust | Signed Proof Packs → case studies | ≥8 proofs, ≥2 case studies |
| Market | Agency partners, GEO content, referrals | 1–2 partners, referral codes distributed |

عند الدرجة الثانية من السلّم نملك حقّ ادّعاء "Service-Assisted Platform"
لأن المنتج صار يُسرّع التسليم فعليًا بدليل عملاء. At L2 of the evolution
path we *earn* the "Service-Assisted Platform" claim — proven by customers.

### 6.3 Unfreeze rule

البناء يُفتح **انتقائيًا** فقط: ميزة طلبها ودفع لها ≥2 عميل، أو ميزة
تخفّض ساعات تسليم المؤسس قياسًا. لا يُفتح المنصة المؤسسية بعد. Building
unfreezes *selectively*: a feature ≥2 paying customers requested, or one
that measurably cuts founder delivery hours. Enterprise platform stays
frozen until Horizon 3.

### 6.4 Org & first hires (trigger-based, never ahead of revenue)

- **Hire 1 — Delivery/Ops Associate:** trigger = 3 concurrent paying
  customers straining founder time. Funded by collected revenue.
- **Hire 2 — Founder-led growth (partnerships/SDR):** trigger = a
  *repeatable* conversion rate from a *repeatable* message. Not before.
- No hires before revenue. The founder remains chief seller all year.

## 8. النموذج المالي / Financial Model

### 8.1 Pricing — resolve the contradiction first

المستودع يحمل سلّمَي تسعير متناقضَين: Sprint بـ499 ريال
(`docs/OFFER_LADDER_AND_PRICING.md`) مقابل "Sector Sprint" بـ5,000 ريال
و"Managed Partner" بـ12,000 ريال/شهر (وثائق V14). شركةٌ بقصّتَي تسعير
ليس لها تسعير. **القرار الموصى به:** اعتمد سلّم الـ499 — أقل احتكاك لأول
proof — وجمّد كل ما عداه حتى L5. The repo carries two contradictory price
ladders. **Recommendation:** adopt the 499 SAR ladder (lowest friction to
first proof); freeze the 5,000 / 12,000 numbers until L5 evidence justifies
a deliberate, founder-approved repricing (honoring NO_PRICE_CHANGES_BEFORE_PROOF).

### 8.2 Unit economics (499 SAR Sprint)

- Price 499 SAR · LLM cost <10 SAR · founder time ~9 hrs (→ ~6 hrs by month 12).
- Gross margin ~85%. The Sprint is a *proof-manufacturing* loss-leader, not
  a profit center — its job is to produce L2–L4 evidence and a retainer lead.
- Real economics live in **Managed Ops** (2,999–4,999 SAR/mo, ~70% margin).

### 8.3 Path to sustainability

- 90 days: ~1,500–6,000 SAR collected + 1 retainer conversation.
- 12 months: 30–60K SAR MRR — covers founder + hire 1.
- Break-even is effectively immediate (founder-funded, low burn). The true
  cost is **founder opportunity cost** — every month without a customer.
