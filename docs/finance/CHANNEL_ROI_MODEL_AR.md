# Channel ROI Model — نموذج عائد القنوات
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كيف نقيس ROI لكل قناة، ونقرر أيها يستمر، وأيها يتوقف.

---

## 1. ROI Definition

**ROI = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100**

or **ROI Multiple = Revenue / Cost**

Example: 5x ROI = 5 SAR revenue per 1 SAR cost

---

## 2. Per-Channel ROI

### 2.1 Inbound
- Cost: 5,000 SAR/mo (content + tools)
- Leads: 50/mo
- Customers: 2-5/mo
- Revenue: 50-100K SAR/mo
- ROI: 10-20x

### 2.2 Warm Email
- Cost: 5,000 SAR/mo (founder time)
- Sends: 1,000/mo
- Replies: 50
- Meetings: 10
- Customers: 2
- Revenue: 40-60K SAR
- ROI: 8-12x

### 2.3 LinkedIn
- Cost: 5,000 SAR/mo
- Connections: 100
- Replies: 10
- Customers: 1
- Revenue: 20-40K SAR
- ROI: 4-8x

### 2.4 Partners
- Cost: 2-4K SAR commission per deal
- Customers: 2
- Revenue: 40-80K SAR
- ROI: 10-20x

### 2.5 Content
- Cost: 3,000 SAR/mo (founder time + tools)
- Customers: indirect
- Revenue: indirect
- ROI: hard to measure, long-term

### 2.6 Press
- Cost: 5,000 SAR per release
- Customers: 0-1
- Revenue: indirect
- ROI: brand value

## 3. Per-Channel Allocation

### 3.1 Year 1 Budget (suggested)
- Inbound + Content: 30%
- Warm email: 10%
- LinkedIn: 5%
- Partners: 5%
- Press: 5%
- Tools: 20%
- Reserve: 25%

### 3.2 Re-allocation Monthly
- Cut low-ROI
- Add to high-ROI

---

## 4. ROI Calculation Detail

### 4.1 For Each Channel

```
ROI = (Revenue from Channel - Direct Cost) / Direct Cost

Direct Cost = (founder time × hourly) + tools + commission + content
```

### 4.2 Example: Warm Email
- Founder time: 1h/day × 30 days × 250 SAR/h = 7,500 SAR
- Tools: 500 SAR
- Direct cost: 8,000 SAR
- Revenue: 50,000 SAR
- ROI: (50,000 - 8,000) / 8,000 = 525% = 5.25x

## 5. Multi-Channel Attribution

### 5.1 Problem
- Hard to attribute one customer to one channel
- Customer often sees multiple touchpoints

### 5.2 Models
- **Last touch:** simple, biased
- **First touch:** biased
- **Multi-touch:** complex, accurate
- **Time decay:** recent more valuable

### 5.3 Default
- Last touch (simple, trackable)
- Tag in CRM
- Founder approves attribution

---

## 6. Channel Saturation

### 6.1 When Channel Saturated
- Volume drops despite same effort
- Conversion drops
- Diminishing returns

### 6.2 Mitigation
- Refresh approach
- New audience
- New content
- New partner
- Add new channel

---

## 7. Channel Diversification

### 7.1 Target
- No channel > 50% of customers
- 3+ active channels
- Mix of high-volume + high-value

### 7.2 Risk
- One channel dies = major impact
- Diversify for resilience

---

## 8. Channel ROI Reporting

### 8.1 Weekly
- Volume per channel
- Cost per channel
- Quick check

### 8.2 Monthly
- Full ROI per channel
- Best/worst
- Re-allocation

### 8.3 Quarterly
- Strategy review
- Add/remove channels
- New tests

---

## 9. The Channel Mix

### 9.1 Healthy Mix
- 30% inbound (organic)
- 20% warm outreach
- 20% partner
- 15% content-driven
- 10% events
- 5% press

### 9.2 Skewed Mix (warning)
- 80% one channel = risky
- 0% inbound = no compounding
- 100% paid = unsustainable (we don't use paid)

---

## 10. The Decision Matrix

| ROI | Action |
|-----|--------|
| > 10x | Scale |
| 5-10x | Maintain |
| 1-5x | Optimize |
| < 1x | Stop or rethink |

---

## 11. Channel-Specific ROI

### 11.1 Inbound
- **Strengths:** long-term, compounding
- **Weaknesses:** slow start
- **Optimization:** content, SEO, AEO

### 11.2 Warm Email
- **Strengths:** direct, founder-controlled
- **Weaknesses:** founder time, DMARC risk
- **Optimization:** personalization, list quality

### 11.3 LinkedIn
- **Strengths:** high intent
- **Weaknesses:** high time, low volume
- **Optimization:** niche targeting, content

### 11.4 Partners
- **Strengths:** trust transfer, scale
- **Weaknesses:** margin
- **Optimization:** better partners, more volume

### 11.5 Content
- **Strengths:** long-term, brand
- **Weaknesses:** slow ROI
- **Optimization:** SEO, AEO, distribution

---

## 12. New Channel Test

### 12.1 When
- All current channels saturated
- New ICP reachable
- New budget available
- Founder time available

### 12.2 How
- Small budget
- Short test (30 days)
- Clear metrics
- Stop if not working
