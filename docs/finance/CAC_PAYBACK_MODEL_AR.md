# CAC and Payback Model — نموذج CAC والاسترداد
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كيف نحسب CAC لكل قناة، payback period، ومتى نوقف القناة.

---

## 1. CAC Definition

**CAC = Total Sales & Marketing Spend / New Customers Acquired**

Includes:
- Founder sales time
- Content creation
- Tools (CRM, email, etc.)
- Events
- Partner commission
- Anything that drives acquisition

Excludes:
- CS (post-acquisition)
- Delivery (post-acquisition)
- Founder time on existing clients

---

## 2. Per-Channel CAC

### 2.1 Inbound
- Spend: content creation + SEO tools (5K SAR/mo)
- Customers: 2-5/mo
- CAC: 1,000-2,500 SAR
- Time to customer: 30-60 days
- ROI: high (long-term)

### 2.2 Warm Email
- Spend: founder time (1h/day = 5K SAR/mo)
- Customers: 1-3/mo
- CAC: 1,500-3,000 SAR
- Time to customer: 14-30 days
- ROI: high

### 2.3 LinkedIn (manual)
- Spend: founder time
- Customers: 0.5-1/mo
- CAC: 4,000-8,000 SAR
- Time to customer: 30-60 days
- ROI: medium

### 2.4 Partners (referral)
- Spend: 10-20% commission
- Customers: 1-3/mo
- CAC: 2,000-4,000 SAR
- Time to customer: 30-60 days
- ROI: high (low founder time)

### 2.5 Content (long-term)
- Spend: founder time + tools
- Customers: indirect
- CAC: hard to measure
- ROI: long-term compounding

### 2.6 Events
- Spend: 2-10K SAR/event
- Customers: 0-2/event
- CAC: high
- ROI: medium (relationship)

## 4. Payback Period

### 4.1 Formula
```
Payback (months) = CAC / (Monthly Revenue × Margin)
```

### 4.2 Examples

| Type | CAC | Monthly Rev × Margin | Payback |
|------|-----|----------------------|---------|
| Marketing Agency | 2,000 | 1,250 | 1.6 mo |
| Clinic | 3,000 | 2,300 | 1.3 mo |
| Local SaaS | 5,000 | 9,000 | 0.6 mo |
| Real Estate | 2,500 | 1,000 | 2.5 mo |

### 4.3 Target
- < 6 months: healthy
- < 12 months: acceptable
- > 12 months: warning

## 7. CAC Optimization

### 7.1 Levers
- Better ICP targeting (lower CAC)
- Better qualification (less waste)
- Better conversion (more from same leads)
- Channel mix (highest ROI)
- Founder time efficiency

### 7.2 Trade-offs
- Better targeting = fewer leads
- Better conversion = more time per lead
- Channel mix = risk concentration

---

## 8. CAC Stop Conditions

### 8.1 Stop Channel If
- CAC > 5,000 SAR
- CAC > LTV
- Conversion < 5%
- ROI < 1x
- Spam rate > 0.1%

### 8.2 Pause and Review
- CAC trending up
- Conversion trending down
- Volume dropping
- Time investment high

## 11. The Payback Strategy

### 11.1 Short Payback (< 6 months)
- Best channels
- Best ICPs
- Best offers
- Scale up

### 11.2 Medium Payback (6-12 months)
- Acceptable
- Mix with short payback
- Optimize

### 11.3 Long Payback (> 12 months)
- Review
- Reduce cost or raise price
- Or stop
