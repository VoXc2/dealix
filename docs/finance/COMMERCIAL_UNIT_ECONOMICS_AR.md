# Commercial Unit Economics — اقتصاديات الوحدة التجارية
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** الأرقام الحقيقية وراء كل ريال. Revenue، margin، CAC، LTV، payback. بدون أرقام = تخمين.

---

## 1. The Core Formula

```
LTV = Avg Annual Revenue × Avg Lifespan (years)
CAC = Total Sales & Marketing Spend / New Customers
LTV:CAC = LTV / CAC
Margin = (Price - Cost) / Price
Payback (months) = CAC / (Annual Revenue × Margin / 12)
```

## 4. CAC by Channel

### 4.1 Inbound
- Cost: SEO + content + tools (~5K SAR/mo)
- Leads: ~50/mo
- Cost per lead: 100 SAR
- Conversion: 5%
- Cost per customer: 2,000 SAR

### 4.2 Warm Email
- Cost: founder time (1h/day = 5K SAR/mo)
- Sends: ~1,000/mo
- Replies: ~50 (5%)
- Meetings: ~10 (20% of reply)
- Customers: ~2 (20% of meeting)
- Cost per customer: 2,500 SAR

### 4.3 LinkedIn (manual)
- Cost: founder time
- Connections: ~100/mo
- Messages: ~30
- Replies: ~10
- Meetings: ~3
- Customers: ~1
- Cost per customer: 5,000 SAR

### 4.4 Partners
- Cost: 10-20% commission
- Customers: ~2/mo
- Cost per customer: 2,000-4,000 SAR
- ROI: high (low effort)

### 4.5 Content
- Cost: founder time + tools
- Long-term compounding
- Cost per lead: low
- Conversion: slow

### 4.6 Best Channel
- Inbound + Partners (lowest CAC)
- Warm email (medium CAC)
- LinkedIn (high CAC, low volume)

## 6. Payback Period

### 6.1 Formula
```
Payback (months) = CAC / (Monthly Revenue × Margin)
```

### 6.2 Examples
| Scenario | CAC | Monthly Rev × Margin | Payback |
|----------|-----|----------------------|---------|
| Inbound | 2,000 | 25K × 50% = 12.5K/mo | 0.16 mo |
| Warm email | 2,500 | 25K × 50% = 12.5K/mo | 0.2 mo |
| LinkedIn | 5,000 | 25K × 50% = 12.5K/mo | 0.4 mo |
| Partner | 3,000 | 25K × 50% = 12.5K/mo | 0.24 mo |

### 6.3 Target
- < 6 months (healthy)
- < 12 months (acceptable)
- > 12 months (warning)

## 7. Margin Erosion Risks

### 7.1 Common
- Discount > 15%
- Below margin floor
- Custom at standard price
- Scope creep
- High delivery cost
- High founder time
- High partner commission
- Long cycle time

### 7.2 Mitigation
- Pricing guardrails (PHASE 5)
- Margin floor
- Scope policy
- Capacity planning
- Margin review

## 8. Founder Time Economics

### 8.1 Time as Real Cost
- 1 hour founder = real cost (salary + opportunity)
- If founder's value = 500 SAR/hour, 1h = 500 SAR

### 8.2 Allocation Targets
- Sales: 40%
- Delivery oversight: 20%
- Strategic: 20%
- CS: 10%
- Admin: 10%

### 8.3 Red Flags
- Delivery > 50% → hire
- Sales < 30% → pipeline low
- Strategic < 10% → no time to think
