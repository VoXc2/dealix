# Sales Capacity Model — نموذج السعة البيعية
**Dealix — Agent #3**

> **الغرض:** كم نستطيع أن نبيع ونسلّم؟ ما هي حدود founder time؟ متى نزيد الفريق؟

---

## 1. Capacity = Time

**Sales capacity = عدد الساعات البيعية المتاحة شهرياً.**

founder = 1 person, ~20 days/mo, ~8h/day = 160h/mo
- 40% sales = 64h
- Other: strategy, delivery, CS, admin

**Real sales hours = 50-60h/mo (with overhead)**

---

## 2. Activity Capacity

### 2.1 Discovery Calls
- 1h/call + 30 min prep + 15 min follow-up = 1.75h
- 60h/mo ÷ 1.75h = ~34 calls/mo
- Realistic: 25-30 calls/mo

### 2.2 Proposals
- 4h/proposal (after discovery)
- 25 proposals/mo = 100h
- Realistic: 5-8 proposals/mo

### 2.3 Negotiations
- 2h/negotiation
- 5-8 negotiations/mo = 10-16h

### 2.4 Total Sales Activity
- 30 discovery × 1.75h = 52.5h
- 8 proposals × 4h = 32h
- 8 negotiations × 2h = 16h
- Total: 100.5h

**At 60h available: over capacity!**

---

## 3. Reality Check

### 3.1 If 60h Sales Available
- 25-30 discovery calls
- 5-8 proposals
- 5-8 negotiations
- 2-5 closes (depending on conversion)

### 3.2 If 100h+ Available
- Hire or reduce delivery

### 3.3 If 30h Available
- Cut to highest-ROI activities
- Outsource or hire

---

## 4. Per-ICP Capacity

### 4.1 Wedge (Marketing Agency)
- High volume
- Fast cycle
- 10-20 active opportunities
- 3-5 closes/mo target

### 4.2 Mid-Tier (Clinic, Education, Real Estate, Local SaaS)
- Medium volume
- Medium cycle
- 5-10 active each
- 1-2 closes/mo each

### 4.3 Long Cycle (Custom, Enterprise)
- Low volume
- Long cycle (3-6 months)
- 2-5 active
- 0.5-1 closes/mo

---

## 5. Pipeline Capacity

### 5.1 Healthy Pipeline
- 3x monthly target in qualified
- 1.5x monthly target in proposal
- 1x monthly target in negotiation

### 5.2 Example
- Target: 5 closes/mo
- Qualified needed: 15
- Proposal needed: 8
- Negotiation needed: 5

---

## 6. Delivery Capacity

### 6.1 Founder Delivery Capacity
- 60-100h/mo on delivery
- Per offer:
  - Diagnostic: 5-10h
  - Workflow: 15-25h
  - Starter: 40-60h
  - Full OS: 100-150h
  - Retainer: 8-15h/mo
  - Custom: 200+h

### 6.2 Per Month Capacity (founder)
- Diagnostic: 6-12
- Workflow: 3-4
- Starter: 1-2
- Full OS: 0.5
- Retainer: 5-10 active
- Custom: 0.25

---

## 7. Total Founder Capacity

### 7.1 If 160h/mo Total
- 60h Sales
- 60h Delivery
- 20h CS
- 20h Strategy + admin
- = 160h balanced

### 7.2 If Imbalanced
- Sales > 70% → delivery slipping
- Delivery > 60% → sales slipping
- CS > 30% → client issue
- Admin > 20% → process issue

---

## 8. When to Hire

### 8.1 Hire Sales If
- Sales > 60h/mo sustained
- Pipeline 3x target
- Conversion rate good
- Just need more activity

### 8.2 Hire Delivery If
- Delivery > 80h/mo sustained
- Quality slipping
- Founder time on delivery > 50%
- Quality feedback negative

### 8.3 Hire CS If
- Active retainers > 10
- Health score dropping
- Founder time on CS > 20%

### 8.4 Hire Admin If
- Admin > 20% of time
- Process automation possible
- Cost of hire < cost of time

## 10. The Cost of Capacity

### 10.1 Founder Time Cost
- 1h founder = 250-500 SAR (assumed)
- Use in margin calculation

### 10.2 Hire Cost
- Junior: 8-12K SAR/mo
- Mid: 15-25K SAR/mo
- Senior: 25-40K SAR/mo
- Founder saved time: 1.5-2x salary

## 12. Capacity vs Demand

### 12.1 If Demand > Capacity
- Hire
- Outsource
- Raise price (reduce demand)
- Decline work
- Reduce scope

### 12.2 If Capacity > Demand
- More marketing
- Better funnel
- Hire sales
- New ICP
