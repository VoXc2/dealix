## 1. Why Retainers

- Predictable revenue

## 11. Retainer Pricing Strategy

### 11.1 New Clients
- Within published range
- No discount for negotiation
- Pilot discount (-20%) for 3-month commit

### 11.2 Existing Clients
- Same price (mostly)
- Inflation adjustment (5% max)
- Loyalty discount (5% if long-term)

### 11.3 Discounts Allowed
- Pilot: -20% for 3-month commit
- Annual: -10% for 12-month commit
- Case study: -5% after delivery
- Volume (multi-team): -5-10%

---

## 12. The Recurring Revenue Math

### 12.1 Formula
```
MRR = Active Retainers × Avg Price
ARR = MRR × 12
LTV = MRR × Avg Tenure (months)
```

### 12.2 Example
- 10 retainers × 7,000 SAR avg = 70,000 MRR
- ARR = 840,000
- LTV (12mo) = 840,000
