# Dealix — Founder Unit Economics Model

## الهدف

ربط التسعير والتكلفة والوقت بنتيجة تجارية واضحة حتى لا يتحول المنتج إلى خدمة مخصصة غير قابلة للتوسع.

## 1) Revenue model

| Package | Target | Price range | Delivery |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Diagnostic | first paid step | 3k-10k SAR | 7-14 days |
| 30-day pilot | pipeline build | 15k-45k SAR | 30 days |
| Retainer | ongoing revenue ops | 10k-35k SAR / month | monthly |
| Enterprise | governance + integrations | custom | quarterly |

## 2) Cost model

| Cost | Driver | Control |
|---|---|---|
| LLM/API | runs, enrichment, generation | budget per workflow |
| Data tools | search, maps, enrichment | fallback providers |
| Delivery time | founder/operator hours | templates and automation |
| Infra | Railway, DB, Redis, observability | service health and right sizing |
| Support | customer questions and reviews | onboarding playbooks |

## 3) Target gross margin

| Stage | Target |
|---|---:|
| Diagnostic | 60%+ |
| Pilot | 65%+ |
| Retainer | 75%+ |
| Enterprise | 70%+ |

## 4) Founder decision formula

```text
Expected Value = Deal Size × Close Probability × Strategic Fit - Delivery Risk - Opportunity Cost
```

Only pursue accounts where:

- pain is clear
- decision maker exists
- Saudi B2B need is real
- pilot can show evidence in 30 days
- delivery does not require custom one-off engineering

## 5) KPI targets

| KPI | Early target |
|---|---:|
| Discovery to meeting | 5-10% |
| Meeting to pilot | 20-40% |
| Pilot to retainer | 30-50% |
| Time to first evidence | < 7 days |
| Time to first paid step | < 21 days |

## 6) Red flags

- Customer asks for custom platform before pilot proof.
- No clear owner.
- No access to needed info.
- Requires legal/compliance claims beyond evidence.
- Wants unpaid strategy work without next step.

## 7) Weekly financial review

- New pipeline value.
- Weighted pipeline.
- Closed won/lost.
- Delivery hours per customer.
- Tool cost per workflow.
- Gross margin estimate.
- Next pricing adjustment.
