# Academy Strategy (Ecosystem View)

Academy is not a course catalog. It is **distribution + authority + partner enablement**.

## Launch conditions

- 10+ paid projects
- 3+ proof-backed assets
- Stable Method
- Repeatable templates
- QA rubric
- Governance rules

## Tracks

AI Ops Executive, Revenue AI Operator, Company Brain Builder, AI Governance Lead, Dealix Certified Partner, Dealix Delivery Analyst.

## Discipline

- Method version pinning.
- Annual recertification.
- Certified partners only deliver under Dealix QA.
