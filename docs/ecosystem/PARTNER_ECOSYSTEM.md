# Partner Ecosystem

Six tiers.

1. Referral Partner — refers leads only.
2. Advisory Partner — co-delivers diagnostics/roadmap.
3. Implementation Partner — delivers sprints under Dealix QA.
4. Certified Partner — full delivery for a subset of offers.
5. Strategic Partner — co-go-to-market.
6. White-label Partner — last; highest brand risk.

Match the tier to the partner's discipline, not their enthusiasm.
