# Partner Gate

A partner advances only when:

- Understands the Dealix Method.
- Accepts no unsafe automation.
- Uses Proof Pack.
- Accepts QA review.
- Accepts governance rules.
- Does not make guaranteed claims.
- Accepts audit rights.

## Partner Score — 6 dimensions, total 100

| Dimension | Weight |
| --- | ---: |
| Lead quality | 20 |
| Method alignment | 20 |
| QA compliance | 20 |
| Governance compliance | 20 |
| Client feedback | 10 |
| Expansion potential | 10 |

Tiers: 85+ certified/strategic, 70–84 implementation, 55–69 referral only, <55 reject.
