# Anchor Partner Outreach

## Positioning
Dealix is starting commercially in Saudi Arabia and structuring its doctrine to become GCC-ready.
The goal is not to announce a GCC launch.
The goal is to build a partner-led, evidence-backed path to governed AI operations for regulated and B2B service buyers.

## English Draft
Subject: Governed AI operations for Saudi/GCC clients — partner discussion

Hi [Name],

I'm building Dealix, a governed AI operations company starting with Saudi B2B services and regulated-adjacent buyers.

- runtime governance before client-facing output
- proof of value before claims
- agent identity before agent autonomy

I'd like to explore whether one of your Saudi or GCC clients needs a governed AI operations path that is safer than a generic automation tool and more practical than a long strategy deck.

Would you be open to a 30-minute discussion?
