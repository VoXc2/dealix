# Agency Partner Pitch — عرض الشراكة للوكالات

> Pitch for B2B marketing agencies who could resell or co-deliver Dealix.
> **Hard rule:** Founder-approved warm intros only. No cold partnership outreach.

**Audience:** B2B marketing/growth agencies in KSA.
**Goal:** Establish 3 partnership channels by month 3.

---

## What partners do NOT do

- ❌ Resell Dealix to enterprises (>200 employees) — that's direct
- ❌ White-label without disclosing Dealix on the Proof Pack
- ❌ Promise outcomes Dealix doesn't promise (no "guaranteed leads")
- ❌ Bring Dealix to their cold-outreach list (warm only)

---

## Partnership rubric

Before formal partnership:

| Criterion | Required |
|---|---|
| Established Saudi B2B agency | yes |
| ≥ 1 customer in our 3 wedges | yes |
| Compliance posture aligned (no scraping/cold practices) | **non-negotiable** |
| Founder-to-founder relationship | yes |
| Decision-maker identified | yes |

If any "no" → defer or decline.

## How partners get paid

```
Partner closes Pilot → Dealix invoices customer → customer pays via Moyasar
→ Dealix recognizes revenue → Dealix issues partner invoice (30% × 499 = ~150 SAR)
→ Partner paid within 30 days
```

NOT before customer pays. NOT a referral fee. Aligned commission.
