# Hiring — Customer Success Manager (First Hire) — Dealix

**Status:** DRAFT — activate when 3 paid Sprints + 1 Partner signed
**Owner:** Sami (founder)
**Last updated:** 2026-05-07
**Companion docs:** `docs/sales-kit/dealix_hiring_plan.md` · `docs/CUSTOMER_SUCCESS_SOP.md` · `docs/FOUNDER_DECISION_ESCALATION.md` · Plan §23.5.7

> **Why this doc exists:** First hire is the most-leveraged commitment in a startup. Wrong hire = founder gets pulled back into 100% delivery and Wave 7 caps re-bind. This doc: when to hire, who to hire, what to ask, what to offer.

---

## 1. Hiring trigger (when to start)

ALL of the following must be true:

- ✅ 3 paid Sprint customers (`payment_confirmed` state)
- ✅ 1 paid Partner customer (Month 1 invoiced)
- ✅ Founder hours/customer trending toward >40h cap
- ✅ Cash runway ≥9 months OR confirmed angel-round commitment
- ✅ MRR ≥36K SAR/month run-rate (covers CSM cost + 50% buffer)

If any FALSE → wait. Hiring early = burn cash without scaling impact.

## 2. Job description (publish)

### Title (Arabic + English)

**English:** Customer Success Manager · Saudi B2B SaaS (Founding team, employee #1)
**Arabic:** مسؤول نجاح العميل (CSM) — أوّل موظّف في فريق Dealix

### One-paragraph pitch

> Dealix builds Saudi-PDPL-compliant AI Operating Teams for SMBs. We just shipped Wave 6 (revenue activation) and signed our first paid pilots. We need our first CSM to own customer relationships during the 14-day Sprint and 4-month Partner commitment — keeping NPS ≥50, churn ≤20%, and lifting customers' KPIs through approval-first workflows. You'll work directly with the founder, take ownership of 3-5 customer relationships in your first quarter, and grow with the company as we scale to 30+ customers.

### What you'll own

- Customer onboarding (Day 0-7 of every new Sprint)
- Daily Decisions queue review + draft approval (low-risk only)
- Weekly Pipeline Audit drafts (founder approves before send)
- Weekly Proof Pack assembly from `proof_ledger`
- NPS deployment + categorization (per `CUSTOMER_SUCCESS_SOP.md` §3)
- Renewal call preparation (NOT renewal close — that stays founder)
- Customer support replies (drafts → founder approval, then send)

### What you will NOT own (founder Cat A — see `FOUNDER_DECISION_ESCALATION.md`)

- Pricing decisions
- Refunds > 5,000 SAR
- Contract signing
- Hire/fire of future team members
- Public statements / press

### Required qualifications

- Native Arabic (Saudi or Khaliji dialect strongly preferred)
- Working English (read API docs, technical Slack with founder)
- 3-5 years in B2B SaaS customer success OR Saudi B2B agency account-management
- Demonstrated comfort with WhatsApp Business as primary channel
- Process discipline (you'll own checklists + SLAs)
- Coachability (no-cold-outreach commitment must be genuine, not performative)

### Nice-to-have

- Prior exposure to PDPL / Saudi data privacy
- Familiarity with Salesforce / HubSpot / Zoho CRMs (we use light CRM, not heavy)
- Real-estate, agency, or consulting sector background (Tier 1 customers)
- Saudi network (referrals = expansion vector)

### Compensation (founder-only)

| Component | Range |
|---|---|
| Base salary | 15,000 - 22,000 SAR/month |
| KPI bonus (paid quarterly) | up to 3,000 SAR/quarter, tied to: NPS≥50 + churn≤20% + 1 NPS-promoter testimonial published |
| Equity (after probation passed) | 0.5% - 1.5% (4-year vest, 1-year cliff) |
| Tools + laptop | provided |
| Saudi GOSI + medical | as required by Saudi labor law |

Total OTE year 1: ~200K-280K SAR including bonus.

### Working arrangement

- Saudi-based (Riyadh / Jeddah / Eastern Province acceptable)
- Hybrid: 3 days Saudi co-working / 2 days remote OR fully remote (founder discretion)
- 5 days/week, 8 hours/day
- Saudi-Riyadh timezone (08:00-18:00 working hours)
- 21 days annual leave + Saudi public holidays

### Application process

1. Submit CV + 1-page Saudi-Arabic intro letter (why Dealix?)
2. 30-min screening call with founder
3. Take-home: 1-page customer recovery plan for a hypothetical Detractor (NPS=4)
4. 60-min final interview (founder + advisor)
5. References (2 minimum, must be reachable in Saudi business hours)
6. Offer + 3-month probation start

Total timeline: 2-3 weeks ideal.

## 3. Interview scorecard — 5 dimensions

Use `docs/wave6/live/csm_interview_<date>_<candidate>.md` (gitignored) per candidate.

| Dimension | Score 1-5 | What good looks like | Red flag |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Saudi customer empathy** | _/5 | Has fielded 100+ Saudi B2B customer calls; can role-play "آسف، تأخّرت في الردّ" with grace | Talks like a Western SaaS PM about "users" |
| **Written Arabic quality** | _/5 | Drafts a Pipeline Audit summary in Saudi-dialect Arabic in 15 min, no typos | Defaults to MSA, awkward Khaliji |
| **Process discipline** | _/5 | Comfortable with checklists; describes their last role's CS SOP without prompting | "I prefer to play it by ear" |
| **Coachability + boundary holding** | _/5 | When asked "can you push customer to upgrade now?" they say "no, that's founder's call" | Will do whatever pleases customer / boss |
| **No-cold commitment** | _/5 | Explicitly buys into "no scraping, no cold WhatsApp" — sees it as advantage | Asks "but how do we scale outreach?" |

**Hire threshold:** average ≥4.0 AND no single dimension below 3.

## 4. Take-home assignment (1 page)

Send candidate this brief (anonymize customer):

> Customer "Acme Riyadh Real Estate" (Sprint customer, Day 14) gave NPS=4 and wrote: «الـ Sprint كان مفيد لكن ما حسّيت بفرق كبير في صفقاتي الفعلية — قسم تقني فقط.»
>
> Write a 1-page Saudi-Arabic recovery plan:
> - Same-day response (verbatim WhatsApp message)
> - 7-day follow-up plan
> - Whether you'd recommend refund / extension / Partner
> - One question you'd ask the founder before acting

Score: clarity, customer empathy, no-overpromise. They should NOT promise +20% revenue, NOT offer to do unauthorized actions, NOT blame the customer.

## 6. Day-1 onboarding checklist (first 30 days)

### Week 1 — read + shadow

- Read 5 key docs: Constitution · Wave 7 plan §23 · TURNKEY_PACKAGE · CUSTOMER_SUCCESS_SOP · FOUNDER_DECISION_ESCALATION
- Shadow 3 customer calls (no speaking, just listening)
- Set up tools: WhatsApp Business · `/decisions.html` access · Customer Portal access · Slack with founder
- Sign DPA + NDA + IP assignment

### Week 2 — observe + ask

- Pair with founder on 1 onboarding (new customer Day 0)
- Pair with founder on 1 Pipeline Audit draft
- Pair with founder on 1 NPS follow-up call
- Q&amp;A session: 1h with founder, no taboo questions

### Week 3 — limited ownership

- Own NPS deployment for 1 customer (founder reviews wording)
- Own weekly Proof Pack draft for 1 customer (founder reviews)
- Draft 5 customer support replies (founder approves before send)

### Week 4 — full ownership of 1 customer

- Take ownership of customer #2's daily/weekly cadence (under founder oversight)
- Founder spot-checks weekly, monthly performance review

By end of month 3: own 3-5 customers, founder spot-checks weekly only.

## 7. Failure modes (how to know it's not working)

Within 90 days, if any of these emerge → honest conversation, possibly part ways:

- NPS scores trending down on customers CSM owns
- Customer churn spike on CSM-owned customers
- CSM tries to disable a hard gate ("can we just send this WhatsApp?")
- CSM consistently over-promises to customers
- CSM cannot complete Saudi-Arabic written drafts without founder rewrite
- Founder finds themselves doing CSM's work to "speed it up"

The 3-month probation exists for these reasons. Use it.

---

## 8. Hard rules

- ❌ Never hire CSM before 3 paid Sprints + 1 Partner signed
- ❌ Never grant equity before 90-day probation passed
- ❌ Never delegate Cat A decisions to CSM (per `FOUNDER_DECISION_ESCALATION.md`)
- ❌ Never hire someone who can't write Saudi-Arabic at 4/5 quality
- ❌ Never sign offer without lawyer-reviewed contract
- ✅ Always run a 30-day shadow before granting customer ownership
- ✅ Always reference-check 2 minimum, in Saudi business hours
- ✅ Always document hire decision in Friday review
- ✅ Always include KPI bonus tied to objective metrics, not subjective "feel"
