## 1. Escalation Overview

### 1.1 Escalation Philosophy

- **تصعيد مبكر أفضل من تصعيد متأخر**
- **الشفافية مع العميل أساسية**
- **كل تصعيد فرصة للتعلم**
- **الهدف: الحل، ليس اللوم**

### 1.2 Escalation Levels

```
Level 0: Normal Operations
         └─> CS Lead handles day-to-day

Level 1: CS Lead Escalation
         └─> Complex issues, requires CS Lead attention

Level 2: Founder Escalation
         └─> Strategic issues, client-at-risk

Level 3: Executive Escalation
         └─> Critical situations, executive involvement
```

## 3. Escalation Process

### 3.1 Level 1 Process

```
1. Issue identified by CS Lead
       ↓
2. Document issue in tracking system
       ↓
3. Initial response to client within 4h
       ↓
4. Develop resolution plan
       ↓
5. If not resolved in 48h → escalate to Level 2
```

### 3.2 Level 2 Process

```
1. Founder notified by CS Lead
       ↓
2. Founder reviews issue documentation
       ↓
3. Founder determines action plan
       ↓
4. Founder communicates with client (if needed)
       ↓
5. CS Lead executes resolution plan
       ↓
6. Weekly follow-up until resolved
       ↓
7. If not resolved in 7 days → escalate to Level 3
```

### 3.3 Level 3 Process

```
1. Founder escalates to Executive
       ↓
2. Executive reviews full history
       ↓
3. Executive-to-executive call scheduled
       ↓
4. Custom resolution plan developed
       ↓
5. Enhanced monitoring implemented
       ↓
6. Daily check-ins until resolved
```

## 4. Issue Categories and Responses

### 4.1 Communication Issues

| Issue | Response | Owner |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Slow response | Proactive outreach | CS Lead |
| No response | Multi-channel follow-up | CS Lead |
| Miscommunication | Clarify in writing | CS Lead |
| Expectation mismatch | Re-align, document | CS Lead |
| Stakeholder conflict | Identify decision maker | CS Lead |

### 4.2 Technical Issues

| Issue | Response | Owner |
|-------|----------|-------|
| System bug | Log, diagnose, fix | Tech Lead |
| Integration failure | Debug, reconnect | Tech Lead |
| Performance issue | Optimize, monitor | Tech Lead |
| Data quality | Clean, validate | CS Lead + Tech |

### 4.3 Delivery Issues

| Issue | Response | Owner |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Timeline delay | Assess impact, communicate | Delivery Lead |
| Quality concern | QA review, fix | Delivery Lead |
| Scope creep | Document, get approval | CS Lead |
| Resource constraint | Re-prioritize, hire | Founder |

### 4.4 Business Issues

| Issue | Response | Owner |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Budget concern | Review contract, negotiate | Founder |
| Strategy change | Re-assess fit, adapt | CS Lead + Founder |
| Competitor switch | Value reinforcement | CS Lead + Founder |
| Acquisition/Merger | Assess new entity | Founder |
| Bankruptcy | Exit plan | Founder |

## 6. Escalation Metrics

### 6.1 Tracking Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Level 1 escalations | <5 per month | Count |
| Level 2 escalations | <2 per month | Count |
| Level 3 escalations | 0 | Count |
| Average resolution time (L1) | <48 hours | Hours |
| Average resolution time (L2) | <5 days | Days |
| Average resolution time (L3) | <7 days | Days |
| Escalation to renewal ratio | <20% | Percentage |

### 6.2 Post-Escalation Review

After each escalation:
1. Document what happened
2. Identify root cause
3. Determine preventive measures
4. Update processes if needed
5. Share learnings with team

## 7. Prevention Strategies

### 7.1 Early Warning Signs

| Sign | Action |
|------|--------|
| Declining engagement | Increase touchpoints |
| Missed meetings | Proactive rescheduling |
| Feedback gaps | Request feedback |
| Change in contacts | Re-establish relationships |
| Budget scrutiny | Demonstrate ROI |
| Competitor mentions | Value reinforcement |

### 7.2 Proactive Measures

- Weekly health score monitoring
- Regular client check-ins
- Clear communication cadence
- Documented expectations
- Early issue identification
- Quick response to concerns

## 8. Recovery Plans

### 8.1 At-Risk Recovery

```
1. Schedule call within 48h
       ↓
2. Acknowledge issues honestly
       ↓
3. Co-create improvement plan
       ↓
4. Execute with enhanced monitoring
       ↓
5. Weekly updates until healthy
```

### 8.2 Blocked Recovery

```
1. Founder involvement immediately
       ↓
2. Executive-to-executive call
       ↓
3. Custom recovery plan
       ↓
4. Enhanced support resources
       ↓
5. Daily monitoring
       ↓
6. Bi-weekly executive updates
```

### 8.3 Churn Risk Recovery

```
1. Executive involvement
       ↓
2. Root cause analysis
       ↓
3. Executive sponsor outreach
       ↓
4. Retention offer (if appropriate)
       ↓
5. Survival plan with commitments
       ↓
6. 30-day probation period
       ↓
7. Decision: renewed or graceful exit
```
