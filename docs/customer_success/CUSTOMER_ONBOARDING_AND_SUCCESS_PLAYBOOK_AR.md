# Dealix — Customer Onboarding & Success Playbook

## الهدف

تحويل كل عميل من تجربة أولى إلى قيمة مثبتة خلال 30 يوم، ثم إلى retained customer.

## Day 0 — قبل البداية

- تحديد decision maker.
- تحديد ICP.
- تحديد success metric.
- تحديد channels المسموحة.
- تحديد بيانات مسموح استخدامها.
- تحديد approval owner.

## Week 1 — Discovery and setup

Deliverables:

- ICP definition.
- target account criteria.
- messaging constraints.
- data sources.
- risk and compliance notes.

Success evidence:

- signed scope.
- approved ICP.
- initial account list.

## Week 2 — Pipeline build

Deliverables:

- enriched account list.
- lead scoring.
- outreach messaging.
- objections map.

Success evidence:

- scorecard.
- approved outreach pack.
- first outreach batch ready.

## Week 3 — Execution and feedback

Deliverables:

- response tracking.
- prioritization.
- follow-up drafts.
- weekly founder memo.

Success evidence:

- replies.
- meetings.
- blocked reasons.
- lessons learned.

## Week 4 — Decision point

Deliverables:

- pilot recap.
- ROI/progress summary.
- next 30-day plan.
- retain/expand/stop recommendation.

Success evidence:

- customer decision.
- case study material if allowed.
- expansion opportunity.

## Health score

| Signal | Green | Yellow | Red |
|---|---|---|---|
| ICP clarity | approved | partial | unclear |
| data access | enough | limited | blocked |
| feedback cadence | weekly | irregular | none |
| pipeline movement | measurable | weak | none |
| approval owner | assigned | unclear | absent |

## Escalation rules

- No feedback for 7 days: founder escalation.
- No ICP approval: pause outreach.
- Compliance concern: require approval before action.
- No measurable value by day 21: run rescue plan.

## Renewal question

"Based on the evidence from the last 30 days, is the next best step to expand, refine, or stop?"
