# Capacity Ledger

Weekly view: **load vs limit**. Prevents silent quality burn.

| Week | Active projects | Units used | Max units | Risk |
|------|-----------------|-----------:|----------:|------|
| W– | Lead Sprint A, Quick Win B | 4 | 5 | Low |
| W– | Brain C, Lead D | 6 | 5 | **Over** — fix |

## If capacity > 100%

- Do **not** accept a new standard sprint without a decision row in [`DECISION_LEDGER.md`](DECISION_LEDGER.md)  
- **Raise price** for marginal deals, **extend** dates, or add **contractor** with same workbench rules  
- Never silently compress QA ([`STOP_DOING.md`](../company/STOP_DOING.md))

**Model:** [`DELIVERY_CAPACITY_MODEL.md`](../company/DELIVERY_CAPACITY_MODEL.md).
