# Business Unit Command

Every Business Unit has a **Scorecard** that the Command Center reads at the same cadence as the firm-level views.

## 1. Dealix Revenue

- **Primary Offer:** Revenue Intelligence Sprint
- **Recurring Offer:** Monthly RevOps OS
- **Product Module:** Revenue OS
- **Proof Type:** Revenue Proof Pack
- **KPIs:**
  - accounts scored
  - pipeline value
  - draft packs approved
  - retainer conversion

## 2. Dealix Brain

- **Primary Offer:** Company Brain Sprint
- **Recurring Offer:** Monthly Company Brain
- **Product Module:** Knowledge OS
- **Proof Type:** Knowledge Proof Pack
- **KPIs:**
  - docs indexed
  - citation coverage
  - insufficient-evidence rate
  - knowledge gaps closed

## 3. Dealix Governance

- **Primary Offer:** AI Governance Review
- **Recurring Offer:** Monthly Governance
- **Product Module:** Governance Runtime
- **Proof Type:** Risk Proof Pack
- **KPIs:**
  - rules created
  - risky actions blocked
  - approvals logged
  - audit coverage

## 4. Dealix Operations

- **Primary Offer:** AI Quick Win Sprint
- **Recurring Offer:** Monthly AI Ops
- **Product Module:** Workflow OS
- **Proof Type:** Time/Quality Proof Pack
- **KPIs:**
  - hours saved
  - workflows created
  - approvals completed
  - error reduction

## 5. Scorecard rules

- A scorecard without a named owner is rejected.
- KPIs are versioned alongside the Dealix Method version.
- Cross-BU comparisons use composite scores, not raw KPIs.
- Red KPIs surface as decisions in the Command Center, not as “alerts.”

## 6. Cadence

- **Weekly** — internal scorecard review.
- **Monthly** — BU-level executive readout.
- **Quarterly** — promotion / kill review for offers and features.

## 7. Failure modes

- Scorecards becoming KPI piles.
- Letting a BU run “special” offers that bypass the productized stack.
- Hero metrics that do not reflect customer health.
- A BU that ignores governance KPIs because they live in another BU.

## 8. The principle

> A Business Unit without a scorecard is not a Business Unit. It is a wish.
