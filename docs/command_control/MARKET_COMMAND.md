# Market Command

Measure **market power**, not sales velocity alone. Market Command is the operating layer that decides when to invest in distribution.

## 1. Market Power Metrics

- Category language adoption
- Inbound diagnostic requests
- Partner referrals
- Proof Pack requests
- Enterprise trust inquiries
- Benchmark downloads
- Academy waitlist
- Competitor copy signals

## 2. Interpretation

- **Proof Pack requests rising** — the trust product is working.
- **Capability Score requests rising** — category language is spreading.
- **Partner certifications requested** — distribution is forming.
- **Enterprise trust inquiries rising** — enterprise readiness is recognized.
- **Competitor copy signals rising** — the category is being shaped.

## 3. Escalation rule

Escalate market spend only when at least three indicators are sustained for two consecutive quarters. This is enforced by code in `command_control_os/control_metrics.py` and `endgame_os/market_power.py`.

## 4. Anti-vanity discipline

- Follower counts, likes, and impressions do **not** enter Market Command.
- Awards not tied to proof do not enter Market Command.
- Press hits without conversion to a Diagnostic do not enter Market Command.

## 5. Channels

- Founder voice — long-form, proof-anchored.
- Arabic-first content — strategic, not translated.
- Benchmarks — quarterly sector data based on Proof Library.
- Briefings — small-group sessions with senior buyers.
- Stage — national events as distribution moments.

## 6. Content discipline

- Every claim sources to a Proof Pack ID.
- Every benchmark sources to a dataset Dealix owns or licenses.
- Every case asset is gated by consent and evidence level.
- Every Arabic piece has an Arabic-native voice review.

## 7. Operating cadence

- **Monthly:** founder essay tied to one proof.
- **Quarterly:** sector benchmark report.
- **Quarterly:** executive briefing series.
- **Annually:** State of Governed AI Operations report.

## 8. Failure modes

- Treating content as media instead of a sales motion.
- Optimizing for reach over Capability Diagnostic conversion.
- Sponsoring events without measuring inbound impact.
- Publishing claims that exceed the underlying evidence level.

## 9. The principle

> Buyers, partners, and regulators all using Dealix language is the only durable form of market power.
