# Delivery FAQ

## When is a project “closed”?

When **Project Definition of Done** in [`DEFINITION_OF_DONE.md`](../company/DEFINITION_OF_DONE.md) is met — including `POST_PROJECT_REVIEW.md`.

## Who updates `DELIVERY_COMMAND.md`?

Delivery owner, at least twice weekly during active work.

## What if QA fails on Arabic?

Fix or escalate; do not ship “good enough” for client-facing AR without reviewer sign-off.

## Where do templates live?

`docs/templates/` (canonical) and `docs/assets/templates/` (library copies / examples).
