# Governance FAQ

## Can we scrape leads for the client?

Only with **explicit permission** and legal review. Default: **client-provided** data only.

## Can an LLM send email for us?

**No** without productized approval workflow. Draft-only unless explicitly approved.

## What is logged?

See `docs/governance/AUDIT_LOG_POLICY.md` — correlate decisions to human approvers where required.
