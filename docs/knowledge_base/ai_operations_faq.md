# AI Operations FAQ

## What if client data is messy?

Start with **Data Readiness** or **Lead Intelligence Sprint**. Do not promise full automation before a data review and scope boundary.

## What if the client asks for WhatsApp automation?

Reject **cold** WhatsApp. Allow **draft creation** and **consent-based** sends with explicit human approval per `APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`.

## What if Company Brain has no source?

Return **insufficient evidence**. Do not invent. See `no source = no answer` in [`DEALIX_STANDARD.md`](../company/DEALIX_STANDARD.md).

## What if the client wants guaranteed meetings or revenue?

Refuse as a guarantee. Offer **measurable process outputs** (ranked list, drafts, proof pack) tied to their inputs.

## What if PII appears in exports?

Stop, redact, log in `governance_events.md`, and re-export with lawful basis documented.
