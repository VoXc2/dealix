# Proof-to-Retainer System

Every Proof Pack ends with exactly one of three motions: `Continue`, `Expand`, or `Pause`. The Proof-to-Retainer System is the discipline that connects the Proof Pack to the firm's recurring revenue.

## 1. The three motions

- **Continue** — same capability, recurring contract.
- **Expand** — adjacent capability, new sprint.
- **Pause** — value not yet sufficient, or client not yet ready.

## 2. The Retainer Gate

A retainer is offered only when **all** of the following hold:

- `proof_score >= 80`
- `client_health >= 70`
- Workflow is recurring (not a one-off task)
- Monthly value is clear and stated
- A stakeholder is actively engaged

## 3. Retainer mapping by proof type

| Strong proof type | Recommended retainer |
| --- | --- |
| Revenue Proof | Monthly RevOps OS |
| Knowledge Proof | Monthly Company Brain |
| Risk Proof | Monthly Governance |
| Time / Quality Proof | Monthly AI Ops |

## 4. Expansion mapping

| Trigger | Expansion sprint |
| --- | --- |
| Revenue proof, scattered reporting | Reporting follow-on |
| Revenue proof, scattered knowledge | Company Brain Sprint |
| Risk proof, unclear approvals | Governance Review |
| Time proof, manual workflows | Workflow OS Sprint |

## 5. Pause discipline

A `Pause` is **not** "we'll see." It is:

- A written reason in the Decision Log.
- A specific re-engagement trigger (e.g., "when the client appoints a workflow owner").
- A time window (e.g., 90 days) after which the pause is reviewed.

## 6. Operating discipline

- Retainer offers are made from the Proof Pack, not from a follow-up call.
- The Capital Ledger captures the proof and retainer linkage.
- The Intelligence OS reads the gate per engagement and flags violations.
- A retainer offered without passing the gate is logged as an exception with the founder's sign-off.

## 7. Anti-patterns

- Pushing a retainer when proof is weak.
- "Continue" decisions when the workflow is one-off.
- Pause decisions that drift without a re-engagement trigger.
- Expansion offers without the underlying proof.

## 8. The principle

> The Proof Pack tells the client what changed. The Retainer Gate decides whether the firm earns the right to keep changing things.
