# Dealix Operating Identity

## 1. Public identity

- **EN:** Dealix is a Saudi Governed AI Operations Company that turns business chaos into measurable, governed, AI-assisted operating capabilities.
- **AR:** Dealix شركة سعودية لتشغيل AI المحكوم، تحوّل الفوضى داخل الشركات إلى قدرات تشغيلية مدعومة بالذكاء الاصطناعي، قابلة للقياس، ومثبتة بالأثر.

## 2. What Dealix is **not**

- AI agency
- Automation agency
- Lead-generation tool
- Chatbot builder
- Consulting firm

These categories underprice the firm and lock it into a labor business.

## 3. What Dealix **is**

- Governed AI Operations Company
- AI Operating Capability Builder
- Saudi AI Operations OS

These categories describe an operating company that owns its method, governance, proof, and capital — not a service vendor.

## 4. How to use this identity

- Every external statement (deck, contract, proposal, podcast) uses the public identity verbatim.
- Internal communication may refer to the firm by any short name, but never relabels Dealix as something it is not.
- Hiring, partnering, and contracting decisions are screened against the identity.

## 5. Anti-patterns

- "We're an AI agency that also does ..."
- "We're a Saudi consulting firm specializing in AI."
- "We build chatbots and automation."
- "We're a lead-gen platform."

Each of these signals a quiet identity drift that costs pricing power within a quarter.

## 6. The principle

> Identity is a strategic constraint. Drift in identity is drift in pricing, hiring, and offers.
