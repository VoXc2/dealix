# Dealix Ultimate Operating Manual

> Dealix is a Saudi **Governed AI Operations** company that turns business chaos into measurable, governed, AI-assisted operating capabilities.
>
> بالعربي: Dealix شركة سعودية لتشغيل AI المحكوم، تحوّل الفوضى داخل الشركات إلى قدرات تشغيلية مدعومة بالذكاء الاصطناعي، قابلة للقياس، ومثبتة بالأثر.

This document is the **capstone** of the Dealix doctrine. It does not replace the earlier strategic layers — it unifies them into one operating manual that governs every decision: what we sell, how we deliver, how we prove, how we accumulate assets, how we refuse chaos, and how we become an operating holding company.

## 1. Operating identity

Dealix is **not**:

- an AI agency
- an automation agency
- a lead-generation tool
- a chatbot builder
- a consulting firm

Dealix **is**:

- a Governed AI Operations Company
- an AI Operating Capability Builder
- the Saudi AI Operations OS

See `DEALIX_OPERATING_IDENTITY.md`.

## 2. Core belief

> AI alone does not create value. AI creates value when it is attached to: data, workflow, human approval, governance, QA, proof, and operating cadence.

See `DEALIX_CORE_BELIEF.md`.

## 3. The operating equation

```
Business Chaos
→ Capability Diagnosis
→ Productized Sprint
→ Governed AI Workflow
→ QA
→ Proof Pack
→ Retainer
→ Capital Asset
→ Product Module
→ Business Unit
→ Standard
→ Partner / Academy
→ Venture
→ Holding Company
```

This is not a roadmap. It is **the transformation chain inside Dealix**. Engagements that fall outside this chain become weak custom work.

## 4. The Dealix Decision Rule

Every decision passes through six questions:

1. Does it sell?
2. Does it deliver?
3. Does it prove?
4. Does it govern?
5. Does it compound?
6. Does it scale?

Scoring:

| Yeses | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| 0–2 | Do not act |
| 3 | Act with caution |
| 4 | Priority |
| 5–6 | Strategic bet |

See `DEALIX_DECISION_RULE.md`.

## 5. Non-negotiables

- No scraping systems.
- No cold WhatsApp automation.
- No LinkedIn automation.
- No fake proof.
- No guaranteed sales claims.
- No PII in logs.
- No source-less knowledge answers.
- No external action without approval.
- No AI output without governance status.
- No project close without a Proof Pack.
- No project without a capital asset.

See `DEALIX_NON_NEGOTIABLES.md`.

## 6. The strategic wedge

The first wedge remains the **Revenue Intelligence Sprint**. Closest to money, easy to explain, fast Proof Pack, opens the Monthly RevOps OS retainer, and exercises `data_os`, `revenue_os`, and `reporting_os` in one engagement.

## 7. The offer stack

```
1. Capability Diagnostic
2. Revenue Intelligence Sprint
3. AI Governance Review
4. Company Brain Sprint
5. AI Quick Win Sprint
6. Monthly RevOps OS
7. Monthly Company Brain
8. Monthly AI Governance
9. Governed AI Operations Program
```

Start with offers 1–3. Expand only on proof.

## 8. Proof economy and the retainer gate

Every engagement ends with one of `Continue`, `Expand`, `Pause`. A retainer is offered only when:

- `proof_score >= 80`
- `client_health >= 70`
- Workflow is recurring
- Monthly value is clear
- A stakeholder is engaged

See `PROOF_TO_RETAINER_SYSTEM.md`.

## 9. Capital and productization

Every project leaves at least:

- 1 Trust Asset
- 1 Product or Knowledge Asset
- 1 Expansion Path

A feature is built only when `manual_step_repeated >= 3`, `time_cost >= 2 hours/project`, the path is tied to a paid offer, it reduces risk or improves margin, and it is testable and reusable.

## 10. Intelligence and decisions

`intelligence_os` reads events, ledgers, metrics, proof packs, client health, sales notes, governance events, delivery results, and partner signals. It emits structured decisions: `SCALE`, `BUILD`, `PILOT`, `HOLD`, `KILL`, `RAISE_PRICE`, `PRODUCTIZE`, `OFFER_RETAINER`, `CREATE_BUSINESS_UNIT`, `CREATE_VENTURE_CANDIDATE`.

## 11. The kill system

Stop weak services, features, markets, and ventures before they erode the firm. Kill is preferred to drift.
