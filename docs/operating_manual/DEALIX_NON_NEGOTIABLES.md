# Dealix Non-Negotiables

These rules do not change. They make Dealix premium, not slow.

## 1. The list

- No scraping systems.
- No cold WhatsApp automation.
- No LinkedIn automation.
- No fake proof.
- No guaranteed sales claims.
- No PII in logs.
- No source-less knowledge answers.
- No external action without approval.
- No AI output without governance status.
- No project close without a Proof Pack.
- No project without a capital asset.

- لا مشروع بلا أصل متراكم.

## 3. Why this list exists

- It protects clients from a class of failures that compound.
- It protects the firm from a class of trust losses that take years to repay.
- It signals to senior buyers that the firm operates a real boundary.
- It makes refusal a built-in feature, not a friction.

## 4. Operating discipline

- A request that violates the list is refused in writing, with the rule cited.
- Refusals are logged in the Decision Log as evidence of the firm's posture.
- A partner who violates the list is paused, not warned.
- An employee who violates the list is corrected through coaching; a repeated violation is treated as a structural problem in the engagement, not as a personal failure.

## 5. Anti-patterns

- "Just-this-once" exceptions.
- Partners selling under the Dealix brand outside the list.
- Marketing copy that drifts close to a forbidden practice.
- Treating the list as advisory.

## 6. The principle

> The list is a feature, not a constraint. It is what makes Dealix trustworthy at scale.
