## 1. The funnel — 1 picture

```
STAGE                    TARGET RATE        WEEK 1 GOAL    WEEK 4 GOAL
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Warm intros sent           100%              5              15
Replied (within 48h)       60%               3              9
Diagnostic call booked     50% of replies    1-2            4-5
Diagnostic completed       80% of booked     1              3-4
Diagnostic signed          40% of completed  0-1            1-2 paid
Sprint / Retainer upsell   80% of Diagnostic 0              1
```

**Interpretation:** 15 warm intros → 9 replies → 4-5 diagnostic calls → 3-4 completed → 1-2 paid 7-Day Diagnostics → 1 Sprint/Retainer upsell. This is the math behind §23.4 calendar. Pricing: `docs/OFFER_LADDER_AND_PRICING.md`.

If conversion drops below the target rate at any stage, **fix that stage before sending more leads**. More volume on a leaky funnel = more burnout, no revenue.

## 2. ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) — Wave 7 Tier 1

Pick warm intros that match **all** of these:

- ✅ Saudi-based B2B owner (real-estate office / B2B agency / B2B services / consulting / training)
- ✅ Has 5-30 employees OR 1-3 partners with shared sales process
- ✅ Already does B2B sales via WhatsApp + email (not pure walk-in)
- ✅ Has a CRM/Excel/Sheet of leads, even if messy
- ✅ Knows the founder personally OR has 1 mutual contact (NOT cold)
- ✅ Decision-maker is on the call (no "let me ask my partner" block)
- ✅ Can pay the 7-Day Diagnostic fee (Starter from 4,999 SAR) within the month (not "let me wait for next quarter")

**Disqualifiers (decline politely):**

- ❌ B2C only (Dealix is B2B-shaped)
- ❌ Government contracts only (PDPL + procurement complexity, defer)
- ❌ Asks for "white-label" or "ownership of the AI" (not a fit for Wave 7)
- ❌ Wants WhatsApp automation for cold outreach (kept blocked by NO_COLD_WHATSAPP)
- ❌ Industry that requires sectoral compliance we don't cover yet (healthcare patient data, fintech regulated entities)

## 4. Qualifying questions (top 5, run during diagnostic call)

Use these in the 30-min diagnostic call (free). Take notes verbatim — these go into `docs/V14_CUSTOMER_SIGNAL_SYNTHESIS.md` later.

1. **«وش أكثر ٣ قرارات إيرادات تهمك هذا الأسبوع؟»** — surfaces real pain
2. **«كيف تتابع leads واتساب اليوم؟ كم وقت تأخذ متابعة كل lead؟»** — surfaces operational burn
3. **«وش أحدث صفقة كبيرة فقدتها؟ ليش؟»** — surfaces measurable opportunity cost
4. **«هل عندك ICP محدّد ولا نشتغل عليه أوّل ١٤ يوم؟»** — surfaces readiness
5. **«التشخيص في ٧ أيّام يبدأ من ٤٬٩٩٩ ريال (شريحة Starter) — يناسبك؟»** — surfaces buying capacity (LITMUS)

If question 5 → "نحتاج نراجع المالية" or "ممكن بعدين" → not Diagnostic-ready. Park for follow-up in 30 days.

## 6. Close cadence — Day 0 / Day 1 / Day 3 / Day 7

After diagnostic call:

| Day | Action | Channel | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 0 (call day) | Within 1 hour: log outcome + send brief | WhatsApp text + Sprint Brief PDF | ≤30 min |
| Day 1 | Personal voice note: "How did the brief land? Any questions?" | WhatsApp voice | ≤2 min |
| Day 3 | Decision check-in: "Should we start Sprint Monday?" | WhatsApp text | ≤1 min |
| Day 7 | Final close OR park: "OK to follow up in 30 days?" | WhatsApp text | ≤1 min |

**Rule:** never more than 4 touches in 7 days. If no signal by Day 7, park for 30 days.

## 9. Hard rules during sales

- ❌ Never claim guaranteed revenue. Use "KPI commitment" framing always.
- ❌ Never promise WhatsApp automation. Manual + draft only.
- ❌ Never invent benchmark numbers. Quote sources or say "we don't have data on this yet."
- ❌ Never accept payment before DPA is lawyer-signed (Wave 7 §23.5.5).
- ❌ Never break the 3-customer cap in Days 1-30.
- ✅ Always offer 100% Sprint refund within 14 days, no questions.
- ✅ Always log every demo outcome same day.
- ✅ Always send the Sprint Brief PDF within 1 hour of "yes interest."
- ✅ Always cc yourself on important emails for retention.

## 10. Weekly Friday review — 30 minutes

Every Friday end-of-day:

1. Open `docs/wave6/live/demo_outcomes.jsonl` — read past 7 days
2. Count: warm intros sent · replies · demos done · paid · follow-ups
3. Compare to §1 funnel targets — any stage below 50% of target?
4. If yes: identify single bottleneck, write 1-line fix on `docs/wave6/live/sales_review.md` (gitignored)
5. Plan next 7 days: top 3 outreach + 1 demo + customer-success blocks

This is the only meeting Sami has with Sami all week. Don't skip it.
