# Road to Enterprise AI OS

## Step 1: Diagnostic

Assess capabilities, data, risk.

## Step 2: First Sprint

Build first proof.

## Step 3: Pilot

Operate workflow for 30 days.

## Step 4: Managed OS

Run monthly improvement.

## Step 5: Expand Capabilities

Add support, knowledge, operations, governance.

## Step 6: Enterprise Workspace

Centralize workflows, approvals, reports, and governance.

## Rule

Enterprise is not the opening pitch. Enterprise is the result of maturity.
