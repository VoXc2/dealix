# AI Agent Governance Council

## Purpose

Govern AI agents, workflows, risks, and value across client environments.

## Internal roles

- Business Owner  
- Delivery Owner  
- Technical Owner  
- Governance Reviewer  
- QA Reviewer  
- Client Success Owner  

## Responsibilities

- approve new agents  
- classify autonomy level  
- approve data access  
- monitor agent behavior  
- review incidents  
- retire redundant agents  
- approve enterprise deployments  

## Meeting cadence

- weekly for active enterprise clients  
- monthly for internal governance
