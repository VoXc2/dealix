# Enterprise Governance Layer

Dealix enterprise readiness requires governance across:

1. **People**  
   Who owns workflows, approvals, and decisions?

2. **Processes**  
   Which workflows are AI-assisted, human-approved, or blocked?

3. **Data**  
   What information can AI access?

4. **Agents**  
   What agents exist, what can they do, and who owns them?

5. **Actions**  
   What actions can AI recommend, queue, or execute?

6. **Risk**  
   What must be reviewed, logged, blocked, or escalated?

7. **Value**  
   What business value is being proven?

---

## Why this layer?

Large buyers ask not only “does it work?” but also:

- Who owns this workflow?  
- Who approved this action?  
- What data entered the model?  
- Was there PII?  
- Can we roll back mistakes?  
- What is the audit trail?  
- What did AI cost?  
- What proof shows value was realized?  

Solid answers move Dealix above typical AI vendors.
