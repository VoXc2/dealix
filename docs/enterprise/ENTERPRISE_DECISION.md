# Enterprise Decision

**Do not sell Enterprise AI OS** until operational prerequisites are met — customization and governance load will overwhelm an immature operating system.

## Requirements (all before “yes”)

- [ ] **10+** completed paid projects (meaningful variety, not trivial)
- [ ] **3+** retainers active or **completed** successfully
- [ ] **Zero major** governance incidents (unresolved)
- [ ] **Audit logs** available per policy ([`AUDIT_LOG_POLICY.md`](../governance/AUDIT_LOG_POLICY.md))
- [ ] **Role / permissions** model designed (not ad-hoc admin passwords)
- [ ] **Support** process exists (routing, severity, response expectation)
- [ ] **SLA template** exists (even if v1)
- [ ] **Data handling** policy path with customer (DPA / processing terms as applicable)
- [ ] **Project / delivery** capacity (you or PM) for multi-workstream rollout

Related: [`ENTERPRISE_READINESS.md`](ENTERPRISE_READINESS.md), [`../company/DO_NOT_SELL_YET.md`](../company/DO_NOT_SELL_YET.md).

## Why

Enterprise without readiness dies from **meetings**, **custom edge cases**, **security review**, and **permission complexity** — protect margin and reputation.
