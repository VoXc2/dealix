## القسم 12 — The 10-Year Test

For any major decision, ask:
1. **"Will this matter in 10 years?"**
2. **"Does this build the moat or burn it?"**
3. **"Would I be proud of this in 2035?"**
4. **"Is this consistent with our values?"**
5. **"Does this compound or just add?"**

If 4/5 = YES, do it.
If 2/5 = NO, don't do it.
Otherwise, sleep on it.
