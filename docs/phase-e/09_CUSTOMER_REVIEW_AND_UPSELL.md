# Customer Review + Upsell

After Day 7, the founder runs a 30-minute review call with the
customer. This is the moment for honest feedback **and** a clear
next-step offer.

## Pre-call prep (30 min)

- Re-read the Proof Pack
- Note 2 wins + 1 limitation (be honest about the limitation)
- Have the upsell options ready (see below)
- Have the refund process ready in case the customer is unhappy

## Call agenda (30 min)

1. **5 min** — what was delivered (read summary section out loud)
2. **10 min** — customer feedback per opportunity
3. **5 min** — pack review: what's safe to publish, what's internal,
   what needs revision
4. **10 min** — next-step decision

## Next-step options (customer chooses)

| Option | What it means | Pricing |
|---|---|---|
| `refund_and_close` | Pilot didn't fit; full refund | -499 SAR |
| `pause_and_reflect` | Customer wants 2 weeks before deciding | hold |
| `extend_pilot` | Run another 7 days at same scope | +499 SAR |
| `upgrade_to_custom` | Custom monthly engagement (TBD) | quote-only, NOT a fixed tier |

## Hard rules — re-asserted

- ❌ NEVER push a public mention without signed permission
- ❌ NEVER claim revenue / ROI in the call without customer-supplied
  metrics
- ❌ NEVER lock the customer into a long-term contract
- ❌ NEVER add a `live_charge_subscription` flow
- ✅ Always offer the refund as a real option
- ✅ Always treat the customer's data + name as `internal_only`
  unless they explicitly opt in
- ✅ Always log the outcome in the founder's private notes (NEVER
  in this repo)

## Upsell ladder (sequenced, never skipped)

The upsell ladder is:
1. Pilot → Extended Pilot (only if customer asks)
2. Extended Pilot → Custom (only if ≥ 3 archived proof events from
   this customer alone, OR customer explicitly requests custom scope)
3. Custom → Multi-month (only if ≥ 6 months of delivery data)

Skipping a rung breaks the trust + the unit economics. NEVER
go directly from Pilot → Custom on the first review call.
