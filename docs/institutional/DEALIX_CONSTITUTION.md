## Identity

Dealix is a **Saudi Governed AI Operations Holding Company**.

## Mission

Turn business chaos into governed AI operating capabilities with proof of value.

## Core Beliefs

1. AI without workflow is noise.
2. Workflow without governance is risk.
3. Delivery without proof is incomplete.
4. Projects without capital creation are not strategic.
5. Saudi Arabic quality is a moat.
6. No source, no trust.
7. No approval, no external action.
8. No proof, no claim.
9. No repeated demand, no product feature.
10. No governance, no scale.

## Non-Negotiables

- No scraping systems.
- No cold WhatsApp automation.
- No LinkedIn automation.
- No fake proof.
- No guaranteed outcome claims.
- No PII in logs.
- No source-less knowledge answers.
- No client-facing AI output without QA.
