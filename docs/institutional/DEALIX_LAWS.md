# Dealix Laws — قوانين التشغيل

**تحت الدستور:** قوانين تنفيذية تمنع التشتت. **الكود المرجعي:** `auto_client_acquisition/institutional_os/laws.py`.

---

## Law 1: The Proof Law

Every client project must create a **Proof Pack**. لا مشروع مكتمل بلا proof.

## Law 2: The Capital Law

Every project must create **at least one reusable capital asset** (template · playbook · module · benchmark · case-safe insight · sales script · governance rule · QA checklist · …).

## Law 3: The Governance Law

Every AI output must have a **governance status** (مسار موافقة / مستوى خطر / تدقيق حسب التصميم).

## Law 4: The Productization Law

Every **repeated manual step** becomes a productization candidate (تسجيل في الـ ledger).

## Law 5: The Retainer Law

Every Proof Pack must recommend **Continue**, **Expand**, or **Pause**.

## Law 6: The Focus Law

No new business line without a **strong wedge**, **proof**, and **repeatability**.

## Law 7: The Kill Law

Anything that does not **sell**, **prove**, **govern**, **compound**, or **scale** must be **stopped**.
