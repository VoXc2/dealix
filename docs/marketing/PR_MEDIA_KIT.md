## Key Talking Points (for journalist interviews)

### Q: "Why Saudi-specific AI? Can't customers just translate US tools?"

> "Two reasons. First, PDPL Article 18 mandates 5-year audit log
> retention with specific format requirements that US tools don't
> support without 12-24 months of custom work. Second, ZATCA Phase 2
> e-invoice integration requires UBL 2.1 XML compliance that's
> uniquely Saudi. We didn't translate — we built native. The
> 18-month gap is the moat."

### Q: "Solo founder risk?"

> "Real, and I disclose it openly on every page of our investor
> materials. Mitigations: hiring path documented (CS lead at
> customer #10, CTO at customer #30), DPO appointment kit ready,
> Decision Passport architecture means every customer can self-export
> their data if I disappeared tomorrow. The repo is open-source-able
> to customers — they own their continuity."

### Q: "What about Salesforce / HubSpot launching Arabic versions?"

> "They will. They're 18-24 months out. By then we'll have 200+
> Saudi customers, 5 sector verticals, agency channel reaching another
> 200 SMB customers. The translated version will compete with
> price; the native version competes with trust. We bet on trust."

### Q: "Tadawul Nomu IPO realistic?"

> "Yes, eligibility requires 2 years audited financials and 10M+
> SAR revenue. Year 5-6 realistic at our trajectory. Foodics and
> Salla are pattern. Saudi B2B + Saudi LPs + Tadawul = clean exit
> without selling sovereignty to international VC."

### Q: "What's the biggest risk?"

> "Founder burnout. Year 2 hire #1-3 is non-negotiable."

## Embargo Rules

- No exclusives that bury other outlets — equal-access policy

## What NOT to Say to Press

❌ "We're the next [Salesforce / Foodics / etc.]" — pattern-matching backfires when story diverges.

❌ Customer names without their explicit signed permission.

❌ Revenue specifics ("we have 1.27M ARR") — ranges only ("over 1M SAR ARR").

❌ Competitor disparagement — neutralize via "different value props" framing.

❌ Future-tense product claims unless code exists. Underpromise, overdeliver.

❌ Religious or political commentary. Stay laser-focused on business + tech.
