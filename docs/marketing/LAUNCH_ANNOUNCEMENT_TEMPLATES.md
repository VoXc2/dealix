## Customization Notes

### Before Using These Templates:

1. **Replace placeholders:**
   - [الاسم] / [Name] → Actual recipient name
   - [المجال] → Industry/sector
   - [رابط الحجز] → Your actual booking URL
   - [اسمك] → Your name
   - [Date] → Launch date

2. **Customize messaging:**
   - Adjust tone to match your brand voice
   - Add specific examples relevant to your target market
   - Include actual customer stories if available
   - Update pricing if different from templates

3. **Test before sending:**
   - Send test emails to yourself
   - Preview LinkedIn posts
   - Test WhatsApp messages with team
   - Check all links work correctly

4. **Track performance:**
   - Monitor open rates
   - Track click-through rates
   - Measure conversion rates
   - Adjust messaging based on results
