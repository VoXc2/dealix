# Employee Guide — Using AI at Work

## What you can do

- use approved tools only  
- keep customer and employee data out of tools unless allowed  
- label drafts as drafts  
- ask for approval before external send  

## What you must not do

- paste passwords, keys, or highly sensitive exports  
- automate messaging to people who did not consent  
- rely on AI for legal, medical, or regulated advice without experts  

## If unsure

Ask your manager or security owner **before** pasting data or sending output externally.

## Reporting

Report incidents immediately per company channel.
