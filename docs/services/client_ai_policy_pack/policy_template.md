# Policy Template — Client AI Usage

Use with legal/compliance review as needed. English symbols; client-facing copy may be Arabic.

## 1. Purpose

Define how AI may be used, what data may enter AI tools, and how approvals work.

## 2. Scope

Applies to: employees, contractors, approved tools, company data classes.

## 3. Allowed uses

- drafting with human review  
- summarization of non-sensitive internal docs (per data class)  
- internal research assistance with source discipline  

## 4. Prohibited uses

- cold outbound automation without consent  
- uploading regulated/sensitive data to unapproved tools  
- presenting AI output as fact without verification  
- guaranteed revenue or outcome claims  

## 5. Data classes

List: public, internal, confidential, regulated (examples only—client to finalize).

## 6. Approved tools

Inventory table: tool, owner, data allowed, logging expectations.

## 7. Approvals

Which actions require manager / security / executive approval.

## 8. Incidents

How to report leakage, wrong external send, or policy breach.

## 9. Review cadence

Quarterly policy review; owner assignment.
