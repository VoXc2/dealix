## Context
Most Saudi and Gulf enterprises now run AI tools without a coherent internal policy: marketing copies into public chatbots, sales pastes deal data into free tools, HR uploads CVs. The exposure is real — but the remediation is not a long policy project; it is a packaged Client AI Policy Pack that Dealix delivers in days. This offer is the on-ramp to the rest of Layer 5 and a natural cross-sell to all Dealix services. It rides alongside the broader trust pack at `docs/TRUST_AND_COMPLIANCE_BUSINESS_PACK.md` and aligns with PDPL practice in `docs/ops/PDPL_RETENTION_POLICY.md`.

## What we deliver
A pragmatic policy pack the client's leadership, legal, and managers can implement immediately:

- A clear AI usage policy.
- An allowed / prohibited use list.
- Data handling rules (especially around customer data and sensitive data).
- An approval matrix mapping action class to approver.
- An employee guide written for non-technical staff.
- A risk register pre-populated with the typical exposures we see.

## Who it is for
- Founder-led companies adopting AI tools across teams.
- Mid-size enterprises preparing for AI procurement reviews.
- Regulated organizations (financial services, healthcare, government suppliers) whose own auditors will ask for a policy.

## Outcome
At the end of the engagement the client owns:

1. A signed policy document the CEO/CIO can stand behind.
2. A trained workforce that knows what is OK to do with AI.
3. A documented decision trail for AI tool selection and data handling.
4. A foundation that all later Dealix engagements plug into.

## Packaging
- Standard turnaround: 2 weeks from kickoff to signed policy.
- Inputs from client: list of currently used AI tools, sample policies (if any), data classification baseline, named stakeholders.
- Inputs from Dealix: policy template, employee guide, approval matrix, tool usage rules, two workshops, a final sign-off review.
- Deliverables: all five files in this directory plus a one-page executive summary.

## Pricing posture
Fixed-fee, no hourly billing. Pricing tiered by company size and number of AI tools currently in active use. Includes one quarterly refresh.

## Cross-sell path
The Policy Pack unlocks: governance program work (`docs/governance/GOVERNANCE_PRODUCT_LADDER.md`), Capability Maturity assessments, and ongoing Dealix retainer services across Revenue, Customer, Operations, Knowledge, and Reporting.
