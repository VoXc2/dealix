## Best for

Clients continuing after a first Revenue Intelligence Sprint who want a
governed, repeatable monthly operating cadence.

## Duration

Monthly, rolling. One delivery cycle per calendar month; minimum 3 months.

## Deliverables

- Monthly revenue review
- Pipeline quality review
- AI-assisted decision review
- Approved follow-up queue (draft-first, approval-gated)
- Risk register
- Value report
- Board memo

## Price

4,999–15,000 SAR / month. Scope-dependent — quoted after the first sprint.

## Not included

- Scraping, cold WhatsApp, or LinkedIn automation.
- Automated external sends — the follow-up queue is approval-gated.
- Guaranteed revenue or pipeline outcomes.
- One-off sprint delivery (that is the Revenue Intelligence Sprint).

## Success metric

Each month closes with a delivered review cycle: revenue and pipeline reviews
issued, follow-up queue approved, risk register updated, value report and board
memo delivered, confirmed by a monthly Proof Pack (score >= 70) and at least one
Capital Asset.

## Governance

Every output carries a `governance_decision` field. The follow-up queue is
draft-first; no send happens without an `approved` transition. Each cycle
produces a Proof Pack and at least one Capital Asset.
