# Scope

## Included (per monthly cycle)

- Monthly revenue review and pipeline quality review
- AI-assisted decision review
- Approved follow-up queue (draft-first)
- Risk register update
- Value report and board memo

## Excluded

- WhatsApp sending, LinkedIn automation, scraping
- Automated external sends
- Manual sales execution on behalf of the client
- Net-new sprint scope (separately quoted)

## Client responsibilities

- Provide monthly CRM / pipeline data and confirm lawful usage
- Review and approve the follow-up queue
- Join the monthly review call
