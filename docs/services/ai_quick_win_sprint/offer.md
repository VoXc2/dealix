# AI Quick Win Sprint

## Promise

Turn one repetitive business process into an AI-assisted workflow with human approval, audit, and proof of time saved.

## Price

SAR 7,500–15,000

## Duration

7 business days

## Best For

- weekly reports
- email summaries
- classification tasks
- proposal drafts
- internal request routing
- SOP generation

## Deliverables

- process map
- workflow design
- one AI-assisted step
- human approval point
- SOP
- QA checklist
- proof report
- next workflow recommendation

## Not Included

- fully autonomous actions
- integration-heavy automation
- external sending
- high-risk decisions

## Success metric

Agreed time metric improves vs baseline OR error rate drops; sign-off on approval path documented.
