## Context
Executive deliverable closing every AI Quick Win Sprint. Documents the workflow before/after, AI-assisted step, time saved, and risks blocked. Pairs with `docs/templates/PROOF_PACK_TEMPLATE.md` and `docs/quality/QUALITY_STANDARD_V1.md`.

## Structure

### 1. Executive Summary
One paragraph: which workflow was mapped, what AI step was added, hours saved, proof captured.

### 2. Workflow Before
Inputs, steps, owners, time cost per cycle, error rate.

### 3. Workflow After (AI-Assisted)
New step diagram, AI handoff point, human approval point, audit event, fallback path.

### 4. Time Saved
Baseline minutes/hours per cycle vs new estimate, plus method of measurement.

### 5. SOP Delivered
Link to SOP document with owner, frequency, exception handling.

### 6. Governance Events
Approvals captured, risks blocked, no autonomous external action taken.

### 7. Business Value
Time Value (hours saved) + Quality Value (errors reduced).

### 8. Risks & Limitations
What this workflow does not handle. Open items.

### 9. Recommended Next Step
Continue (Monthly AI Ops) / Expand (Workflow Automation Sprint) / Pause.

## Output rules
- Bilingual summary.
- No guaranteed productivity claims; tie every claim to a measurable baseline.
- Workflow diagram embedded as ASCII or SVG.
- Anonymized version filed for case use after client approval.
