# AI Quick Win Sprint — Intake

## Day-1 Discovery (30 min)

1. Which process is most painful right now?
2. How often does it run? (daily / weekly / monthly)
3. Who is the process owner today?
4. What are the inputs? (systems, files, channels)
5. What are the outputs? (report, decision, task, message)
6. Where do errors typically happen?
7. What requires human approval today?
8. What would "success" look like in 7 days?
