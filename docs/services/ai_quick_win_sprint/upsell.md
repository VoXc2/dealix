# AI Quick Win Sprint — Stage-8 (Expand) Renewal Pathways / مسارات التجديد

Stage-8 in the 8-stage customer loop. Renewal opened at Day 7 handoff; decision point at Day 30. HoCS owns; CRO countersigns deals >= SAR 15,000.

## When to pitch / متى نقترح
- Trigger: process owner runs the automation alone >= 5 times without escalation.
- Window: Day-14 proof-pack review (Stage-7 Prove).
- Disqualifier: error rate above customer-stated tolerance — fix first, expand later.

## Pathway 1 — Monthly AI Ops Retainer / احتفاظ شهري للعمليات
**SAR 15,000–60,000 / month, 6-month commit.**
- Run + maintain the automation in customer's environment.
- Monthly tuning + edge-case coverage expansion.
- Weekly ops report (errors / approvals / runtime).
- Quarterly business review with COO/CFO.
- Best for: customers without internal AI ops capacity.

## Pathway 2 — Workflow Automation Sprint (additional sprint) / سبرنت أتمتة موسّع
**SAR 15,000–60,000, 14–28 business days, fixed scope per process.**
- Adds a SECOND use case (not on the curated list).
- Custom integrations, more elaborate approval flows.
- Reuses the audit-log + approval-matrix scaffolding from the Quick Win.
- Best for: customers ready to expand from 1 -> 2-3 automated processes.

## Pathway 3 — SOP Automation Program / برنامج أتمتة الإجراءات
**SAR 60,000–180,000, 1–3 months, multi-process.**
- 3-5 internal processes automated.
- SOP documentation, employee assistant on top.
- Cross-process audit dashboard.
- Best for: ops-heavy customers digitizing a department (Finance / HR / Support).

## Pathway 4 — Enterprise AI Ops / مؤسسي
**SAR 250,000+ / year, MSA + DPA required.**
- Multiple business units, KSA-resident storage option.
- Named delivery team, SLA-backed.
- Integration into customer's BI stack.
- Best for: enterprises with procurement and InfoSec processes.

## Renewal Math / حساب التجديد
- Hours saved per week * SAR-per-hour * 50 weeks = annual savings anchor.
- If annual savings >= 3x of Pathway 1 retainer floor (SAR 180k vs SAR 60k floor) -> offer Pathway 1 immediately.
- If customer asks "what about X process too?" -> route to Pathway 2.
