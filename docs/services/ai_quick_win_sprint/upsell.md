## Context
Every Quick Win Sprint must end with a clear next step. This file maps observed proof signals to next-tier offers, implementing the ladder logic in `docs/DEALIX_REVENUE_PLAYBOOK_FINAL.md` and `docs/COMPANY_SERVICE_LADDER.md`. The strategic monetization path is in `docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`.

## Operating Principle
> The hours saved tell you what to upsell. The owner's enthusiasm tells you when.

## Proof → Next-Offer Mapping

### Proof: Hours saved ≥ owner's target
**Signal**:
- `hours_saved >= success_target_hours_saved`.
- Owner expresses interest in more workflows.

**Recommended next offer**: **Monthly AI Ops Retainer** (SAR 18,000–40,000/mo).
- 2–4 new workflows per quarter, full proof pack each.
- Talking point: "We saved 2.3 hours on one workflow. Imagine 8 workflows per quarter, every quarter."

### Proof: Workflow is one of many similar workflows
**Signal**:
- During Sprint, the team mentioned 3+ analogous workflows.
- The chosen workflow's pattern (data extraction, summary, approval) repeats elsewhere.

**Recommended next offer**: **Workflow Automation Sprint** (SAR 25,000–50,000) or **Monthly AI Ops**.
- Take the same pattern, harden it across 3–5 workflows.
- Talking point: "This pattern works. Let's replicate it where it'll save the most time."

### Proof: Owner became a champion
**Signal**:
- Owner exceeded the 1h/day expectation.
- Owner requested SOP refinements.
- Owner asked "what else can we do".

**Recommended next offer**: **AI Champion Program** (custom) or **Monthly AI Ops**.
- Train the owner to identify next-candidate workflows.
- Monthly clinic with the owner.
- Talking point: "You have a champion. Let's give them a process."

### Proof: Cross-team interest emerged
**Signal**:
- Multiple teams asked about the new flow during the Sprint.
- Sponsor mentioned other departments.

**Recommended next offer**: **Pilot Conversion Sprint** (SAR 18,000–35,000) or scoped Pilot.
- Take the same pattern, deploy in a second department with proper change management.
- Talking point: "Other departments want this. Let's give them a structured rollout, not a copy-paste."

### Proof: Data quality holes appeared during the Sprint
**Signal**:
- Owner mentioned messy data sources.
- Designer flagged inconsistent inputs.

**Recommended next offer**: **Lead Intelligence Sprint** or **Data OS retainer**.
- Talking point: "Your workflow is now efficient. Your data is the next bottleneck."

### Proof: Knowledge / policy gaps appeared
**Signal**:
- Owner had to look up internal policy multiple times.
- The Sprint exposed missing or scattered knowledge.

**Recommended next offer**: **Company Brain Sprint** (SAR 20,000–60,000).
- Build a source-cited internal assistant.
- Talking point: "Your team is asking the same questions repeatedly. Let's give them a Brain."

### Proof: Strong proof pack + publication consent
**Signal**:
- Hours saved is publishable (rounded, clean).
- Owner agrees to a named or anonymized case study.

**Recommended next offer**: **Co-marketing slot on Dealix Trust Page** (value exchange).
- Talking point: "Your win is a story other founders need."

## CSM Motion (Day 7–14)

| Day | Action | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Day 7 | Final delivery + handoff signed | DL |
| Day 8 | CSM reviews proof pack | CSM |
| Day 9 | Recommendation embedded into the executive output Section 8 | CSM + DL |
| Day 10 | CSM emails sponsor + meeting link | CSM |
| Day 12 | CSM follow-up if no response | CSM |
| Day 30 | Final follow-up | CSM |
| Day 60 | Re-categorize as nurture if not converted | CSM |

## Conversion Targets
- **Sprint → Monthly AI Ops** within 60 days: ≥ 30%.
- **Sprint → Workflow Automation Sprint** within 90 days: ≥ 20%.
- **Any conversion** within 6 months: ≥ 50%.

## Anti-Patterns
- Pitching an offer the proof pack does not justify.
- Pushing a Pilot before the owner is a champion.
- Discounting the retainer below floor to win conversion.
- Promising outcomes that depend on data the client has not provided.
