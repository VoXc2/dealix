# Scope

## Included

- Review of supplied revenue and governance evidence
- Synthesis into a concise board memo
- Top decisions, risks, capital allocation, build / hold / kill calls

## Excluded

- New data collection or net-new analysis
- Investment advice
- Board attendance or representation

## Client responsibilities

- Provide the underlying evidence (diagnostic, sprint, retainer outputs)
- Confirm the audience and decision questions
- Review the draft memo
