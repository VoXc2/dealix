## Promise

**EN —** We turn revenue and AI-governance evidence into a concise,
decision-ready board memo: top revenue decisions, pipeline risks, AI governance
risks, capital allocation, and build / hold / kill recommendations.

**AR —** نحوّل أدلة الإيراد وحوكمة الذكاء الاصطناعي إلى مذكرة مختصرة جاهزة
للقرار: أهم قرارات الإيراد، مخاطر خط الأنابيب، مخاطر حوكمة الذكاء الاصطناعي،
تخصيص رأس المال، وتوصيات البناء / الإبقاء / الإيقاف.

## Best for

Founders and boards who need a decision-ready memo, typically following a
Diagnostic, Sprint, or Retainer cycle.

## Duration

3–5 business days from evidence handover.

## Deliverables

- Top revenue decisions
- Pipeline risks summary
- AI governance risks summary
- Capital allocation view
- Build / hold / kill recommendations

## Price

Scope-dependent — quoted after intake. A compact add-on to a larger engagement.

## Not included

- New data collection or fresh analysis beyond supplied evidence.
- Investment advice or guaranteed financial outcomes.
- Board representation or attendance.

## Success metric

A delivered board memo accepted as decision-ready: top decisions, pipeline and
AI-governance risks, capital allocation, and build / hold / kill
recommendations — each line backed by a source_ref.

## Governance

Every output carries a `governance_decision` field. Every recommendation cites
a source_ref. No guaranteed-outcome language is used.
