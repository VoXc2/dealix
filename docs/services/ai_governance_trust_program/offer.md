## Promise

A complete, auditable, PDPL-aligned AI governance framework that lets an
enterprise adopt AI across teams with clear policy, approval classes, audit
logging, and an executive view of risk. This is the enterprise tier above the
[AI Governance Program](../ai_governance_program/offer.md).

## Price

SAR 50,000–180,000 — one-time, phased project (no monthly retainer). Final
figure is scoped per proposal.

## Duration

~45 days.

## Best For

Organizations running AI in multiple departments that need board-grade
governance, vendor/model risk review, and PDPL readiness in one program.

## Deliverables

- company AI policy + AI risk matrix
- approval classes + data-handling rules
- employee AI usage rules
- audit logging + human-approval workflows
- PDPL readiness assessment
- vendor / model risk review
- executive governance dashboard

## Not Included

- legal advice or legal sign-off — this is a readiness program, not counsel
- full penetration testing
- in-product engineering of all controls (separately scoped)

## Success Metric

We commit to an auditable, PDPL-aligned governance framework. If it does not
pass the agreed readiness review, we keep working at no charge.
