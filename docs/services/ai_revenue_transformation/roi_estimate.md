# ROI Estimate / تقدير العائد

This page explains how ROI is **estimated**. All figures are estimates, not
verified results. Verified figures appear only in the monthly Proof Pack once
real data is available.

## How we estimate

ROI is estimated from four measurable inputs, agreed with the client at kickoff:

- **Qualified leads** — leads scored above the agreed threshold per month.
- **Conversion rate** — share of qualified leads that reach a sales meeting,
  using the client's own historical rate where available.
- **Response time** — median time from lead to first follow-up; faster response
  is associated with higher conversion.
- **Pipeline value** — qualified leads multiplied by the client's average deal
  size, shown as a range.

## Estimate formula

```
estimated_pipeline_value = qualified_leads
                         × estimated_conversion_rate
                         × average_deal_size
```
