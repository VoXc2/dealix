# Client Handoff

What the client receives at the end of the 60-day build sprint, before the
retainer begins.

## Delivered assets

- ICP analysis and lead-machine design document
- lead scoring and enrichment model, with documented thresholds
- outbound strategy and Arabic sales messaging library
- WhatsApp/Email follow-up workflows (draft-first)
- CRM pipeline with AI-assisted follow-up cadence
- executive revenue dashboard
- Proof Pack from the build sprint

## Access and operation

- deliverables location (workspace / export)
- how to review and approve outreach drafts
- how to operate the CRM pipeline and dashboard
- KPI definitions and where they are tracked

## Retainer transition

- monthly Proof Pack and ROI report cadence confirmed
- support window and escalation path documented
- retainer scope and review schedule agreed

## Governance reminder

All external messaging stays draft-first under client approval. No scraping,
no cold WhatsApp, no LinkedIn automation, no automatic external send.
