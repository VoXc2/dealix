## Promise

Build an end-to-end revenue system that surfaces, qualifies and follows up leads
with measurable KPIs — under human approval, with audit and monthly proof.

## Price

Sprint: SAR 35,000–100,000 (custom) + monthly retainer SAR 10,000–40,000.

## Duration

60-day build sprint, then an ongoing monthly retainer.

## Best For

- enterprise and upmarket companies with an existing sales team
- organizations graduating from a [Lead Intelligence Sprint](../lead_intelligence_sprint/offer.md)
- companies needing a managed revenue operation, not a one-off report

## Deliverables

- ICP analysis and lead-machine design
- lead scoring and enrichment model
- outbound strategy and Arabic sales messaging
- WhatsApp/Email follow-up workflows (draft-first)
- CRM pipeline build with AI-assisted follow-up
- executive revenue dashboard
- Proof Pack and monthly ROI report

## Not Included

- scraping or bulk data harvesting
- cold WhatsApp or LinkedIn automation
- automatic external sending without approval
- guaranteed meeting or sales counts

## Success Metric

We commit to a revenue system with measurable KPIs. If the agreed pipeline KPIs
do not improve, we keep working at no charge. / نلتزم ببناء نظام مبيعات يكشف
ويؤهل ويتابع الفرص بمؤشرات قابلة للقياس. إن لم تتحسن مؤشرات الـ pipeline المتفق
عليها، نواصل بدون مقابل.
