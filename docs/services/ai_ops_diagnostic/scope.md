# Scope — AI Ops Diagnostic

## In scope

- Stakeholder interviews (as scheduled) and artifact review **client provides**
- Data inventory **from supplied exports** (no covert collection)
- Use-case scoring (value · feasibility · risk · governance)
- Written report + roadmap + recommended sprint

## Out of scope

- Building production integrations
- Training bespoke models
- Unapproved external messaging
- Access to systems without documented authorization

## Assumptions

- Client assigns **owner** for data access and approvals
- Materials may be **redacted** for PDPL minimization

## Deliverables

- AI readiness score + rationale
- Data readiness summary
- Top 5 use cases
- Risk map
- Phased roadmap
- Proof-style annex: what was reviewed, what was **not** available

## Timeline

Typical: **3–10 business days** after full intake (adjust per deal).
