# AI Ops Diagnostic — Offer

**Pillar**: Strategy / Pre-Sprint Gateway · **Modules**: Strategy OS (when built) + Data OS + Governance OS · **Tier entry**: Any tier (gateway to Sprints)

## Promise
Identify the best AI use cases, data readiness gaps, and operational risks
**before** committing to an AI Sprint. Output is a written roadmap and a
recommended Sprint (or "not yet — fix this first").

## Commercials
- **Price**: SAR 7,500–25,000 (scope-dependent)
- **Duration**: 5–10 business days
- **Payment**: Net 14; fully credited toward a follow-on Sprint if signed within 30 days.

## Best for

Prospects who say one of:
- "We want AI but don't know where to start."
- "We have data but it's messy."
- "We tried AI tools and didn't see results."
- "Leadership wants AI in our 2026 plan."
- "We need a roadmap, not a single feature."

## Deliverables

1. **AI Readiness Score** — 0–100 across 5 dimensions (data, process, people, risk, opportunity).
2. **Data Readiness Review** — what data exists, what's missing, what's PDPL-relevant.
3. **Top-5 Use Cases ranked** — by RW-RICE (Reach × Impact × Confidence × Revenue × V2030) ÷ Effort.
4. **Risk Map** — top 5 governance / data / AI-quality / change-management risks with mitigations.
5. **Implementation Roadmap** — 30 / 60 / 90-day plan, named owners, milestones.
6. **Recommended next engagement** — Lead Intel / AI Quick Win / Company Brain Sprint, or "not yet, fix X first".

## Not included

- Building any AI workflow (that's a Sprint).
- Cleaning data (that's a Sprint).
- Implementing governance (that's a Sprint).
- Guarantees of revenue or specific outcomes.

## Success criteria (binding)

- Customer leaves with a written, prioritized roadmap.
- Top-5 use cases are SAR/time/quality-scored.
- Customer can pick the next Sprint with confidence.
- ≥ 50% of Diagnostics convert to a Sprint within 30 days.

## Why this exists

Per `docs/sales/CLIENT_EDUCATION.md`: many prospects don't know how to buy
AI. Selling them a Sprint when they need a Diagnostic is the fastest way
to fail. The Diagnostic is the honest gateway: it earns trust, qualifies
the customer, and almost always leads to a larger Sprint.

It also creates Dealix-side learning: every Diagnostic adds insight to
`docs/market/AI_OPS_MARKET_INTELLIGENCE.md` (when added) about what Saudi
companies actually need.
