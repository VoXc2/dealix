# Company Brain Sprint — Stage-8 (Expand) Renewal Pathways / مسارات التجديد

Stage-8 in the 8-stage customer loop. Renewal opened at Day 21 handoff; decision point at Day 30. HoCS owns; CRO countersigns deals >= SAR 15,000.

## When to pitch / متى نقترح
- Trigger: team has asked >= 30 real questions in week 4, citation coverage stays >= 95%.
- Window: Day-30 proof-pack review (Stage-7 Prove).
- Disqualifier: citation coverage < 90% in week 4 -> tune first, expand later.

## Pathway 1 — Monthly Brain Retainer / احتفاظ شهري للذاكرة المؤسسية
**SAR 8,000–35,000 / month, 6-month commit.**
- Monthly document refresh (up to 100 new docs / month).
- Weekly eval pass on a customer-owned question bank.
- Access-rule edits + monthly audit-log review with DPO.
- Quarterly business review with COO.
- Best for: customers wanting the assistant to stay current without internal effort.

## Pathway 2 — Second Team Expansion (additional sprint) / توسعة فريق ثانٍ
**SAR 12,000–25,000, 14 business days.**
- Brings a SECOND team onto the same brain instance (e.g., add HR after Sales).
- New permissions matrix + 200 docs additional ingest.
- Reuses the audit + erasure scaffolding.
- Best for: customers proven on team #1 and ready to expand horizontally.

## Pathway 3 — Sales Knowledge Assistant / مساعد المبيعات المتخصّص
**SAR 15,000–60,000, 21 business days.**
- Vertical specialization on top of the brain: product Q&A, proposal snippets, objection handling.
- Bilingual brand-voice tuning.
- Tighter eval bar (>= 98% citation coverage for product claims).
- Best for: customers whose first team was Sales.

## Pathway 4 — Policy & Compliance Assistant / مساعد السياسات والامتثال
**SAR 20,000–100,000, 28 business days.**
- HR / compliance Q&A with approved-answer library.
- Mandatory citation + version-pinned policy doc.
- Audit log piped to compliance OS (`auto_client_acquisition/compliance_os/`).
- Best for: HR / Legal / Compliance teams.

## Pathway 5 — Enterprise Company Brain / المؤسسي
**SAR 90,000+ setup + SAR 35,000+ / month, MSA + DPA required.**
- Multi-team, multi-instance, KSA-resident storage option.
- Custom channels (Teams + Slack + web + custom intranet).
- Named delivery team, SLA-backed.
- Best for: enterprises >= 500 seats.

## Renewal Math / حساب التجديد
- Hours saved per week (across the team) * SAR/hour * 50 weeks = annual savings anchor.
- If team-wide annual savings >= 4x the Pathway 1 retainer floor (SAR 96k vs SAR 8k floor) -> Pathway 1 is the obvious renewal.
- If the customer cross-shopped a US RAG vendor -> emphasize PDPL fit, KSA-resident storage option, AR tone, audit-log compliance.
