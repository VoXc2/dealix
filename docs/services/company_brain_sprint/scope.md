# Company Brain Sprint — Scope

## Included
- Document ingestion (up to 500 docs: PDF / DOCX / KB / policies).
- PII detection + redaction before indexing.
- RAG indexing with source / chunk citations.
- Query interface for ONE team (≤ 20 seats).
- 3-tier access rules (Admin / Team / Read-only).
- Freshness tracking with > 90-day auto-flag.
- 2-hour recorded team training + admin guide.

## Excluded
- Documents beyond 500 (overage billed).
- More than one team (requires higher tier).
- Custom CRM / professional-network integrations.
- Live document editing (read-only assistant).

## Customer responsibilities
- Document inventory with categories + owners.
- Permissions matrix (who sees what).
- Sensitive-document flagging.
- 10 sample questions for eval.

## Hard stops
- Customer requests that the assistant answer without sources.
- Sensitive documents not flagged.
- PII not redacted before indexing.
