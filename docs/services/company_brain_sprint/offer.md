# Company Brain Sprint — Offer

**Pillar:** Build Company Brain · **Modules:** Knowledge + Governance + Reporting + Intake

## Promise
Turn the customer's files into an internal AI assistant with **cited** answers in 21 business days.

## Commercials
- **Price:** SAR 20,000 (excl. VAT)
- **Duration:** 21 business days
- **SOW template:** `templates/sow/company_brain_sprint.md`

## Deliverables
- Up to 500 documents ingested.
- PII detection + redaction before indexing.
- RAG with source-level citations.
- Query interface (web / Slack / Teams) for ≤ 20 seats.
- 3-tier access rules.
- Freshness tracking (auto-flag > 90-day docs).
- 2-hour recorded team training.

## Hard product rule
**No source = no answer.** The assistant refuses to answer when no qualifying source exists.

## Best For
- Teams with rich documents (sales, HR, support, ops).
- "Where is that PDF?" pain points.
