# Company Brain Sprint

## Promise

Turn scattered company documents into a source-backed AI knowledge assistant.

## Price

SAR 20,000–60,000

## Duration

3–4 weeks

## Best For

- sales teams
- support teams
- policy-heavy companies
- training teams
- founders with scattered docs

## Deliverables

- document inventory
- source registry
- searchable knowledge base
- Q&A assistant
- citations
- insufficient evidence mode
- knowledge quality report
- update workflow

## Non-Negotiable

No source = no answer.

## Not included

- legal advice or regulated medical claims
- bypassing access controls
- auto customer-facing replies without approval

## Success metric

Eval pass rate on agreed cases + citation coverage on sample set + blocked answers when no source.
