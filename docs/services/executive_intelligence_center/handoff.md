# Handoff

## What is handed over

- access to the weekly executive brief and the signal layer
- the approval queue, with the client's named approvers configured
- Proof Ledger access for the executive sponsor and nominated viewers
- decision memo archive and quarterly growth strategy documents
- the monthly Proof Pack (see [`proof_pack_template.md`](proof_pack_template.md))

## How access is set up

- the executive sponsor nominates approvers and viewers in writing
- each person receives scoped access — approvers can act on the queue, viewers read only
- access is reviewed at every quarterly growth review and on any role change
- connected data sources remain owned and controlled by the client

## Operating model after handoff

- recommendations and decision memos stay advisory; the client's executive approves
- nothing is auto-executed — `approval_required` mode is permanent for this service
- hard gates remain enforced: no_live_send, no_live_charge, no_cold_whatsapp, no_linkedin_auto, no_scraping, no_blast
- the delivery team continues the weekly, monthly, and quarterly cadence under the retainer

## Continuity

- escalation contact and response window confirmed at handoff
- offboarding terms: data export and access revocation documented before close
