# Scope

## Included

- signal layer over agreed revenue, customer-health, and operational-risk data
- strategic, partnership, and expansion opportunity radar
- AI recommendations and decision memos (advisory)
- approval queue for proposed actions
- weekly executive brief delivered to leadership
- quarterly growth strategy review
- Proof Ledger access and monthly Proof Pack
- onboarding, access setup, and signal calibration

## Excluded

- virtual CFO, financial controllership, or accounting work
- legal advice or contract drafting
- external representation, outreach, or negotiation on the client's behalf
- auto-execution of any recommendation — the executive approves; nothing runs unattended
- live sending or live charging (hard gate: `no_live_send`, `no_live_charge`)
- cold WhatsApp, LinkedIn automation, scraping, or bulk outreach (hard gates)
- production integrations without a security review (change request)

## Action mode

`approval_required`. Recommendations and decision memos are advisory. The executive approves through the approval queue; nothing is auto-executed.
