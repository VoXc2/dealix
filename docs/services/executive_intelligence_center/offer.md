A retainer-led intelligence layer for executives: revenue, customer-health, and operational-risk signals plus AI recommendations and decision memos — so leadership decides faster on evidence, not noise.

Setup SAR 80,000–250,000 + monthly retainer SAR 20,000–70,000. Pricing is custom and scoped per engagement.

## Duration

Ongoing, retainer-led engagement.

## Best For

- mid-to-large companies with a leadership team making weekly high-stakes calls
- organisations that already hold revenue, customer, and operational data
- executives who want signals and decision memos, not dashboards to interpret alone

## Deliverables

- revenue, customer-health, and operational-risk signals
- strategic, partnership, and expansion opportunity radar
- AI recommendations and decision memos
- approval queue for advisory actions
