# ROI Estimate

This page explains how value is **estimated**. Estimates are not verified outcomes. Verified figures appear only in the monthly Proof Pack, sourced from the Proof Ledger.

## What we estimate

Value is estimated across three areas. Each is shown as a labelled estimate, never as a fact.

### 1. Decision time saved

- baseline: hours leadership spent gathering and interpreting data before the engagement
- after: time to decision using signals, the radar, and decision memos
- estimate = (baseline hours − post hours) × loaded executive hourly cost
- the time-saving commitment is the agreed target for this figure

### 2. Opportunities surfaced

- count of strategic, partnership, and expansion opportunities surfaced by the radar
- estimated value uses the client's own pipeline or deal-size assumptions
- only opportunities the client chooses to act on are counted toward estimated value

### 3. Risks caught early

- count of revenue, customer-health, and operational risks flagged before impact
- estimated avoided cost uses the client's own impact assumptions and lead time
- shown as a range, not a point figure

## How estimates are presented

- every figure is labelled "estimated" with its method and assumptions
- assumption inputs come from the client, not invented by Dealix
- estimates are reconciled against verified Proof Ledger data each month

## Commitment

We commit to saving executive decision time via signals and outcome-linked decisions. If the agreed time-saving commitment is unmet, we keep working at no charge.
