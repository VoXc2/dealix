# Monthly Proof Pack Template

The Proof Pack is compiled monthly from the Proof Ledger. Every figure cites its source and is labelled estimated or verified.

## 1. Summary

- engagement month and reporting period
- time-saving commitment status vs baseline
- one-line outcome statement

## 2. Decisions supported

| Date | Decision | Memo reference | Approved by | Outcome status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| | | | | |

- count of decision memos delivered
- count of decisions approved through the queue

## 3. Time saved

- estimated executive decision time saved this month
- method used for the estimate (see [`roi_estimate.md`](roi_estimate.md))
- cumulative estimate since engagement start

## 4. Opportunities surfaced

| Opportunity | Type (strategic / partnership / expansion) | Source signal | Status |
|---|---|---|---|

- count surfaced, count actioned by the client

## 5. Risks flagged

| Risk | Category (revenue / customer-health / operational) | Date flagged | Lead time before impact | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## 6. Notes and calibration

- signal threshold changes this month
- false-positive review notes
- confirmation that no hard gate was crossed

*All revenue and outcome figures are sourced from the Proof Ledger. No figure is fabricated (gates: no_fake_proof, no_fake_revenue).*
