# Scope

## Included

- CRM / account data intake
- Account prioritization and deal-risk scoring
- Next-best-action drafts (draft-only)
- Follow-up template drafts (draft-only)
- Revenue opportunity ledger
- Decision Passport and Proof Pack

## Excluded

- WhatsApp sending, LinkedIn automation, scraping
- Automated external sends
- Manual sales execution on behalf of the client
- CRM integration unless separately scoped

## Client responsibilities

- Provide CRM / account data and confirm lawful usage
- Review and approve all drafts
- Join intake and review calls
