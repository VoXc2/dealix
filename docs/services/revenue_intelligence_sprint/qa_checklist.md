# QA Checklist

## Data QA

- [ ] All rows have a source or are flagged
- [ ] Duplicates and missing fields reported
- [ ] PII flagged, never logged

## Scoring QA

- [ ] Every score has an explanation
- [ ] Top accounts make business sense
- [ ] Scores are deterministic

## Draft QA

- [ ] No guaranteed claims
- [ ] No fake proof
- [ ] No cold WhatsApp / LinkedIn automation
- [ ] All drafts are draft-only and approval-gated

## Report QA

- [ ] Revenue opportunity ledger complete
- [ ] Decision Passport complete
- [ ] Bilingual disclaimer present
