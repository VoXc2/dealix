# Intake Questions

## From the diagnostic

- Which diagnostic recommendation are we acting on?
- Which proof-of-value opportunities are in scope?

## Accounts

- Which account segment is the sprint focus?
- What defines a high-priority account for you?
- What signals indicate deal risk in your pipeline?

## Follow-up

- What is your current follow-up cadence?
- Who owns follow-up actions?
- Who approves outbound messages?

## Governance

- Confirm: data has lawful basis for any outreach.
- Confirm: all sends route through founder/owner approval.
