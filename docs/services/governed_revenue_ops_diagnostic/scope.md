# Scope

## Included

- CRM / source export intake (CSV or sheet)
- Revenue-workflow interview (short, structured)
- Review of revenue workflow, source/data quality, AI usage, approval boundaries
- Pipeline risk mapping and follow-up gap analysis
- Proof-of-value opportunity identification
- Decision Passport and a Sprint / Retainer recommendation

## Excluded

- Scraping, cold WhatsApp, LinkedIn automation
- Automated external sends (all drafts are review-only)
- CRM migration / integration
- Sprint or retainer delivery
- Manual sales execution on behalf of the client

## Client responsibilities

- Provide a CRM/source export
- Confirm lawful usage of any contact data
- Join the workflow interview and review call
- Approve the final recommendation
