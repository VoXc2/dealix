# Intake Questions

## Business

- What do you sell, and to whom?
- What is your average deal value and sales cycle length?
- Which sectors and cities matter most?

## Revenue workflow

- How do leads enter and move through your pipeline?
- What are your current pipeline stages?
- How do you follow up, and what happens after a reply?

## Data

- Where do accounts/leads live today (CRM, sheet, mixed)?
- Can you export the data? Does every row have a source?
- Does the data contain personal contact info with a lawful basis?

## AI usage

- Which AI tools does the revenue team use today?
- Are there written rules for allowed/forbidden AI actions?
- Who approves AI-assisted external messages?

## Governance

- Who approves external sends?
- Is there an evidence/decision trail today?
