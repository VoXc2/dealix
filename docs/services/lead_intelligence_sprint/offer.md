# Lead Intelligence Sprint

## Promise

Turn scattered account and lead data into ranked revenue opportunities, compliant outreach drafts, and an executive proof report.

## Price

SAR 9,500–18,000

## Duration

10 business days

## Best For

- B2B service companies
- logistics companies
- real estate offices
- clinics with non-sensitive business outreach needs
- companies with spreadsheets or weak CRM

## Deliverables

- data quality score
- cleaned account list
- duplicate report
- top 50 ranked accounts
- top 10 next actions
- outreach draft pack (draft-only)
- mini pipeline board
- proof pack
- next pilot proposal

## Not Included

- scraping
- cold WhatsApp
- LinkedIn automation
- guaranteed meetings
- automated sending
- CRM integration unless separately scoped

## Success Metrics

- accounts validated
- duplicates found
- data quality improvement
- accounts scored
- top actions delivered
- outreach drafts approved
