# Intake Questions

## Business

- What do you sell?
- Who is your ideal customer?
- Which cities matter?
- Which sectors matter?
- What is your average deal value?

## Data

- Where are your leads/accounts today?
- Do you have CRM export?
- Do you have source information?
- Do you have consent/relationship status?

## Sales

- How do you follow up today?
- What messages do you use?
- What happens after a reply?
- What is your current pipeline stage list?

## Governance

- Does the file contain personal contact info?
- Can the data be used for outreach?
- Who approves external messages?
