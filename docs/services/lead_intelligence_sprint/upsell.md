# Lead Intelligence Sprint — Stage-8 (Expand) Renewal Pathways / مسارات التجديد

Stage-8 in the 8-stage customer loop. Renewal proposal opened in CRM at Day 10 (handoff). HoCS owns the conversation; CRO countersigns deals ≥ SAR 15,000.

## When to pitch / متى نقترح
- Trigger: handoff session complete + customer marks ≥ 3 of Top-10 drafts as "approved".
- Window: 14-day proof-pack review meeting (Stage-7 Prove).
- Disqualifier: NPS quick score < 6 in handoff session — wait, run a 30-day check-in instead.

## Pathway 1 — Monthly RevOps Retainer / احتفاظ شهري
**SAR 15,000–60,000 / month, 6-month commit.**
- Monthly refresh of the Top-50 + new drafts.
- Pipeline tracking in Mini-CRM (managed for customer).
- Weekly insights memo for Head of Sales.
- Quarterly business review.
- Best for: SME with 5–20 reps wanting outsourced revenue ops.
- SOW template: `templates/sow/monthly_revops_retainer.md` (to be authored).

## Pathway 2 — Sales Pipeline Setup (additional sprint) / إعداد مسار المبيعات
**SAR 8,000–25,000, 14 business days, fixed scope.**
- CRM stages + dashboards built in customer's environment.
- Follow-up rules, call scripts, weekly reporting.
- Builds on top of the Top-50 dataset and Mini-CRM board.
- Best for: customers wanting to internalize sales ops after the Sprint.

## Pathway 3 — Saudi Lead Engine (Growth tier) / محرك العملاء السعودي
**SAR 70,000–180,000 / year, annual prepay.**
- Continuous enrichment + scoring across customer-defined ICP.
- Decision Passport audit per action.
- Custom integration to one CRM (HubSpot / Salesforce / Bitrix24).
- Dedicated analyst hours (16 / month).
- Best for: mid-market with multi-vertical pipelines.

## Pathway 4 — Enterprise Lead Intelligence / المؤسسي
**SAR 250,000+ / year, MSA + DPA required.**
- Multi-tenant data isolation, named DPO contact.
- Custom data sources, multi-CRM, KSA-resident storage option.
- SLA-backed delivery, named delivery team.
- Best for: enterprises (BFSI / gov-adjacent) with procurement and InfoSec processes.

## Renewal Math / حساب التجديد
- 14-day pipeline-value baseline (Stage-7 proof pack) anchors the value story.
- Default offer: Pathway 1 (Monthly RevOps) at floor SAR 15,000/mo.
- Trade-up to Pathway 3 if the customer asks for a second vertical or a second CRM.
