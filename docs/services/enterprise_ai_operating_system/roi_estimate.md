# ROI Estimate

ROI for the Enterprise AI Operating System is **estimated**, not promised. All figures are modelled from baselines and assumptions agreed with the client, and reconciled to the value ledger in each monthly Proof Pack.

## Inputs

- baseline decision count and average decision time per department
- baseline hours spent on repeatable knowledge work
- current pipeline volume and stage cycle times
- in-scope departments and headcount

## Estimated value drivers

### 1. Decision-time saved

- estimated minutes saved per decision × decision frequency
- driven by Company Brain citations replacing manual research

### 2. Pipeline lift

- estimated effect of faster, draft-first Sales/Support responses
- expressed as a range with stated confidence — never as a fixed figure

### 3. Hours automated

- estimated weekly hours moved from manual work to draft-and-approve
- summed across Sales, Support, and Operations agents

## Method and confidence

- each driver is modelled as a low / expected / high range
- assumptions and confidence level are stated alongside every range
- actuals are tracked monthly against the value ledger and compared to estimates

## What we commit to

We commit to a 90-day transformation across 5 gated phases. If a phase gate is not met, we keep working that phase at no charge. We do not commit to a specific revenue or ROI number.
