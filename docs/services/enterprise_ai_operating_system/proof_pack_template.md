# Monthly Proof Pack

The Proof Pack is delivered each month under the retainer. It reports activity and estimated impact only — no fabricated proof or revenue.

## 1. Summary

- reporting period
- phases or agents in scope this month
- headline changes vs prior month

## 2. Activity

- agent drafts produced (by department)
- drafts approved, edited, or rejected
- approval cycle time

## 3. Company Brain

- knowledge sources added or refreshed
- citation coverage on sampled answers

## 4. Impact (estimated)

- decision-time saved (estimated)
- pipeline lift attributed to AI-assisted drafts (estimated)
- hours automated across departments (estimated)
- all figures reconciled against the value ledger

## 5. Governance

- risk register changes
- audit trail integrity check result
- any gate or policy exceptions

## 6. Optimization cycle

- improvements shipped this month
- recommendations for next month

## 7. Next steps

- proposed Scale Plan adjustments

*Impact figures are estimates reconciled to the value ledger, not verified outcomes.*
