## Promise

**EN —** We assess whether your CRM and revenue data is ready for AI: a hygiene
report, source mapping, missing fields, duplicate accounts, broken lifecycle
stages, a data readiness score, and an AI readiness recommendation.

**AR —** نقيّم جاهزية بيانات الـCRM والإيراد للذكاء الاصطناعي: تقرير نظافة، خريطة
مصادر، الحقول الناقصة، الحسابات المكررة، مراحل دورة الحياة المعطلة، درجة جاهزية
البيانات، وتوصية جاهزية الذكاء الاصطناعي.

## Best for

Teams whose CRM or revenue data is not yet AI-ready and who want a clear,
evidence-backed readiness picture before investing in AI workflows.

## Duration

5–8 business days from data receipt.

## Deliverables

- CRM hygiene report
- Source mapping
- Missing-fields analysis
- Duplicate accounts report
- Lifecycle-stage review
- Data readiness score
- AI readiness recommendation

## Price

Scope-dependent — quoted after intake. Often sold alongside the Diagnostic.

## Not included

- CRM platform migration or rebuild (separately scoped).
- Automated data cleaning at scale without review.
- Guaranteed AI-performance outcomes.

## Success metric

A delivered readiness assessment: hygiene report, source mapping, duplicate and
missing-field analysis, a data readiness score, and an AI readiness
recommendation — confirmed by a Proof Pack (score >= 70) and a Capital Asset.

## Governance

Every output carries a `governance_decision` field. PII is flagged, never
logged. Delivery is confirmed by a Proof Pack and at least one Capital Asset.
