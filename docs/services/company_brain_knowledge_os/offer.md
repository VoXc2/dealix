## Promise

Build an enterprise knowledge system where every answer carries a cited source, a confidence score, a permission record, and an audit trail — and is withheld when no evidence exists.

## Price

Setup SAR 40,000–150,000 + monthly retainer SAR 8,000–35,000. Price unit: custom (scoped per engagement).

## Duration

60 days for setup, then ongoing retainer.

## Best For

- enterprises with large document estates
- regulated teams needing source-tracked answers
- boards and executives needing decision-ready summaries
- sales and support teams answering from internal knowledge

## Deliverables

- document ingestion and knowledge base build
- retrieval with citations and confidence scores
- role-based access controls
- internal employee, sales, and support assistants
- board and executive summaries with source tracking
- no-source-no-answer enforcement
- evaluation report and usage dashboard

## Not Included

- live external sending or messaging on the client's behalf
- scraping or bulk data harvesting
- cold WhatsApp or automated outreach
- decisions made without a human reviewer

## Success metric

We commit that every answer carries a source, confidence, permission and audit record, and is withheld when no evidence exists. If the quality bar is not met, we keep working at no charge.
