# ROI Estimate

All figures below are **estimates**, not measured results. They are modelled with the client before delivery and reconciled against the monthly proof pack.

## Inputs we collect

- average minutes spent searching for an answer today
- number of such searches per employee per week
- number of employees in scope
- onboarding time for a new hire (days to productive)
- estimated cost of acting on a wrong or outdated answer

## How we estimate value

- **Time saved on search:** baseline search minutes × searches per week × headcount, multiplied by an estimated reduction.
- **Faster onboarding:** estimated reduction in days-to-productive for new hires, valued at loaded cost.
- **Fewer wrong answers:** estimated drop in unsourced or outdated answers, multiplied by the cost-of-error input.

## Confidence

- each estimate is labelled with an assumption set and a confidence note
- the monthly proof pack replaces estimates with observed usage patterns over time
- we commit to revising the estimate when actuals diverge from assumptions
