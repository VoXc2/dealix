## Context
The Governance Program creates an operating model that decays without operationalization. The upsell is primarily a retainer motion plus periodic re-assessment plus policy refresh. This file maps proof signals to next-tier offers, implementing the ladder logic in `docs/DEALIX_REVENUE_PLAYBOOK_FINAL.md` and `docs/COMPANY_SERVICE_LADDER.md`. Strategic path: `docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`.

## Operating Principle
> A governance model on paper is a hostage to drift. The retainer keeps it alive.

## Proof → Next-Offer Mapping

### Proof: Governance model is live; client wants continuity
**Signal**:
- `program_delivered = true`.
- Sponsor agrees governance is now a "permanent function".
- DPO requests ongoing engagement.

**Recommended next offer**: **Monthly Governance Retainer** (SAR 20,000–80,000/mo).
- Monthly council meeting, quarterly audit, policy refresh, risk register review, new-AI-use intake.
- Talking point: "You've built a governance OS. The retainer is the OS team. Without it, the OS decays in 6 months."

### Proof: New AI tool / use case enters the org
**Signal**:
- Sponsor mentions a new AI tool being considered.
- A department head asks "is this approved?"
- Vendor inquiry comes in.

**Recommended next offer**: **AI Readiness Review** (SAR 8,000–20,000 per tool).
- Inventory entry, risk assessment, lawful basis check, approval routing.
- Talking point: "Run this through the same engine. One review per new tool."

### Proof: Policy needs updating
**Signal**:
- Regulatory change announced.
- Internal policy review cycle due.
- Counsel raises questions.

**Recommended next offer**: **AI Usage Policy Refresh** (SAR 6,000–15,000 per policy).
- Single policy revised, counsel-reviewed, training updated.
- Talking point: "The regulation moved. Your policy must move with it."

### Proof: Audit cycle approaches
**Signal**:
- Quarterly self-audit window approaching.
- External audit scheduled.
- Board reporting cycle.

**Recommended next offer**: **Audit Run** (SAR 10,000–25,000 per cycle).
- Independent execution of the audit checklist.
- Talking point: "Run it externally. We have no department to protect."

### Proof: Sector overlay needed
**Signal**:
- Healthcare client adds new clinic line.
- Government client wins a regulated contract.
- Finance client expands to a new jurisdiction.

**Recommended next offer**: **Sector Overlay Program** (SAR 15,000–40,000 per overlay).
- Sector-specific policy, risk, and controls additions.
- Talking point: "Your new sector has its own rules. Let's overlay them on your existing model."

### Proof: Strong proof pack + sector consent
**Signal**:
- Program delivered cleanly.
- Sponsor and DPO both consent to publication.
- Sector consent obtained.

**Recommended next offer**: **Co-marketing slot on the Dealix Trust Page** (value exchange).
- Anonymized governance maturity case study (e.g., "from Level 2 to Level 4").
- Talking point: "Your governance leap is a story other enterprises need. With dual consent, we co-publish."

## Conversion Targets
- **Program → Monthly Governance Retainer** within 90 days: ≥ 60%.
- **Program → AI Readiness Review (recurring)** within 6 months: ≥ 40%.
- **Program → Policy Refresh** within 12 months: ≥ 50%.

## Anti-Patterns
- Selling Monthly Governance without a clear continuity rationale.
- Pitching Audit Run before the audit cycle is realistic.
- Promising regulatory immunity.
- Discounting the retainer below floor.
- Publishing without dual consent (sponsor + DPO).
