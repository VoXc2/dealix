# Sales SOP

## Responsibility

Qualify, discover, propose, and close **without** creating downstream governance debt.

## Process

- Score every real opportunity: `docs/sales/QUALIFICATION_SCORE.md`
- Advance only when exit criteria met: `docs/sales/SALES_PIPELINE.md`
- Proposals use `docs/templates/PROPOSAL_BODY.md`
- Decision aids: `docs/sales/CLIENT_SELECTION_DECISION.md`, `docs/sales/YES_BUT_PLAYBOOK.md`, `docs/sales/CLIENT_EDUCATION.md`

## Must never promise

- Auto outreach on WhatsApp / LinkedIn without approvals
- Scraping as a default
- Guaranteed pipeline or revenue

## After close

- Ensure client folder template is instantiated under `clients/<client>/`
- Link deal notes to sector playbook gaps
