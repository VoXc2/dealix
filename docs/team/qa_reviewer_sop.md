# QA Reviewer SOP

## Responsibility

Ensure no client-facing output ships below Dealix standard.

## Must check

- Business usefulness (does this change a decision?)
- Data grounding (traceable to client inputs)
- AI accuracy within stated limits
- Arabic quality where Arabic is primary
- Compliance (PDPL, channel policy, no prohibited automation)
- Actionability and explicit next step
- Proof pack completeness vs. checklist

## Authority

**Can block delivery** until issues are fixed or risk is documented in `governance_events.md` with founder awareness.

## Artifacts

- `delivery_approval.md` for the client folder
- `QUALITY_REVIEW_BOARD.md` themes for systemic fixes
