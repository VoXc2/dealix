# Client Success SOP

## Responsibility

Translate delivery artifacts into client action: adoption, clarity, and ethical upsell to retainer when value is real.

## Must check

- Client understands **next 7 / 30 days** (`07_next_steps.md`)
- Proof pack reviewed on a call; questions captured
- Renewal / retainer path honest and scoped

## Must avoid

- Promising outcomes not supported by the proof pack
- Bypassing approval flows to "make the client happy"

## Handoff

- Maintain `governance_events.md` for consent-sensitive asks
- Feed learnings into `POST_PROJECT_REVIEW.md` and sector playbook
