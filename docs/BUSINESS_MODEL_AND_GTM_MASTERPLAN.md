# Dealix Business Model & GTM Master Plan

## Category

**Saudi Revenue Operating System.** Not a chatbot. Not a CRM clone.
Not a WhatsApp blaster. The product helps Saudi/GCC B2B companies turn
scattered growth activity into a safe, measurable revenue OS.

## Primary ICP

- Saudi/GCC B2B SMEs (10-200 employees)
- selling to other businesses (clinics, real estate, agencies, SaaS, logistics, training)
- average deal value ≥ 2,000 SAR
- founder or sales head is the buyer
- already has *some* inbound — Dealix is not for cold-from-zero

## Excluded ICP (do NOT sell to)

- B2C / influencer marketing
- companies expecting cold WhatsApp blasting
- companies refusing consent / opt-in workflows
- enterprise expecting on-prem deployment in v0
- agencies who plan to resell the WhatsApp gate (until partner program ships)

## Service Tower (live on prod)

| Bundle | Price | Cadence | Offer |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free Diagnostic | 0 SAR | one-time, 24h | 3 improvement areas + recommended bundle |
| Growth Starter | 499 SAR | one-time, 7 days | 10 opportunities + 6 messages + Proof Pack |
| Data to Revenue | 1,500 SAR | one-time, 10 days | clean + score + safe channel + drafts |
| Partnership Growth | 3,000-7,500 SAR | one-time, 30 days | partner shortlist + scorecard + proof |
| Executive Growth OS | 2,999 SAR / month | recurring | role briefs + weekly Proof Pack |
| Full Growth Control Tower | custom | recurring | enterprise / sales-led |

## First 30 days

- Week 1: STOP gates closed (this PR + redeploy + Arabic safety verified).
- Week 2: 10 warm LinkedIn DMs / 5 Free Diagnostics delivered.
- Week 3: 2-3 paid Pilots @ 499 SAR (manual fallback / Moyasar invoice).
- Week 4: 2-3 Proof Packs delivered. First testimonial collected.

## First 90 days

- 1-2 paying customers on Executive Growth OS (after proof).
- 1 agency partner enrolled in Partnership Growth.
- 1 case study published.
- weekly learning loop running for every active customer.

## Agency partner motion

Run `partnership_growth` on an agency that already serves 5+ Saudi B2B
clients. Co-deliver a Pilot for ONE of their clients before talking
revenue share. Always referral-attributed; never blanket revenue share.

## Proof-led upsell ladder

```
Free Diagnostic → Growth Starter (499) → Data to Revenue (1.5k) →
Executive Growth OS (2.999/mo) → Full Growth Control Tower (custom)
```

Never skip steps. Never offer the next tier before the current tier's
Proof Pack is signed.

## Metrics that matter

- Diagnostic → Pilot conversion rate
- Pilot → Proof Pack on-time rate
- Proof Pack grade distribution
- Risks blocked per customer (`compliance/check-outreach`)
- Weeks active on Executive Growth OS

## Positioning

> Dealix helps Saudi/GCC B2B companies turn scattered growth activity
> into a safe, measurable revenue operating system — with consent,
> approvals, and proof at every step. No cold WhatsApp. No guarantees.

## Anti-positioning (we are NOT)

- a WhatsApp spam bot
- a generic AI assistant
- a CRM clone
- a guaranteed lead generator
- a LinkedIn automation tool

## What we do NOT build

- LinkedIn auto-DM / scrape automation
- cold WhatsApp campaigns to purchased lists
- live card-capture flows
- "guaranteed sales" marketing language
