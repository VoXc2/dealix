## Problem Statement

Saudi and GCC B2B services companies are adopting AI faster than they
can govern, measure, and operationalize it. They lack source clarity,
human approval gates, runtime governance, proof of value, and
auditability.

## Buyer Persona

- Founders / COOs of Saudi B2B services firms (40–500 headcount,
  > 20M SAR/year revenue).
- Heads of Operations at regulated-adjacent buyers (assurance,
  fintech-adjacent, healthcare-admin).

## Primary Offer

- **Free Strategic Diagnostic** (qualify → recommend paid path).
- **Revenue Intelligence Sprint** (25,000 SAR; 5 working days; produces
  Proof Pack + Capital Asset + retainer recommendation).

## Recurring Offer

- **Governed Ops Retainer** — 4,999 SAR/month: operating cadence,
  value reporting, governance review, proof maintenance.

## Product Modules
