# Saudi/MENA Localization System

Dealix outputs must fit Saudi and MENA business contexts.

## Localization Areas

- Arabic business tone  
- bilingual executive reporting  
- local sales etiquette  
- sector-specific terms  
- city/region awareness  
- PDPL-aware data handling  
- WhatsApp sensitivity  
- formal vs semi-formal tone  

## Quality Rule

Arabic output is not translation. It must be business-native.
