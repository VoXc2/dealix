# Onboarding Video Script — 3-Minute Loom (W6.5)

> **Purpose:** the founder records this 3-minute Loom and shares it with every
> new pilot customer in the Pre-Day-1 welcome email. Replaces the 30-min
> Discovery call with async context-setting, then the call focuses on the
> customer's specific data.
> **Audience:** Saudi B2B founder paying 499 SAR for the Managed Pilot.
> **Tone:** professional, direct, no hype, Arabic primary.

---

## Production Notes

- **Tool:** Loom or Vidyard (whichever has easier Arabic captions)
- **Format:** 1080p, founder webcam in bottom-right, screen share main
- **Length:** 2:30-3:15 hard limit (research: B2B attention drops sharply > 3 min)
- **Language:** Arabic primary, English subtitles available
- **Re-record cadence:** quarterly OR whenever pricing/product changes
- **Hosting:** Loom URL embedded in welcome email + landing/welcome-video.html

## Internal: Why a video at all?

1. **Filters non-serious prospects.** Watching a 3-min video before the call
   weeds out the curious-not-committed.
2. **Compresses Discovery time.** Call becomes 15 min (their specifics) instead
   of 45 min (60% of which is generic context).
3. **Reusable trust artifact.** Once recorded well, every prospect sees the
   same calm professional message — no founder fatigue.
4. **Honest disclosure of limitations.** Section 4 prevents the worst outcome:
   an enterprise customer signing then churning when SSO isn't available.
