## Delivery Notes

### Founder-only delivery (not founder + investor banker)

At Series A, founder still delivers. CFO comes Y4+. Founder-led pitch
gets premium valuation because investors see the operator.

### 60-minute meeting structure

| Minutes | Activity |
|---------|----------|
| 0-3 | Slide 1 intro |
| 3-10 | Problem (slide 2) + Insight (3) |
| 10-25 | Product (4) + Traction (5) — most time spent |
| 25-35 | Why Now (6) + Why Us (7) |
| 35-45 | Business Model (8) + Ask (9) |
| 45-55 | Q&A (most important section) |
| 55-60 | Path to Exit (10) + next steps |

### Closing line

> "We're not the biggest AI company. We're the most aligned to Saudi
> reality. That's the only fight we want to win."

### Follow-up cadence

- Same-day thank-you email + deck PDF
- Day 3: short answers to any questions raised
- Day 7: introduce a customer for reference call
- Day 14: term sheet expectation reset
- Day 30: decision deadline

### "No" politely received

If they pass:
- Thank them genuinely
- Ask: "What signal would change your mind in Q4?"
- Mark "dormant" in tracker, not "dead"
- Re-engage at next milestone (customer #100)
