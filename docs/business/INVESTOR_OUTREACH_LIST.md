## What NOT to Do (Saudi VC Etiquette)

❌ **Don't pitch into a room.** Coffee/dinner relationship first; pitch follows.

❌ **Don't email PDFs cold.** Saudi VCs prefer WhatsApp text → public-link → conversation.

❌ **Don't oversell metrics.** Saudi LPs are pattern-matchers; one inflated number burns trust.

❌ **Don't sign first SAFE you see.** Saudi market norms: capped equity + standard pro-rata, not SAFE-without-cap.

❌ **Don't take money from competing portfolios.** If a VC funds two LaaS companies, conflict of interest dilutes value.

❌ **Don't raise without customer #10.** Reason: leverage. Pre-customer rounds happen at 2-5x worse valuations.
