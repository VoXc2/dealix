# Moat Dashboard — لوحة قياس الخندق

الواجهة الداخلية تقيس **عمق الخندق** لا حجم الحماس.

---

## أبعاد مقترحة للعرض

- Governance depth  
- Proof strength  
- Product reuse  
- Capital assets  
- Market language adoption  
- Partner certification depth  
- Academy readiness  
- Benchmark maturity  
- Venture signals  
- Saudi/Arabic quality score  

**الكود المرجعي:** `weighted_moat_score` · `moat_tier` · `moat_compound_index` — `moat_os/moat_score.py`

---

## مثال قرار

```json
{
  "moat_signal": "Proof Pack adoption",
  "status": "emerging",
  "evidence": "3 clients asked for monthly proof reporting",
  "decision": "build Proof Timeline in Client Workspace"
}
```
