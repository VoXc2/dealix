# Proof Score

Eight components weighted to 100.

| Component | Weight |
| --- | ---: |
| Metric clarity | 15 |
| Source clarity | 15 |
| Evidence quality | 15 |
| Governance confidence | 15 |
| Business relevance | 15 |
| Before/after comparison | 10 |
| Retainer linkage | 10 |

Tiers: 85+ case candidate, 70–84 sales support, 55–69 internal learning, <55 weak proof.

Hard rules:
- No Proof Pack <85 → no public case.
- No Proof Pack <70 → no sales use.
