# Proof Pack v2

14-section structure including Source Passports + Blocked Risks + Limitations.

## Sections

1. Executive Summary
2. Problem
3. Inputs
4. Source Passports
5. Work Completed
6. Outputs
7. Quality Scores
8. Governance Decisions
9. Blocked Risks
10. Value Metrics
11. Limitations
12. Recommended Next Step
13. Retainer / Expansion Path
14. Capital Assets Created

## Why these three additions

- **Source Passports** prove that AI ran on permitted data.
- **Blocked Risks** record what governance prevented (risk-as-value).
- **Limitations** keep claims honest and compound trust.
