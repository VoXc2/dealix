# Proof-Driven Pricing

Raise price when proof rises.

## Triggers

- Proof score consistently > 85.
- Retainer conversion exists.
- Delivery repeatable.
- Client value visible.
- Governance trust strong.
- Product modules reduce delivery time.

## Do not raise price merely with time

Raise price because you sell: capability, governance, proof, operating cadence, reduced risk — not "more work."
