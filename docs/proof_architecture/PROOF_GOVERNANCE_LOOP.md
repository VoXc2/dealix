# Proof + Governance Loop

Every Proof Pack records what was blocked.

## Blocked Risk Events

- cold WhatsApp blocked
- unknown-source PII blocked
- guaranteed claim removed
- source-less answer rejected
- external action queued for approval

## Why

Risk prevented = business value. The Proof Pack acknowledges what didn't happen, not only what did.

## Operating discipline

- Reporting OS pulls blocked events from `governance_os` and inserts them into the Proof Pack.
- Risk Proof Packs count blocked events as a primary metric.

## Principle

> Trust protected is value created.
