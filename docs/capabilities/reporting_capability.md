# Capability Blueprint: Reporting Capability

## Business Purpose

Give leadership a consistent, honest view of performance and next actions.

## Typical Problems

- manual slide decks and spreadsheets  
- conflicting numbers  
- late monthly reports  
- no link from metric to action  

## Required Inputs

- metric definitions  
- systems of record for numbers  
- reporting cadence  
- audience (exec vs ops)  

## AI Functions

- assemble executive summaries  
- draft narrative from approved metrics  
- highlight deltas and risks  
- propose next actions (non-binding)  

## Governance Controls

- no fabricated metrics  
- source attribution for numbers  
- redaction for sensitive sections  
- human sign-off for external boards  

## KPIs

- time to produce report  
- QA score  
- consistency vs prior periods  
- action completion rate  
- stakeholder satisfaction  

## Services

- Reporting Diagnostic  
- Executive Reporting Automation  
- Monthly Exec Pack  
- Board / Investor Reporting (governed)
