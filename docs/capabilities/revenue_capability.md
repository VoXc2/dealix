# Capability Blueprint: Revenue Capability

## Business Purpose

Help the company identify, prioritize, and act on revenue opportunities.

## Typical Problems

- messy leads  
- weak CRM  
- unclear ICP  
- no prioritization  
- manual follow-up  
- poor pipeline visibility  

## Required Inputs

- account/lead list  
- ICP  
- offer  
- sales stages  
- source attribution  
- previous outcomes if available  

## AI Functions

- clean data  
- score accounts  
- segment opportunities  
- draft outreach  
- recommend next actions  
- generate revenue report  

## Governance Controls

- no cold WhatsApp  
- no LinkedIn automation  
- source required  
- claims review  
- human approval before external use  

## KPIs

- accounts scored  
- qualified accounts  
- data quality improvement  
- pipeline value  
- next actions completed  

## Services

- Revenue Diagnostic  
- Lead Intelligence Sprint  
- Pilot Conversion Sprint  
- Monthly RevOps OS
