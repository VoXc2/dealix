# Capability Blueprint: Operations Capability

## Business Purpose

Help the company automate repetitive work, reduce errors, and make operations observable.

## Typical Problems

- manual weekly/monthly reports  
- no workflow map  
- duplicate data entry  
- unclear owners  
- brittle spreadsheets  

## Required Inputs

- process list and pain points  
- systems of record  
- sample artifacts (forms, reports)  
- roles and approval points  

## AI Functions

- map and document workflows  
- draft automation specs  
- generate checklists and SOP drafts  
- fill structured outputs from approved inputs  
- surface bottlenecks in reports  

## Governance Controls

- owner and approval for any state change  
- no silent writes to systems of record  
- separation of draft vs executed action  
- logging for internal updates after approval  

## KPIs

- hours saved per workflow  
- error rate / rework  
- cycle time  
- adoption of structured outputs  
- compliance with approval gates  

## Services

- Process / Ops Diagnostic  
- AI Quick Win  
- Workflow Automation Sprint  
- Monthly AI Ops
