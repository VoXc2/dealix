# Capability Blueprint: Customer Capability

## Business Purpose

Help the company understand customers and respond with speed, consistency, and quality across channels.

## Typical Problems

- channel overload (e.g. WhatsApp)  
- no shared FAQ or playbook  
- inconsistent tone or answers  
- long resolution time  
- weak handoff between teams  

## Required Inputs

- channel list and volume samples  
- product/service definitions  
- policy boundaries  
- escalation paths  
- CRM or ticket metadata (if any)  

## AI Functions

- classify and route messages  
- draft replies (approval-first for external)  
- summarize threads  
- suggest next best action  
- generate weekly support report  

## Governance Controls

- PDPL / contactability rules  
- no unsolicited outbound on restricted channels  
- PII minimization in outputs  
- human approval before external send where required  
- audit of approved replies for sensitive cases  

## KPIs

- response time  
- first-contact resolution (where measurable)  
- consistency score / QA  
- manual hours reduced  
- escalation rate  

## Services

- Support Diagnostic  
- AI Support Desk Sprint  
- Support Playbook + KB alignment  
- Monthly Support Ops
