# Capability Blueprint: Governance Capability

## Business Purpose

Use AI safely with approvals, logs, and clear rules—not optimism.

## Typical Problems

- no AI usage policy  
- shadow AI in teams  
- unclear approval paths  
- no audit trail  
- risky external actions  

## Required Inputs

- org roles  
- systems and data classes  
- channel policies  
- risk appetite from leadership  

## AI Functions

- draft policies and checklists  
- run governance checks on outputs  
- classify risk of proposed actions  
- produce governance event summaries  

## Governance Controls

- runtime checks mandatory for production workflows  
- permission mirroring  
- approval for external and high-risk internal actions  
- incident hooks  

## KPIs

- blocked unsafe actions (count + type)  
- approval latency  
- audit completeness  
- repeat risk reduction  
- training / attestation coverage  

## Services

- AI Readiness Review  
- AI Usage Policy Pack  
- AI Governance Program  
- Governance Retainer
