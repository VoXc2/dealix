# Dealix — Support FAQ (English)

> **Authoritative source.** This file is the canonical source for
> Support OS answers. Any question outside this file is automatically
> escalated to founder review and is NEVER answered automatically.

## How do I start with Dealix?

We begin with a free **Governed Revenue & AI Ops Risk Score** — a
risk score for your revenue and AI operations plus a sample proof
pack. If it fits, we offer the **7-Day Governed Revenue & AI Ops
Diagnostic** (from 4,999 SAR).

## What's the difference between the Risk Score and the Diagnostic?

- **Risk Score** is free — a risk score plus a sample proof pack.
- **7-Day Diagnostic** runs 7 days across four tiers (Starter 4,999 ·
  Standard 9,999 · Executive 15,000 · Enterprise 25,000 SAR) and
  includes a workflow map + top 3 decisions + a proof pack. Prices:
  `docs/OFFER_LADDER_AND_PRICING.md`.

## Do you contact my customers on my behalf?

**No.** Dealix never sends any message to any customer on your
behalf. Every message is prepared as a draft, you approve it, you
send it manually.

## How fast do you respond to support questions?

- **Urgent** (payment/privacy/security): within 1 hour
- **Blocked** (can't work): same day
- **Normal**: within 24 hours
- **Question or improvement**: within 48 hours

## How do I connect Dealix to my existing system?

Today Dealix reads but does not write to external systems. We send
the setup guide after the Diagnostic is approved. HubSpot / Zoho /
Moyasar read-only integrations available.

## What if I'm not satisfied?

Refund policy is documented in `docs/REFUND_SOP.md`. A refund
applies if delivery did NOT match the documented spec for the
offer.

## Do you retain my data?

We comply with Saudi PDPL. We don't share your data with third
parties and we delete on request. Details in
`privacy_pdpl_ar_en.md`.

## Do you support the Gulf Arabic dialect?

Yes — Modern Standard Arabic + Khaleeji. English is secondary.

## Do you guarantee ROI or revenue?

**No.** Dealix does NOT guarantee revenue or sales results. We
commit to the work and to delivering the documented Proof Pack
only.

## How do I submit a support question?

Send via inbound channel (website / email). Every message is
classified by Support OS and reviewed by a human before any
external reply.
