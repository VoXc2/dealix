# Dealix — Cold Email Sequences (EN mirror)

> **Defaults:** `dry_run=true` · `approval_required=true` · `send_enabled=false`
> Every text below is an **illustrative draft** — "illustrative example" — using
> placeholder names and **non-real** numbers. No send before gates + founder approval.

This mirrors the Arabic sequence (`COLD_EMAIL_SEQUENCES_AR.md`). Five drafts:
`first_touch` → `follow_up_1` → `follow_up_2` → `proposal_intro` → `close_loop`.
Every cold draft uses:
- a **plain** subject (no fake `Re:/Fwd:` threads),
- one pain hypothesis + one light question,
- the mandatory opt-out line: **"To opt out, reply STOP."**,
- allowed verbs only (help · prepare · organize · measure · surface improvement
  opportunities · suggest · prepare drafts for approval). No forbidden claims.

Example prospect: **Digital Rise Agency** (`marketing_agencies`,
`pain_hypothesis: lead_leakage`, `offer_match: DLX-L1`).

## 6. Writing Rules

- Plain subject describing value — no fake `Re:/Fwd:`, no hype.
- One pain hypothesis + one light question per draft.
- No performance numbers without `evidence_level`; examples marked "illustrative".
- Signature uses a role/team and a general inbox only — no recipient PII.
- The opt-out line "To opt out, reply STOP." appears in every cold draft.
- Drafts stay `approval_status: pending` · `send_status: not_sent` until approval.

---

## 7. Non-Negotiable Limits

- Draft production only · no send before gates + founder approval.
- No forbidden claims (guaranteed revenue · double your revenue · guaranteed
  results · no risk · risk-free · 10x) · allowed verbs only (above).
- No fabricated clients, no fabricated metrics · placeholder names only.
- No PII · no fake `Re:/Fwd:` · every cold draft carries the opt-out line.
