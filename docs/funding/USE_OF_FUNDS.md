# Use of Funds

## Principle
Dealix does not use capital to skip proof.

Capital must accelerate:
- revenue
- trust
- delivery repeatability
- evidence infrastructure
- partner distribution

Capital must not fund:
- premature full SaaS
- marketplace
- autonomous external agents
- broad GCC launch before Saudi proof
- vanity hiring

## Bootstrapped Use of Cash
1. Founder-led sales
2. Revenue Intelligence delivery
3. Proof Pack automation
4. Capital Asset Library
5. Partner motion

## Angel / Pre-Seed Use of Funds
1. AI Ops Engineer
2. Delivery / RevOps Operator
3. Trust and Evidence productization
4. Partner development
5. Security and audit readiness

## Capital Allocation Rule
Every spend must map to one of:
- faster invoice
- stronger proof
- better margin
- lower risk
- repeatable delivery
- partner leverage
