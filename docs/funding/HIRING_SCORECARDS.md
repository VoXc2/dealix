# Hiring Scorecards

## Hire 1 — AI Ops Engineer

### Owns

- Source Passport pipeline
- Governance Runtime
- Proof Pack automation
- Evidence Graph foundation

### Do Not Hire If

- no repeatable sprint delivery
- no founder-led sales pipeline
- no paid customer signal

### First 30 Days

- stabilize MVP pipeline
- improve tests
- reduce manual proof generation
- document technical boundaries

### Success Metric

Delivery automation reduces founder delivery load by 30%.

## Hire 2 — Delivery / RevOps Operator

### Owns

- Revenue Intelligence Sprint delivery
- client data intake
- draft proof packs
- monthly value reporting

### Do Not Hire If

- playbooks are not documented
- founder has not delivered 3 similar projects
- offer scope is unstable

### First 30 Days

- run delivery checklist
- prepare client handoff drafts
- track adoption friction

### Success Metric

Can deliver 70% of sprint workflow without founder intervention.

## Hire 3 — Partnerships / GCC Growth Operator

### Owns

- anchor partner pipeline
- outreach tracking
- partner meeting preparation
- partner covenant process

### Do Not Hire If

- no partner interest
- no clear rev-share model
- no proof-backed partner narrative

### First 30 Days

- manage 20 targeted partner contacts
- book 5 meetings
- track objections

### Success Metric

Generate 2 qualified pilot intros.
