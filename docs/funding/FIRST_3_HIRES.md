# First 3 Hires — Sequencing Rules

## Principle
The first 3 hires are not staffing.
The first 3 hires turn founder-led delivery into a repeatable operating system.

## Sequencing Gate
A hire is only triggered when the corresponding "Do Not Hire If" conditions in HIRING_SCORECARDS.md are cleared.

## Hire 1 — AI Ops Engineer
Trigger:
- Invoice #1 delivered
- at least one Proof Pack produced
- founder spending more than 50% of time on delivery plumbing

## Hire 2 — Delivery / RevOps Operator
Trigger:
- 2+ paying retainers
- Revenue Intelligence Sprint delivered at least twice
- documented delivery checklist
- offer scope stable

## Hire 3 — Partnerships / GCC Growth Operator
Trigger:
- at least one qualified anchor partner conversation
- documented rev-share terms
- one Saudi case study with verified proof
- founder's sales calendar saturated

## Anti-Patterns (Do Not Do)
- hiring a marketer before Invoice #1
- hiring a "Head of Growth" before retainer motion is proven
- hiring a customer success role before retainer motion is proven
- hiring an SDR before founder has personally closed 3 deals
- hiring engineers to build features that no buyer asked for

## Failure Mode To Avoid
Raising capital, hiring fast, building features no one paid for, and discovering after burn that the offer ladder never closed.
