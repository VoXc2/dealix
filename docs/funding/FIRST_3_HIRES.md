# First 3 Hires — Sequencing Doctrine

> This document is the **order-of-operations** companion to
> `HIRING_SCORECARDS.md`. The scorecards describe *who*. This describes
> *when*.

## Rule of Sequence

Hire #2 cannot start until Hire #1's success metric is hit.
Hire #3 cannot start until Hire #2's success metric is hit.

This is a chain, not a team formation. We refuse to spend capital to
parallelize uncertainty.

## Hire #1 — AI Ops Engineer

### Trigger to Hire

- ≥ 1 paid Revenue Intelligence Sprint delivered
- ≥ 2 paying Governed Ops Retainers
- Founder is the bottleneck on Proof Pack generation, not on sales

### Why First

Founder retains sales motion. Engineering is the first thing to remove
from the founder's plate — not the last.

## Hire #2 — Delivery / RevOps Operator

### Trigger to Hire

- ≥ 3 sprints delivered (3rd repeats playbook without re-design)
- Sprint scope frozen for 30 consecutive days
- Hire #1 has shipped Proof Pack automation

### Why Second

Delivery scale comes after delivery shape is stable. Hiring delivery
into an unstable scope guarantees waste.

## Hire #3 — Partnerships / GCC Growth Operator

### Trigger to Hire

- ≥ 1 anchor partner has co-sold a paying client
- ≥ 1 published case study (verified Proof Pack + client approval)
- Hire #2 is delivering 70% of sprint workflow

### Why Third

Partners need a reference customer, a productized offer, and an
ops-capable delivery layer — not a founder doing every meeting.

## Hires Forbidden in This Phase

- VP of anything
- General Counsel
- Brand / Content Manager
- Country GM (Saudi, UAE, or otherwise)
- Solutions Architect with no delivery scope
- Marketing hire with no proof to amplify

## Capital Discipline

The cheapest way to get the wrong company is to make these hires early.
The verifier will register a Hiring violation if Hire #2 or Hire #3 is
appointed before its trigger conditions are recorded in the Capital
Asset Index.
