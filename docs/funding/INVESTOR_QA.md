# Investor Q&A

A condensed answer set for the most common investor questions about Dealix.

## Why now?
AI adoption is accelerating in Saudi Arabia and the GCC, but operational governance, evidence, and ROI discipline are lagging. Dealix sells the missing operating layer.

## Why Saudi first?
Beachhead clarity. The Saudi B2B services and regulated-adjacent buyer feels the governance pain most acutely. Saudi proof unlocks GCC partner distribution.

## Why services first, not SaaS?
Services produce proof, proof produces capital assets, capital assets compound into a product. Premature SaaS without paid demand is the failure mode Dealix is built to avoid.

## How is Dealix different from generic AI consulting?
- doctrine-first brand
- publicly verifiable commitments (Dealix Promise API)
- test-backed non-negotiables
- Source Passport before AI use
- Governance Runtime before client-facing output
- Proof Pack before public claims
- Capital Asset Library that compounds
