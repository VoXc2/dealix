## The Governed Revenue & AI Ops ladder

| Rung | Offer | Price | Trigger to unlock NEXT rung |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Governed Revenue & AI Ops Risk Score + Sample Proof Pack | Free | Diagnostic sold |
| Public entry | 7-Day Governed Revenue & AI Ops Diagnostic | 4,999 / 9,999 / 15,000 / 25,000 SAR (4 tiers) | 1 diagnostic delivered with a customer-approved proof pack |
| Sprint | Revenue Intelligence Sprint | 25,000 SAR+ | Sprint delivered with proof score ≥ 70 |
| Retainer | Governed Ops Retainer | 4,999–35,000 SAR/mo | 3+ months of consecutive paid retainer |
| Adjacent | Board Decision Memo · AI Governance / Trust Pack Lite · CRM/Data Readiness for AI | Scoped | Offered to retainer customers or on explicit demand |

## Rung 0 — Governed Revenue & AI Ops Risk Score (entry)

- **Promise:** a Governed Revenue & AI Ops Risk Score plus a sample proof pack that shows the delivery shape.
- **Inputs needed:** short questionnaire via `diagnostic.html`.
- **Output:** risk score across revenue and AI-ops dimensions + sample proof pack.
- **Hard rule:** NO Diagnostic invoice until the Risk Score is in the customer's hands.

## Public entry — 7-Day Governed Revenue & AI Ops Diagnostic

- **Promise:** workflow map → source/data quality review → approval-boundary map → evidence gaps → top-3 decisions → proof pack → sprint recommendation, in 7 days.
- **Price:** four tiers — Starter 4,999 SAR · Standard 9,999 SAR · Executive 15,000 SAR · Enterprise 25,000 SAR.
- **Payment:** Moyasar or bank transfer per the live payment SOP.
- **Hard rule:** No external send, no guarantee, no fake metric in the proof pack.

## Sprint — Revenue Intelligence Sprint

- **Promise:** governed execution of the top decisions surfaced by the Diagnostic, with a full proof pack.
- **Price:** 25,000 SAR+, scoped.
- **Hard rule:** Cannot upsell from Diagnostic → Sprint without a customer-approved proof pack and a named workflow owner.

## Retainer — Governed Ops Retainer

- **Promise:** a monthly governed cycle — diagnosis, prepared decisions, monthly proof pack, KPI report, strategic review.
- **Price:** 4,999–35,000 SAR/mo, scoped.
- **Hard rule:** Cannot offer until at least one Sprint has been delivered with proof for the customer.

## Adjacent — Board Memo · Trust Pack Lite · Data Readiness for AI

- **Promise:** scoped one-off offers for retainer customers or specific demand.
- **Price:** scoped.
- **Hard rule:** Each adjacent offer ships its own proof; no offer with metrics the customer did not supply.

## Forbidden across all rungs

- Skipping rungs (e.g. Risk Score → Retainer without a Diagnostic).
- Adding a tier with metrics the customer did not supply.
- Public mention of a customer name without signed permission.
- Cold outreach / scraping / live charge without policy at any rung.
- Discount without an asset in return (testimonial, referral, faster payment, public anonymous case, partner intro).

## Pricing governance

Pricing changes require an explicit, logged founder decision in the Decision Ledger
(this ladder: D-003, 2026-05-17). The ≥3-paid-pilot rule is a validation target to
confirm the price, not a precondition to publish it. See
[`ledgers/DECISION_LEDGER.md`](ledgers/DECISION_LEDGER.md).
