# Dealix Company Service Ladder

> **Canonical source of truth:** `auto_client_acquisition/service_catalog/registry.py`.
> One offer per maturity stage. Each rung unlocks ONLY when the prior
> rung has shipped real evidence.

## The 5-rung ladder

| Rung | Offer | Price (SAR) | Purpose | Trigger to unlock NEXT rung |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Free Diagnostic | 0 (Free) | clarity | 3 diagnostics delivered |
| 2 | Sprint — Value Proof Sprint | 2,500 (one-time) | first paid proof | 1 paid pilot fully delivered |
| 3 | Pilot — Operating Pilot | 9,500 (one-time) | operational validation | 3 paid pilots in the same sector |
| 4 | Retainer — Managed Operations | 6,000–18,000 / month | continuity | 3+ months of consecutive paid retainer |
| 5 | Enterprise — Enterprise & Custom AI | 45,000–120,000 (per SOW) | scale | — |

Plus one distribution channel (NOT a ladder rung): **Agency Partner OS**
— custom; co-branded diagnostics for a partner's clients + 30% commission.

## Rung 1 — Free Diagnostic (entry)

- **Promise:** 1-page bilingual diagnostic in 48 hours.
- **Output:** 3 ranked opportunities + 1 message draft + 1 risk +
  best-channel recommendation + next-step decision passport.
- **Hard rule:** NO paid offer until the Diagnostic is in the customer's hands.

## Rung 2 — Sprint (first paid product)

- **Promise:** Company Brain v1, cleaned lead board, Top 20 opportunities,
  Decision Passports, Arabic Draft Pack, follow-up plan, Proof Pack.
- **Price:** 2,500 SAR. **Refund:** full 100% within 14 days.
- **Hard rule:** No `auto-send`, no guarantee, no fake metric in the Proof Pack.

## Rung 3 — Pilot (operational validation)

- **Promise:** 30-day operating pilot — 4 weekly pipeline cycles with
  executive reports and a closing Proof Pack.
- **Price:** 9,500 SAR. **Refund:** 75% if the KPI commitment is unmet within 45 days.
- **Hard rule:** Cannot upsell to a retainer without a customer-approved
  Proof Pack from the Pilot.

## Rung 4 — Retainer / Managed Operations

- **Promise:** Monthly operating cadence — revenue + support + ops +
  delivery + proof; weekly audits; board pack at the upper band.
- **Price:** 6,000–18,000 SAR / month (scoped by operations volume).
- **Hard rule:** Cannot offer until a paid Pilot has been delivered.

## Rung 5 — Enterprise / Custom AI

- **Promise:** Custom AI build + integrations + governance program.
- **Price:** 45,000–120,000 SAR — scope, milestones, and refunds fixed
  in a signed SOW before work begins.
- **Hard rule:** Scope is never open-ended; the SOW governs.

## Forbidden across all rungs

- ❌ Skipping rungs (e.g. Diagnostic → Retainer without a Pilot)
- ❌ Promising a tier with metrics the customer didn't supply
- ❌ Public mention of a customer name without signed permission
- ❌ Live charge / cold outreach / scraping at any rung

## Bilingual one-liner

**Arabic:** سُلَّم خدمات من خمسة مستويات — كل مستوى يفتح فقط بعد دليل حقيقي من المستوى السابق.
**English:** A five-rung service ladder — each rung unlocks ONLY after real evidence from the rung below.
