# Dealix Sales Qualification Score

Don't waste hours on every lead. Score each one out of 100 before scheduling
a 30-minute discovery call.

## Scoring rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|------:|
| Has a clear pain (named in 1 sentence) | 20 |
| Has data or process (not greenfield) | 15 |
| Has budget signal (mentions SAR or "what's the price?") | 20 |
| Has a named decision-maker (owner) | 15 |
| Fits Saudi ICP (`docs/go-to-market/icp_saudi.md`) | 15 |
| Open to review/approval workflow (no "just send it") | 10 |
| Does NOT request forbidden actions | 5 |
| **Total** | **100** |

## Decision

- **80+ — Pursue aggressively**. Discovery call within 48 hours, SOW template prepared.
- **60–79 — Nurture / Diagnostic**. Recommend the SAR 3,500–7,500 Revenue Diagnostic to validate.
- **40–59 — Low priority**. Add to monthly nurture; no synchronous time invested.
- **< 40 — Disqualify**. Polite no with referral to free content. Save your time.

## Hard disqualifiers (auto-zero)

These cancel any score above and route immediately to "Disqualify":

- Requests cold WhatsApp / SMS / LinkedIn automation.
- Requests guaranteed-outcome contract ("نضمن X leads").
- Refuses to acknowledge PDPL.
- No decision-maker access.
- No budget at all (not even Diagnostic).
- Asks to "white-label as ours and resell" without partner agreement.

## How to score (5 minutes per lead)

```
1. Read the inbound message or LinkedIn profile.
2. Score each criterion 0 / half-weight / full-weight.
3. Sum.
4. Pick action based on band above.
5. Record the score in CRM (or `docs/sales/sales_pipeline_log.md`).
```

## Bias guardrails

- Don't over-score because you "feel a good vibe". Stick to evidence.
- Don't under-score because the company is small. SME tier is valid.
- Don't over-score because the prospect knows AI buzzwords. Pain is what matters.
