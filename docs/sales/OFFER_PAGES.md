## Entry framing (scope variations) — تأطير نقاط الدخول (تنويعات نطاق)

These are **not new products and not new prices.** They are different ways to present the **Free Mini Diagnostic (0 SAR)** and the **Revenue Proof Sprint (499 SAR)** so the buyer faces less resistance at the first step. Each item below is a scoping or positioning variation of an existing canonical offer.

هذه ليست منتجات جديدة وليست أسعاراً جديدة. هي طرق مختلفة لعرض **التشخيص المصغّر المجاني (0 ريال)** و**Revenue Proof Sprint (499 ريال)** لتقليل مقاومة الشراء عند الخطوة الأولى. كل بند أدناه هو تنويع نطاق أو تأطير لعرض قانوني موجود.

| Framing | What it is | Variation of |
|---|---|---|
| Lead Follow-up Audit | Review ~10 leads and show where follow-up stopped | Free Mini Diagnostic / Revenue Proof Sprint |
| WhatsApp/Email Follow-up Pack | Prepare follow-up messages classified by reply type | Scope of the Revenue Proof Sprint |
| Agency Proof Pack | A proof artifact showing what happened after the campaign | Scope of the Revenue Proof Sprint |
| CRM Hygiene Snapshot | Check whether CRM data is fit for operation/AI | Framing of the Free Mini Diagnostic |
| AI Approval Policy Lite | Define what AI may draft vs what needs approval | Scope of the Revenue Proof Sprint |
| 30-min Revenue Workflow Review | A fast session to find one workflow worth diagnosing | Entry to the Free Mini Diagnostic |
