# Client Education — How to Buy AI Implementation

> Most Saudi B2B prospects don't know how to buy AI. They've been pitched
> tools, agents, and chatbots without an operating model. Dealix's first
> job in every sales conversation is to **educate** — not sell. The sale
> follows naturally when the prospect understands the right path.

## The 7 things a good AI project must have

Use this checklist with prospects in discovery. Score their readiness against it. A "1" is missing, a "5" is strong.

| # | Element | Why it matters |
|---|---------|----------------|
| 1 | A clear business problem named in 1 sentence | "Make AI work" is not a problem; "our weekly CEO report takes 6 hours" is |
| 2 | Usable data (or a path to it) | Without data, you're not implementing AI — you're talking about it |
| 3 | A process owner | One named person responsible for the workflow; without this, the project dies |
| 4 | Human approval points | AI proposes, human approves — for every side-effect action |
| 5 | A quality review step | What does "good output" mean? Score it before shipping |
| 6 | A measurable output | Hours saved, errors reduced, leads ranked, citations covered — pick at least one |
| 7 | Governance: source, PII, consent, audit | Especially for PDPL-regulated work in Saudi Arabia |

If a prospect scores < 25/35, they are NOT ready for an AI Sprint — they need a Diagnostic.

## The 5 ways AI projects fail (warn the prospect)

1. **Started with the tool, not the problem.** Bought ChatGPT/agents/automation, then looked for a problem to solve. The fix: pick the painful workflow first.
2. **Skipped data readiness.** Built an AI that hallucinates because the underlying data is garbage. The fix: data quality before any model.
3. **No process owner.** Project drifts; no one validates outputs; quality collapses. The fix: name the owner on Day 1.
4. **No governance.** Cold WhatsApp goes out, PDPL is breached, reputation suffers. The fix: governance gates in the workflow, not bolted on.
5. **No proof of impact.** Project ends with a deliverable nobody can measure. The fix: Proof Pack with before/after numbers.

## How to use this doc in a sales call

1. Open the discovery with: "Most companies struggle to operationalize AI. Let's walk through 7 things every good AI project needs."
2. Walk the 7-element table. Score the prospect's readiness in real time.
3. If < 25: pitch the Diagnostic.
4. If ≥ 25 + clear pain: pitch a Sprint (Lead Intel / AI Quick Win / Company Brain).
5. End with: "Want me to send a sample executive report from a similar customer?" → uses Proof Pack as social proof.
