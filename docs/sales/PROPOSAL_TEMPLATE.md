## Context
This is the canonical Dealix proposal template for Sprint and Pilot
sales. It removes the constraint of bespoke proposals written from
scratch and enforces the legal / commercial discipline established in
`docs/business/MANAGED_PILOT_OFFER.md`, `docs/sales/PILOT_AGREEMENT_DRAFT.md`,
and `docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`. Every section below is
mandatory; nothing else gets added without compliance review.

## Required Sections

### 1. Problem

One paragraph describing the **specific** pain in the client's words.
No generic AI marketing language.

### 2. Goal

One concrete sentence describing what success looks like — measurable,
time-bound, and tied to a business function.

### 3. Scope

**Included.** Bullet list of deliverables; reference the chosen Sprint
sales page (Lead Intelligence / AI Quick Win / Company Brain).

**Not included.** Bullet list — non-negotiable.

| Excluded | Why |
|---|---|
| Automated mass sending (email / WhatsApp) | Compliance + brand risk |
| Scraping or unknown-source data | Lawful basis required |
| Guaranteed meetings or revenue | Forbidden claim |
| Live WhatsApp automation | Not productized yet |
| LinkedIn automation | ToS + risk |
| CRM integration with HubSpot / Salesforce | Separate scope |

### 4. Timeline

Per chosen Sprint, copy from the relevant sales page.

### 5. Client Inputs

Bullet list of what the client must provide:
- Data exports / access.
- A single owner with decision authority.
- 2–3 hours over the engagement.
- Sign-offs on access to sensitive data.

### 6. Price

| Item | Amount | Schedule |
|---|---|---|
| Sprint fee | SAR <X> | 50% kickoff / 50% delivery |
| Optional add-ons | SAR <Y> | As agreed |
| VAT | per ZATCA rules | At invoice |

ZATCA invoicing per `docs/INVOICING_ZATCA_READINESS.md`.

### 7. Success Metrics

Concrete, measurable, **delivery-based** — never revenue-guaranteed.
Examples:
- Number of accounts cleaned.
- Top opportunities ranked.
- Hours saved per workflow.
- Citation rate.

### 8. Next Step

One sentence: "Sign this proposal and we begin within 5 business
days." Plus a contact for the next call.

## Forbidden in Any Proposal

- "Guaranteed X meetings."
- "Increase revenue by Y%."
- "Replace your sales team."
- "Fully autonomous AI."
- "Zero risk."
- "No human required."

## Interfaces

| Inputs | Outputs | Owners | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Discovery decision template | Filled proposal | Growth / Founder | Within 48h |
| Compliance flags | Removed / re-scoped items | Compliance Owner | Pre-send |
| Signed proposal | Kickoff scheduled | Delivery + CSM | Per win |

## Metrics

- **Proposal acceptance rate**.
- **Time-to-proposal** after discovery (target ≤ 48h).
- **Average sprint price**.
- **Proposal-to-Sprint conversion**.
