# Cold-outreach sequence — 3 touches to a Saudi B2B prospect (EN)

> The first-30-days outbound playbook for the founder.
> Goal: land 5 paying customers (SAR 499 / seat / month Growth tier
> or higher) in the first 30 days of the commercial launch.

## Pre-flight — what to have ready

- A vertical-tagged brochure PDF URL (one click away):
  `https://api.dealix.me/api/v1/marketing/brochure/<vertical>.pdf?locale=en`
- A live trial signup link the prospect can use:
  `https://dealix.me/trial?vertical=<vertical>`
- The Moyasar SAR checkout URL on standby:
  `https://dealix.me/checkout.html?tier=growth`
- A "competitor comparison" page that fits the prospect's existing
  tool (`landing/comparisons/{hubspot,salesforce,gong,salesloft,outreach,apollo}.html`).

## Touch 1 — Email (Day 0)

**Subject:** Quick question on `<their commercial stack>`

```
Hi <FirstName>,

I run Dealix — the Saudi B2B growth OS built around Arabic Khaliji
agents, ZATCA Phase 2 invoicing, and PDPL-clean contactability.

Two questions a procurement officer at <their company> would ask
that <competitor> doesn't answer well today:
  1. Where does <competitor> store your customer data?
  2. How does <competitor> issue a ZATCA Phase 2 invoice?

If those land, here's a 90-second walkthrough:
  https://dealix.me/comparisons/<competitor>.html

And a sector-specific brochure your team can read on the way home:
  https://api.dealix.me/api/v1/marketing/brochure/<vertical>.pdf?locale=en

Worth a 20-minute call this week?

— <Founder>
```

## Touch 2 — WhatsApp (Day 3)

Short, Khaliji-tone, value-only. Keep under 5 lines.

```
السلام عليكم <FirstName> — أرسلت لكم بالأمس عن Dealix
(ZATCA Phase 2 + PDPL مدمج + وكلاء عربيين). هل
الأسبوع القادم 20 دقيقة يناسب فريقكم؟ — <Founder>
```

## Touch 3 — Trial-link nudge (Day 7)

A no-card 14-day pilot. The CTA the prospect remembers.

```
Hi <FirstName>,

If you'd rather not block a meeting, the 14-day no-card pilot lets
your team try Dealix on your own data first:
  https://dealix.me/trial?vertical=<vertical>

Day-7 deliverable: a "Proof Pack" you can hand a procurement team
showing your ROI vs <competitor>. SAR-billing flips on at day 14
only if you say yes.

— <Founder>
```

## Closer — when they accept the trial

Day 7 we run them through the brochure + Proof Pack. On the day-14
upgrade conversation, the founder hands them:

1. The Moyasar checkout URL with the right tier pre-selected:
   `https://dealix.me/checkout.html?tier=growth`
2. The procurement bundle:
   `https://dealix.me/procurement/index.html`
3. The vendor-questionnaire pre-fill (helps their security team):
   `docs/procurement/vendor_questionnaire_prefill.md`

Closing rate target: 30% of trials become paying customers by day 21.
