## Context

Proposals at Dealix are not creative documents. They are
machine-assembled outputs from approved building blocks, priced by
`docs/company/PRICING_ENGINE.md`, scoped against
`docs/company/PROJECT_ACCEPTANCE_SYSTEM.md`, and aligned with the
pilot offer template `docs/business/MANAGED_PILOT_OFFER.md`. Every
client-facing proposal must include the ten mandatory sections
below. Anything else is a red flag.

## Proposal Principles

A Dealix proposal is:

- **Outcome-led** — opens with the buyer's outcome, not our pitch.
- **Scoped** — explicit Included and Excluded lists.
- **Priced** — single number derived from Pricing Engine, no ranges.
- **Governance-anchored** — approvals, PDPL, audit named.
- **Time-bounded** — delivery dates not estimates.
- **Proof-promised** — Proof Pack named as a deliverable.
- **Next-step-clear** — single CTA at the end.

## The 10 Mandatory Sections

Every proposal contains these in this order:

1. **Problem** — restated in the buyer's words (max 4 sentences).
2. **Goal** — one outcome statement + one measurable metric.
3. **Scope — included** — bullet list of what we will deliver.
4. **Scope — excluded** — bullet list of what we will not (the
   exclusion block below).
5. **Required inputs** — data, access, sponsor time, decisions.
6. **Timeline** — milestones with dates.
7. **Price** — total in SAR; payment terms; what triggers each
   tranche.
8. **Governance** — approvals model, PDPL handling, audit promise.
9. **Metrics** — success metric, measurement method, when measured.
10. **Next step** — one sentence: what the buyer signs and when.

## Universal Exclusion Block

Paste verbatim in section 4 of every proposal.

> **Excluded from this engagement (unless added in writing):**
>
> - Spam automation, cold WhatsApp, LinkedIn automation, scraping
>   of personal data.
> - Guarantees of business results (deals, revenue, rankings).
> - Custom integrations beyond the named systems above.
> - Unlimited revisions; two rounds included, further revisions
>   billed at standard rate.
> - Hosting fees, third-party SaaS subscriptions, paid
>   advertising spend.
> - Translations into languages other than Arabic and English.
> - On-call support outside business hours unless a retainer is
>   signed.
> - Regulatory advice (legal, medical, financial). We provide
>   tooling and process, not regulated advice.
> - Any work that would breach PDPL, internal client policy, or
>   the Dealix Operating Constitution.

## Governance Block Standard

- All external actions require named-approver sign-off in the
  Approval log.
- All AI runs are recorded in the AI Run Ledger
  (`docs/product/AI_RUN_PROVENANCE.md`).
- PDPL handling per `docs/DPA_DEALIX_FULL.md`; data residency in
  client tenant unless agreed otherwise.
- Forbidden actions list from
  `docs/DEALIX_OPERATING_CONSTITUTION.md` applies.
- Audit log accessible to client during and after engagement.

## Workflow

1. After Acceptance + Pricing, sales drafts the proposal using this
   library.
2. Sales Lead reviews against the 10-section checklist.
3. CEO sign-off for any proposal ≥ SAR 100,000 or any discount.
4. Proposal sent. Signed proposals filed in the Engagement record.
