## Context
This is the public-facing sales page for the Lead Intelligence Sprint —
Dealix's hero offer for the first ICP (B2B services, 5–50 staff). It
operationalizes the offer ladder defined in
`docs/OFFER_LADDER_AND_PRICING.md` and the strategic positioning in
`docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`. It removes the constraint
of bespoke pitching and gives Sales a single, fixed, repeatable page.

## Headline

> **Turn scattered B2B opportunities into a ranked, actionable pipeline
> in 10 days.**

## The Problem

B2B service companies in KSA typically have:
- Leads spread across sheets, WhatsApp, inbox, and paper.
- A CRM that is empty, abandoned, or filled inconsistently.
- Sales reps unsure which 20 accounts to call **this week**.
- Proposals drafted from scratch every time.
- No shared, source-cited view of pipeline health.

## What You Get (Deliverables)

1. **Data cleanup** — consolidated, de-duplicated, normalized.
2. **Duplicate / missing detection** — explicit gaps report.
3. **Top 50 opportunities ranked** — using Dealix scoring rules.
4. **Top 10 immediate actions** — ranked by speed-to-revenue.
5. **Outreach draft pack** — manually reviewable message templates.
6. **Mini CRM board** — usable on day one (Notion / Sheets / Trello).
7. **Proof Pack** — before/after metrics, time saved, value created.

## What's NOT Included

- Automated mass sending (email / WhatsApp).
- Scraping or unknown-source data.
- Guaranteed meetings or revenue.
- Live WhatsApp automation.
- LinkedIn automation.
- CRM integration with HubSpot / Salesforce (separate scope).
- Cold lists from third parties.

## Timeline

| Day | Milestone |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | Data intake + access setup |
| 3–4 | Cleanup, dedup, normalization |
| 5–6 | Scoring + ranking |
| 7–8 | Draft pack + mini CRM build |

| 10 | Delivery, walkthrough, Proof Pack |

## Price

**SAR 9,500 – 18,000** (fixed) depending on data volume and number of
sources to consolidate. 50% on kickoff, 50% on delivery.

## Who It's For

- B2B service companies, 5–50 staff.
- Riyadh / Jeddah / Eastern Province.
- Founder, GM, or Sales Manager as buyer.
- Owner willing to allocate 2–3 internal hours over 10 days.
