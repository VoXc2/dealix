## Context
The AI Quick Win Sprint is the smallest, fastest entry to Dealix —
designed for prospects whose pain is a recurring weekly workflow rather
than scattered pipeline data. It enforces the constraint defined in
`docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md` and the service ladder in
`docs/COMPANY_SERVICE_LADDER.md`: we never sell autonomy, we sell
governed, human-in-the-loop AI workflows that produce measurable time
savings in 10 days.

## Headline

> **Pick one weekly workflow. Save hours. Prove value.**

## The Problem

Teams use AI tools, but:
- Each person uses them differently.
- There is no policy or review step.
- No one tracks hours saved.
- Outputs are not auditable.
- Sensitive data sometimes ends up in public chatbots.

## Target Workflows (Examples)

- Weekly operations / sales report.
- Lead enrichment from public sources.
- Message drafting (proposals, follow-ups, support replies).
- Document summarization for management.
- Meeting note summarization with action items.

## What You Get (Deliverables)

1. **Workflow map** — current vs. AI-assisted, step by step.
2. **AI-assisted draft generator** — prompt + template + guardrails.
3. **Human approval step** — explicit review checkpoint.
4. **Time-saved estimate** — measured before/after.
5. **Governance note** — what data can / cannot be used.
6. **Proof Pack** — outputs, hours saved, risks identified.

## Governance Rules

- No sensitive PII ever enters a public AI tool.
- Every output passes through a human reviewer.
- Source citations required when the workflow consumes documents.
- "Insufficient evidence" mode supported when inputs are missing.
- Conforms to the AI usage stance in
  `docs/AI_STACK_DECISIONS.md` and `docs/ops/PDPL_RETENTION_POLICY.md`.
