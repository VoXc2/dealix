# Sales Playbook

## Opening

We help Saudi companies turn messy operations and data into AI-powered workflows with measurable outcomes.

## Discovery questions

- Where do you lose time?
- Where do you lose leads?
- What reports are manual?
- Where is knowledge scattered?
- What AI tools do employees already use?

## Qualification

- Budget
- Data availability
- Owner
- Urgency
- Risk
