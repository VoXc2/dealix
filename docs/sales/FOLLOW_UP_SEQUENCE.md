## Context
The follow-up sequence is the post-discovery, post-proposal cadence
used to convert interest into a signed Sprint without spamming the
prospect. It removes the constraint of "follow-up forgotten or done in
random tone" and enforces the manual-only discipline of
`docs/sales/SALES_PIPELINE_V1.md` and the strategic posture in
`docs/V14_COMPREHENSIVE_STRATEGIC_PLAN.md`. Every message in this
sequence is tagged **"draft only"** — sent manually, never automated.

## Sequence Overview

| Day | Step | Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Thank-you + recap | Email or WhatsApp business | Reinforce mutual understanding |
| 2 | Diagnostic / Sprint proposal | Email (PDF) | Move to commercial step |
| 7 | Anonymized proof-pack example | Email | Build belief via evidence |
| 14 | Close-or-nurture decision | Founder call / Email | End ambiguity |

## Day 0 — Thank-You + Recap

**Trigger.** End of discovery call.
**Send within.** Same day, ≤ 4 hours after call.
**Includes.**
- 3-line recap of the dominant pain.
- 2-line recap of the recommended Sprint.
- Promise of proposal within 48 hours.
- Single CTA: "Reply if anything needs correcting."

> Draft only. Sent manually by the call owner.

## Day 2 — Proposal

**Trigger.** Day 0 sent + no blockers found.
**Send within.** 48 hours after call.
**Includes.**
- Proposal PDF from `docs/sales/PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md`.
- One-paragraph cover note.
- One sentence on payment terms.
- Single CTA: "Sign or send questions."

## Day 7 — Proof-Pack Nudge

**Trigger.** Day 2 proposal sent + no reply.
**Send within.** Day 7 from call.
**Includes.**
- Anonymized example of a previous Proof Pack (or a representative
  preview when no prior delivery exists).
- One paragraph framing the next 10 days if signed today.
- Single CTA: "Want to schedule kickoff for next week?"

## Day 14 — Close-or-Nurture

**Trigger.** Day 7 nudge sent + no reply OR ambiguous reply.
**Send within.** Day 14.
**Includes.**
- Direct ask: "Should we close this out or move to nurture?"
- 1-line offer of a free 10-minute call to decide.
- Single CTA: "Pick one — Close / Nurture / Call."

If no reply by Day 17 → set stage to **Lost / Nurture** in
`docs/sales/SALES_PIPELINE_V1.md`.

## Tone Rules

- Saudi/Gulf business register; concise; no emojis.
- Never imply scarcity or pressure.
- Never re-pitch — refer back to the proposal.
- No forwarded "did you see this?" prompts.
- One CTA per message.

## Manual-Only Rule

This sequence is a **draft cadence**, not an automation. No tool sends
on behalf of a rep. Drafts are written, reviewed against
`docs/sales/OBJECTION_HANDLER.md`, and sent by a human from an
authorized channel.

## Interfaces
