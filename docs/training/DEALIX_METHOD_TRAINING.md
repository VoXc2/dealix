# Dealix Method Training

## Goal

Train team members to deliver Dealix services **consistently** — same standard as the founder.

## Method (Dealix Method)

1. Discover  
2. Diagnose  
3. Design  
4. Build  
5. Validate  
6. Deliver  
7. Prove  
8. Expand  

Reference: [`DEALIX_METHOD_AR.md`](../company/DEALIX_METHOD_AR.md), [`DEALIX_STANDARD.md`](../company/DEALIX_STANDARD.md).

## Exercise (simulation)

Take a **sample client** (use `simulations/` packs) and produce:

- Scope (`00_scope.md` pattern)
- Delivery checklist
- QA checklist
- Proof pack outline
- Next-step proposal (`docs/templates/PROPOSAL_BODY.md`)

## Pass criteria

- All eight Dealix Standard items addressed in writing
- Forbidden channels / claims never promised
- Trainer sign-off on [`docs/audit/CLIENT_PROJECT_AUDIT.md`](../audit/CLIENT_PROJECT_AUDIT.md) (practice)
