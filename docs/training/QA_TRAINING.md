# QA Training

## A reviewer must check

- **Business usefulness** — does this change a decision?
- **Data grounding** — traceable to client inputs / approved sources?
- **AI accuracy** — within stated limits; uncertainty visible?
- **Arabic quality** — where Arabic is primary
- **Compliance** — PDPL, channels, no prohibited automation
- **Actionability** — clear next step
- **Proof pack** — complete vs. template

## Reviewer authority

A QA reviewer **can block delivery** until issues are fixed or risks are escalated with written approval.

Artifacts: `docs/quality/OUTPUT_QA_SCORECARD.md`, `clients/<client>/delivery_approval.md`.
