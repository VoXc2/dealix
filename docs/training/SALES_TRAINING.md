# Sales Training

## Do not sell “AI tools.”

Sell **business outcomes**: clearer pipeline, faster proposals, governed outreach, measurable proof.

## Default pitch

Dealix turns AI from experiments into **operating systems** with data readiness, workflow design, governance, measurable outputs, and proof of impact — **Arabic-first**, **Saudi-aware**, **approval-first** for external actions.

## Qualification

Do **not** pursue clients who:

- want **spam** or cold blast outreach
- want **guaranteed** results you cannot ground
- have **no owner** for data and decisions
- have **no budget** or procurement path
- **refuse** approvals for external communications
