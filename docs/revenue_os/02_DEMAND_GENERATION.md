**Featured:** (1) Sample Proof Pack — (2) Diagnostic Deck — (3) لقطة Dashboard —
(4) AI & Revenue Ops Risk Score — (5) Book Diagnostic Review.

**محاور المحتوى:** فشل حوكمة AI · جاهزية CRM/البيانات · تسرّب الإيراد ·
أدلة موثقة · decision passports · automation بموافقة أولاً · دروس بناء Dealix.

50 حساب مستهدف · 20 warm/semi-warm · 20 ICP مباشر · 10 partners.

### Machine D — Partner Channel

fractional COOs/CROs. العرض: أنت تجيب العميل أو تدخلنا؛ Dealix تعمل التشخيص

### Machine E — Risk Score Funnel

Lead magnet: **AI & Revenue Ops Risk Score**. الأسئلة:

1. هل عندكم CRM؟
2. هل عندكم follow-up workflow؟
3. هل تستخدمون AI في المبيعات/العمليات؟

5. هل تعرفون مصدر كل قرار؟

النتيجة: Low → nurture · Medium → sample proof pack · High → diagnostic review.
**جمع البيانات بموافقة أولاً؛ لا PII في السجلات.**

كل تشخيص ينتج: درس مجهول الهوية · framework post · objection insight ·
proof snippet · partner angle. **بدون ذكر العميل أو بياناته إلا بموافقة.**

## English

### Machine A — Founder LinkedIn

The strongest first channel. Its goal is trust, not a direct sale.

**Headline:** Founder at Dealix | Governed Revenue & AI Ops for GCC Teams

**About:** I help GCC teams turn AI experiments and revenue operations into
governed, measurable workflows — with source clarity, approval boundaries,
evidence trails, and proof of value. Dealix is not an AI chatbot company or an
automation reseller.

**Featured:** (1) Sample Proof Pack — (2) Diagnostic Deck — (3) Dashboard
screenshot — (4) AI & Revenue Ops Risk Score — (5) Book Diagnostic Review.

**Content pillars:** AI governance failures · CRM/data readiness · revenue
leakage · evidence trails · decision passports · approval-first automation ·
founder lessons building Dealix.

### Machine B — Proof-led landing page

Page: `/dealix-diagnostic`

> **Hero:** Turn AI experiments and revenue operations into governed,
> measurable workflows.
>
> **Sub:** A 7-day diagnostic for GCC teams that need source clarity, approval
> boundaries, evidence trails, and proof of value.
>
> **CTA 1:** Get Sample Proof Pack — **CTA 2:** Book Diagnostic Review.

The Proof Pack is the smart bait — not "Book a call" alone.

### Machine C — Direct Outbound (small ABM, not spam)

50 target accounts · 20 warm/semi-warm · 20 direct ICP · 10 partners.
One message, one follow-up, one proof asset. **Every send is a draft +
founder approval.**

### Machine D — Partner Channel

Target: HubSpot/Zoho/ERP/AI/GRC consultants, VC operators, agency owners,
fractional COOs/CROs. The offer: you bring the customer or bring us in; Dealix
runs the diagnostic and Proof Pack; you take implementation or a referral fee.

### Machine E — Risk Score Funnel

Lead magnet: **AI & Revenue Ops Risk Score**. The questions:

1. Do you have a CRM?
2. Do you have a follow-up workflow?
3. Do you use AI in sales/operations?
4. Is there approval before any external send?
5. Do you know the source of every decision?
6. Can you link a workflow to a financial outcome?
7. Do you have a proof pack for any AI initiative?

Result: Low → nurture · Medium → sample proof pack · High → diagnostic review.
**Data collection is consent-first; no PII in logs.**

### Machine F — Delivery-to-Content Loop

Every diagnostic produces: one anonymised lesson · one framework post · one
objection insight · one proof snippet · one partner angle. **Never naming the
customer or their data without consent.**
