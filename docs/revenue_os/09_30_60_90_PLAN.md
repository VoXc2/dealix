## English

### A best-founder operating week — 90 minutes/day

- **30m Revenue Control:** review leads · approve drafts · move stages ·
  review invoices · review blocked approvals.
- **30m Trust Building:** comment on 5 target accounts · 5 connections ·
  3 partner messages · post a proof snippet or framework.
- **30m Conversion:** follow up interested leads · push a scope · review a
  meeting brief · send a proof pack to whoever asked.
- **Friday — CEO Review:** see `07_EXPANSION_AND_LEARNING.md`.

### The 30-day plan

| Week | Focus | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — System Setup | landing page · risk-score form · CRM pipeline · lead scoring · gated Proof Pack · booking link · payment process · LinkedIn profile · first 50 accounts + 20 partners | 10 conversations · 3 proof pack requests · 1 meeting |
| 2 — Market Proof | daily LinkedIn · 10 DMs · 10 emails · 5 partner messages · first calls · first scopes | 3 meetings · 2 scopes · 1 invoice |
| 3 — Revenue Proof | close a paid diagnostic · deliver first proof pack · extract objections · anonymised content · ask for a referral | 1 paid diagnostic · 1 proof pack delivered · 1 sprint candidate |
| 4 — Repeatability | repeat the winning ICP · tighten the message · FAQ · partner loop · a second diagnostic opportunity | a second paid diagnostic or a clear retainer path |

### The 90-day plan

| Month | Name | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Proof | 1–2 paid diagnostics · one repeatable ICP · one partner relationship |
| 2 | Repeat | 3–5 diagnostics · 1 Revenue Sprint · 2 partner-sourced opportunities · objections library |
| 3 | Retainer | 1–2 retainers · repeatable delivery checklist · active partner channel · first anonymised case note |

> ⚠️ **Funding gate:** no fundraising and no team expansion before: 3 paid
> diagnostics · 1 sprint · 1 retainer · proof packs · documented KPIs · clear
> unit economics.
