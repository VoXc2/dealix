## العربية

### زوايا الرسائل الخمس

1. **AI without governance** — السؤال ليس هل يستخدم فريقك AI، بل هل تقدر
   تُثبت المصدر والموافقة والفعل والقيمة.
2. **CRM readiness** — إذا كان CRM لا يثبت ما حدث، فإن AI سيؤتمت الفوضى فقط.
3. **Revenue leakage** — معظم تسرّب الإيراد غير ظاهر في الـ dashboards؛ يختبئ
   في متابعات ضائعة، ملكية غير واضحة، جودة مصدر ضعيفة، أفعال غير معتمدة.
4. **Executive safety** — قبل توسيع AI agents، ابنِ حدود موافقة وأدلة موثقة.
5. **Diagnostic entry** — لا تحتاج منصة جديدة أولاً؛ تحتاج workflow واحداً
   محكوماً ومُشخّصاً من البداية للنهاية.

## English

### The five messaging angles

1. **AI without governance** — the question is not whether your team uses AI,
   but whether you can prove the source, approval, action, and value.
2. **CRM readiness** — if your CRM cannot prove what happened, AI will only
   automate confusion.
3. **Revenue leakage** — most revenue leakage is not visible in dashboards; it
   hides in missed follow-ups, unclear ownership, weak source quality, and
   unapproved actions.
4. **Executive safety** — before scaling AI agents, build approval boundaries
   and evidence trails.
5. **Diagnostic entry** — you do not need a new platform first; you need one
   governed workflow diagnosed end-to-end.

### LinkedIn template 1 — after connection accepted

> Hi [Name], thanks for connecting. Quick question: do you currently have AI
> or revenue automation inside the team, but without full clarity on sources,
> approvals, or impact measurement? I have a short diagnostic that maps risk
> and practical opportunities without buying a new system.

### LinkedIn template 2 — on interest

> Great. The best step is to take just one workflow — like pipeline follow-up,
> CRM quality, or AI usage — and examine it from four angles: source clarity,
> approval boundaries, evidence trail, proof of value. I can send you a sample
> proof pack or we book 20 minutes. Which works better?
