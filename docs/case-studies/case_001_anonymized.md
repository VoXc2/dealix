## Problem — المشكلة

The consultancy had grown its account base organically over four years. The CRM held inconsistent records, duplicate accounts, and no prioritization layer. The sales lead reported spending most of his week deciding *who to call next* rather than *what to say*. Past attempts to clean the data died in spreadsheets. Past attempts to write bilingual outreach produced AR drafts that read like literal translations of EN drafts.

كانت الشركة تعاني من بيانات حسابات مكرّرة وغير متناسقة، وكان فريق المبيعات يستهلك وقته في الفرز لا في التواصل.

---

## Inputs — المدخلات

- **One CSV** with **1,200 records** exported from the client CRM.
- A signed [Source Passport](../04_data_os/SOURCE_PASSPORT.md) declaring: `owner=client`, `source_type=crm_export`, `allowed_use=sprint_only`, `pii_flag=true`, `retention_days=90`.
- One named workflow owner on the client side (Sales Lead).
- One primary workflow declared: revival outreach to dormant mid-tier accounts.

---

## Work performed — الأعمال المُنفَّذة

1. **Source Passport agreed** before any byte was imported (non-negotiable #1, #4 honored).
2. **DQ baseline computed** by `data_os.compute_dq`: **DQ = 47/100**. Weak dimensions: uniqueness (duplicates), completeness (missing decision-maker role), conformance (phone number format drift).
3. **Dedupe** identified and merged **17 duplicate accounts**. The merge rules became a capital asset candidate.
4. **Account scoring** ranked the cleaned set and surfaced the top 10 dormant-but-warm accounts with explicit feature attributions (last interaction recency, sector fit, decision-maker presence).
5. **Draft pack:** **8 Arabic drafts + 4 English drafts** generated by `revenue_os.draft_pack`. Each marked `draft_only` until client approval.
6. **Governance review** via `governance_os.decide` returned a 7-decision log. Notable outcomes:
   - **3 drafts BLOCKED** for unsafe outreach attempts: one used a fabricated reference ("we heard from your CFO..."), one promised a fixed revenue outcome, one embedded an unverified claim about a competitor.
   - **2 drafts REDACTED** for PII over-exposure in the preview field.
   - **All remaining drafts** marked `DRAFT_ONLY` with explicit per-send approval required.

---

## Outputs — المخرجات

- **Proof Pack** assembled with all 14 sections, **proof_score = 82**.
- **Value tier mapped:** `sales_support` (Tier 2 of 4 in the Value Ledger).
- **Capital assets registered (2):**
  1. A dedupe rule for "same-domain, similar-name account merging" (estimated reusable across professional-services sectors).
  2. A governance rule for refusing drafts that contain "we heard from..." constructs without a Source Passport-linked citation.
- **Bilingual deliverables:** AR proof report, EN proof report, governance decisions CSV, capital asset registration certificate.

## Observed value — القيمة المُلاحَظة (Estimated, not verified)

- **DQ baseline → post-sprint:** 47 → **82** (estimated; recomputed on the cleaned dataset, not a market measurement).
- **Duplicates resolved:** **17 duplicate accounts** avoided.
- **Unsafe outreach attempts blocked:** **3** (would-be brand-damaging sends).
- **Founder/team time saved:** estimated **~32 hours** of manual list cleaning and drafting compressed into the 7-day governed sprint. This is a methodology estimate, not a measured productivity study.

**These figures describe what the sprint produced. They do not describe verified pipeline impact or revenue outcomes.**

## Limitations — حدود هذه الحالة

- **Single sector:** results are based on one consultancy; transferability to other sectors is hypothesized, not proven.
- **Estimated value, not verified pipeline impact:** the value figures above describe internal data improvement and governance enforcement. They do not claim closed deals, pipeline acceleration, or revenue lift.
- **Anonymized for client confidentiality:** real revenue figures, account names, and sector sub-segment are intentionally withheld.
- **Time window:** this is a 7-day snapshot. Long-term retention of gains depends on the client maintaining the governance discipline after handoff.
