## What ran — ما الذي شُغِّل

The Sprint orchestrator was invoked end-to-end. Steps 1–8 executed sequentially:

1. **Kickoff Source Passport** — passport written and validated by the gate.
2. **Data Quality (DQ)** — completeness, uniqueness, validity, consistency, timeliness, conformance computed on the 20-row demo file.
3. **Account scoring** — `revenue_os.account_scoring` ranked the 20 accounts by composite score.
4. **Draft pack outline** — Arabic + English bilingual outreach drafts generated for the top 10, marked `DRAFT_ONLY`, never sent.
5. **Governance review** — every output object stamped with a `governance_decision`; the audit trail captured each decision.
6. **Proof Pack assembly** — the 14-section pack assembled from the live engagement state; proof_score computed.
7. **Capital Asset registration** — at least 1 reusable asset deposited into the Capital Ledger.
8. **Retainer readiness check** — `adoption_os.retainer_readiness.evaluate(...)` invoked on the simulated post-Sprint state.

Steps 9–10 (customer-facing close-out and retainer proposal generation) were skipped because there is no customer.

## Limitations — الحدود

This case demonstrates methodology only. It does NOT claim:

- A verified revenue lift for any party.
- A specific customer outcome.
- Performance characteristics on real, messy, regulated production data — only on a 20-row synthetic file.

Value evidence in this case is intentionally low; without a real customer there is no client-confirmed value tier. The Proof Pack score will reflect that — it is expected to land in the `sales_support` or `internal_learning` band rather than `case_candidate`. This is the honest read.

البيانات تركيبية، لا يوجد عميل حقيقي، لا يوجد رفع إيراد مُتحقَّق. المنهجية هي الأثر، لا الادّعاء التجاري. درجة الحزمة المتوقَّعة في نطاق `sales_support` أو `internal_learning`، وهذا متّسق مع غياب تأكيد العميل.
