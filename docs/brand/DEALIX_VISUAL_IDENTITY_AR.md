# Dealix Visual Identity

## Positioning

Dealix is a Saudi-first Revenue Operating System.

## Brand Promise

من فوضى الفرص والمتابعات إلى نظام إيراد واضح.

## Personality

- Executive
- Sharp
- Trustworthy
- Saudi-first
- Approval-first AI

## Colors

- Midnight Navy: #06111F
- Electric Cyan: #22D3EE
- Emerald Proof: #34D399
- Amber Warning: #FBBF24
- Slate Text: #CBD5E1

## Visual Rules

- Dark executive backgrounds.
- Clear hierarchy.
- Big claims only with proof.
- No clutter.
- No generic AI robot visuals.
- Use dashboards, decision rooms, revenue maps, proof packs.

## Logo Usage

Use Dealix as a serious operating system brand, not a playful chatbot brand.
