**Subject:** Governed AI operations — a question about one of your client segments

Hi [Partner Contact Name],

This is not generic AI automation. This is governed AI operations — AI used inside
revenue, trust, and enterprise workflows with approval boundaries, evidence trails,
and proof of value.

I'm building Dealix: the operating control layer for companies using AI in revenue,
trust, and enterprise workflows. Practically, that means source clarity on every
output, explicit approval boundaries before any action, an evidence trail behind each
decision, tenant isolation, trust gates, and no unsupported external action.

I'm being straight with you on stage: Dealix is at controlled-proof stage. The first
engagement is deliberately designed to produce a Proof Pack and a Value Ledger — not
to make claims we can't yet evidence. I'm not bringing you case studies or numbers I
can't stand behind.

The reason I'm writing to you specifically: assurance partners see the part of the
market I care about most — clients who have started using AI but cannot defensibly
buy generic automation without governance, source clarity, and an audit trail.

So one concrete question, not a partnership pitch:

**Do you have a client segment that has started using AI but is not ready to buy
generic automation without governance?**

If yes, I'm not asking for a referral or a program. I'm asking for one
client-segment conversation, or one regulated workflow we could test together — a
low-commitment intro so you can judge whether this is real before anything formal.

Would 20 minutes work? Senior to senior, no deck.

Best,
