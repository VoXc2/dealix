> - مسوّدات عربيّة لكلّ ردّ (تستخدمونها كما هي أو تعدّلونها)
> - متابعة 72 ساعة منظَّمة
> - Proof Pack موقَّع لكلّ مشروع — قابل للنشر بعد إذن

> Pilot 7 أيّام بـ 499 ريال يكشف لكم الفجوة بدقّة قبل أيّ التزام أكبر.

### English

> Dealix doesn't replace your expertise. It solves the OTHER problem:
>
> **You have great training content and strong consulting methodology,
> but your sales cycle is slow, follow-up is ad-hoc, and proof from
> past clients isn't documented in a measurable way.**
>
> Dealix adds to your operations:
> - Unified lead capture (WhatsApp + email + warm LinkedIn)
> - Fast qualification (is this trainee/client a fit?)
> - Arabic-first reply drafts (use as-is or edit)
> - Structured 72h follow-up
> - Signed Proof Pack per engagement — publishable with consent
>
> A 7-day Pilot at 499 SAR reveals the gap precisely before any bigger commitment.

| Their concern | Dealix answer |

| Brand sensitivity (no spam!) | Hard rule: zero cold outreach. Approval gate for every external send. |

## What's specifically forbidden when working with this segment

- ❌ Promising specific number of trainees/clients booked
- ❌ Suggesting Dealix replaces the trainer/consultant
- ❌ Auto-publishing testimonials without explicit per-customer consent
- ❌ Using one customer's content/methodology to pitch another
- ❌ Bulk-emailing past students/attendees

→ Dealix لا يكتب محتواكم. Dealix يكتب ردود تنفيذيّة (مسوّدات إجابات

**"هل تتنافسون مع شركاتكم؟"**
→ Dealix منصّة تنفيذيّة، لا شركة تدريب. لا نقدّم محتوى منافس لكم.

## The unique angle: documented proof

Most training/consulting firms struggle to capture outcomes
quantitatively. Dealix solves this by design:

- Every engagement = ProofEvent records (with PII redaction)
- End of engagement = signed Proof Pack
- Quarterly = aggregated proof report (anonymized) for marketing
- The Proof Pack itself becomes a sales asset for the NEXT client

This is worth more than the 499 SAR Pilot price.
