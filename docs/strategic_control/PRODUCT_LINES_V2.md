# Product Lines v2

Four product lines anchor the firm. Each has a clear target, a clear pain, fixed outputs, and a natural retainer.

## 1. Revenue Intelligence

- **Target:** SMEs and mid-market B2B companies.
- **Problem:** scattered opportunities and revenue.
- **Output:**
  - Data Quality Score
  - Ranked Accounts
  - Draft Pack
  - Mini Pipeline
  - Proof Pack
- **Retainer:** Monthly RevOps OS

## 2. Company Brain

- **Target:** companies with scattered docs and repeated internal questions.
- **Problem:** fragmented knowledge.
- **Output:**
  - Source Registry
  - Q&A with Citations
  - Knowledge Gaps
  - Insufficient Evidence Mode
  - Proof Pack
- **Retainer:** Monthly Company Brain

## 3. Governance Runtime

- **Target:** mid-market and enterprise.
- **Problem:** AI usage without control.
- **Output:**
  - Policy Engine
  - Approval Matrix
  - AI Run Ledger
  - Risk Reports
  - Audit Trail
- **Retainer:** Monthly Governance

## 4. Workflow OS

- **Target:** operations-heavy companies.
- **Problem:** repeated manual operations.
- **Output:**
  - Workflow Map
  - AI-assisted Step
  - Approval Flow
  - SOP
  - Time/Quality Proof Pack
- **Retainer:** Monthly AI Ops

## 5. Cross-line rules

- All four lines share the Core OS, Governance Runtime, and Proof Pack format.
- A line never invents its own governance.
- A line never sells outside the productized stack.
- Cross-line opportunities (e.g., Brain inside a Revenue engagement) are explicit add-ons, not free expansion.

## 6. Anti-patterns

- A line that drifts toward custom AI consulting.
- A line that ships outputs without a Proof Pack.
- A line that grows by hiring instead of by repeating playbooks.
- A line that pursues volume at the expense of QA.

## 7. The principle

> Four lines, one operating model, one proof unit.
