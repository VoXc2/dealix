# Strategic Product Sequence

The order in which Dealix builds matters more than the speed.

## 1. The sequence

1. Revenue Intelligence Sprint
2. Data Readiness Engine
3. Governance Runtime
4. Proof Pack Generator
5. Founder Command Center
6. Client Workspace
7. Company Brain
8. Monthly Retainer Engine
9. AI Control Plane
10. Partner / Academy Portal

## 2. Why this order

- **Revenue Intelligence** brings cash and proof first.
- **Data Readiness** + **Governance Runtime** establish trust before anything else scales.
- **Proof Pack Generator** turns delivery into compounding capital.
- **Founder Command Center** disciplines the firm itself.
- **Client Workspace** is built *after* there is pull for one.
- **Company Brain** expands after the first proof works.
- **Monthly Retainer Engine** stabilizes revenue.
- **AI Control Plane** is the enterprise unlock.
- **Partner / Academy Portal** powers external distribution last.

## 3. Forbidden inversions

- Building **Client Workspace** before there is repeated client demand for one.
- Building the **Partner Portal** before the Dealix Method is documented and stable.
- Building **Academy** before there are proof-backed cases.
- Building **AI Control Plane** as a product before it has run inside engagements.

## 4. Operating discipline

- Each step is reviewed when its predecessor has produced repeat usage.
- Items added to the sequence go through the Office of the Standard.
- Steps are not skipped, even when a buyer asks for a later step early.
- A buyer who needs a later step receives a custom-scoped engagement only with a Decision Log entry justifying the exception.

## 5. The principle

> Order is a strategic decision. The wrong order burns the firm’s capital faster than any single bad project.
