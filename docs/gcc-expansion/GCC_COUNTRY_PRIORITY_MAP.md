# GCC Country Priority Map

## Priority 0 — Beachhead
Saudi Arabia
- Beachhead market for commercial motion.
- Primary buyer: Saudi B2B services and regulated-adjacent buyers.
- Distribution: founder-led + anchor partner intros (Big 4, SAMA-regulated processors, Saudi VC platform teams).
- Trigger to graduate: Invoice #1 paid + one repeatable retainer.

## Priority 1 — First Expansion
United Arab Emirates
- Strong fit for governed AI operations narrative.
- Buyer concentration in financial services, professional services, government-adjacent.
- Activation gate: one signed anchor partner agreement + named buyer signal.
- Risk: noise from generic AI consulting; Dealix differentiates via doctrine and proof.

## Priority 2 — Second Wave
Qatar
- Strong governance and assurance demand.
- Buyer concentration in financial services and state-adjacent enterprises.
- Activation gate: Saudi case study with verified proof + anchor partner.

Bahrain
- Smaller market, faster decision cycles.
- Buyer concentration in financial services and SME services.
- Activation gate: partner-led only.

## Priority 3 — Long Tail
Kuwait
- Activation only after Priority 1-2 retainer motion is proven.

Oman
- Activation only after Priority 1-2 retainer motion is proven.

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- No market is activated commercially without an anchor partner.
- No market is announced before Invoice #1 in that market.
- No market is staffed before retainer motion is proven in at least one prior market.
- No marketing budget is allocated to expansion markets until the previous priority tier has at least one retainer.
