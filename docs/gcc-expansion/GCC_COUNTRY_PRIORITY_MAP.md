## Hard Rule

Dealix **will not** open commercial motion in a country before:

1. Saudi beachhead is healthy (MRR ≥ baseline, churn ≤ baseline).
2. A named partner in the target country is willing to co-sell.
3. A Capital Asset entry has been registered for the country move.

Any deviation is a doctrine violation and the verifier will flag it.
