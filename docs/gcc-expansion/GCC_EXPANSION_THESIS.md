# GCC Expansion Thesis

Dealix is Saudi-first commercially and GCC-ready doctrinally.

The expansion strategy is:
Saudi proof
→ GCC doctrine
→ anchor partner distribution
→ regulated buyer trust
→ country-by-country expansion

Dealix is not launching broadly across the GCC before Invoice #1.

Saudi Arabia remains the commercial beachhead.
UAE is the first expansion/partner market.
Qatar and Bahrain are second-wave.
Kuwait and Oman follow after proof and partner pull.

## Doctrinal vs Commercial Readiness
- Doctrinal readiness: the 11 non-negotiables, governance runtime, evidence plane, and agent safety are designed to work across the GCC regulatory landscape.
- Commercial readiness: country-by-country, gated by an anchor partner relationship and at least one named buyer signal.

## What "GCC-Ready" Does Not Mean
- It does not mean a marketing campaign across all GCC countries.
- It does not mean a localized sales motion in every market.
- It does not mean staffing in every capital city.
- It does not mean self-service signup from every country.

## Failure Mode To Avoid
Announcing a "GCC launch" without one Saudi paying customer and one anchor partner relationship per expansion market.

## Trigger To Expand
For any market beyond Saudi Arabia:
- one anchor partner agreement signed
- one named buyer conversation with budget and timeline
- one Saudi case study with verified proof
- regulatory and data residency review documented

Only after all four are true does the second market activate.
