# Board-Level Thesis

The single sentence Dealix uses with investors, enterprise buyers, and strategic partners.

## 1. EN

> Dealix builds the governed AI operations layer for Saudi and MENA companies. We turn scattered business data, workflows, and knowledge into measurable AI-assisted operating capabilities. Every engagement creates revenue, proof, reusable capital, and product signals.

## 2. AR

> Dealix تبني طبقة تشغيل AI المحكومة للشركات السعودية والإقليمية. نحوّل البيانات والعمليات والمعرفة المبعثرة إلى قدرات تشغيلية مدعومة بالـAI وقابلة للقياس. كل مشروع ينتج إيرادًا، Proof، أصلًا قابلًا لإعادة الاستخدام، وإشارة Productization.

## 3. Why this thesis wins

- It is **explainable in 10 minutes**.
- It is **auditable in 10 hours** (Trust Pack, Proof Pack, AI Run Ledger, audit exports).
- It is **scalable across years** (Core OS, Capital Ledger, Business Units, Ventures).

## 4. The principle

> The thesis is the constraint, not the slogan. Every offer, deck, and contract is checked against it.
