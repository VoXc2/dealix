# Saudi Differentiation

## 1. The differentiation

- Arabic-first business operations
- Saudi-aware AI governance
- PDPL-aware data workflows
- Bilingual executive reporting
- Local business etiquette
- No unsafe outreach automation

## 2. Arabic QA rubric

Every Arabic output measured on: clarity, executive tone, Saudi business fit, no exaggeration, no literal translation, no guaranteed claims, actionability.

## 3. Right vs. wrong language

| Wrong | Right |
| --- | --- |
| نضمن لك مبيعات | نرتب فرص الإيراد ونقيس أثر التحسين عبر Proof Pack |
| نجيب لك عملاء مؤكدين | نساعدك على تحديد الحسابات الأعلى أولوية وتجهيز خطوات تواصل قابلة للمراجعة |

## 4. The principle

> Saudi differentiation is a quality bar, not a localization claim.
