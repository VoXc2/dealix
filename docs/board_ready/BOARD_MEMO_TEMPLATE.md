# Board Memo Template (v12)

Twelve-section monthly memo. Same shape every month so it compares.

## 1. Sections

1. Executive Summary
2. Revenue
3. Retainers
4. Proof
5. Governance
6. Capital Creation
7. Productization
8. Delivery
9. Market
10. Business Units
11. Stop List
12. Next Strategic Bet

## 2. Drafting discipline

- Drafted by the founder or COO; reviewed by BU leads.
- Cites the Decision Log and Proof Library.
- Stop and Scale recommendations cite criteria.
- Strategic bets cap at 1–3 per month.

## 3. Typed surface

`board_ready_os.board_memo.BoardMemoV12` enforces section completeness; `render_board_memo_v12` produces deterministic Markdown.

## 4. The principle

> A board memo turns a founder into a CEO.
