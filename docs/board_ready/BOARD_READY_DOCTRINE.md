AI-assisted operating capabilities. Every engagement creates revenue, proof,
reusable capital, and product signals.

## 7. Agent Risk Board

For each agent: `agent_id`, `owner`, `purpose`, `autonomy_level`, `allowed_inputs`, `allowed_tools`, `forbidden_actions`, `last_audit`, `risk_level`, `incident_count`, `decommission_status`.

Rules: 0–2 allowed in MVP. 3 with approval queue. 4 limited internal post-audit. 5 enterprise-only. 6 forbidden.

## 8. Privacy Runtime Board

PII detected, redactions applied, policy decisions, blocked disclosures, external-use attempts, source passports missing, approval required, approval completed.

## 9. Financial Model — five revenue lines

Diagnostics, Sprints, Retainers, Platform/Workspace, Academy/Partners.

Unit economics per offer: price, delivery hours, AI cost, gross margin, proof strength, retainer conversion, reuse assets created.

## 10. Pricing Power System

Raise price when: proof score consistently >85, delivery repeatable, demand exceeds capacity, retainer conversion exists, governance trust strong, product modules reduce delivery time.

- **Phase 1: Proof Engine** — Capability Diagnostic, Revenue Intelligence Sprint, Source Passport, Governance Runtime basic, Proof Pack, Capital Ledger.
- **Phase 2: Retainer Engine** — Monthly RevOps OS, Client Health, Proof Timeline, Expansion Map, Founder Command Center.
- **Phase 3: Productization Engine** — Data Readiness Engine, Account Scoring Module, Proof Pack Generator, Productization Ledger, Client Workspace MVP.
- **Phase 4: Enterprise Trust Engine** — AI Run Ledger, Agent Registry, Approval Engine, Audit Exports, Incident Response, Trust Pack.
- **Phase 5: Distribution Engine** — Dealix Method, Partner Program, Academy Tracks, Benchmark Reports, Certification.
- **Phase 6: Holding Engine** — Dealix Revenue BU, Dealix Governance BU, Business Unit Scorecards, Venture Signal Engine, Vertical Venture Candidates.
