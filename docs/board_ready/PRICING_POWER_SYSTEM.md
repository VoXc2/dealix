# Pricing Power System

Raise price only when proof rises.

## 1. Triggers (6, ≥ 4 required)

- Proof score consistently > 85
- Delivery is repeatable
- Demand exceeds capacity
- Retainer conversion exists
- Governance trust is strong
- Product modules reduce effort

## 2. Do not lower price

When the client wants heavy custom work, weak data, high risk, or unsafe automation — say instead:

> We start with a smaller Diagnostic to define readiness and risk.

## 3. Typed surface

`board_ready_os.pricing_power.should_raise_price()` requires ≥ 4 of 6 triggers.

## 4. The principle

> Price for capability transformation, not for hours.
