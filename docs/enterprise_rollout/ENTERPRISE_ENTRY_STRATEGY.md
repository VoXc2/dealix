# Enterprise Entry Strategy

لا تدخل برسالة «نعمل AI». ادخل بـ **control + proof**.

## رسالة (إنجليزي)

```text
Your AI risk is not tool access.
Your risk is unmanaged AI execution:
unclear data, unapproved outputs, unlogged agents, no ROI proof, no operating cadence.
```
