# Governance Product Ladder

## Level 1: AI Usage Policy

Basic rules for employees.

## Level 2: Data & AI Risk Review

Review data, tools, use cases.

## Level 3: AI Governance Program

Approval workflows, audit logs, risk register.

## Level 4: Runtime Governance

Governance checks embedded in workflows.

## Level 5: Enterprise AI Control Tower

Central monitoring across agents, workflows, cost, and risks.

This unlocks a strong standalone governance line.
