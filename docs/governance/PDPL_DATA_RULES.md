# PDPL Data Rules

Dealix must document:

- source of data
- purpose of processing
- lawful basis
- retention period
- access permissions
- redaction rules

## Lawful basis (orientation)

Processing of personal data requires a valid basis under PDPL and implementing regulations — commonly **consent**, **contract performance**, **legitimate interest** (where applicable and balanced), or **publicly available data from a lawful public source** when criteria are met. Document the chosen basis per dataset; legal review for high-risk processing.

## AI-ready data practices

Strong metadata, governance, and proportionality reduce delivery risk for AI features.

Reference: [Gartner — AI-ready data](https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2025-02-26-lack-of-ai-ready-data-puts-ai-projects-at-risk).
