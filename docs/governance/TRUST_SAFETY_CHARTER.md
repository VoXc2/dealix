# Trust & Safety Charter

Dealix commits to clients, partners, and the public:

1. **No spam automation** for outbound channels.
2. **No unsourced personal data** use beyond agreed lawful basis.
3. **No fake proof** or fabricated metrics.
4. **No guaranteed sales** claims without defensible, client-grounded evidence.
5. **No source-less factual answers** where the product promises citations (Company Brain / RAG modes).
6. **Human approval** for sensitive external actions (see [`APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`](APPROVAL_MATRIX.md)).
7. **Audit logs** for sensitive operations per [`AUDIT_LOG_POLICY.md`](AUDIT_LOG_POLICY.md).
8. **Proof packs** for every client engagement per [`CLOSED_LOOP_EXECUTION.md`](../company/CLOSED_LOOP_EXECUTION.md).

**Public copy:** outline in [`../public/PUBLIC_TRUST_PAGE.md`](../public/PUBLIC_TRUST_PAGE.md).

Violations → [`../ledgers/GOVERNANCE_LEDGER.md`](../ledgers/GOVERNANCE_LEDGER.md) + escalation.
