# Client Review Meeting

Delivery is not complete when the file is uploaded; it completes when the client **understands** decisions and next steps.

## Agenda

1. Restate **original goal**
2. Show **what we received** (inputs, constraints)
3. Show **what we produced** (artifacts list)
4. Explain **top findings** (3–5 bullets)
5. Walk through **proof pack** ([`../templates/proof_pack_2.md`](../templates/proof_pack_2.md))
6. Discuss **risks / blockers** and governance flags
7. Confirm **next 10 actions** (owner + date where possible)
8. Present **next-step options** ([`../growth/EXPANSION_OFFER_SYSTEM.md`](../growth/EXPANSION_OFFER_SYSTEM.md))

## End state — pick one

```text
Do it yourself
Pilot with Dealix
Monthly retainer
Pause
```

Document the choice in `08_client_handoff.md` (workbench) or `07_next_steps.md` (client portal pattern).

## Rule

Do not leave the call without a **decision** or a **dated** follow-up to decide.
