# Handoff Process

## 1. Context

Stage 6 (Deliver) is where Dealix earns the right to ask for renewal. A
disciplined handoff converts an internal "we built it" into a customer
"we own it" — the customer leaves the session able to run the deliverable
without us standing next to them. Sloppy handoffs are the #1 cause of
non-renewal.

## 2. Audience

CSM (handoff host), assigned engineer (technical Q&A), customer's day-to-day
owner (must attend), customer's exec sponsor (must attend the first 15
minutes), AE (shadowing, runs renewal discussion later).

## 3. Gate Conditions (Before Handoff Can Be Booked)

All must be true:

- Stage 5 (Validate) QA report has `ships=True` (`qa_review.py`).
- Quality Score ≥ 80 (see [`../quality/QUALITY_STANDARD.md`](../quality/QUALITY_STANDARD.md)).
- All 5 QA gates pass.
- Executive report exists and has been internally reviewed by HoCS.
- Audit log is queryable and complete.
- A draft renewal / next-step proposal exists.

If any condition fails, the handoff is rescheduled — never compressed by
skipping a check.

## 4. The Handoff Packet

Generated by `client_handoff.build_handoff(...)`. Every field is mandatory
unless the SOW explicitly waived it.

| Item | Owner | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Deliverables (dashboard, workflow, model, dataset) live in customer env | Engineer | Linked, accessible |
| Executive report (AR + EN, 1–2 pages) | CSM | Non-technical reader test |
| SOP / runbook (how to run, restart, escalate) | Engineer | AR + EN if customer needs both |
| Training video (≤ 30 minutes, recorded) | CSM | Hosted in customer portal |
| Audit log link | Engineer | Read-only to customer admins |
| Proof pack stub | CSM | Will be completed at Stage 7 (Prove) |
| Next-step proposal | AE + CSM | The Stage 8 conversation anchor |

## 5. The Session

60 minutes, recorded with consent, agenda:

1. **0–5 min** — Welcome + agenda. Exec sponsor present.
2. **5–25 min** — Walk the executive report; tie outcomes to the signed
   success metrics.
3. **25–45 min** — Live deliverable demo. Customer's day-to-day owner
   *drives* the demo, CSM and engineer narrate. This is the readiness test.
4. **45–55 min** — Q&A. SOP and runbook walked through.
5. **55–60 min** — Next steps. Renewal proposal previewed (full discussion
   booked separately per [`RENEWAL_PROCESS.md`](RENEWAL_PROCESS.md)).

## 6. Signoff

The customer signs the handoff packet (electronic OK) within 24 hours.
Signoff is binary: accepted OR a written punch-list with a target close date.

`HandoffPacket.mark_signed()` is called by the router once signature is
received; this fires the `handoff_signed` event into the event store and
advances the Stage Machine.

## 7. Anti-Patterns

- **Demo by Dealix**: if Dealix drives the demo, the customer is not yet
  trained. Reschedule training.
- **Acceptance by silence**: no countersign = no signoff. Chase within 48h.
- **Punch-list creep**: a long punch-list is really a CR. Route it through
  [`CHANGE_REQUEST_PROCESS.md`](CHANGE_REQUEST_PROCESS.md).
- **No exec sponsor**: handoff without exec sponsor present de-anchors
  renewal. Reschedule.
