## Context
This is the kickoff template that opens every Dealix engagement. It
turns a signed scope into a working project by aligning people,
scope, data, governance, and success metrics in a single session. It
sits inside the Delivery Lifecycle
(`docs/delivery/DELIVERY_LIFECYCLE.md`) and feeds the Discover stage
of the Delivery Standard (`docs/delivery/DELIVERY_STANDARD.md`). It
inherits the client relationship motion from
`docs/CUSTOMER_SUCCESS_PLAYBOOK.md` and the productized pilot shape
from `docs/business/MANAGED_PILOT_OFFER.md`.

## Onboarding Session Agenda (60-75 min)
1. **Introductions** (10 min) — Dealix delivery lead, AI platform
   lead, QA reviewer; client sponsor, business owner, technical
   contact, data owner.
2. **Scope confirmation** (10 min) — read back the signed scope,
   confirm in-scope and out-of-scope items, surface any drift.
3. **Data request handoff** (15 min) — walk through
   `docs/templates/data_request.md`; agree owners, formats, access,
   and sensitivity.
4. **Governance flags review** (10 min) — declare action classes,
   PII presence, approval routing per
   `docs/governance/APPROVAL_MATRIX.md`.
5. **Success metric definition** (15 min) — define the hero metric,
   baseline measurement, and proof-pack target.
6. **Weekly cadence** (5 min) — confirm standup time, check-in time,
   demo time, and emergency contact.
7. **Next steps** (5 min) — owners, dates, blocking dependencies.

## Pre-Session Checklist
- Scope and DPA signed.
- Workspace provisioned with named owners and access logged.
- Data request template populated with placeholders.
- Governance pre-assessment completed (PII, channel, approval class).
- Success metric drafted in collaboration with the client sponsor.

## Post-Session Artifacts
- Completed `intake_form.md` filed under the project.
- Populated `data_request.md` with owners and dates.
- Governance plan with declared action classes.
- Weekly cadence calendar invites sent.
- Kickoff note signed by client sponsor.

## Roles
- **Delivery lead** — runs the agenda, owns the post-session
  artifacts.
- **AI platform lead** — confirms feasibility and workflow shape.
- **QA reviewer** — confirms QA criteria and bilingual coverage.
- **Governance lead** — confirms approval routing.
- **Client sponsor** — final decision-maker for scope and metrics.
- **Client data owner** — accountable for the data request.

## Failure Modes And Recovery
- **Sponsor not present** — reschedule; do not run without the
  decision-maker.
- **Data owner not identified** — defer Data Request handoff; assign
  an interim owner.
- **No agreed hero metric** — escalate to founder; do not proceed to
  Build.
- **PII present but no lawful basis** — block engagement until basis
  is documented.

## Metrics
- **Onboarding completion** — share of engagements with all five
  post-session artifacts. Target: 100%.
- **Time to kickoff** — business days from contract sign to kickoff.
  Target: ≤ 3 days.
- **Sponsor attendance rate** — share of kickoffs with the sponsor
  present. Target: 100%.
- **Hero metric agreed** — share of kickoffs ending with a recorded
  hero metric. Target: 100%.
