### مهام يومية

- [ ] تشغيل Risk Score المجاني مع أول مرشح
- [ ] جدولة مكالمة تشخيص مع كل من رد

**يوم 6–7:**

## English summary

This is the 90-day execution plan for the **Governed Revenue & AI Ops** category.
The public offer is the **7-Day Governed Revenue & AI Ops Diagnostic** (Starter
4,999 · Standard 9,999 · Executive 15,000 · Enterprise 25,000 SAR). The first 90
days focus on three ICPs only — **B2B Services, Clinics / Healthcare operators,
and Consultants / Implementers**. The ladder then runs Diagnostic → Revenue
Intelligence Sprint (25,000 SAR+) → Governed Ops Retainer (4,999–35,000 SAR/mo).
All figures above are operating goals, not guarantees; revenue is a function of
Governed Value Decisions Created (the North Star), not customer count alone.
