# 12 — Category Dominance and Expansion

## Objective

Build and defend category leadership in governed revenue + AI operations through narrative control, proof discipline, trust posture, and repeatable GCC outcomes.

## Dominance pillars

1. Clear category narrative and language control
2. Proof-backed customer outcomes
3. Trust-led enterprise credibility
4. Productized execution quality
5. Regional expansion discipline

## Expansion model

- Expand by:
  - Segment fit
  - Regulatory fit
  - Delivery fit
  - Partner fit
- No expansion without readiness evidence and risk controls.

## Category asset system

- Benchmark reports
- Executive case narratives
- Trust and governance whitepapers
- Sector-specific playbooks
- Procurement acceleration kits

## Dominance KPIs

- Category share of voice in target segments
- Enterprise win rate on governed AI positioning
- Expansion revenue from existing accounts
- Time-to-trust in enterprise procurement cycles
