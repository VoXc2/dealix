# 08 — Founder-Led GTM System Playbook

## Objective

Convert founder-led enterprise selling into a repeatable revenue operating system without losing quality, trust, or close-rate strength.

## GTM pipeline

1. Target account selection
2. Qualification and intent mapping
3. Discovery and diagnostic framing
4. Pilot proposal and trust framing
5. Proof review and conversion
6. Expansion and retainer growth

## Operating rules

- No outbound tactic outside governance policy.
- Every opportunity maps to one service ladder path.
- Every proposal includes proof strategy and risk boundaries.
- Every lost deal includes structured loss-reason learning entry.

## GTM assets

- Discovery script standard
- Diagnostic template standard
- Pilot offer standard
- Trust/compliance objection handling pack
- Procurement response snippets
- ROI narrative templates by segment

## Core GTM KPIs

- Stage conversion rates
- Cycle time from discovery to pilot
- Pilot-to-retainer conversion
- Expansion revenue ratio
- Win/loss quality metrics
