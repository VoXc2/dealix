# 10 — Delivery Control Tower

## Objective

Operate delivery as a managed system with live risk visibility, quality gates, and predictable throughput.

## Control tower capabilities

- In-flight engagement visibility
- Dependency blocker tracking
- Quality gate status by stage
- Governance exception monitoring
- Proof readiness tracking

## Stage gates

1. Intake readiness gate
2. Scope clarity gate
3. Data readiness gate
4. Governance gate
5. QA gate
6. Proof pack gate
7. Handoff and expansion gate

## Risk model

- Severity and probability scoring per engagement
- Risk aging and escalation policy
- Owner and due date enforcement
- Run-level trace linkage for critical risks

## Delivery KPIs

- Cycle time per service
- Rework rate
- On-time milestone ratio
- Governance exception rate
- Proof acceptance rate
