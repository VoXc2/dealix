# 09 — Unit Economics Governance

## Objective

Ensure growth is economically durable by governing offer-level and lifecycle-level unit economics.

## Economic layers

- Offer-level margin
- Delivery cost per engagement
- Tooling and compute cost envelope
- Acquisition efficiency
- Expansion efficiency

## Governance checks

- Margin floor policy per offer tier
- Delivery overrun threshold alerts
- CAC payback monitoring
- Churn and retention quality tracking
- Discount governance with approval policy

## Required dashboards

- Gross margin by offer
- Contribution margin by customer cohort
- CAC payback by acquisition channel
- Expansion revenue by account maturity
- Cost-to-serve trendline

## Decision policy

- Offers below margin floor require redesign or pricing update.
- High expansion with low margin triggers delivery/process redesign.
- Strong margin with weak conversion triggers GTM messaging and qualification revision.
