## القسم 4 — Procurement Policy

### 4.1 Approval Levels
| المبلغ | الموافق |
|--------|---------|
| <500 ر.س | Manager |
| 500-5,000 ر.س | Director / Founder |
| 5,000-20,000 ر.س | Founder + Advisor |
| >20,000 ر.س | Board approval |

### 4.2 RFP Process (for >5,000 ر.س)
1. Define requirements (1 page)
2. Identify 3+ vendors
3. Send RFP (1 week response)
4. Compare proposals (1 week)
5. Decision + negotiation
6. Contract signed
7. Performance review (3 months)

### 4.3 Vendor Review (Quarterly)
- Performance vs SLA
- Cost vs value
- Security posture
- Support quality
- Renewal decision

## القسم 10 — Cost Optimization Tactics

### 10.1 Annual Reviews
