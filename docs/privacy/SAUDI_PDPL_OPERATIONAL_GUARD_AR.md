## 1. Purpose

This document specifies operational requirements for Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) compliance.

---

## 2. PDPL Key Requirements

### 2.1 Lawful Basis

| Basis | When Applicable | Documentation |
|-------|------------------|----------------|
| Consent | Marketing, WhatsApp | Written consent record |
| Contract | Service delivery | Agreement on file |
| Legal obligation | Tax, regulatory | Law cited |
| Legitimate interest | Internal ops | Balance test documented |

### 2.2 Data Subject Rights

| Right | Response Time | Implementation |
|-------|---------------|----------------|
| Access request | 10 days | DSR process |
| Rectification | 10 days | Update records |
| Erasure | 10 days | Deletion process |
| Restriction | Immediate | Mark as restricted |
| Portability | 10 days | Export format |
| Objection | Immediate | Stop processing |

### 2.3 Breach Notification

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Notification authority | SDAIA (Saudi Data & AI Authority) |
| Timeframe | 72 hours from awareness |
| Customer notification | If high risk to individuals |
| Documentation | Record all breaches |

---

## 3. Operational Requirements

### 3.1 Data Mapping

| Data Type | Source | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|--------|---------|-----------|
| Business contact | Outbound | Sales | 2 years post engagement |
| Client data | Contracts | Service delivery | Contract + 5 years |
| Lead data | Website/forms | Sales pipeline | 1 year without engagement |
| Consent records | Forms/emails | Compliance | Consent + 3 years |

### 3.2 Security Measures

| Measure | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Encryption | At rest and in transit |
| Access control | Role-based, minimum necessary |
| Audit logging | All data access logged |
| Incident response | 72-hour breach notification |
| Regular review | Annual PDPL compliance review |

---

## 4. Cross-Border Transfer

| Rule | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Within KSA | No restriction |
| To adequate countries | Allowed |
| To other countries | Explicit consent or legal basis |
