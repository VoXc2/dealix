## Preamble

Whereas Party A is a provider of an integrated AI platform for CRM and sales automation,
and Party B wishes to subscribe to the platform services, the parties agree as follows:

## Article 1: Definitions

1.1 **Platform:** The Dealix AI Platform.
1.2 **Services:** The services agreed upon in Appendix (A) of this Agreement.
1.3 **Data:** All data and information uploaded by Party B to the platform.
1.4 **Users:** Party B's authorized personnel licensed to use the platform.

## Article 2: Scope of Services

2.1 Party A shall provide Party B with the services detailed in Appendix (A).
2.2 Services include: Multi-tenant AI platform, data analytics tools, marketing automation, and performance reporting.
2.3 Party A reserves the right to develop the platform and add new features with notice to Party B.

## Article 3: Financial Obligations

3.1 Party B shall pay the setup fees as specified in Appendix (A).
3.2 Party B shall pay monthly/annual subscription fees in advance.
3.3 All amounts are in Saudi Riyals and subject to 15% VAT.
3.4 Late payment may result in service suspension after 30 days of notice.

## Article 4: Party B Obligations

4.1 Provide accurate information required for service delivery.
4.2 Maintain confidentiality of access credentials and not share with third parties.
4.3 Comply with the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL).
4.4 Not use the platform for any illegal activity or in violation of Saudi regulations.

## Article 5: Data Protection and Privacy

5.1 Party A shall protect Party B's data in accordance with PDPL.
5.2 Data processing occurs within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
5.3 Party B acknowledges that data processing follows the Data Processing Agreement attached hereto.
5.4 Party B has the right to request data export or deletion at any time.

## Article 6: Service Level

6.1 Party A guarantees 99.9% monthly platform availability.
6.2 Support response time: 4 hours during business hours, 12 hours outside business hours.
6.3 If service level falls below the agreed threshold, Party B is entitled to credits per the penalty schedule in Appendix (B).

## Article 7: Intellectual Property

7.1 The platform and all associated intellectual property rights remain the property of Party A.
7.2 Party B's data remains its sole property.
7.3 Party A may use anonymized data for service improvement.

## Article 8: Confidentiality

8.1 Each party agrees not to disclose the other party's confidential information.
8.2 Confidentiality obligations continue for 5 years after agreement termination.

## Article 9: Term and Termination

9.1 This Agreement is effective for {{term}} commencing from the signing date.
9.2 The contract auto-renews for a similar period unless either party gives 30 days' written notice of non-renewal.
9.3 Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice.

## Article 10: Force Majeure

10.1 Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to circumstances beyond its reasonable control.

## Article 11: Governing Law

11.1 This Agreement is governed by Saudi law.
11.2 Any dispute shall be settled by arbitration at the Riyadh Chamber of Commerce.

## Article 12: General Provisions

12.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
12.2 Party B may not assign this Agreement without Party A's written consent.
12.3 Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both parties.
