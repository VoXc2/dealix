## 3. Scope — Inclusions

- Ingest up to 5,000 customer-supplied records (CRM export / spreadsheet / seed list).
- Data cleanup + dedupe + Saudi entity normalization.
- ICP refinement (or co-defined ICP on Day 1 if not present).
- Scoring engine run → top 50 ranked accounts.
- Top 10 ranked next-best-actions with bilingual outreach drafts.
- Mini CRM board (kanban with stages, owner, due date).
- Bilingual executive report (PDF + JSON).
- 60-minute recorded handoff session.

## 4. Out of Scope — Exclusions

- Paid digital marketing campaigns.

## 6. Success Criteria (binding)

The Sprint is considered successfully delivered when **all** are true:
- Data quality score ≥ 90 on the cleaned dataset.
- Top 50 accounts delivered within 10 business days.
- ≥ 8 of 10 ranked outreach drafts approved by the Customer without major edits.
- The Customer can independently use the Mini CRM by the handoff session.
- PDPL Art. 13 notice text is included in every outreach draft.
